I Think I'll Take Off Walkin'    (Q 933)  Jerry Routh

OPENER



Grand Square
MY HEAD KEEPS POUNDIN' FROM A LATE NIGHT DROWNIN'
IN THE COFFEE AND THE BOOZE
THAT LOUD MOUTH WOMAN JUST DRIVES ME CRAZY
WITH HER KNOW-IT-ALL ATTITUDE,
Allemande Left & Weave
I CAN SAY ONE THING AND SHE SAYS WRONG
Swing that girl and Promenade
SHE NEVER SAYS, WELL YOU MIGHT BE RIGHT
SO I THINK I'LL TAKE OFF WALKIN'

FIGURE



Heads Square Thru count 'em 4 hands round
You make a Right Hand Star
The Heads Star Left, roll it one time and then
Same pair and do a Dosado
Swing Thru and then the Boys Trade
Swing the corner lady round and Promenade
I THINK I'LL TAKE OFF WALKIN'
AND LOSE THESE BLUES

MIDDLE BREAK



Grand Square
THE DOOR BELL'S RINGIN' CAUSE THE MAILMAN'S BRINGING ME
GREETINGS FROM THE IRS
THE BILL COLLECTOR KEEPS WRITING ME LETTERS
AND MY CAR'S BEEN REPOSSESSED,
Allemande Left & Weave
I'D LIKE TO TELL THE BOSS TO SHOVE IT
THERE AIN'T NO USE A TALKIN'
AIN'T WASTIN' MY TIME ON SAD GOODBYES
SO I THINK I'LL TAKE OFF WALKIN'

CLOSER



Circle Left
I THINK I'LL TAKE OFF WALKIN CAUSE
THIS OLD PICKUP WON'T GO
Left Allemande the corner, Dosado you own
Left Allemande and Weave The Ring
THERE'S ONLY ONE WIPER WORKIN'
AND THE METAL'S SHOWIN' THROUGH
I THINK I'LL TAKE OFF WALKIN'
AND LOSE THESE BLUES
