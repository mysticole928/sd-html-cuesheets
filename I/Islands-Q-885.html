Islands   (Quadrille 885 )   Bob Huff

OPENER



Four ladies chain, you turn that girl and go
You roll away and circle left
Four ladies roll away, you circle and then
Allemande left, weave that ring
Wind in and out, swing your girl and promenade
We could be sailing out under the southern cross

FIGURE



Head 2 you square thru 4 hands around you know
Dosado and make your wave
Swing thru, girls circulate, boy trade
Boys run right, bend the line, square thru 3 hands around
Left allemande, swing the next and promenade
Where is that harbor, where is the one I love

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Sides face grand square
I hear you calling "wanderin' boy come home soon"
I hear the music, I see your face in the moon
Allemande left, weave the ring
Wind in and out, swing your girl and promenade
I may sail forever and never find that island again

TAG



Allemande left, swing your own
I'll sail forever and never find that island again
