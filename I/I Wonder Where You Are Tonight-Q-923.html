I Wonder Where You Are Tonight   (Quadrille 923 )   Sam Lowe, Stan Russell

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies promenade, go once around
Back home, swing with your own
Circle to the left, go moving round
Left allemande that corner lady, weave the ring
The rain is cold and slowly falling
Swing and promenade by two
And though your love is even colder
I wonder where you are tonight

FIGURE



Heads promenade, travel halfway
Walk in, square thru go 4
Go all the way around, swing thru
Boys run to the right and ferris wheel
Square thru and count 'em 3 hands around
Swing the corner, promenade
And though your love is even colder
I wonder where you are tonight
