It Takes A Worried Man    (CARD 4)   Jerry Routh

OPENER



4 ladies promenade, go once inside the ring
Get back home, find your man, give him a great big swing
Join all your hands, circle to the left and go
Do an allemande the corner, weave the ring
It takes a worried man to sing a worried song
Swing that girl around & promenade along
It takes a worried man to sing a worried song
I'm worried now, but I won't be worried long

FIGURE



Head/side 2 couples promenade, 1/2 way around
Into the middle right & left thru, turn that girl around
Now square thru, go 4, 4 hands around
when you meet the outside 2, swing thru
Boys run to the right, wheel & deal around
Swing that corner lady round, promenade around you go
It takes a worried man to sing a worried song
I'm worried now, but I won't be worried long
