Island In The Sun
Harry Belafonte 1957
(Tanz 17009) Dennis Gagne
OPENER

Four ladies chain 3/4 around
Join your hands , circle left around the town
Put the ladies in, men sashay and go left allemande
Weave by two , it's in and out, turn partner by the right
Four ladies promenade inside the ring
Get back home and give that man a swing
You promenade that ring with fun
All the way to my island in the sun

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) pass thru , separate round one
Make a line of four, go up to the middle and back
Girls half sashay , boys half sashay
Centers rollaway , all go right & left thru
Square thru and go 3/4 round
Swing that corner girl and promenade home
I hope the day will shortly come
That I'll be home to my island in the sun
