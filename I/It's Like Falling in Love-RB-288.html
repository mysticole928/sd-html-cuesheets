It's Like Falling In Love    (RB 288)   Ron Dunbar

OPENER



Four ladies chain across the ring
Turn that girl, chain her home again
Join hands and circle to the left
Left allemande, come weave the ring
You get a little closer in my arms
Dosado and promenade along
It's just like falling in love
Over and over again

FIGURE



Heads promenade about halfway
Walk into the middle and square thru four hands I say
Four hands around with a right & left thru
You veer to the left, hey, ferris wheel you do
Centers square thru 3/4 around
Swing the corner and promenade the ring
It's just like falling in love
Over and over again
