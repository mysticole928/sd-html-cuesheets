Four ladies chain 3/4 go round
Join hands and then you circle the town
It's up to you, where do we go from here?
Roll away and weave the ring
I don't want a love I have to share
Swing your darlin' and promenade the square

FIGURE 1



Join hands and circle, halfway you go
Head couples do a right & left thru
Same two do a 1/2 square thru
Dosado and do an eight chain four (keep in time)
I don't want just a part of you
Swing your corner, then promenade you do
There just can be no in between - If you want me

MIDDLE BREAK



Well four ladies chain
Rollaway and then you circle left and then
It's up to you, where do we go from here?
Rollaway and weave the ring
I don't want just a part of you
Swing your darlin', promenade her too
There just can be no in between - If you want me

FIGURE 2



Heads promenade half way
Come down the middle and 1/2 square thru
Sides rollaway with a 1/2 sashey, slide thru
Centers trade, everybody pass the ocean
All eight circulate
Swing and promenade
There just can be no in between - If you want me
