I Overlooked An Orchid    (BS 1543)   Jack Livingston

Walk all around your corner and see saw round your taw



2 & 4 go right & left thru and you turn them twice and then
The heads lead to the right, left allemande
Grand ole right & left around and dosado your maid
Allemande left your corner, come back one and promenade
I overlooked an orchid, while searching for a rose
But I found an orchid just like you

FIGURE



Four ladies chain 3/4 round, turn 'em round you do
The heads go forward up and back with you
Half square thru to the outside two, square thru 3/4 round
And down the line we'll do a left allemande
Your partner dosado, then swing that corner maid
Allemande left your corner, come back one and promenade
I overlooked an orchid, while searching for a rose
But I found an orchid just like you
