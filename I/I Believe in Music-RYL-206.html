I Believe In Music    (RYL 206)   Tony Oxendine

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
rollaway and circle left go round I sing
so clap your hands, stomp your feet and shake your tambourine
do an allemande left that corner and weave the ring
I believe in music
swing and whirl the girl and promenade
I believe in music, I believe in love

FIGURE



heads/sides square thru 4 hands you go
all the way and do a right & left thru & turn your beau
pass thru, trade by and do a little dosado
make a wave, swing thru, double, all 8 circulate
swing and promenade
I tell you I believe in music, I believe in love
