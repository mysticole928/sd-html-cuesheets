I Want A Girl
Al Jolson; Eddie Cantor; many others 1911
(Top 25208) Paul Hartman
BREAK

All 4 ladies chain the ring
Circle left now while I sing
About the girl who married dear old Dad
Roll the girl a 1/2 sashay
And circle left in the same old way
Roll away and then you weave the ring
And when you meet a good old fashioned girl with heart so true
Dosado your honey and she'll promenade with you and sing
I want a girl just like the girl that married dear old Dad

FIGURE

Head two couples promenade
Halfway round there with your maid
Lead to the right and circle to a line
In lines of 4 go up to the middle and back
Right & left thru across the track
* Two ladies chain and keep in time
Send them back a dixie style and balance , all 8 circulate
Allemande left a new corner
Come back and promenade , sing
I want a girl just like the girl that married dear old Dad

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

*Turn the girl and then star thru
Go right & left thru and then you pass thru once more
Swing the corner girl and promenade the floor, sing
