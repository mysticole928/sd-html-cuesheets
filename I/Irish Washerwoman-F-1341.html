Irish Washerwoman
(Folkraft 1341) Frank Kaltman
OPENER - CLOSER

Swing with your partners
Swing with your corners
Dosado with your partners all
Dosado with your corners all
Allemande left
Grand right & left all the way around the ring
Swing when you meet

PATTERN

Gentlemen lead to the right of the ring
There you balance, there you swing
Hear the call (keep swinging for 8 total beats)
Allemande left and promenade all

(Repeat PATTERN four times so each boy swings and promenades each girl home once.
Then repeat pattern four times having the girls lead right to get a new boy each
time. Finish with INTRO again.)
