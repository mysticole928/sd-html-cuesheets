If You've Got The Money
Willie Nelson 1950
   (Lightning S 5025)    Ed Fraidenburg
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Four ladies chain 3/4 round you go
Join hands and circle to the left around you know
Ladies in the men sashay , circle left and then
Face your partner, weave by 2 , and then get a right & left grand
Hand over hand you go until you meet again
Promenade your lady, go walking down the line
If you've got the money, honey I got the time

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

1 & 3 (2 & 4) you flutter wheel and roll it round you go
Do a curlique and then the boys run right you know
Circle four with the outside two you break and make a line
Forward up and you come on back and curlique in time
Boys run around that girl, trade by and then
Do an allemande left and you come on back and promenade again
Promenade your honey, go walking down the line
If you've got the money, honey I got the time

TAG

If you run out of money, honey I'll run out of time

Editor's note: For the opener-middlebreak-closer, the original cue sheet said: Ladies in the men sashay, circle left. Swing thru go forward two, then right & left grand. Clearly swing thru can not be done from a circle left. I changed the sequence to face your partner (original opposite) and weave by 2.
