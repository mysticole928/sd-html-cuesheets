FIGURE # 1

FIGURE # 2



Heads promenade halfway
Walk into the middle and square thru 4 hands, 4 hands round
Do a right & left thru, turn your girl

Move up, do a 1/2 tag and scoot back, your corner swing
Swing a little corner lady and promenade go round the ring
It don't mean a thing, if it ain't got that swing
Do-wa, do-wa, do-wa, do-wa-do-do
