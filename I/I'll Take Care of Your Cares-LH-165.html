I'll Take Care Of Your Cares    (LH 165)   Ralph Silvius

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande your corner, dosado around your pet
4 men star by the left go round that ole set
Turn the partner go once and a half and then
4 ladies promenade go one time around
Home you go, dosado and get to the corner now
Allemande your corner, promenade somehow
Keep me in your thoughts, your dreams, and your prayers
And I'll take care of your cares

FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain 3/4 round you go
Head 2 go right & left thru and turn 'em you know
Side ladies chain 3/4, head men turn them and then
Roll away, make a line of 3, get up to the middle and back and then
Slide thru, left allemande and walk by your own
Swing with the next girl and you promenade home
Keep me in your thoughts, your dreams, and your prayers
And I'll take care of your cares

SEQUENCE: (Opener, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides, ending)
