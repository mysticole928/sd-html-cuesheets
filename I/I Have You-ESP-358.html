I Have You
Glen Campbell 1987
(ESP 358) Tom Miller
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK

4 ladies chain 3/4 round you go
You rollaway and circle left
Sailors have the seven seas and four strong winds that blow
Allemande left & weave the ring
And the flowers have the early April rain
Swing your girl and promenade
And the dreamers have tomorrow if today does not come true
You have eyes that shine and I have you

FIGURE

Heads (sides) promenade and go halfway
Walk in , square thru go 4
All the way around and do the right & left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel
Square thru and go 3 hands
Swing that corner girl and promenade
And the dreamers have tomorrow if today does not come true
You have eyes that shine and I have you

CLOSER

4 ladies chain about 3/4
Rollaway and then you circle left
Heroes have their name engraved forever in a stone
Left allemande & weave the ring
And the flowers have the early April rain
Swing your girl and promenade
And the dreamers have tomorrow if today does not come true
You have eyes that shine and I have you
