I'll Go Steppin' Too
   (Crown 178)    Tom Roper
OPENER    Basic

Walk around your corner , seesaw your own
Join up your hands and circle left you go
Boys star right inside the ring
Allemande left your corner, turn your partner right
4 ladies promenade inside the ring, come back home
Star thru and promenade
From now on when you step out, I'll tell you what I'll do
I'll lock the door, put out the cat, and I'll go steppin' too

FIGURE    B - SSD Week 10, corner progression

Head (side) couples promenade half way around the ring
Side (head) couples square thru and you get 4 hands around
Go all the way do a right & left thru and then
Veer over to the left and ferris wheel you do
Centers square thru 3 hands around
Swing your corner girl and promenade the ring
From now on when you step out, I'll tell you what I'll do
I'll lock the door, put out the cat, and I'll go steppin' too

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

4 girls (boys) promenade inside the ring
Run back home and everybody swing
Join up your hands and circle left go round
Allemande left your corner, weave around the town
Yes, I'll go stepping too, my honey, I'll go steppin' too
Swing your girl and promenade you do
From now on when you step out, I'll tell you what I'll do
I'll lock the door, put out the cat, and I'll go steppin' too
