I Want A Girl (Just Like The Girl)
Al Jolson; Eddie Cantor; many others 1911
(TNT 253) Al Brundage
OPENER

Four little ladies promenade once around the ring and then
Get on home and swing the one you know
Join hands and make a ring , circle to the left and then
Left allemande and weave the ring
A good old fashioned girl with heart so true
Meet and dosado and promenade you two, singing'
I want a girl just like the girl
That married dear old Dad

FIGURE

Head two couples square thru four hands around and go
Round your corner lady, dosado
Swing thru two by two, spin the top and then
Men move up and do the right & left thru
Flutter wheel across, sweep 1/4 more
Swing the corner lady and promenade the floor
I want a girl just like the girl
That married dear old Dad
