I Saw Your Face In The Moon    (BO 1245)   Keith Thomsen

INTRO - ENDING



Allemande left your corner, turn the partner by the right
4 ladies promenade 'em inside
Come home, you swing, swing, swing your man and then
Allemande left and weave around that ring
In and out you go, until you meet your Joe
Dosado and then you promenade
Promenade that ring, take her home, swing and sway
I saw your face in the moon

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain, you turn the girl and then
Then heads promenade 'em halfway around
Now in to the middle and square thru 4 hands you go
To the outside two, star thru, then sweep 1/4
You dive thru, pass thru, your corner allemande
Come home, swing the girl and promenade
You promenade that ring, take her home and hear me sing
I saw your face in the moon
