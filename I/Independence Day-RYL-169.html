Independence Day    (RYL 0169)  Jerry Story

OPENER



Circle Left
Well she looked all right by dawn's early light
Though she looked a little worried and weak
Allemande on the corner and you dosado
Allemande the corner and weave the ring
Let freedom ring,
(let the white dove sing)
Swing and Promenade
Roll the stone away, let the guilty pay,
It's Independence Day

FIGURE



H/S promenade half way 'round --
Walk in and square thru 4 --
Go all the 'round -- right and left thru
Veer left and ferris wheel once more--
Square thru 3, swing corner promenade
Roll the stone away, let the guilty pay
It's Independence Day

CLOSER



Circle Left
Let freedom ring, let the white dove sail
Left Allemande and Dosido
Allemande and Weave the Ring --
Let the weak be strong,
(let the right be wrong)
Swing and Promenade
Roll the stone away, let the guilty pay
It's Independence Day
