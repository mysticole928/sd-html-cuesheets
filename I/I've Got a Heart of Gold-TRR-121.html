I've Got A Heart Of Gold    (TRR 121)   Gerald McWhirter

Four little ladies chain, go straight across that ring



Gonna join hands and circle to the left go movin' round
Left allemande, weave the ring
Gonna weave it in and out, until you meet your own
Swing your partner round and promenade
You promenade along, with the one you call your own
She's got a heart of gold

FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru 4 hands around you go
Make a right hand star with the outside 2 and turn it once you know
Heads (sides) star by the left once around and then
To the same old two you swing thru and then, boys trade
Turn thru and then, you go left allemande
Come back one and swing and promenade
You promenade along, with the one you call your own
She's got a heart of gold

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Four little ladies chain 3/4 round you go
1 & 3 (2 & 4) you promenade halfway
2 & 4 (1 & 3) go right & left thru and then you roll away
Join sixteen and circle left you know
Four ladies (gents) square thru 4 hands around you go
Swing that man around and promenade
Promenade along, with the one you call your own
She's got a heart of gold
