I'll See You In My Dreams    (BO 1169)   Bob Radford

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain across the ring
Turn and chain her home then you swing
You promenade, well don't you slow down
Head two couples wheel around, right & left thru the town
Why don't you cross trail thru, go allemande left
Come on back and dosado, promenade the set
Take your lady round the ring and then
Swing her in your dreams

FIGURE



Head two couples promenade go halfway around
Into the middle and star thru, pass thru you do
Dosado make an ocean wave, up and back and then
Square thru 4 hands, 4 hands and then
Bend the line and rollaway, up and back you go
Allemande left that corner, pass the one you know
Swing the right hand lady, promenade the ring
And then you see her in your dreams
