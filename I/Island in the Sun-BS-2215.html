Island in the Sun   (Blue Star 2215 )   Nate Bliss

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande the corner lady, turn the partner right hand around
Boys star left across, turn the opposite lady right
Allemande new corner, go forward three
With a right, left, right, turn thru with three
Allemande the corner, come weave the ring with me
Go in, go out, till you meet that girl again
Promenade the lady go round the ring you run
Take her right back home to your island in the sun

FIGURE



One and three promenade go 3/4 round
Two and four star thru and pass thru you do
Same two promenade the ring while the other two ladies chain
Turn that girl and all promenade keep walking round the ring
Sides wheel around, a right and left thru
Star thru, pass thru, swing somebody new
Promenade the lady round the ring you run
Take 'em right back home to your island in the sun
