Island In The Sun    (GR 12168)   Ed Fraidenburg

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande left your corner walk on back a do sa do
Curlique and the boys run right you know
Circle left go moving round the land
Left allemande and weave around the ring
Weave in and out until you meet your maid
Do sa do, then we'll all promenade
Promenade your lady round the ring you run
Take her home and swing her on your island in the sun

FIGURE



Four ladies chain three quarters round the ring
Heads promenade three quarters round I sing
Sides square thru three quarters round ant then
Do sa do and make a wave my friend
Swing thru and then go right and left grand
On third hand promenade go round the land
Promenade this lady round the ring you run
Take her home and swing her on your island in the sun
