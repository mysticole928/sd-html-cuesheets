INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Join hands circle left go walkin' round the town

Go in and out when you meet her box the gnat
4 ladies promenade the track

Allemande left your corner, promenade this honey bee
I'll be around if your new love turns you down
And I'll come runnin' to you
