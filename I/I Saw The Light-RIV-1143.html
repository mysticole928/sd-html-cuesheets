FIGURE (Basic)

Head ladies chain
Heads lead left
Dosado
Swing thru
Boys run
Bend the line
Forward and back
Pass thru
Reverse wheel around
Reverse flutter wheel
Keep her promenade

FIGURE (Mainstream)

Heads square thru
Right hand star
Heads star left
Right & left thru
1/2 sashay
Touch 1/4
Scoot back
Swing corner and promenade

Heads square thru
Sides half sashay and spread
Centers in
Cast off 3/4
Crossfire**
Split circulate
Explode the wave
Ends fold
Pass thru
Allemande left
Promenade home
