GRAND PARADE

From a static square
The heads step forward to make a wave
The wave turns 1/2 by the right, then centers 3/4 left
Side ladies now step forward to become partners with the head men as the side men courtesy turn the head ladies and all promenade 1/2 way around the square to face in. Repeat the action with the heads leading once more and then repeat two more times with the sides leading each time ending with all dancers at home with partners.
