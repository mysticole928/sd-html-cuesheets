I'm A Swinger
   (Belco 113)    Walt McNeel

1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru 4 hands around with you
And with the sides (heads) do a dosado
Make an ocean wave , rock up and back , swing thru along the track
Girls circulate , boys trade , turn thru
Allemande left the corner, walk by your partner
Swing the right hand girl, promenade
Promenade go round the square get back home and when you're there
Sides (heads) face and do the old grand square (now go)
I'm a swinger, I'm a swinger
I'm a swinger, alright (Reverse)
I'm a swinger, a real humdinger
