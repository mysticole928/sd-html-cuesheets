I Can Hear Kentucky Calling Me
The Osborne Brothers; Chet Atkins 1974
(Chinook 141) Daryl Clendenin
OPENER

4 ladies chain , turn with that girl
Rollaway , circle left go round the world
4 ladies rollaway , circle left I sing
Left allemande and weave around the ring
Sounds and scenes I left behind
Swing the girl and promenade in time
Are evergreens upon my mind
I can hear Kentucky calling me

FIGURE

Heads (sides) promenade halfway around
Walk in and square thru 4 hands around
Go all the way then a right & left thru
Veer left , couples circulate move up and then
Half tag , scoot back , scoot back again
Star thru and promenade my friend
I can hear Kentucky calling me

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Sides face grand square
Front porch screamers pickin' old guitars
Bony hound-dogs singing to the stars
Allemande left and weave the ring
Sounds and scenes I left behind
Are evergreens upon my mind
Swing the girl and promenade in time
I can hear Kentucky calling me

TAG

Allemande left , swing at home
I can hear Kentucky calling me
