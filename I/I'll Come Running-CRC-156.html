I'll Come Running    (CRC 156)   Drew Scearce

OPENER



Circle left
If you ever want somebody to love
If you ever get to feeling blue
The men star right and roll it one time around
Left allemande and weave the ring
It's been so long since I held you tight
Swing your girl around and promenade
I don't care who was wrong or who was right
I'll come running to you

FIGURE



The heads promenade halfway around the ring
Walk in and pass the ocean, extend
Swing thru and then boys run right
Bend the line and go right & left thru
You flutterwheel straight across, slide thru and swing
Swing your corner girl and promenade
I'll be around if your new love turns you down
And I'll come running to you

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade, go once inside the ring
Come back and give your man a swing
Join hands and circle, go walking around the set
Left allemande and weave the ring
It's been so long since I held you tight
Swing your girl and promenade
I'll be around if your new love turns you down
And I'll come running to you
