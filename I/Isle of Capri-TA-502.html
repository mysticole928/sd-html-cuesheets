Isle Of Capri   (Tahoe 502 )   Lloyd Springer

FIGURE



Twice for heads, twice for sides

Now 1 & 3 (2 & 4) go up and back and then you right & left thru
California twirl
Dosado in the center, then you pass thru
Split the ring, go round one, four in line

Pass thru, bend the line
Centers partner trade and u turn back
Ends star thru, square thru 3
Look for corner, swing and promenade

Promenade, don't slow down with this lady
Girls backtrack, go twice around that set
Partners right, box the gnat, then change hands
Left allemande, a grand old right and left

Right and left till you meet and dosado her
Promenade around that ring just for me
Home you go with this lady, then you swing her
Swing this gal on the Isle of Capri
