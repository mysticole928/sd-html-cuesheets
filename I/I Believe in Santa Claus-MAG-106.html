I Believe In Santa Claus   (Magnolia 106)   Jay Wiggins

OPENER



Four Ladies promenade go one time along
Swing old Santa and you swing him round in song
Join hands Circle to the left go moving around the land
Do an Allemande left the corner Weave the ring
I believe in Santa Clause
Swing old Santa and you Promenade on home
I believe in Santa Clause
I believe in Santa Clause

FIGURE



Heads Promenade halfway and then
Sides pair do the Right & left thru and turn the girl my friend
Flutterwheel across the floor and Sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and a Right & left thru and turn
Touch 1/4, Walk & dodge, do the Partner trade
Reverse your flutter boys and all Promenade
I believe in Santa Clause
I believe in Santa Clause
