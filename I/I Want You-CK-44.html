I Want You    (CK 044)   Joe Saltel

Head ladies center, teacup chain



I need you to want me, to share your life with mine
Four ladies chain, turn the girl around now

Loving you is reason for living tomorrow
Reason my dreams will come true

'Cause I love you so much I can't imagine
Living a day without you
