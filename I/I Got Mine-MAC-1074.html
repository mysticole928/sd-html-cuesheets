I Got Mine   (Mac Gregor 1074 )   Bill Ball
INTRO

Men star by the left hand, go full around that way
Dosado your lady, then weave the ring I say
I found a hundred pounder, big and ripe and round
I whispered to my buddy
Promenade, don't slow down
Girls backtrack, same man..box the gnat
Pull 'em by, left allemande, then promenade that patch
I've got mine.. I've got mine
In that watermelon patch..I got mine
