I'll Swing You Baby    (SIO 123)   Johnny LeClair


Four little ladies promenade, go inside of the hall



Right hand turn your partner, turn your corner by the left
Twirl your own and promenade the set
Oh, oh, oh, oh, all wheel around, promenade the other way
Girls star right, the gents backtrack I say
The second time you meet, turn her left and promenade
Then take her home with you
Say, "Baby, I'll swing you"

FIGURE



Head two couples forward, come back and don't be late
Square thru 3/4, then you separate
(Go) round one to the middle, dosado so neat
Then pass thru and swing the one you meet
Oh, oh, oh, oh, allemande your corner, dosado your own
Allemande left again, then promenade your own
Promenade this cutie, go full around the ring
Then take her home with you
Say, "Baby, I'll swing you"
