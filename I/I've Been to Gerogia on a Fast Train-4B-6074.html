I've Been To Georgia On A Fast Train    (4B 6074)   Bob Carmack

OPENER



Sides face grand square
on a rainy windy mornin' in the day I was born
in that old share croppin' one room country shack
they said my mammy left me, the day before she had me
said she hit the road and never once looked back
4 little ladies promenade one time around
come on back, swing and promenade
I got a good christian raisin' and an 8th grade education
ain't no need in ya'll treatin' me this way

FIGURE



Head/side pair promenade 1/2 way round
Side/head pair do the right and left thru
square thru & go now, 4 hands around somehow
do a dosado and 8 chain 4
I've been to georgia on a fast train honey
swing a little lady and promenade
I got a good christian raisin' and an 8th grade education
ain't no need in ya'll treatin' me this way

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies promenade one time around
come on back swing your man around
join hands circle to the left go movin' round
do an allemande left with the corner, weave the ring
I've been to georgia on a fast train honey
swing your little lady, promenade
I got a good christian raisin' and an 8th grade education
ain't no need in ya'll treatin' me this way

CLOSER



sides face grand square
oh my sweet carolina I don't guess I'll ever find
another woman put together like you are
I love the wiggle in your walkin' your big city talkin
and your brand new shiny plymouth rag top car
4 boys promenade one time around
come on back, swing and promenade
I got a good christian raisin' and an 8th grade education
ain't no need in ya'll treatin' me this way

TAG



bow to the partner, corners all and whoa!
