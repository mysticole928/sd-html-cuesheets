I'll Always Remember That Song   (Blue Star 2399 )   Ken Jefferies

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
Monday evening the band played the polka
A new lively polka, with a military air
Monday evening the band played the polka, the polka
And me and my new love, we were there (circle left)

A-Rum-Ba-Ba-Ba-Pum, A-Rum-Ba-Ba-Ba-Pum
Left allemande, come back swing your girl, promenade
A-Rum-Ba-Ba-Ba-Pum, as you promenade along
And I'll always remember that song

FIGURE



Heads square thru and you count to 4 hands
You'll make a right hand star, and you turn it round to go
Now heads star by the left in the middle and turn it
All the way around to the outside pair and do a right & left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel, center two pass thru
Swing the corner girl, promenade
A-Rum-Ba-Ba-Ba-Pum, as you promenade along
And I'll always remember that song

BREAK



Sides face, grand square
Tuesday evening the band played a polka
The same lively polka, we heard the night before
Tuesday evening the band played a polka, a polka
And me and my new love shared amour (circle left)
