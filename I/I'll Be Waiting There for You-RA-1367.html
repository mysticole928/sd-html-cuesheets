I'll Be Waiting There For You    (RA 1367)   Otis Getts

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 little ladies promenade go once inside the ring
Get back home & swing there with your man
Then join your hands, circle to the left
Go walking, moving round the ring
Left allemande your corner, weave the ring
Go in & out around until you meet your maid
Dosado and promenade
You promenade that ring, get her home & swing
And I'll be waiting there for you

FIGURE



Heads/sides promenade go 1/2 way round the ring
Come down the middle & do the right & left thru
Then square thru & go 4 hands around
And then dosado that corner waiting there
Swing thru and the boys trade
You turn thru and then, left allemande
Come back and promenade
You promenade that ring, take her home and swing
And I'll be waiting there for you
