I'd Like It
(Hi Hat 316) Bill Green
FIGURE

Head (side) 2 couples promenade, 1/2 way round the square
Same two lead right to circle 1/2
(You can) dive thru , pass thru , & circle with that pair
Head gents break and make a line , move up and back
Pass thru , a wheel & deal , then a double pass thru
First 2 left, next 2 right , you know, dosado
Left square thru & count to 4 as you round
Swing ol' number 5, don't you let her go

BREAK

Join your hands and circle , yes, circle left tonight
Reverse in single file from where you are
Ladies all backtrack , you can turn your partner right
The men back in and make a wrong way thar
Now shoot the star, go left & right and there you box the gnat
Dosado this girl, then swing her too
Promenade and tell her, she's cute as she can be
And you'd like to take her home to swing with you
