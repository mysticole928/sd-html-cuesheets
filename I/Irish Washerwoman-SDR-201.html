Irish Washerwoman    (SDR 201)   "Uncle" Otis Blanchard

All join hands, go in to the middle



Shake your feet, keep time with the fiddle
All left and promenade all (16 beat promenade)
4 ladies chain the middle
Hurry up, keep time with the fiddle
Chain them back in the same old track
All left and promenade

FIGURE (4 times for the girls)



Ladies lead to the right of the ring
When you're there just balance and swing
After you've swung remember my call
All left and promenade all

MIDDLE BREAK (see above)



FIGURE (4 times for the boys)

Men you lead to the left of the ring



After you've swung remember my call
All left and promenade all (16 beat promenade)

CLOSER (see above)
