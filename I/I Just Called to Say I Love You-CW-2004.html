I Just Called to Say I Love You   (CW 2004 )   Al Stevens

FIGURE



Four ladies chain 3/4, turn them and heads promenade halfway
Star thru and pass thru and here touch 1/4
Girls run right I say, square thru 4 hands around the land
Walk in and do a right & left grand around the land
Meet your lady, promenade her home, stay there
Sides face grand square
I just called to say I love you
And I mean it from the bottom of my heart

TAG



Of my heart, of my heart!

SEQUENCE (Repeat figure four times through, twice for heads, twice for sides)
