I'd Like To Be    (LIS 602)   Tod Williams

FIGURE (four times through)



The head two couples bow and swing, the side two ladies chain
The heads go forward up and back with you
Then a right & left thru and turn your girl and cross trail thru
Around just one you line up four, go up and back
Pass thru and bend the line, go up and back
Pass thru, and then you wheel & deal, face your own
Now back away and make a ring and circle 8
You circle to the left and to the right
And now the gents square thru, go full around
Go all the way and swing that lady there
And now the girls star left go once around
You box the gnat and then you promenade
The heads wheel around and do a right & left thru
Then cross trail back and allemande left you do
Promenade your partner and go full around
I'd like to be at home and dance alone with you
