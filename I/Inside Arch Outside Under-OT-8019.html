Saguaro Whirl / Inside Arch Outside Under
(Old Timer 8019 side B ) Joe Boykin
FIGURE

First couple out and you lead to the right
Circle four halfway round
With an inside arch and outside under
Inside arch outside under
Inside arch outside under
Inside arch outside under
And circle four with the odd couple
And four hands up and round you go
Break that ring with a docey doe
Partner left, opposite right
Turn your partner and away you go
Circle four halfway round
Inside arch outside under
Inside arch outside under
Inside arch outside under
Inside arch outside under and balance home

(Repeat for couples 2, 3 and 4 as the travelling couples)

(Note: On these instructions the call for "Inside arch and outside under" is
written four times, which is necessary to complete the figure, however, the call
on the record is only three times because the dancers can do the figure faster
than it can be called.)
