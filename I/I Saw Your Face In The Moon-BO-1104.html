I Saw Your Face In The Moon
Ray Whitley's Range Ramblers; Mac Wiseman 1936
   (Bogan 1104)    Charley Bogan
 
FIGURE   

Docey your corner girl, your pretty little corner honey
The gents star left one time
Arm around your own little partner
A star promenade round the town
Four little ladies backtrack honey
Twice around , then a right hand swing
Gents star left and hear me say
Swing your corner , swing and sway
I saw your face in the moon

MIXER

Four little ladies chain the ring, chain the ring honey
Turn, bow , now swing
Allemande left round your own little corner
A grand right & left round the town
Docey your honey when you meet her
Girls star left one time
Box the gnat and promenade
Take her home and hear me say
I saw your face in the moon

(SEQUENCE OF DANCE: Figure twice, mixer, figure twice, mixer for ending.)

In mixer, when ladies chain across, they go back to original partner and dance is done with original partner, then again figure twice puts everyone with opposites, but when mixer is used at end, everyone is back home.
