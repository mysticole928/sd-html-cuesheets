I Dream Of You
   (Keeno 2260)    Harold Bausch
BREAK    Basic

Dosado that corner girl, seesaw your own
4 little ladies chain across straight across you roam
Rollaway half sashay , circle left hear me say
Rollaway , weave the ring in and out you go
Weave 'em in, weave 'em out, dosado your pet
Allemande that corner girl, promenade the set
Take her home, swing and sway
Listen boys hear me say
All I do the whole day through is dream of you

FIGURE    A1, corner progression

Head (side) two ladies chain across, turn the girl so fine
Star thru , pass thru , circle to a line
Head men break, here's what you do, right & left thru across from you
Turn the girl, cross trail , swing the corner do
Swing and whirl that corner girl and then left allemande
Come on back, promenade , go walkin' hand in hand
Take her home, swing and sway
Listen girls and hear him say
All I do the whole day through is dream of you
