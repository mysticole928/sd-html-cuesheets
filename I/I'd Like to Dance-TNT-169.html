I'd Like To Dance    (TNT 169)   Garland King

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain across
Join hands and circle left
Do an allemande left and an alamo style, swing thru, turn thru
Allemande left and weave the ring, winding in and out you go
Promenade two by two
Gee I'd like to dance with you

FIGURE 1



Head couples square thru 4, all the way round
Dosado corner, swing thru 2 by 2
Boys run right, ferris wheel
Come to the center, slide thru, pass thru
U-turn back, slide thru, swing your corner, promenade
Promenade 2 by 2
Gee I'd like to dance with you

FIGURE 2



Head couples square thru 4 hands
To your corner swing thru
Boys run, wheel & deal
Slide thru, touch 1/4, triple scoot
Boys run right, slide thru, pass thru
Swing your corner, promenade
Promenade 2 by 2
Gee I'd like to dance with you
