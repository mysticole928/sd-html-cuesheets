I Believe In Music    (LO 1236)   Dick Demeritt

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain you turn the girl and then
Turn and chain those ladies right back home again
Join your hands and circle to the left around you go
Allemande left your corner, and weave around you know
I believe in music
Swing your girl and promenade
I believe in music
Yes, I believe in love

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands you go
With those sides you make a right hand star and roll it around
Heads star by the left in the middle one time you go
To the corner girl you touch 1/4
Scoot back and then scoot back again
Swing the corner, promenade
I believe in music
Yes, I believe in love

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands you go
Out to the corner lady, you do a dosado
Touch 1/4 then, scoot back go once and a half
Keep movin', now to a wheel & deal
You pass to the center, new centers square thru 3
Swing the corner lady and promenade for me
I believe in music
Yes, I believe in love
