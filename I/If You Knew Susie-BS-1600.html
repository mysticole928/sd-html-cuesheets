If You Knew Susie    (BS 1600)   Buford Evans

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Circle left you know, break that ring, dopaso
Partner left, corner right, partner left (full turn)
4 ladies chain straight across the ring
Rollaway with a 1/2 sashay, swing the gal coming your way
Allemande left new corner, pass the girl you swung
Swing and whirl the next little girl, promenade go round the world
If you know Susie like I know Susie
You'll swing, swing with your gal

FIGURE



Head (side) two couples pass thru, partner trade and then you do
A star thru, pass thru, star thru those two
Go forward eight and back, pass thru and partner trade
And then you do a star thru, allemande left with your left hand
Dosado your partner, your corner you swing
Allemande left new corner girl, promenade go round the ring
If you know Susie like I know Susie
You'll swing, swing with that gal
