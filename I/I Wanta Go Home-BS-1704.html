I Wanta Go Home    (BS 1704)   Larry Faught

FIGURE



1st & 3rd promenade around the outside
Halfway round lead right and circle there
Spread on out and form a line, go up and back in time
Two ladies chain across the ring and turn the girls so fine
Diagonally the end two ladies chain now
Rock forward 8 and back and then star thru
Dive thru and circle 4, halfway round and then no more
Frontier whirl and swing the corner, promenade and sing
I want to go home, I want to go home
Oh, how I want to go home

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies chain across the inside
Turn these girls, chain back a dopaso
Turn your corner right hand round, turn partner left around
Gents star right in the center, turn it once around
Pass your own, left allemande
Come back home, swing and stay there at home
