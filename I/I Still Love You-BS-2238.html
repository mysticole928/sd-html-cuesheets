I Still Love You    (BS 2238)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO



Circle left
I saw you for the first time in a long time yesterday
I felt it all again but couldn't think of much to say
Left allemande that corner lady, do a dosado
Left allemande that corner, weave the ring
I felt again the way your touch can thrill me
Dosado then promenade
You still bring that old time feeling
I still love you in the same old way

FIGURE



Now the head 2 couples square thru 4 hands around you go
And with the sides make a right hand star and roll it round you go
Those heads star by the left and turn it one time and then
To the same old 2 you do the right & left thru
Now touch 1/4, walk & dodge, partner trade and then
Reverse the flutter wheel, you'll promenade
You still bring that old time feeling
I still love you in the same old way

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Do an allemande left in the alamo style and balance there awhile
Swing thru, go forward 2 and rock it once again
Swing thru, go forward 2 and turn thru you know
Left allemande that corner, weave the ring
I felt again the way your touch can thrill me
Dosado then promenade
You still bring that old time feeling
I still love you in the same old way
