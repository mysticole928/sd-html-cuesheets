I Overlooked An Orchid    (RA 1330)   J.P. Jett

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around that corner, see saw around your taw
Promenade go walking round that ring
4 couples wheel around, fold the girls and star thru
Join up hands and circle round that ring
Allemande, it's a grand old right & left around, and when you meet your own
Dosado and then you promenade
I overlooked an orchid while searching for a rose
The orchid now I find my dear was you

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain across, turn the girl and then
2 & 4 go forward up and back, go right & left thru
1 & 3 go up and back, spin the top I sing
Box a gnat, pull by, corner swing
Swing that corner lady round, allemande new corner girl
Dosado your own then promenade
I overlooked an orchid while searching for a rose
The orchid now I find my dear was you
