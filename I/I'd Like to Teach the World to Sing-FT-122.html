I'd Like To Teach The World To Sing    (FT 122)   Rod Shuping

OPENER



Circle left
I'd like to teach the world to sing
In perfect harmony
Left allemande, dosado
Allemande that corner, weave the ring
I'd like to build the world a home
And furnish it with love
Promenade
Grow apple trees and honey bees
And snow white turtle doves

FIGURE



Head two you promenade go halfway and then
Down the middle, square thru 4
Go all the way, swing thru I say
Those boys run, ferris wheel
Zoom, double pass thru, leaders partner trade
Your corner swing and promenade
Grow apple trees and honey bees
And snow white turtle doves

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four girls promenade go one time and go
Get back home and give that boy a swing
Join hands, circle left, go around and go
Allemande that corner, weave the ring
I'd like to build the world a home
Swing your girl and promenade
Grow apple trees and honey bees
And snow white turtle doves
