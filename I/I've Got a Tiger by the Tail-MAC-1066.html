Same 2 go up and back, a 1/2 square thru
Dosado, make an ocean wave, you rock up and back
You swing thru and you go along that track, rock up and back
Box the gnat, men circulate* and swing
You promenade you go walking around the ring
I'm as lost as a leaf in a gale
And it looks like I got a tiger by the tail

*For newer dancers, instead of 'circulate', say 'pull by', which means the same.
