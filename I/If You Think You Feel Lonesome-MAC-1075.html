If You Think You Feel Lonesome    (MAC 1075)   Chuck Raley

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Dosado with the corner, see saw your taw
Join your hands, circle to the left to walkin' round the hall
Allemande the corner, go forward two
Turn back one and box the gnat and a right & left grand you do
Touch and feel, the tremble in their hands
Dosado and promenade, you got yourself a handsome man
Lookie here you've been cryin', lonesome plain to see
Don't feel so bad, if you think you feel lonesome, feel of me

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4, turn 'em with the old left hand
Head promenade outside the ring, about halfway round
Side 2 star thru, then substitute
Square thru 3/4, allemande then weave the ring
Don't touch their hands, their tremblin' so
Dosado and promenade, walkin' round the ring you go
Lookie here you've been cryin', lonesome plain to see
Don't feel so bad, if you think you feel lonesome, feel of me
