Into Each Life Some Rain Must Fall    (SS 2336)   Johnny Reagan

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and circle, circle to the left you know
All the way around the ring you go
Allemande left that corner girl, your own you dosado
Those gents star by the left, turn it one time you know
Meet your partner, turn thru, those corners allemande
Come back one and swing, promenade go around that land
Into each life some rain must fall
Too much has fallen in mine

FIGURE



Head two couples promenade halfway round I say
2 & 4 roll a 1/2 sashay
Head ladies chain, turn those girls and then
Join hands and circle, you circle left again
4 men cross trail around one and then
Come down the middle and pass thru then swing that corner Jane, promenade
Into each life some rain must fall
Too much has fallen in mine
