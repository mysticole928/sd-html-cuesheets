BS 2621

“I'M BEGINNING TO SEE THE LIGHT”

Opener, Break (Circle Left)

I never cared much for moonlit skies

I never wink back at fireflies

Men Star Right ... one time around

Left Allemande – Weave that ring

I never made love by lantern shine

I never saw rainbows in my wine

But now that the stars are in your eyes

I'm beginning to see the light


Figure

Heads you Square Thru four hands you go

Round that corner do a Do Sa Do

Swing Thru and the Boys you Run Right

Bend the Line go Right & Left Thru

Flutterwheel and then Slide Thru

Swing the corner - Promenade you do

But now that your lips are burning mine

I'm beginning to see the light


Close

Four Ladies Promenade go inside the ring

Get back home and give her a Swing

Join your hands, man, Circle I sing

Left Allemande – Weave that ring

I never went in for afterglow

Or candlelight on the mistletoe

But now that you turn the lamp down low

I'm beginning to see the light


Tag: (Allemande and Swing) I'm beginning to see the light
