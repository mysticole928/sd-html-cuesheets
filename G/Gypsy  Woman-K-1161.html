Gypsy Woman    (K 1161)   John Saunders

Four ladies chain about 3/4



Sides face, grand square eight steps
And then allemande left, weave the ring
*Going down to see that gypsy woman
Dosado and promenade the land
Please tell me good news, gypsy woman
That my baby's coming home again

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*Going down to that place called lonesome

FIGURE



Head (side) couples square thru four hands
Around the corner dosado
Curlique and turn to the right 3/4
Girls trade and then go right & left thru
Now flutter wheel go full around
And then the corner swing
Swing the lady and promenade the land
Please tell me good news, gypsy woman
That my baby's coming home again
