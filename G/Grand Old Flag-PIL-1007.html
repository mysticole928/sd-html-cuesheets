Grand Old Flag
Billy Murray; many others 1906
(Pilgrim 1007) Dan Dedo
OPENER

Sides face grand square
You're a grand old flag, a high flying flag
And forever in peace may you wave
You're the emblem of the land I love
The home of the free and the brave
Left allemande the corner girl, then weave that ol' ring
Go in and out and then promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
Keep your eye on the grand old flag

FIGURE

1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade 3/4 and then
Those side (head) ladies chain that old floor
Sides (heads) dosado , it's back to back you go
Box the gnat , star right around the floor
With that corner allemande , dosado your own
Take that corner, promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
Keep your eye on the grand old flag

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Walk all around that corner girl, see saw your taw
Join your hands , circle left go round the ring
Do an allemande left , go allemande thar
Go right & left, you've got a star
Shoot that star , four ladies chain 3/4 and then
Roll promenade with your maid
Should old acquaintance be forgot
Keep your eye on the grand old flag
