Gal With A Broken Heart    (RA 1335)   Bill Wilson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Well join hands and make a ring, circle to the left while I sing
Swing corner lady round and round, circle left
Allemande left and allemande thar, right & left the 4 gents star
Gents back in, you've got a right hand star
Slip the clutch, left allemande, partners all a right & left grand
Hand over hand around that ring you go, promenade
Promenade this lady, tell her from the start
That I can mend her broken heart

FIGURE



Heads up to the middle and back with you, swing star thru, that's what you do
I can mend your broken heart, circle 4
Head gents break and make a line, dance up to the middle and back in time
Star thru, square thru 3/4 round you go
Allemande left with the corner Joe, dosado the one you know
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Promenade this lady, tell her from the start
That I can mend her broken heart
