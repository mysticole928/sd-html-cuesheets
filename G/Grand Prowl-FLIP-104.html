Grand Prowl    (FLIP 104)   Horace Hall

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across, turn the girls that way
Head girls chain back, sides 1/2 sashay
1 & 3 promenade 3/4 round, stop there
Behind those sides settle down
Forward 8 and hear me say
Face your partner, back away
Pass thru, turn left, go single file
Men move up, new girl promenade
Go round the world, promenade with that new pal
Home you go and star prowl
