Grand Prowl
   (Flip 104)    Horace Hall
FIGURE

4 ladies chain across, and you turn the girls that way
Head ladies chain back, and sides 1/2 sashay
1 & 3 you promenade 3/4 round
Stop there behind those sides and then you settle down
Forward 8 and hear me say
Face your partner , back away
Pass thru , turn left , go single file
Gents move up, new girl
Promenade go round the world
Promenade with that new pal and home you go and grand prowl
