Give My Regards To Broadway   (Jay-Bar-Kay 137 )   Birdie Mesick

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande left in the alamo style, balance out and in
Swing thru, go forward two and then, turn thru
Allemande left, go allemande thar, go forward two and star
Men back in and make a right hand star
Shoot that star and dosado your own
Promenade that lady round for me
Give my regards to old Broadway

FIGURE



4 ladies promenade one time and then
Swing that girl in old times square
Head (side) two star thru, pass thru, square thru
3/4 round and then trade by
Allemande left on the corner, walk right by your own
Swing the right hand lady, promenade
Promenade 'em to old Broadway
And tell them I will soon be there
