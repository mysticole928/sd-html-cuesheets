Good To Go To Mexico    (GLDR 110)   Scott Brown

OPENER



4 ladies chain the ring
You turn the girl and then
4 ladies rollaway and circle left I say
4 ladies rollaway again and circle left and then
Allemande left and weave the ring
Baby if you're good to, we'll go down to Mexico
You swing your lady and promenade that land
It'll be just you and me and the moonlight dancing on the sea
To the Spanish guitar melody of a Mariachi band

FIGURE



Heads couples promenade 1/2 way around I say
Down the middle and pass the ocean there
Extend, swing thru tonight
All the boys run to the right
Ferris wheel in the middle of the ring and do the right & left thru
Centers square thru and go 3/4 down to Mexico
You swing your lady and promenade that land
It'll be just you and me and the moonlight dancing on the sea
To the Spanish guitar melody of a Mariachi band

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
I got 2 tickets bought, there won't be no second thought
The weather's always nice, down there in paradise
We'll find that little man, who owns the Taco stand
We'll be drinking Margaritas
Allemande left and weave the land
Baby if you're good to, we'll go down to Mexico
You swing your lady and promenade that land
If you'll get off the telephone, put your shorts and sandals on
Tomorrow we'll be dancing to a Mariachi band

CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
Cancun don't get me high, that's where the snowbirds fly
They like to winter there, they come from everywhere
I'll take the Baja sun, man it ain't overrun
With all them Gringos and Touristas
Allemande left and weave for fun
Baby if you're good to, we'll go down to Mexico
You swing your lady and promenade that land
It'll be just you and me and the moonlight dancing on the sea
To the Spanish guitar melody of a Mariachi band
