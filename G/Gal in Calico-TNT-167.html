Gal In Calico   (TNT 167 )   Mike Trombly

BREAK



Four boys promenade once inside the ring
Get back home, swing your gal, everybody swing
Join your hands, circle to the left, all the way around you know
Allemande left with the corner and weave the ring you go
Met a gal in calico down in Santa Fe
Dosado your baby and promenade I say
But who makes my heart sing Yipee-i-yipee-o
That little gal in calico

FIGURE



Heads pass thru, partner trade and then
Square thru 4 hands all the way around my friends
Right & left thru, outside 2 turn the girl you know
Dive thru, square thru 4 hands you go
When you're there, you're facing out, partner trade and then
Swing that corner gal around and promeande the ring
But who makes my heart sing Yipee-i-yipee-o
That little gal in calico
