Grand Square Six Quadrille
   (Square Dancetime 9)    Jack Murtha
FIGURE (4 times through)

Sides (heads) pass thru
Separate, go round two
Home you go
Heads (sides) pass thru
Separate go round two
Home you go
Sides (heads) face, grand square 6
Bow to your partner
Swing, promenade

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

Sides (heads) slow square thru*
Split two round one
At home dosado
Sides (heads) face grand square 6
Bow to your partner
Swing, promenade

(*Note: footwork for the slow square thru is a Slow, Slow, Quick, Quick, Quick per hand. Allow 16 beats of music.)
