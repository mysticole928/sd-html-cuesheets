Goody, Goody   (Sets In Order 1108 )  
SEQUENCE

Intro, figure twice, break, figure twice

OPENER - BREAK



4 gents star & turn the opposite girl
Star back home and left hand 'round you whirl
Heads promenade 1/2 you do, side couples right & left thru
Heads down the center with a right & left thru
Side couples right & left thru
Corners dosado and give your own a swing
Then allemande left & right & left grand the ring
Hail and Hallelujah
Promenade when she gets to ya
Goody, goody for her..goody, goody for you
Swing 'til you're satisfied you rascal you

FIGURE



Join your hands and circle 1/2 the town
Heads right & left thru on down
Sides pass thru, partner trade..star right on the inside track
Left hand round your corner, right hand round your own
Corners dosado, same girl swing
Swing and swing and promenade that ring
Promenade and Hallelujah, you got it coming to ya
Goody, goody for her
Goody, goody for you
Swing 'til you're satisfied you rascal you
