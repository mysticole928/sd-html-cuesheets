Gal From Pennsylvania
Frankie Yankovic and His Yanks 1942
(Windsor 4931) John Butler
FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)

1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around and then
Make a right hand star with the outside two and move it round my friend
Heads center, left hand star, once around you know
Put an arm around your corner, star promenade her so
Back out a full turn , circle to the left somehow
Swing the girl that's closest to you, then you promenade
Everybody has the mania
To swing the gal from Pennsylvania

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

All four ladies chain across, turn the girl you know
Join hands circle to the left around the ring you go
Allemande left the corner, then you do an allemande thar
Right, left, all 4 gents back right up and star
Shoot that star , dosado , go once around that girl
Left allemande that corner, come back and promenade
You promenade that ring, take that lady home and swing
You swing that gal around, we'll dance our blues away
