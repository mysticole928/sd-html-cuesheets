Green River    (MAC 979)   Chuck Raley

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain the ring, turn 'em boys, send 'em back
Star thru and promenade
Head 2 couples wheel around, go right & left thru
Cross trail thru and go left allemande
You weave the ring, now meet Johnny and Joe
You'll meet your partner, latch on and promeno
Sail right down that ol' green river
On the good ship Rock and Rye

FIGURE



Head couples up and back, and 1/2 square thru
Circle 4 and break to a line
Forward 8 and back with you
And then you star thru
Dosado to an ocean wave, rock it up and back
You're gonna swing thru and then, when you do
Box the gnat, swing and promenade
And then you sail right down that ol' green river
On the good ship Rock and Rye

SEQUENCE: (Intro, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides)
