Give My Regards To Broadway    (MAC 1057)   Fenton Jones

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



1 & 3 you'll promenade, just half around the town
Face the middle a right & left thru, we'll turn the girls around
Whirlaway, star thru
Circle up 4 and then halfway round, dive thru, pass thru
Left allemande, go right & left around that ring
Now, hurry don't be slow, meet your lady promenade that square
Just give our regards to old Broadway
And tell the gang we'll soon be there

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right, you'll circle down the track
Break 2 lines of 4, you go walking up and back
Pass thru, wheel & deal, double pass thru
Cloverlead, it's up to you, center 4 star thru
Cross trail, left allemande your own, a dosado
Take your corner, promenade that square
Just give our regards to old Broadway
And tell the gang we'll soon be there

SEQUENCE: (intro, figure heads twice, break, figure sides twice, closer)
