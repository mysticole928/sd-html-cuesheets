Goofy Square
(Top 25047) Dick Jones
FIGURE

4 ladies chain you know 3/4 round you go
Head couples promenade halfway round and then
Sides pass thru , turn back you do, circle 8 go round with you
You move it round from there, men face, grand square
1, 2, cha cha cha; 1, 2, cha cha cha
1, 2, cha cha cha; 1, 2, reverse it now
1, 2, cha cha cha; 1, 2, cha cha cha
1, 2, cha cha cha; Girls square thru go
4 hands around like that, meet somebody box the gnat
Same lady swing and whirl, what a pretty girl
Promenade 'em 2 by 2, go struttin' round with you
Don't let this lady swoon to that goofy tune

MIDDLE BREAK

Join hands and circle left halfway around the set
Walk around your corner girl, see saw your own
Men star right go once around , then when your corner's found
Allemande left and hear me say, do the grand sashay
Dosado, right hand pull by
See saw, left hand pull by
Dosado, right hand pull by
See saw, left hand pull by
Swing the next one, do your best, well now the men star left
One time around you go, then dosado
Promenade go round the hall, go struttin' round you all
Please, don't despair to this goofy square
