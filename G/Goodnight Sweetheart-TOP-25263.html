Goodnight Sweetheart    (TOP 25263)   Bruce Welsh

BREAK



Join 8 hands and circle left and may be
Reverse back, single file, trail behind your baby
4 boys backtrack, meet partner box the gnat
Same girl a dosado and right after that
Left allemande that corner girl, weave the ring don't sorrow
We'll meet again and just promenade tomorrow
Whisper softly as you promenade her
Goodnight, sweetheart, goodnight

FIGURE



Head 2 cross trail, behind the sides you star thru
Side 2 square thru count 3/4 round too
Split 2 go round 1 and make a line like that
Go forward up and back, box the gnat across the track
The cross trail, skip one girl, swing that corner lady
Swing that corner gal and then you'll promenade your baby
We'll walk together through all joy and sorrow
Goodnight, sweetheart, goodnight

ALTERNATE BREAK (easy version)



Join 8 hands and circle left and may be
You'll go all the way around there, dancin' with your baby
Girls star right 3/4 and then
Left allemande, go right & left grand
Goodnight, sweetheart, we'll meet again tomorrow
Dosado and promenade don't sorrow
Whisper softly as you promenade her
Goodnight, sweetheart, goodnight

ALTERNATE FIGURE (easy version)



4 ladies promenade, go once inside the ring
Swing with your man, yes, you give that man a swing
4 men star left, it's one time you go
Same girl dosado, to the corner go
Left allemande that corner girl and walk by your baby
Swing the next one waiting there and promenade that lady
We'll walk together through all joy and sorrow
Goodnight, sweetheart, goodnight
