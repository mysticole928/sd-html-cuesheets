Goody, Goody   (Roofers 127 )   Gerald McWhirter
INTRO

Four ladies chain across the ring, goody goody
Turn & chain 'em home again
Join hands & circle to the left go movin', walkin' round
Left allemande, weave the ring
Wind it in & out until you meet her
Swing your doll & promenade
Goody goody for him, I hope you're satisfied you rascal you

FIGURE



Heads promenade half way, goody goody
Down the middle and pass the ocean, extend the tag
Swing thru & then spin the top boys move up, right & left thru
Square thru 3/4, goody goody
Swing the corner promenade

Four ladies chain 3/4 round, sides promenade half way
Heads right & left thru, turn your girl
Pass the ocean & ladies trade, extend the tag
All 8 circulate & then swing thru
Swing your corner promenade

MIDDLE BREAK & ENDING



Sides face, grand square
