Goodnight Ladies
   (Top 25079)    Stan Burdick
 
OPENER    Basic

Allemande left to an allemande thar , go forward 2
Right & left, the men turn in, back 'em up boys you do
Shoot that star like an alamo style , balance , then turn by the right
Balance , with a left pull by , take your partner, promenade full around
Goodnight ladies, goodnight ladies
Get on home and the 4 little ladies chain across the square
Merrily we chain 'em now, (chain 'em now), chain 'em back
Home you go and settle down, I gotta say goodnight now

FIGURE   

Head 2 couples right & left thru , turn the girls right there ( backaway )
Same ladies chain 3/4 round the square
New men turn this girl around, whirl her now a 1/2 sashay
Lines pass thru , u-turn back , girls divide, round one man
Goodnight ladies , men face left , open your arms
Swing that girl and give her a smile, promenade the ring
Merrily we roll along, rock along, roll along
Home you go and settle down, I can't say goodnight now
