Gal With A Smile On Her Face   (Old Timer 1602 )   Shannon Duck

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Walk around your corner, hey, seesaw your own
Join your hands & circle left you roam
Allemande left, go allemande thar, forward 2 & star
Men back in with right hands in, star by the right & then
Shoot that star, 4 ladies chain 3/4 round
Roll, promenade her, go walking round the town
Promenade this gal with a smile on her face
And she will help you chase your blues away

FIGURE



Now heads (sides) you promenade 1/2 way around you go
Lead to the right & circle up 4 1/2 way around you know
Veer to the left & bend the line, go forward up & back
Pass thru with a partner trade & square thru I say
It's 4 hands around on the corner you swing
Allemande left new corner and promenade the ring
Promenade this gal with a smile on her face
And she will help you chase your blues away
