Georgia Sunshine    (BS 1908)   Al Brownlee

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round
Down the center do the right & left thru there
Square thru my friend, 4 hands and then
Make a right hand star with the outside pair
Heads star left in the middle to the same two
Dosado go once around
Swing thru and then boys run my friend
Wheel & deal and do the right & left thru
Dive thru and square thru 3/4
Swing that corner, promenade
Oh how I long, since I been gone
To get out in that old Georgia sunshine

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain, hey turn the Jane
Roll away and circle round the ring now
You roll away, circle that way
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
Oh how I miss that Georgia sunshine
Dosado go once around
4 men make a left hand star one time but not too far
Turn your partner right two times around
4 ladies hit the road out in that sunshine
Swing that gal, promenade
Wake up in the morning and smell them Georgia pines
Oh how I miss that Georgia sunshine
