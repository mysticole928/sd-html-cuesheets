All four ladies promenade, watch 'em strut inside the ring
Box the gnat, the men star left I sing
Put your arm around your partner, star promenade
Back right out, a full turn, circle with this maid
Make this a night to remember, from now 'til December
Left allemande, come back and promenade (full around)
We'll play that good ol' good time polka

Down the middle do a right & left thru
Same two ladies chain, couples 2 & 4 dixie chain
Both turn left, single file once more
Well now those heads pass on thru, swing that corner maid
Left allemande, come back and promenade (full around)
We'll play that good ol' good time polka
