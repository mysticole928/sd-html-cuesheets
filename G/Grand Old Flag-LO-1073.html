Grand Old Flag    (LO 1073)   Red Bates

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk around your corner gal, see saw round your pal
Join your hands, circle left, now hear me say
With your head held high, allemande your corner guy
Dosado round your own, and weave the ring
Every heart beats true for the red, white and blue
Meet your gal, dosado, then promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
Keep your eye on that grand old flag

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4 round, turn this gal with an arm around
Join your hands, circle left, now whirl away
Allemande left, an allemande oh! right & left a dopaso
Partner left and your corner right hand round
Partner left, around you go, back to the corner, dosado
Come back one, swing this girl, and promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
Keep your eye on that grand old flag
