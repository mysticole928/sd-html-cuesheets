Circle left
I'm the official historian on Shirley Jean Berrell
Walk all around the corner and see saw your taw
The gents star by the right hand, go once round the land
Left allemande the corner and you weave around the ring

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

Heads (sides) promenade halfway round
Down the middle and do a right & left thru
Square thru in the middle, count four hands
Get to the corner do a right and left thru , veer left ,
Ferris wheel in time, centers pass thru
Swing the corner, promenade
*Singing peace in the valley and preaching up a storm
(*First two figures)
