Gordo's Quadrille
(Square Dancetime 2) Jack Murtha
FIGURE (4 times through)

Heads (sides) lead right
Split two round one, down the middle with a right & left thru
Sides (heads) lead right
Split two round one, down the middle with a right & left thru
Circle left
Allemande left
Pass your partner, take the next and promenade*

(*Note: dancers pass home and promenade all the way around to home.)
