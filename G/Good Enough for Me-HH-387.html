Good Enough For Me    (HH 387)   Dan Schmelzer

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Head two ladies chain to the right I say
New side ladies chain across the way
Sides face, grand square, just eight steps and then
Join hands, circle left, go walkin' round my friend
And then you allemande left, weave around the town
Go in and out then promenade around, I tell you
All the other fellers will agree
This girl is good enough for me

FIGURE



Now 1 & 3 do a half square thru
Go right & left thru with the outside two
Slide thru, dosado to an ocean wave and then
Swing thru, box the gnat, then a right & left thru again
And then the ladies lead, dixie style to a wave
All eight circulate, boys turn back, promenade
Beggars can't be choosers, can't you see
This girl is good enough for me
