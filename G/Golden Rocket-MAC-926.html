Golden Rocket   (Mac Gregor 926 )   Bill Ball

OPENER



Now all 4 ladies chain across, turn 'em round, don't get lost
Join your hands & make a great big ring--circle to the left
Allemande left, allemande thar, go right & left, make that star
Back 'em up boys & make a right hand star
Shoot that star, go right & left grand
Every other girl with every other hand
Meet a little lady, promenade her home (all the way around)
Get along home & swing & whirl
Go 'round & round with the pretty little girl
This golden rocket is a rollin' my blues away
