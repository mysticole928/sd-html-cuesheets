Grab The Rope
(Solid Gold 1015) Tom Roper
OPENER

Four ladies promenade inside the ring
Get back and swing your man
Join up your hands and circle left that way
Allemande left and weave the ring
A warm breeze rocked my boat until
Swing your girl and promenade
Then through the dark I saw a rope
And heard a calm voice say

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) promenade halfway around the ring
Sides (Heads) square thru go 4
4 hands and then everybody square thru 3
3 hands and then, trade by
Touch 1/4 , scoot back
Swing your corner girl and promenade
Then through the dark I saw a rope
And heard a calm voice say

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Sides face grand square
Grab the rope
Hold it tight
In the distance
Shines a light
Circle left
Neither fear, nor feel (alone)
Allemande left and promenade
There is one who'll lead you home
