Go Tell It On The Mountain    (IR 105)  Larry Belcher

OPENER



Grand Square
Go tell it on the mountain
Over the hills and everywhere
Go tell it on the mountain
That Jesus Christ is born
Four ladies promenade around the ring go
Get back home swing and promenade
Go tell it on the mountain,
That Jesus Christ is born

FIGURE



Heads square thru go for now
Round that corner lady dosado
Swing thru and then the girls you fold
Peel the top, boys move up, slide thru
Touch 1/4, follow your neighbor and spread then
Boys run right, promenade that ring
Go tell it on the mountain,
That Jesus Christ is born

MIDDLE BREAK



Grand Square
The shepherds all were watching
Over their sheep at night
When a guiding star shone from heaven
And followed that Holy light
Four ladies promenade around the ring go
Get back home swing and promenade
Go tell it on the mountain
That Jesus Christ is born

CLOSER



Grand Square
He brought with Him forgiveness
He lived to show us the way
He came to redeem all creation
And to wash our sins away
Four ladies promenade around the ring go
Get back home swing and promenade
Go tell it on the mountain
That Jesus Christ is born
