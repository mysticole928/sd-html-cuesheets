Good Old Girls    (RIV 129)   Scot Byars

OPENER



Circle left
Saturday night, a smoke-filled bar
Waylon and Willie and the boys
Walk around that corner girl, see saw your own
Left allemande, weave the ring
We all love Waylon, we all love Willie
Dosado, promenade them home
You wouldn't see one single good old boy
If it wasn't for the good ole girls

FIGURE



The head two couples square thru go four hands around
With the sides make a right-hand star
Heads star left in the middle, one time you go
To the same two, go right & left thru
Swing thru once, swing thru again
Swing that corner, promenade
You wouldn't see one single good old boy
If it wasn't for the good ole girls

Left allemande, weave the ring
Good ole girls by the dozen
Dosado, swing your lady, promenade, you know we love 'em
