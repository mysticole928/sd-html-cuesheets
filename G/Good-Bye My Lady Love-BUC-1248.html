(Buckskin 1248) Pat Shevokas

All four ladies promenade the ring
Get back home, give your man a swing
Join hands , circle left
Allemande left that corner, weave the ring
Good-bye my lady love, farewell my turtle dove
Swing that lady, promenade the ring
But someday you will come back to me
Love me so tenderly
Good-bye my lady love, good-bye!

FIGURE (twice for the heads - twice for the sides)

Head (side) ladies chain across the ring
Heads (sides) promenade halfway I sing
Walk in and square thru 4
With the outside two, touch 1/4 more
Walk & dodge you do
Face your partner , pass thru
Swing the corner lady, promenade the ring
But someday you will come back to me
Love me so tenderly
Good-bye my lady love, good-bye!
