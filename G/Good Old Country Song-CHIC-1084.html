Good Old Country Song
Lynn Anderson 1976
(Chic 1084) Mike Seastrom , Vic Kaaria
OPENER

All four ladies chain chain straight across the ring
You rollaway and circle left I sing
Four ladies rollaway , you circle left I say
Left allemande and weave around that ring
Play me a good old country song
Swing that girl and promenade along
I can groove on rock & roll, rhythm & blues and I kinda like soul but
Play me a good old country song

FIGURE

Heads you promenade about halfway
Come down the middle and square thru four I say
Go all the way and then, you do a right & left thru
Veer to the left and all ferris wheel
Centers touch 1/4 and box circulate twice to the corner swing
Swing that girl and promenade so nice
I can groove on rock & roll, rhythm & blues and I kinda like soul but
Play me a good old country song

MIDDLE BREAK

Four little ladies promenade that ring
Get back home and give your man a swing
Join hands and circle left , man, circle left you do
Allemande left and weave the ring
Play me a good old country song
The kind that makes you want to sing along
I can groove on rock & roll, rhythm & blues and I kinda like soul but
Play me a good old country song

CLOSER

Four men promenade in the middle of the ring you go
Get on home and swing your lady round and round you know
Join up hands and circle to the left go round the ring
Left allemande and weave around that ring
Play me a good old country song
The kind that makes you clap and sing along
I can groove on rock & roll, rhythm & blues and I kinda like soul but
Play me a good old country song
