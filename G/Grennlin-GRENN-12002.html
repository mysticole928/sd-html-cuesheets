Grennlin
(Grenn 12002 side A )
INTRO

Bow to your partner, corners all, circle to the left around the hall
Circle to the left, around you trot, roll away, go red hot
Turn the right hand lady with a right hand round, back to your own
with a left hand round
All the way round, turn the corner by the right, partner left and roll promenade
Promenade, it's home you go, swing your partner high and low

LINE PAIR-IN PRACTICE

Heads to the right and circle 4, head gents break and line up 4
Forward 8 and back again, pass thru, lines pair-in
Double pass thru across the way, 1/4 in and back away
Bend the line, go left allemande, partner right, go right & left grand

LINE PAIR-OUT PRACTICE

Sides to the right, circle 4, side gents break to lines of 4
Forward and back, don't be late, lines pair-out and separate
Single file, walk past two, line up four that's what you do
Forward eight and then fall back, forward again and box the gnat
Right & left thru the other way back, turn around, cross trail thru
Allemande left and right & left grand

DEUCES WILD

Heads to the right and circle 4, head gents break to lines of 4
Forward 8 and back again, pass thru, lines pair-in
Double pass thru across the way, 1/4 in and back away
Forward and back, don't be late, lines pair-out and separate
Single file walk past 2, line up 4 that's what you do
Forward 8 and back again, pass thru, lines pair-in
Double pass thru across the way, 1/4 in and back away
Forward and back, don't be late, lines pair-out and separate
Single file, walk past two, line up 4 that's what you do
Forward 8 and back to the land, bend the line, left allemande

HALF SQUARE PAIRS

Sides to the right and circle 4, side gents break two lines of 4
Forward 8 and back again, pass thru and lines pair-in
Inside couples pass thru, half square thru the outside 2
Forward out and back with you, bend the line, pass thru, and lines pair-in
Inside 4 pass thru, half square thru the outside 2
Lines of 4 up and back, bend the line, pass thru, and lines pair-in
Inside 4 pass thru, half square thru the outside 2
Lines of 4 out and back, bend the line, pass thru and lines pair-in
Inside 4 pass thru, half square thru the outside 2
Lines of 4 out and back, bend the line, left allemande

LINE PAIR-IN BREAK

Couple No. 1 down the middle, split the ring and line up 4
Forward 4 and back again, cross the floor and the lines pair-in
Forward 4 then face the sides, box the gnat, then box it back
Change hands, left allemande

EXPLANATION of "Lines pair-in" or "Lines pair-out":
To make pairs from even-numbered line, face center of line,
center two pair together going in toward center of set or out from center.
Others fall in behind them as pairs.
