Grenn And Bear It
(Grenn 12005 side A ) Ron Schneider
INTRO

1st & 3rd go forward and back, star by the right but not too far
Back by the left, pick up your corner arm around
Star promenade, go around the town
Back on out to an 8-hand ring, circle to the left til you hear me sing
Those who can left allemande, others rollaway, right & left grand

LINE CRAZY

Heads to the right, circle up 4, head gent break, line up 4
Forward 8 and back with you, bend the line and pass thru
Lines divide and pass thru, with them new lines go up and back
Those who can right & left thru
Forward 8 and back with you, bend the line and pass thru
Lines divide and pass thru, with them new lines go up and back
Those who can right & left thru
Forward 8 and back with you, bend the line and pass thru
Lines divide and pass thru, with them new lines go up and back
Those who can right & left thru
Forward 8 and back with you, bend the line and pass thru
Lines divide and pass two, with them new lines go up and back
Those who can cross trail thru and find your corner, left allemande

PROSSIBLE

Couple #3 bow & swing
Split your corner around one to a line of 3
Go forward and back
Same 6 star by the right not too far, back by the left a 6-hand star
Those who can pick up your corner arm around, star promenade
Back on out to an 8-hand ring, circle to the left til you get straight
Those who can left allemande, others whirlaway
Right & left grand

PASIVIDE

2 & 4 half sashay, go to the right, circle that way
Ladies break and make a line, forward 4 and back you glide
Pass thru, lines divide, lady pass 2, gent pass one
Line up four, go forward and back, crosstrail thru, u-turn back
Pass thru, lines divide, gent pass 2, lady pass one
Line up four, go forward and back, crosstrail thru, u-turn back
Pass thru, lines divide, lady pass 2, gent pass one
Line up four, go forward and back, crosstrail thru, u-turn back
Pass thru, lines divide, gent pass 2, lady pass one
Line up four, go forward and back, crosstrail thru, u-turn back
Arch in the middle, ends duck out around one in the middle
Star by the right 3/4 around, corners all left allemande

SIX BIT SQUARE THRU

1 & 3 backtrack and pass thru
On to the next and square thru 3/4 around that's what you do
Those who can California twirl, all promenade just as you are
2 & 4 backtrack and pass thru
On to the next and square thru 3/4 around that's what you do
Then pass thru the next ole two, box the gnat the ones you meet
Right & left thru the other way back
Crosstrail thru and find your corner, left allemande
