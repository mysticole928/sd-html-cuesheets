Genghis Khan    (FTC 32034)   Holger Willm

INTRO



All 4 ladies chain across the ring you go
Then roll away, circle left
Ladies roll away, you circle left I say
Left allemande, weave it
Weave in and out around, go all the way
Dosado and then you promenade
Genghis Khan is riding again tonight

FIGURE



Heads promenade go halfway round
Sides do a right & left thru, and turn the lady there
Square thru and go 4 hands you know
Everybody pass thru, left allemande and weave
Weave in and out go all the way around
Dosado and then promenade home
Genghis Kahn is riding again tonight

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
They rode the fastest horses, left the wind behind ten thousand men
And one man led the way, the others followed blind Genghis Kahn
Circle left
Left allemande the corner, grand right & left
Meet your own, promenade the set
Genghis Kahn is riding again tonight
