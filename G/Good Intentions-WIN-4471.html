Let The Bells Keep Ringing   (Windsor 4471 )  

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Bow to your partner, ladies chain across the ring
Join hands and circle to the left and once again
4 ladies chain, just to get across the set
Walk around your corner, come on back and swing your pet
(And while the) bells keep ringing
Allemande the corners all
Grand ol' right and left as you go walkin' round the hall
Promenade 'em when you meet your turtle dove
(While the) bells ring out with happy love

FIGURE



Head (side) gents take the corner, forward up and back again
Star by the right and turn it once around the ring
Turn partner by the left, go to the corner - box the gnat
Gents star left and swing the girl across the track
(And let the) bells ring out
While you swing and dance with joy
Allemande left the corner, promenade each girl and boy
(Go walkin') round the ring, then swing 'neath stars above
(While the) bells ring out with happy love
