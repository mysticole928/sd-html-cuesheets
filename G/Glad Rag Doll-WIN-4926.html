Glad Rag Doll
   (Windsor 4926)    Bill Snailum
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Allemande left, allemande thar , forward 2 the men you star
They call her glad rag doll
Shoot that star and on you go, go right and left , dopaso
It's her by the left and the corner by the right, partner left
Allemande thar , the men swing in, you're in that star again
Shoot that star , dosado and promenade my friend
Promenade your baby, dancin' with that pretty little lady
They call her glad rag doll

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

1 & 3 you promenade halfway there with your maid
Into the middle and square thru 4 hands
Dosado the outside two, step to a wave and when you do
All 8 circulate , star thru
Pass thru , partner trade , left allemande and then
Dosado around your own, left allemande again
Promenade your baby, dancin' with that pretty little lady
They call her glad rag doll

Contains a "blended" movement: Circulate (RH Waves) followed by Star Thru.
