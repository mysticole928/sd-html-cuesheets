Ding Dong Daddy From Dumas / Ghost Riders In The Sky
Vaughn Monroe 1949
   (Windsor 7113 side B )    Bruce Johnson
OPENER    B - SSD Week 1

You all join hands and circle left , let's circle round the set
Now allemande left your corner and a grand old right & left
Hand over hand around that ring, you're riding mighty high
Now promenade your partners all, and listen to 'em cry
(All sing) Yippee-i-ooo, yippee-i-aaa; ghost riders in the sky

FIGURE   

Gents star left a left hand star , go once around the set
You turn your partner by the right , your corner by the left
Now right hand star 3/4 round, and a left round your own
Now promenade your corners all, and sing it while you roam
(All sing) Yippee-i-ooo, yippee-i-aaa, ghost riders in the sky
Allemande left that corner girl and see saw round your taw
Allemande left that corner again, and now the old gee haw
A grand old right & left around, until you meet your own
Then promenade your lady fair, and listen to 'em moan
(All sing) Yippee-i-ooo, yippee-i-aaa, ghost riders in the sky
