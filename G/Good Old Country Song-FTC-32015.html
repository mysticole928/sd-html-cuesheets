Good Old Country Song    (FTC 32015)   Ed Fraidenburg

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade the ring
Get home and there you want to swing
Join hands and circle to the left around and then
Left allemande and weave the ring
Play me a good ol' country song
Dosado and promenade along
I can't groove rock and roll
Rhythm and blues and I don't dig soul
Play me a good ol' country song

FIGURE



1 & 3 roll away and then, star thru
Dosado, make an ocean wave you do
Recycle, when your straight, sweep 1/4 more
Square thru 4 hands around the floor
Count to 4 and trade by, swing the corner maid
Left allemande and promenade
I can't groove rock and roll
Rhythm and blues and I don't dig soul
Play me a good ol' country song

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Side ladies chain across and then
1 & 3 square thru and count 4 hands again
Swing thru and when you do the boys run right
1/2 tag, walk & dodge tonight, partner trade
Allemande your corner, walk by your own
Swing the right hand girl and promenade
I can't groove rock and roll
Rhythm and blues and I don't dig soul
Play me a good ol' country song
