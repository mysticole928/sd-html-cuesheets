Get Me Back To Dixie   (Chaparral 323 )   Gary Shoemake

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies promenade get a walkin' round you go
Get back home and swing the man you know
All join hands and circle, get a movin' 'round you go
Allemande left that corner girl and weave around you know
Get me back to Dixie, I love my Tennessee
Swing your lady get once around and promenade you see

FIGURE



Head two couples square thru 4 hands around you go
Dosado the outside two, get once around you go
Swing thru, without a stop you spin the top
Move right up with a right & left thru, turn your lady pop
Square thru 3/4 to the corner lady swing
Swing that corner go once around then promenade the ring
From the muddy Mississippi to the land of the Cherokee
Get me back to Dixie, I love my Tennessee

TAG



Get me back to Dixie, I love my Tennessee

SEQUENCE (Intro, Figure heads twice, middle break, Figure sides twice, closer, tag)
