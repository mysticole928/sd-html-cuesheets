Good Old Square Dance Time   (Blue Ribbon 243 )   Bill Stone

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Four ladies chain you turn with this girl
Hey, join hands circle left you know
Now ladies center men sashay, you circle and then
Those ladies in the men sashay again

Do an allemande left with your corner, come home dosado
Left allemande and then you all promenade her
I'm happy to say, I'm on my way
To a good old square dance time

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Well now those heads promenade and travel halfway you know
Come down the middle and do the right and left thru
You star thru and pass thru and do the dosado
Make an ocean wave, the ladies trade and then recycle

Pass thru and trade by, touch 1/4 there
Scoot back, you swing that girl and promenade the square
'Cause I'm happy to say, I'm on my way
To a good old square dance time
