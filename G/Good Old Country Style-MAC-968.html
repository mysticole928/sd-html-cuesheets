Good Old Country Style    (MAC 968)   Bill Ball

OPENER - BREAK



Now join your hands and make a ring and circle round you roam
Allemande left your corner, turn a right hand round your own
Girls star by the left now, go all the way round you go
Star promenade your corner, walk on a heel and toe
Back straight out and make a ring, then circle left you know
Left allemande that corner, come back and promeno
Promenade around that square, watch this lady smile
Get along home and swing and whirl in good old country style

FIGURE



1 & 3 separate go round the outside world
Meet your own and star thru, then California twirl
2 & 4 cross trail, to a line of 4 you do
Bend the line and star thru, centers pass thru
Swing that lady round, then left allemande
Come back one and promenade go walkin' round that land
You promenade this lady, go walkin' 'bout a mile
Get along home and swing and whirl in good old country style
