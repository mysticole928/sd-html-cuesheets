Gal From Arkansas    (WIN 4907)   Bill Snailum

FIGURE (4 times through)



4 little ladies chain go about 3/4 round
Roll promenade and don't slow down
Head 2 wheel around, right & left thru the pair you found
Ladies chain, dixie style, balance awhile
Boys trade, left allemande, walk right by your taw
Promenade the next, she's a gal from Arkansas
Promenade a little lady, go walking round from there
Now when you're home, the side face, grand square
In a square dance dress demure, she still knows how to lure
Oh mother there just ought'a be a law
Reverse
She'll forget all other men, but brother until then
She's a gal from Arkansas
