Grand Square   (Sets In Order 102 )   Bob Osgood

FIGURE



Sides face, grand square (32 beats)
Head couples right and left thru turn her right around, right & left back
Side couples right and left thru turn her right around, right & left back
Heads to the right, right and left thru, turn her right around, right & left back
Sides to the right, right and left thru, turn her right around, right & left back

Sides face, grand square (32 beats)
1 & 3 ladies chain, turn her right around and chain back
Side ladies chain, chain right back
Head ladies chain to the right, turn her right around and chain right back
Side ladies chain to the right, turn her right around and chain her right back

Sides face, grand square (32 beats)
1 & 3 half promenade across the inside with your maid, right & left home
Side couples 1/2 promenande, and right and left home
Heads to the right, 1/2 promenade and a right and left home
Sides, right, 1/2 promenade and a right and left home
Sides face, grand square (32 beats)

Bow to your partner
