Grand Square
   (Sets In Order 102)    Bob Osgood
FIGURE   

Sides face, grand square (32 beats)

Head couples right and left thru turn her right around, right & left back ( backaway )
Side couples right and left thru turn her right around, right & left back ( backaway )
Heads to the right , right and left thru , turn her right around, right & left back ( backaway )
Sides to the right , right and left thru , turn her right around, right & left back

Sides face, grand square (32 beats)

1 & 3 ladies chain , turn her right around and chain back
Side ladies chain , chain right back ( backaway )
Head ladies chain to the right , turn her right around and chain right back ( backaway )
Side ladies chain to the right , turn her right around and chain her right back

Sides face, grand square (32 beats)

1 & 3 1/2 promenade across the inside with your maid, right & left home ( backaway )
Side couples 1/2 promenade , and right & left home ( backaway )
Heads to the right , 1/2 promenade and a right & left home ( backaway )
Sides, right , 1/2 promenade and a right & left home

Sides face, grand square (32 beats)

Bow to your partner
