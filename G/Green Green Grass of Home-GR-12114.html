Green Grass Of Home    (GR 12114)   Johnny Davis

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and make a ring, circle left and hear me sing
Then you reverse go single file go walking
Girls step out and backtrack, meet your own and box the gnat
Pull by, left allemande and weave the ring
It's in and out around you go until you meet your own
You dosado that gal and promenade
Yes they'll all be there to meet me, arms outstretched, smiling sweetly
When again I see the green green grass of home

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4 round you turn the gal and then
Heads lead to the right and then you circle
Make a line go up and back, and then you swing thru
Without a stop, spin the top you do
Right & left thru, turn the gal, roll away, pass thru
U-turn back and swing and promenade
Yes they'll all be there to meet me, arms outstretched, smiling sweetly
When again I see the green green grass of home
