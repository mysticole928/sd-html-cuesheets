Give My Regards To Broadway
James Cagney; Judy Garland; Al Jolson 1904
(Jopat 220) Joe Porritt , Bill Peterson
OPENER

Circle left
Give my regards to Broadway,
Remember me to Herald Square
Left allemande come home and box the gnat
4 ladies promenade inside the ring
Back home and turn thru, full turn around
Left allemande , promenade
Give my regards to old Broadway
And tell 'em I will soon be there

FIGURE (1)

Heads promenade to Broadway , halfway around and then
Square thru 4 hands around and to the outside two
Swing thru and the boys run to the right
Do a 1/2 tag , trade and roll , pass thru and trade by
Corner swing and promenade
Give my regards to old Broadway
And tell 'em I will soon be there

FIGURE (2)

Head two square thru on Broadway,
4 hands around and do a dosado
Swing thru for fun, all the boy run
Tag the line go all the way through and face right
Ferris wheel , those centers pass thru , turn thru
Left allemande come back and promenade
Give my regards to old Broadway
And tell 'em I will soon be there

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Sides face grand square
(32 beats of simulated tap dancing)
(caller could pretend to be tap dancing for special effect)
Four ladies chain , chain 'em back , promenade
Give my regards to old Broadway
And tell 'em I will soon be there
