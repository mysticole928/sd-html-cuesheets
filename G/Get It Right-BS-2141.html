Get It Right    (BS 2141)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around that corner, then see saw round your own
Then all join hands and circle to the left around you roam
Those gents star by the right one time around that ring
Left allemande that corner, go weaving round again
How much time does it take to get it right
Do a dosado your own, promenade with all your might
You worry me about it day and night
How much time does it take to get it right

FIGURE



Now the head 2 couples promenade go halfway round the square
Now 2 & 4 go right & left thru and you turn that lady there
Flutter wheel in the middle, you sweep 1/4 more
Then pass thru, dosado, then do an eight chain 4
How much time does it take to get it right
You swing the next gal round, promenade with all your might
You worry me about it day and night
How much time does it take to get it right
