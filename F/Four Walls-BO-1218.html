Four Walls    (BO 1218)   Pat Grymes

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain across, turn 'em left and then
Chain the ladies right on home again
Join hands, circle to the left around you go
Left allemande go weaving round that ring
Go in and out around until you meet your own
Dosado, promenade
Four walls to dance in
Four wall for fun

FIGURE



1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around will do
Face the sides and make a right hand star
Heads star left in the middle once around
Same 2 go right & left thru and then
Dive thru, square thru 3 hands around and swing
Swing the corner, promenade
Four walls to dance in
Four wall for fun
