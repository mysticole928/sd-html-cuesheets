INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies chain go across the ring
You roll away and circle left
Four ladies roll away you circle left again

I don't deserve her you know how I love her

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway down the middle
Pass the ocean, extend
Swing thru and then spin the top my friend
Right & left thru, pass the ocean
Ladies trade, boys cross fold
Swing the corner, promenade

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway down the middle
Pass the ocean, extend
Swing thru, boys run, tag the line face right
Wheel & deal, turn thru and go left allemande
Swing and promenade
