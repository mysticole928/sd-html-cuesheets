Fireball Mail   (Roofers 119 )   Gerald McWhirter

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande left with the corner, do an allemande thar
Go right & left and form a star
Gents to the center, right hand star
Shoot the star, full turn around
Right to the partner, pull on by
Do an allemande with the corner, weave the ring
Look at her ride..look at her sail
Oh let her by, by, by Fireball Mail

FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade, go 1/2 way around
Sides star thru, pass thru and then
Oh, you swing thru tonite, spin the top alright
(Boys move up), let's do the right & left thru, turn your girl
Pass the ocean, go, split circulate, go double
That's 2 times and swing, promenade again
Look at her ride..look at her sail
Oh let her by, by, by Fireball Mail
