Friendship
   (Grenn 12034)    Earl Johnston
OPENER    A1

1st and 3rd go up to the middle and you come on back
Promenade your lady, go halfway round the track
Come into the middle , do a dosado all the way around
Make an ocean wave , go forward and back , cross trail
Allemande left your corner, run home and you swing
Around and round and round, promenade that old ring
I'm in favor of friendship
All over the world

FIGURE   

Allemande left and an allemande O, with a right and a left and a dopaso
Her by the left and the corner by the right hand round
This partner left a full turn , 4 ladies chain , turn a new girl,
promenade
Heads wheel around and pass thru , go on to the next and
star thru
Swing the girl in front of you and then promenade
I'm in favor of friendship
All over the world

"allemande O, with a right and a left" just means a "Right Pull By" (then meet next with a left, for a Dopaso)
