Four Gents Star or Sutters Mill / Cowboy Loop
(Old Timer 8017 side A ) Johnny Melton
FIGURE

Ladies to the center and back to the bar
Gents to the center with a right hand star
Turn your opposite lady with a left hand round
Star right back with a heel and tow
And meet your own with a left elbow
Star right back and stomp the ground
Turn the right hand lady with a left hand round
And star right back as you should know
Turn the left hand lady with a left elbow
Star right back and round you go
Meet your honey with a docey doe
It's partner left and corner right
Your partner left go all the way around
Pick up your corner lady and promenade the town

(Repeat three more times to get back original partner)
