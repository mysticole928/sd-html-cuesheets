Fire Ball Mail    (OT 8169)   Dick Houlton

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK



Allemande left your corner and then go allemande thar
Go right & left and form a star, men center with a right hand star
Shoot that star to the heaven's whirl
Go right & left to the second girl
Men center with a right hand star, back along allemande thar
Shoot that star go right & left grand
Every other girl with every other hand
Meet your girl and promenade, promenade along
Look at her run, look at her sail
Let her by, bye bye, Fireball Mail

FIGURE



1 & 3 go up and back, do a right & left thru
Turn your girl and star thru, California twirl
Do a right & left thru the outside two, turn your girl and then
Dive thru, pass thru, left allemande
Well, walk right by your partner
Swing the next girl round and round
Allemande left your corner, promenade your own girl round
Look at her run, look at her sail
Let her by, bye bye, Fireball Mail
