Ferris Wheel    (LO 1089)   Emanuel Duming

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies chain straight across the ring
Join 8 hands and circle to the left I sing
The ladies roll a 1/2 sashay, circle left and then
Roll 'em left a 1/2 sashay, weave around the ring
Wing in and out around, till you meet your maid
Dosado this lady, then all promenade
A hole in one or maybe a strike, a brand new automobile
The thrill is there, like being on a ferris wheel

FIGURE



1st and 3rd lead to the right, circle round the track
Head gents break, make a line, move it up and back
Pass thru, wheel & deal, centers dosado
All the way, make an ocean wave, balance to and fro
The men trade, go right & left thru, full turn around
You swing your corner lady, promenade the town
The music starts, the squares are set, it's time to wheel & deal
The fun is there, like being on a ferris wheel
