Fly Away   (Red Boot 3060 )   Alan Evans
INTRO

Four ladies chain go straight across and then
Join your hands and circle left around that ring
Roll away and circle left I sing
Roll away and weave the ring
Well fly away, fly away from here
Swing your partner and you promenade her right back home
Put on your smile, break out your riotous cheer
Shake your boots and fly away

Heads promenade go halfway round and then
Go right & left thru and turn the girl you know
Square thru 4 hands around you go
Dosado, do an eight chain four
Well fly away, fly away from here
Swing your corner and you promenade her home
* It's time to go, check your worries at the gate
Shake your boots and fly away

Sides face, grand square
(32 beats of music)
Four ladies promenade go once inside the ring
Get back home, swing your partner, promenade
Put on your smile, break out your riotous cheer
Shake your boots and fly away
