Fortuosity    (BS 1891)   Bob Fisk

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain across, turn with that lady
You roll away and circle left around and maybe
You roll away, circle round that ole ring
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring I sing
Fortuosity, that's my by-word
Dosado, you promenade, then take my word
Promenade her round the ring you see
Hey, luck and fortune have a word, it's fortuosity

FIGURE



Head couples promenade 3/4 round the ring go
2 & 4 go right & left thru
You turn that girl and Joe, you pass thru, swing thru, boys run right
Bend the line, right & left thru, turn the girl tonight
Well now you cross trail thru and swing that corner gal now
Left allemande new corner, promenade around there pal
I keep smiling for my philosophy
Is do your best and leave the rest to fortuosity
