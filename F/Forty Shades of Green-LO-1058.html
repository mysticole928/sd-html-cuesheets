Forty Shades Of Green    (LO 1058)   Dick Kenyon

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join your hands and circle you circle round that way
Left allemande your corner, let's do a grand sashay
Dosado go all the way around, right hand and pull on by
See saw round the next one, left hand and pull her by
Dosado that next girl, right hand and pull her by
See saw the next one, left hand and on you fly
Promenade your pretty lass, she's mighty like a queen

Star thru and pass thru, you swing your new Colleen
Join your hands and circle left to forty shades of green
Left allemande your corner girl, now weave around the square
Go in and out til you meet that girl, dosado that lady fair
Promenade this pretty lass, she's mighty like a queen
Take her home and settle down in the forty shades of green
