For Love    (SC 566)   Jeanne Moody

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain 3/4 round
Join your hands, circle left my friend
Roll away 1/2 sashay
Left allemande, dosado
Men star left once and then
Turn thru, left allemande my friend
Promenade her along
But for love I'd be gone

FIGURE



Heads flutter wheel and then
1 & 3 square thru 4 hands
Circle 4 with the outside 2
Break right out and make a line
Slide thru, square thru 3 hands
Swing your corner, promenade along
Why do I need you so?
But for love I'd be gone
