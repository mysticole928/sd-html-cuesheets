Fire House
(Dash 2551) Dale Durbin
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Head (side) two ladies chain across, turn 'em, rollaway
Star thru , dosado the outside two
All the way round, ocean wave , rock it , swing thru
Balance up and back , box the gnat , pull 'em by
Allemande left the corner, dosado your own
Swing your little lady, promenade the ring
She lived next door to a firehouse
Where the engines went clang, clang

FIGURE

All four ladies promenade inside of the ring
Home you go , swing partner, yes you swing
1 & 3 (2 & 4) right & left thru , turn 'em, cross trail
Face those sides (heads) , hoop-de-doo and then
Substitute , then square thru 3/4
Swing the corner, promenade that ring
She moved next door to a station house
Where the pistols went bang, bang

(Note: Hoop-de-doo is executed by having the inside couples divide and star thru outside [behind] the couple they are facing. You have changed from an eight chain thru position to a double pass thru position.)
