Follow Me    (RIV 259)  Tony Oxendine

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four Ladies Chain, you turn that girl and then
You Rollaway, Circle Left go round
Four Ladies roll away, Circle Left and go
Left Allemande and Weave The Ring
Follow me, up and down
All the way and all around
Take my hand and say you'll follow me.

FIGURE



The heads [sides] Promenade half way around
The sides [heads] Circle four ¾ round, now
Center two Pass Thru, all will Circle four, ¾ round you do
All join hands and Circle left, moving along
Allemande Left come back and Promenade
Take my hand and say you'll follow me.
