Grand Sweep: starts in Grand Square position; dance through 4 times (64 beats). Head position: Flutter wheel, then sweep 1/4, pass thru, partner trade & 1/4 more. Heads then in Side position and back away as in Grand Square. Side position: (sides face to start) back away 3 steps, turn in and walk forward, dosado (full around), star thru.
