Flowers On The Wall    (TOP 25122)   Ray Bohn

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande the corner, then you walk right by your own
Right hand lady right, and turn the partner left at home
Gents star right with all your might, till the corner comes in sight
Allemande left, and bow to the partner, weave the ring tonight
Playin' solitaire is fun, with a deck of fifty-one
You can dosado your maid, allemande left then promenade
I'll hold her hand, I've nothin' to do
Don't blame me, I'll blame you, yes you, you

FIGURE



Head 2 couples square thru 4 hands that's all you do
Then a right & left thru with the outside 2 and turn 'em, yes you do
Dive thru and square thru about 3/4 round
Allemande left, and bow to the partner, weave around the town
Pickin' partners from the hall, it won't bother me at all
You can dosado your maid, swing the corner, promenade
Smokin' cigarettes, and watchin' Captain Kangaroo
Don't blame me, I'll blame you, yes you, you

TAG



Swing once more and then no more
Bow to the partners, corners all
Thank your ladies, that'll be all

SEQUENCE: (Intro, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides, ending, tag)
