Freight Train    (BO 1179)   Billy Dittemore

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies chain across, turn this lady fair
Join 8 hands, circle to the left, go walking around the square
Whirl away, pass by one, swing the right hand girl
Then allemande left and weave around that world
Oh freight train, freight train, down the line you go
Dosado your partner, take her hand and promeno
Promenade your lady fair, take her home and swing
Swing her to that song freight train

FIGURE



Head two ladies chain across, couples 1 & 3
Lead to the right and circle up 4, make a little line for me
Pass thru, wheel & deal, the center 4 square thru
The heads divide, star thru, and the corner swing
Well then you allemande that corner girl,
wagon wheel your own Once around, spin her boys,
promenade her home
Promenade with a brand new girl, take her home and swing
Swing her to that song freight train
