Footprints In The Snow    (BS 1582)   Vaughn Parrish

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Dosado your corner in the summertime, bow down in the fall
Gents star left in the middle, go once around that hall
Turn your partner right for a wagon wheel, the gals hook on awhile
Then all 4 couples wheel around in style (put the lady in the lead)
I traced her little footprints in the snow (yes I did)
4 little ladies backtrack round you go (once around)
Turn your partner left around, then promenade the town
You found her when the snow was on the ground

FIGURE



Heads go right and circle, you circle to a line
Go rockin' up & back now, then a right & left thru in time
Turn the girl to a dixie chain, the lady go left you know
The gents turn right & swing the corner Joe
Well the 4 little ladies promenade the ring (all the way round)
Come on back and swing the same old man (round & round)
Then promenade her home, keep her for your own
You found her when the snow was on the ground

SEQUENCE: (Intro, figure heads twice, break, figure sides twice, ending)
