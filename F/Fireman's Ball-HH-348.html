Fireman's Ball

4 ladies chain , turn the girls and then
Join hands , circle , go round the rind my friend
Allemande left in the alamo style , balance hand in hand
Swing thru , go right & left, then a right & left grand
Go grand old right & left until you meet your maid
Dosado once around, then you promenade
You can hear the band a-playin' at the old town hall
We'll go dancin' at the Fireman's Ball

FIGURE

Head 2 ladies chain go straight across the town
The heads square thru , count 4 hands around
Dosado the sides, go full around my friend
Swing thru along the line, boys run and then
Bend the line , cross trail , swing that corner maid
Left allemande new corner, come back and promenade
You can hear the band a-playin' at the old town hall
We'll go dancin' at the Fireman's Ball
