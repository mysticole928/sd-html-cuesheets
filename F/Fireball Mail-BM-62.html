Fireball Mail   (Big Mac 062 )   John Eubanks

INTRO - BREAK



Well, all 4 ladies promenade 1 time around & then
Come on back & swing your man one time
Now join hands & circle left & roll it round the ring
Allemande left your corner, then weave around the ring
Watch her swerve and look at her sway
Come on, swing your lady, promenade that way
Watch her fly, just huggin that rail
She's Dallas bound, bound, bound, fireball mail

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru 4 hands in the middle & go
Well, meet that corner lady, dosado
Now swing thru & then all the boys run right
Tag your line & then face it..right..wheel & deal
Touch 1/4 now..scoot back and then
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Watch her fly, just huggin that rail
She's Dallas bound, bound, bound, fireball mail

CLOSER



Well, all 4 ladies promenade 1 time around & then
Come on back & swing your man one time
Now join hands & circle left & roll it round the ring
Allemande left your corner, then weave around the ring
Watch her swing and watch her sway
Come on, swing your lady, promenade that way
Watch her run and look at her sail
She's Dallas bound, bound, bound, fireball mail
