Fairweather Sweetheart    (BS 1780)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk around that corner, see saw your taw
Gents star right go once around that hall
Turn partner left, like an allemande thar
The men will back right in, now back the star go round the land
Slip the clutch, left allemande
Grand old right & left you travel
Why meet your girl, she'll promenade with you
You better make your mind up or some rainy day you'll wind up
With a fairweather sweetheart, too

FIGURE



Side ladies chain you turn 'em with a left around
4 ladies chain 3/4 round that ring
Come into the middle go right & left thru
Then turn that pretty thing
1/2 square thru, then swing thru
You'll swing that girl, she'll promenade with you
You better make your mind up or some rainy day you'll wind up
With a fairweather sweetheart, too
