Fickle Heart    (DASH 2535)   Bob Morris

FIGURE



Allemande the corners all, swing now with your taw
1 & 3 promenade halfway tonight
Dosado in the middle you know, back to back you go, star thru and then
Pass on thru, left allemande, you're gone again (bow to your girl)
Dosado your partner, now pass her right on by
Right & left grand around the ring you fly
*Dosado new girl and then, promenade again
Too soon now, you must part, swing that girl
