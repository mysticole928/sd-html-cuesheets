FIGURE



Heads/sides promenade 1/2 around the ring
Down the middle & square thru 4 hands and then
All the way and do a right and left thru for me
Touch 1/4, follow your neighbor and spread
All 8 circulate, boys fold, the girls turn back
Swing your corner and promenade like that
From the word go
I knew I'd never go again
