Fleet Footed Contra
   (MacGregor 303)    Don Ward
 
FORMATION

Alternate-duple (Partners are in the opposite line, start with a man at the top of the line closest to and on the caller's left. Alternate gender the length of each line.)

INTRO: (8 counts, Men face left, ladies right)
1-8 One you face (corner) dosado
9-16 Same one swing (End the swing facing as a couple across the set.)
17-24 Circle left with the couple across 3/4, pass thru (The 3/4 circle results in each couple facing their partner. One is facing up the set, the other down the set. Pass thru the couple you have circled with and face a new couple that is next in line.)
25-32 Allemande left the next (Turning once around doing the allemande will place you facing your partner, who will be on the same side of the set.)
33-40 Turn your partner by the right (leave her there)
41-48 2 men star left in center once around
49-56 Partner swing (End facing across.)
57-64 Ladies chain across
Repeat the dance with the next person, up or down the set.
