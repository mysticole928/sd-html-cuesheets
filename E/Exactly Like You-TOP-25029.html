Exactly Like You    (TOP 25029)   Bill Peterson

OPENER



Join your hands and circle, move around the hall
Walk all around your corner, see saw round your taw
Turn to the corner allemande left, like an allemande thar
Go right & left, gents make a right hand star
Shoot that star full turn, your corner box the gnat
Go right & left grand, around the ring right after that
Promenade your honey, sing along you two
I love to swing with someone exactly like you

FIGURE



Head 2 couples lead to the right, circle 4 you know
Break and make two lines of 4, it's up and back you go
Star thru across the hall, then do a 1/2 square thru
Go on to the next and star thru, square thru 3/4 do
Count 3 hands you know, then swing your corner girl
Swing her round and round, promenade with the pretty thing
Take this lady home with you, go walking 2 by 2,
I love to dance with someone exactly like you
