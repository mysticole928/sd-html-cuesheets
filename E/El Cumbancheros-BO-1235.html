El Cumbanchero    (BO 1235)   Chuck Bryant

FIGURE (4 times through)



Head 2 couples promenade halfway round you go
You lead to the right and circle up 4 halfway you know
You dive thru and pass thru and circle with this pair
Head men break and make a line, move 8 to the middle and back
You pass thru, and wheel & deal, do a double pass thru
First 2 left, the next 2 right, and do the right & left thru
You turn your girl and dosado, go once around and then
Slide thru and pass thru and swing old corner there
Why join hands circle, go walking round the ring
Well reverse trail single file, ladies in the lead
Girls step out take a back track, turn your partner right
Left allemande the corner, well you weave the ring tonight
El Cumbanchero is a lover
You do a dosado and promenade her
Your love is true, he'll leave you blue
He'll break your heart in two
El Cumbanchero, the lover
