Every Rosebud    (GR 12106)   Earl Johnston

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain, turn them with the left hand round
Walk all around the corner, see saw your own and now
Allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and star
4 men in themiddle, put the right hand in and roll that allemande thar
You shoot the star, grand right & left you go
Now when you meet your lady, promenade along
You promenade this lady, go walking two by two
Cause every little rosebud reminds me of you

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right, circle round the track
Ring them out and make a line, go up to the middle and back
Right & left thru, turn them on around, star thru you know
Dosado in front of you and then swing thru
Boys trade, turn thru and you go left allemande
Walk by one, swing the next around and round and then
Promenade this lady, go walking two by two
Cause every little rosebud reminds me of you
