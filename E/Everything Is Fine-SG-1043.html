We dedicate this tune to our good friend Tommy Russell who we lost way too soon. Tommy's last two songs he chose to record were "So Far, So Good" and "Everything Is Fine" it shows what kind of man he was. Even in a fight with illness he had a smile and was uplifting to everyone he met. The Solid Gold family will miss him dearly.
