I'll Swing Yours, You Swing Mine / Elbow Swing
   (Old Timer 8039 side B )    Cal Golden
 
FIGURE

First couple out to the couple on the right and circle four
Two gents swing with an elbow swing
Opposite couples elbow swing
Then two gents do the same old thing
Then your own with an elbow swing
Circle four and don't be slow
Two little ladies docey doe
One more change and on to the next

(Repeat from beginning so that #1 couple visit #3 & #4.
Then repeat entire sequence for couples #2, 3 & 4.)
