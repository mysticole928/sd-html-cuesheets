OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK    Basic

You sashay round your corner and you see saw round your taw
Join up hands and circle left , you circle round the hall
Allemande left your corner, right & left grand go round the ring
Meet your partner, dosado , go once around then swing
Enjoy yourself , the men star left around
Take the same little girl along, star promenade the town
Take her home and balance out as quickly as a wink
And swing her boy, enjoy yourself, it's later than you think

FIGURE   

1 & 3 (2 & 4) bow to your girl, go forward up and back
Half square thru across the set and there you box the gnat (in front of you)
Face those two (release the one you boxed the gnat with and face the other two),
go right & left thru , then box the gnat again (in front of you)
Face those two (as above, release the one you boxed the gnat with, face the other one),
circle half , dive thru and swing
Enjoy yourself , your corners allemande
Dosado your partner, swing the corner when you can
And promenade (corner) around the ring as quickly as a wink
Enjoy yourself, enjoy yourself, it's later than you think
