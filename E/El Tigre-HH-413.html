El Tigre    (HH 413)   Ernie Kinney

FIGURE



The heads promenade halfway, and then square thru
Dosado and then, double swing thru
Boys trade, girls trade, go right & left thru
Square thru count 5 hands, trade by
Swing with your corner too, allemande
Now dosado her, all promenade
Kai ah o kadita
Take me back home, she said

MIDDLE BREAK



Left allemande your corner, dosado
Men star left, star promenade with Juanita round the set
Men back out, full turn, then four ladies chain
Send 'em back with a flutter wheel
Could he be the real El Tigre
Left allemande, weave the set
You'll dosado her, swing and promenade
Kai ah o kadita
Take me with you, she said
