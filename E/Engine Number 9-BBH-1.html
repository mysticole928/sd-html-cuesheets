Convention Souvenir -- Auld Lang Syne / Engine #9
Roger Miller 1965
   (B Bar H 1 side B )    Brian Hotchkies
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
National Convention #9, coming up in June this time
Newcastle is the place you want to go, dancing and discussions too
Nobody will be feeling blue
Just allemande with your left hand, partner by the right and the right & left grand
Round dancing, Contra, and Quadrilles, everyone will have their fill
Dosado then promenade your own
The best of callers will be there, so come along
And join a square at the National Roundup 1968

FIGURE   

Well now the head 2 couples right & left thru , head ladies chain
The side couples promenade halfway
Well now the sides ladies chain across, you turn a girl and don't get lost
Allemande to an allemande thar , right and left and you make a star
Will back up a right hand star, go walking around the ring
Shoot the star and pass by one , promenade
You promenade with a right hand girl and take a walk around the world
*To the Police Boys Club for all 3 nights you know

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*to the National Roundup, 1968

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
There's a dance on Monday too, it's been arranged for you
At the Wemans Baya at the Lake McForay shore
From a half past 10 until you're done, so come along and join the fun
You allemande with your old left hand, partner right, a right & left grand
We'll serve a lunch and afternoon tea, at half past 12 and a quarter to 3
Dosado and promenade
Your club caller will be there to call, so come along and have a ball
At the folks convention day on the bay

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
National Convention #9 coming up in June this time
Come and see all your square dance pals
From Queensland to the Aptium Isle, many people will come from many a mile to
Allemande with the old left hand, partner by the right, a right & left grand
When it's done and all is well, folks will wave and say farewell
Dosado and promenade
Oh well Elbin is the place next year, so everybody give a cheer
May square dance live from now to ever more
