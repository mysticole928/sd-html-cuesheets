Unicorn    (SC 514)   John Winton

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join hands and circle to the left you know
The men star right once around you go
Your corners all left allemande, come home and dosado
Bow real low, weave the ring you go
And take some green alligators and long legged geese
Dosado your partner, promenade her please
Some cats and rats and elephants, and sure as you're born
The loveliest of all was my unicorn

FIGURE



Couples 1 & 3 do a 1/2 square thru
Swing thru the outside two, the boys run you do
Couples circulate, then wheel & deal
Go right & left thru and do an eight chain thru
And take some green alligators and long legged geese
Some humpty-backed camels and some chimpanzees
Swing old number nine (corner) and promenade her home
Don't you forget my unicorn
