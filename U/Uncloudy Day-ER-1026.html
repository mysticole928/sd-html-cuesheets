Uncloudy Day    (ER 1026)   Bob Baier

OPENER



All 4 ladies chain go straight across that ring
Youi roll away and circle left
All 4 ladies roll away, you circle left I say
Allemande left with the corner, weave around that ring
Oh the land of cloudless days
Swing that little girl and promenade around that way
Oh they tell me of a home where no storm clouds rise
Lord they tell me of an uncloudy day

FIGURE



Now the head/side two couples promenade go halfway round the square
Down the middle and square thru count to 4
Go all the way around and then right & left thru you know
You veer to the left and ferris wheel just like before
Centers square thru and get 3/4 round that ring
Swing that girl and promenade around I sing
Oh they tell me of a home where no storm clouds rise
Lord they tell me of an uncloudy day
