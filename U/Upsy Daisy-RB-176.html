Upsy Daisy   (Red Boot 176 )   Harold Bausch
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING

Allemande your corner, swing with your maid
Join hands, you circle round the town
Walk around your corner, see saw your own
Men star by the right hand go round
Allemande your corner, grand right & left
When you meet your lady promenade the set
And I'll bring you flowers most every day
Sweet upsy-daisy, please stay

FIGURE



Four ladies promenade go walkin' round the ring
Come back home and give your guy a swing
1 & 3 curlique, walk & dodge you see
Circle up 4 and make a line for me
Star thru and square thru 3/4 around
Swing corner lady and promenade the town
Well I'll bring you flowers most every day
Sweet upsy-daisy, please stay
