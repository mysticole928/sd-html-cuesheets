Uncle Pen
(Cloverleaf 1) Eddie Millan
OPENER

Sides face, grand square
Oh the people would come from far away
To dance all night to the break of day
The caller would holler docey-do
You knew Uncle Pen was ready to go
Late in the evening about sundown
High on a hill up above the town
Uncle Pen played the fiddle, Lordy how it would ring
You could hear it talk, you could hear it sing

FIGURE

Head couples promenade halfway around with your maid
Down the middle and pass thru , partner trade is what you do
Reverse the flutter wheel and go, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and dosado , one time around you go
Touch 1/4 then, scoot back and gone again
Boys run right and allemande left
Swing a new girl and promenade
Uncle Pen played the fiddle, Lordy how it would ring
You could hear it talk, you could hear it sing

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Four ladies promenade inside that ring I say
Get back and swing your beau one time around you know
Join hands and make a ring , you circle left I sing
Allemande left your corner girl, now weave around that world
Oh the people would come from far away
To dance all night to the break of day
The caller would holler docey-do
Take Uncle Pen and promenade home
Uncle Pen played the fiddle, Lordy how it would ring
You could hear it talk, you could hear it sing

TAG (32 beats)

Four ladies chain straight across that ring and then
Chain that lady home , turn that girl around and go
Allemande left your corner, come back and swing your girl
One time around and then, bow to the partner, corners all
Say whoa, Uncle Pen, that's all
