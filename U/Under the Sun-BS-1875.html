Under The Sun    (BS 1875)   Dick Bayer

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain, you turn your girl and roll away, circle to the left
Go walking round that ring and all 4 ladies roll away
You circle left and then you allemande left on the corner, weave the ring
Oh how I love you, yes I love you
Dosado and then you promenade her home
Oh how I love you, yes I love you
I love you under the sun

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around you go, and get to the corner dosado
One time around and then, star thru and a right & left thru turn your girl
And then those ladies lead do a dixie style to an ocean wave
Well now those girls circulate, boys trade, allemande left
And a grand old right & left, until you meet your gal
You promenade her, go walking round the ring
Promenade her under the sun
