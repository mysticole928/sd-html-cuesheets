Country Style    (TOP 25138)   Chip Hendrickson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Gents star right 3/4 round, and you turn 'em left
All the way 'round, turn the corner right, and then it's all the men
Gents star left 3/4 round, now you turn 'em right
Left allemande, go forward 2, with a right and left
Turn on back and dosado, home you go and swing
Swing your partner round and round, and promenade the ring
Oh, promenade your partner, I want to see you smile
Home you go and square 'em up, in the good old country style

FIGURE



Girls star right and go 3/4, well you turn 'em left
Send 'em left (the girls) with a right hand round
We'll try the girls again
Girls star left 3/4 round, and you turn 'em right
Send 'em right (the girls) with a left hand round
And all 4 ladies chain
Chain across and rollaway, the next young girl you swing
Swing that lady round and round, and promenade the ring
Go walkin' and a-talkin' about a great big mile
We just love to dance along, in the good old country style
