Cross The Brazos    (K 1071)   C.O. Guest

SEQUENCE



figure twice heads, middle break, figure twice sides

FIGURE



heads/sides promenade go just 1/2 way
right & left thru & turn the girl
heads/sides star left in the center to the same two
right & left thru turn the girl, swing thru
boys you run, wheel & deal now, dive thru
square thru 3/4 round
allemande the corner, docey partner, corner swing & promenade
Cross the Brazos in Waco
I'm safe when Ii reach San Antone

MIDDLE BREAK



4 little ladies chain across the ring now
rollaway & circle to the left
4 ladies rollaway, dosado the next
the men star left one time
star promenade with your partner
4 ladies backtrack twice & then second time you meet, dosado her
bow then weave that ole ring
you're gonna cross the Brazos in Waco
swing
you'll make it by dawn
promenade her by Waco

TAG



swing her in ole San Antone
