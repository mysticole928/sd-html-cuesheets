Carolina In The Morning    (NIC 45)   Virginia Callaghan

OPENER



Circle left
Nothin' could be finer than to be in Carolina in the morning
Allemande left your corner, dosado your own
Men star left one time
Turn your partner right, allemande left
Swing your girl, promenade that set
Nothin' could be finer than to be in Carolina in the morning

FIGURE



Head 2 couples promenade halfway round the ring
Lead to the right, circle 4 make a line I sing
Go up to the middle and back, touch 1/4
Coordinate, boys move up, bend the line
Star thru, pass thru, trade by, swing
Swing the corner, promenade home
Nothin' could be finer than to be in Carolina in the morning

MIDDLE BREAK



All 4 ladies chain, turn that girl
Star back and do a dopaso
Partner left, your corner by the right, your partner left
Head ladies center for a teacup chain
If I had Aladdin's lamp for only a day
I'd make a wish and here's what I'd say
Nothin' could be finer than to be in Carolina in the morning

CLOSER



All 4 ladies promenade one time round
Get back home, everybody swing
Join hands, circle left, go moving around the square
Allemande left, weave the ring
If I had Aladdin's lamp for only a day
I'd make a wish and here's what I'd say
Nothin' could be finer than to be in Carolina in the morning
