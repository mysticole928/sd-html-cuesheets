Calcasieu Jambalaya / Shift The Gears
   (Old Timer 8068-78 side A )    Bill Castner
 
FIGURE

Head two couples you balance and swing
Go forward up and back to the ring
Same two couples face back to back
Boomps-sa-daisy on the outside track
Go round the outside ring you go
All the way round and don't be slow
Pass your partner, go to your corner
Star by the right and don't step on her
It's four hands here and four hands there
Now back by the left with your ladies fair
Head couples star in the center you go
Star by the right and don't be slow
You meet your corner with a left allemande
And walk right into a right & left grand

MIDDLE BREAK

Head two couples go forward and back
Side two couples go forward and back
Head two couples right & left thru
Side two couples right & left thru
Head two ladies, ladies chain
Side two ladies do the same
Head two couples right & left thru
Side two couples right & left thru
Head two ladies, ladies chain
Side two ladies, ladies chain
