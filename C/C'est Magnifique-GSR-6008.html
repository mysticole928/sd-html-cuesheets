C'est Magnifique    (GSR 6008)   Jerry Firenzi

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies chain across the way
You circle left then whirl away
Left allemande, grand right & left by hand
Ooh la la, docey I say
Gents star left, pass once and swing the next
Ooh la la, c'est mon cheri
Promenade
Whisper "Je t'adore, c'est magnifique

FIGURE



Heads star thru and California twirl
Circle four and make a line
Pass thru and bend that line, pass thru and then
Cast off 3/4 round, pass thru
Wheel & deal, center four square thru
Go 5 hands, swing mon cheri
Promenade
Whisper "Je t'adore, c'est magnifique

ALTERNATE OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies chain across you go
Then turn and chain 'em back you know
Left allemande, grand right & left by hand
Meet your own, now dosado
Men star left one time around the set
Swing your own, c'est mon cheri
Promenade
Whisper "Je t'adore, c'est magnifique

ALTERNATE FIGURE



1 & 3 go right & left thru
Turn and chain same ladies do
2 & 4 go right & left thru
Turn and chain your ladies, too
All four ladies chain across the ring
Turn your girl, corner swing
Promenade
Whisper "Je t'adore, c'est magnifique
