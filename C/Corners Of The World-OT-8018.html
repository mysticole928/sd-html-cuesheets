Corners Of The World / Arkansas Traveler
(Old Timer 8018 side A ) Joe Boykin
FIGURE

First and third bow and swing
Star by the right in the center of the ring
Side couples whirl to the corners of the world
Right & left through and across the plate
Swing on the corner like a hinge on a gate
Now swing your partners and don't be late Star by the right in the center once more
The outside whirl as you did before
Right & left thru and across the plate
Left allemande and a right & left grand
Meet your partners and give a swing
And promenade those dear little things

(Repeat with side couples as travelling couples.)
