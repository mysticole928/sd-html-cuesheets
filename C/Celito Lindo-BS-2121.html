Celito Lindo    (BS 2121)   Jerry Helt

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain straight across the ring
Walk all around the corner lady
See saw round the partner
Allemande left with the corner go forward 3
Meet your own, weave the ring
I yi yi yi yi
Dosad your own and promenade her
Promenade the ring back home again
Promenade your own senorita

FIGURE



All join hands circle left halfway round
Heads forward up to the middle and back
Square thru 4 hands around, dosado
Eight chain thru 4 hands
I yi yi yi yi
Swing with 5 and promenade her
Promenade the ring, back home again
Promenade with this pretty senorita
