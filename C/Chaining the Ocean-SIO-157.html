Chaining The Ocean
   (Sets In Order 157)    Tommy Cavanagh
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Head (side) couples promenade, go 1/2 way round the ring
Sides a right and left thru , you turn your girl and then
Allemande left the corner, your partner box the gnat
Pull her by , left allemande , promenade your partner Jack
You promenade your lady, take her home and swing
All 4 couples ready when I begin to sing

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Balance up and back , everybody chain *
Head men go right and turn a new girl, then balance once again
It's everybody chain , head gents go right again
Turn a new girl, the corner swing
And then left allemande , come back and promenade her
Walk around the ring, everybody ready when I begin to sing

TAG

Bow to your partner, corners all
That'll do ya, that's all

*NOTE: On the call Everybody Chain the four ladies chain straight across the set.
At the same time the head men face right and give a right to the side gent, pull
by and courtesy turn the lady coming into position. Head men remain head men
throughout.
