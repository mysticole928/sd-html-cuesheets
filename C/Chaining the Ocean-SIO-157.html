Sides a right and left thru, you turn your girl and then
Allemande left the corner, your partner box the gnat
Pull her by, left allemande, promenade your partner back
You promenade your lady, take her home and swing
All 4 couples ready when I begin to sing

Heads lead to the right and circle to a line
Bend the line, and men you walk across
Swing the lady, promenade, and don't you dare get lost
Walk around the ring & everybody ready when I sing
