Country Mind, Country Heart    (G 9006)   Daryl Clendenin

SEQUENCE



figure twice, middle break, figure twice, closer

FIGURE



1&3 (2&4) you square thru, 4 hands around you go
meet that corner lady, dosado
swing thru, 2 by 2, boys run right
wheel & deal & sweep 1/4 more, star thru
then do a flutterwheel around the ring you run
swing the corner lady, promenade
Listen to my country mind, believe in everything
my country heart has to say

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



circle left
Country mind, country heart, tho we live 2 worlds apart
come and listen to the sounds
rollaway and go red hot, turn that right hand lady right
partner left go full around, turn the corner by the right
partner left go allemande thar the men back in & then
slip the clutch, left allemande, come back & promenade
and I'll give my country mind & heart to you
