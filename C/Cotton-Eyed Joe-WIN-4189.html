The Red River Twist / Cotton-Eyed Joe
Bob Wills; Adolph Hofner and His San Antonians; many others 1927
   (Windsor 4189 side B )   
 
(Starting position: couples or lines of 3 or 4; hands may be joined or arms around waist)

MEASURE

1 cross, kick
2 back, 2, 3
3 cross, kick
4 back, 2, 3
5-8 repeat 1-4
9-16 eight forward two-steps (with vigor)

INSTRUCTIONS IN DETAIL

1 cross, kick
With weight on left, cross right over left, swing right toe out in front with upward motion

2 back, 2, 3
Step back right, left, right

3 cross, kick
With weight on right, cross left over right, swing left toe out in front

4 back, 2, 3
Step back left, right, left

5-8 repeat measures 1-4

9-16 eight forward two-steps (right two-step, left two-step, etc.)
Right forward, close left to right, forward right, forward left, close right to left, forward left, etc.

(Dance starts again)
