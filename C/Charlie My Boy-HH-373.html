Charlie My Boy
Eddie Cantor; Billy Murray; others 1924
(Hi Hat 373) Dick Jones
(Note: this instrumental goes through the tune 5 times. The Break is used once and the Figure is called four times. Dick Jones uses the Break as an Opener, however, you may prefer to use it as a Middle Break.)

BREAK

Join hands and circle , until you hear me sing
Corners all, left allemande , then do a grand sashay
Dosado, back to back, right hand pull on by
See saw the next, left hand pull on by
Dosado once again, right hand pull on by
See saw, go once more, left hand and pull on by
You turn thru the next, and then left allemande
Come back one, swing and whirl, promenade the land
With Charlie my boy, oh, Charlie my boy
Oh, oh, Charlie my boy

FIGURE

1 & 3 square thru 4 hands and then
Now meet your corner, square thru go 4 hands again
Count to 4, you're facing out, then wheel & deal I say
Do a double pass thru , first couple go left, the next go right
Slide thru , swing thru , girls trade , boys trade
The boys are gonna run , then wheel & deal that way
Dive thru , pass thru , star thru , cross trail
Skip one corner , swing the next , then promenade
With Charlie my boy, oh, Charlie my boy
Oh, oh, Charlie my boy
