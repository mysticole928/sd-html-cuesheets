Come On Over    (GR 12096)   Earl Johnston

BREAK



Allemande left like that, go home and box the gnat
4 ladies promenade inside, it's once around you wander
Swing your pretty little girl, now circle round the world
You circle to th eleft, left allemande and weave the ring
And come on over to our side for a square dance
Dosado your girl and then you promenade
Come on over to our side and we'll dance to a Grenn hoedown
Unnh, hunnh!

FIGURE



Well now the heads square thru 4 hands around you do
Gonna dosado with the outside 2, it's all the way around
Swing thru and then, boys run around the girl
Now wheel across to the other side, and then you wheel & deal
And you can dive thru, star thru, and cross trail
Swing the corner girl and then you promenade
Come on over to our side and we'll dance to a Grenn hoedown
Unnh, hunnh!
