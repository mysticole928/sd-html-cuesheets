Calling By The River

4 ladies promenade go once inside tonight
Get on down do a curlique and boys run right
Girls turn back , left allemande , dosado your own
Left allemande and weave the ring
Let's all go down to the river
Gonna dosado and then you promenade
You promende for me, for I wanna see
That square dance man a-callin' by the river

Then bend the line and do the right & left thru
Now slide thru , square thru 3/4 round the ring
Swing your lady, promenade
You promenade for me, for I wanna see
That square dance man a-callin' by the river
