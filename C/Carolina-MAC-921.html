Carolina    (MAC 921)   Fenton Jones

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK



1 & 3 a 1/2 sashay, lead to the right, circle away
Ladies break, make 2 lines of 4
Forward 8, come back to the world
Pass thru, dixie twirl*
Circle up 8, go walkin' round the floor
The girls square thru count 4 hands, then
Partners swing and you whirl
Allemande left, an allemande thar, go right & left
The gents back up a right hand star
Shoot that star, you'll hear me say
Go right & left a 1/2 sashay, resashay, go full around
Gents star right, across you go, opposite left, dopaso
Her by the left, corners right hand round
Partners left, go twice around
Walk on your heel and toe, take your corners, promenade
Around the ring you go
Nothing could be finer than to swing with Carolina in the morning

FIGURE



Allemande left your corner, dosado your partner
The gents star left, go once around, partners right, double the gnat
Box it over, box it back
Grand old right & left around the town
Meet your lady, dosado, corners swing and you whirl (new partner)
Allemande left, go twice around
The girls star right, gents promenade around the world
Pass your own, on you go (new partner)
Pass her again, dopaso (original partner)
Her by the left, corners right hand round
Partners left, allemande thar, back up boys, you've got a star
Shoot that star, go full around, catch all eight by the right hand 1/2
Back by the left you know, take new corners, promenade (opposite lady)
Around the ring you go singing
Nothing could be finer than to be with Carolina in the morning

*Dixie twirl: 2 lines of 4 facing out. The right hand lady in the line of 4 ducks through the center arch and the other dancer follows through the arch with her until all are facing in to the center of the set (no one lets go of hands).



Dixie Twirl is an As Couples California Twirl
