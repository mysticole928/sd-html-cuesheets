Cajun Baby   (Top 25227 )   Jim Stewart

BREAK



Join your hands and circle around the old Bayou
Walk around your corner, see saw 'round your taw
Allemande left to an allemande thar, right & left then star
Men back into the middle, got a right hand in
Slip the clutch, skip one girl, the corners allemande
Grand right & left around the big old land
Dosado that Cajun Baby, promenade her home
Promenade that Cajun Baby down to Louisian

FIGURE



Heads lead to the right now circle to a line
Without a stop pass thru and do a wheel & deal
Center 4 you dosado once around and then
Fan the top, step thru, do a partner trade
Swing your corner lady and go left allemande
Grand right & left, meet a pretty black-eyed maid
Dosado that Cajun Baby, promenade her home
Promenade that Cajun Baby down to Louisian

SEQUENCE (break, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, break)
