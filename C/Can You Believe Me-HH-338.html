Can You Believe Me
   (Hi Hat 338)    Red Bates
FIGURE   

Walk around the corner , turn your partner left and then
4 ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Now 1 & 3 dance up to the middle then you back away
Star thru , a dosado , all the way I say
Swing thru , two by two, balance there and then
Turn thru , the corner allemande
Turn partner by the right , men star left around
Same little lady promenade , go walkin' round the town
How could you believe me when I say I love you
When you know I've been a liar all my life

MIDDLE BREAK    B - SSD Week 1

Well 4 little ladies promenade , go full around the ring
Come on home and give your man a swing
Join your hands , circle left , go walkin' round the land
Left allemande the corner, go right & left grand
Hand over hand around, meet your girl with a dosado
Back to back, the men star left one time
Turn your partner by the right , your corner allemande
Come on back, promenade , go walkin' hand in hand
How could you believe me when I say I love you
When you know I've been a liar all my life
