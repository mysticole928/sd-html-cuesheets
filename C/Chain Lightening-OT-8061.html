Oklahoma Cyclone / Chain Lightening
(Old Timer 8061 side B ) Butch Nelson
FIGURE

Four ladies chain and watch them go
Opposite left and don't be slow
Right hand round with your pretty little corner
Left to your own, go all the way round her
Right to the next and on you go
A left to the next and a dopaso
So the coyotes howl and the birdies sing
Pick up your own and make a little ring
Make a little ring and around you go
It's a right hand round with your pretty little corner
Left to your own, go all the way round her
Lead to the right and don't fall down
Swing Sally Goodin' round and round
And promenade when you come down
