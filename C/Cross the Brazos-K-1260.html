Cross The Brazos   (Kalox 1260 )  
SEQUENCE

Figure twice, break, figure twice

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade go just 1/2 way
Right & left thru & turn your girl
Square thru 4 hands with Camelia
Star right with the outside pair
Heads (sides) star left in the center to the same two
Right & left thru, turn the girl (swing thru)
Boys, you run, wheel and deal now
Dive thru, square thru 3/4 round
Allemande the corner, docey partner
Corner swing and promenade
Cross the Brazos in Waco
I'm safe when I reach San Antonio

BREAK



4 little ladies chain across the ring now
Rollaway and circle to the left
4 ladies rollaway and dosado the next
The men star left one time
Star promenade with your partner
4 ladies backtrack twice & then
Second time you meet, dosado her
Left allemande and weave that ole ring
You're gonna cross the Brazos in Waco
Swing the girl, promenade
Cross the Brazos in Waco
And swing her in old San Antonio
