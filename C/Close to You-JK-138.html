Close To You    (JK 138)   Randy Anderson

FIGURE



Heads you promenade halfway
Side ladies chain you know
Sides square thru 4 hands around
And then swing thru, boys run
You bend the line, slide thru and then square thru
3 hands around that corner swing
Allemande left new corner, come back and then you weave around the ring
Turn your partner right, the men star left
Same girl you promenade
Just like me, they long to be
So close to you

MIDDLE BREAK



Walk around your corner, the left hand turn your own
Men star right, you know
One time go, left allemande
Then swing your own, sides face, grand square
On the day the you were born the angels got together
And decided to create a dream come true
So they sprinkled moon-dust in your hair and golden star-light in your eyes
Four ladies chain, chain back
Walk around your corner
Come back and promenade
Just like me, they long to be
So close to you
