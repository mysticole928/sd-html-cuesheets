Four Gents Star or Sutters Mill / Cowboy Loop
   (Old Timer 8017 side B )    Johnny Melton
 
FIGURE

First couple bow and now you swing
Now lead to the right and circle four around you go
Break it up with a docey doe, docey high and docey low
One pretty girl and one old maid
Two old cowboys promenade
Circle four int he middle of the floor
Two hold up and through go four
Now come on around and right back through
With a cowboy loop like Texans do
Now tie your knot with a pig n' string
While the lonesome couple takes a swing
Now pick up two and circle six
Circle six and around you go
And the three little ladies docey doe
And two pretty girls and one old maid
Three old cowpokes promenade
Now circle six and don't get mixed
And two hold up and through goes six
Now come on around, right back through
With a cowboy loop like Texans do
Now tie that knot with a pig n' string
While the lonesome couple takes a swing
And pick up two and circle eight
And docey doe, three pretty girls, one old maid
4 old wranglers promenade
