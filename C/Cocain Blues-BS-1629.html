Cocain Blues
Johnny Cash and many others 1947
(Blue Star 1629) Larry Faught
BREAK

All four ladies grand chain across the lane
Turn the girls, whirl away , circle the ring
Left allemande the corner,
then go forward two go right & left, turn back one ,
swing the corner Sue
4 men a left hand star go one time around
Pass this girl and dosado the next one round
Promenade your girl, take her home with you
And swing the lovin' baby to the cocain blues

FIGURE

Head 2 couples promenade go 3/4 round
2 & 4 go right & left thru a full turn round
Dive thru , star thru , same ladies chain
Turn the girls and square thru go round the ring
A four handed square thru then do a dosado
Same lady swing , oh you swing them around
Left allemande the corner, promenade the girl you swung
Go walkin', talkin', walkin', round the ring you run
