OPENER



Circle left
It was a teenage wedding and the old folks wished them well
Now the men star right, you're doing swell
Left allemande and weave
You could see that Pierre did really love the mademoiselle
Swing your girl, promenade
C'est la vie, says the old folks, it goes to show you never can tell

FIGURE



Heads promenade go halfway around
Square thru 4 hands, go right & left thru
Veer left, ferris wheel, square thru 3
Swing your corner, promenade
C'est la vie, says the old folks, it goes to show you never can tell

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain across the ring, chain them back home
Join your hands and make a ring, circle left
Left allemande and weave that ring, promenade
C'est la vie, says the old folks, it goes to show you never can tell
