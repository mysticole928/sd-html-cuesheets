Cindy Balance
(Blue Star 1503) Andy Andrus
(Sequence: Short OPENER, FIGURE 4 times)

OPENER

Sashay round your corner gal, come back and swing your own
Allemande left your corner gal and promenade Cindy home
You promenade with Cindy , promenade home I say
Git along home Cindy, I'll swing you another day

FIGURE

Head couples down the center and split the ring in two
Come in the open window and balance Cindy Lou
*You balance with old Cindy , you balance Cindy Lou
Dosado round Cindy and swing , swing her too
Allemande left your corner gal, allemande right your own
Go back and swing your corner gal and promenade her home
Promenade home Cindy , promenade home I say
Get along home Cindy, I'll swing you another day

*Balance step is done by first hopping on left foot and swinging right diagonally across in front of left; then hopping on right foot and at the same time swinging left foot diagonally across in front of right foot. Do this for eight beats then, sashay round Cindy. Cindy is lady facing you when you come into center of ring.

Circa 1956-57
