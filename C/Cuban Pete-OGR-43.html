Cuban Pete
   (Omar 43)    Corben Geis
OPENER - CLOSER

Sides face grand square
They call me Cuban Pete, I'm the king of the rumba beat
When I play the mariachis I go chick-chicky-boom, chick-chicky boom
Yes sir, I'm Cuban Pete, I'm the craze of my native street
When I start to dance, everything goes chick-chicky-boom, chick-chicky boom
Four boys promenade inside the ring
And get back home and swing Carmen Miranda and all promenade
So if you like the beat, take a lesson from Cuban Pete
And I'll teach you to chick-chicky-boom, chick-chicky-boom

FIGURE

The heads (sides) promenade halfway around the ring you sway
Come down the middle and square thru 4 hands
4 hands around I say, swing thru and say "ole!"
Boys run to the right and ferris wheel
Centers you square thru and go 3/4 round
You swing and all promenade
So if you like the beat, take a lesson from Cuban Pete
And I'll teach you to chick-chicky-boom, chick-chicky-boom

MIDDLE BREAK

Four ladies promenade inside the ring Cha-cha
Come back home and swing old Cuban Pete
Join all your hands and then, circle to the left my friend
Left allemande, weave the ring ( Snap your fingers )
They call me...
The senoritas they sing and they swing with sombreros
It's very nice, so full of spice
And when they're dancing they bring a happy ring of vacqueros
Singin' a song, all the day long
So if you like the beat, take a lesson from Cuban Pete
And I'll teach you to chick-chicky-boom, chick-chicky-boom

*SPECIAL CLOSER and TAG

Join your hands and circle left
Now go single file, conga style (put your hands on the shoulders of the person in front of you)
Number 1 man break and take your conga line wherever you'd like...
Cha-cha-cha
