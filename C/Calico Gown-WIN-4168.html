Bless Your Pea Pickin' Heart / Calico Gown
Billy Merrin & His Commanders 1933
(Windsor 4168 side B ) Bruce Johnson
FIGURE (4 times through)

Walk all around your corner , turn a left hand round your pet
Go back and swing the corner , she's the one you can't forget
Circle round the land, you're walking hand in hand
Whirlaway , go right & left grand
And when you meet your lady, dosado , well I declare
Turn the corner left , the ladies chain across the square
Turn 'em there and then, you swing the corner Jane
Quickly circle 8 in a ring

Forward 8, come back , face the corner , box the gnat
Pull her by to dopaso , partner left
Corner right so nice, partner left, turn her twice
Hold her tight like allemande thar
Now everybody 1/2 sashay to put the ladies in
Your corners swat the flea , then promenade around the ring
Just walk along on down, with the sweetest girl in town
In her little calico gown
