Calcutta    (AMR 202)   Leif Ekblad

OPENER



Four ladies promenade, go once inside the ring
Get back and swing
Circle left ...
Allemande left and right & left grand
Calcutta, I am a taxi driver in
Calcutta swing and promenade the ring
Calcutta, I am a taxi driver man

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway
Walk in and square thru, go four hands around
And then box the gnat, pull by
Trade by and box the gnat again
Swing thru and then swing thru again
Swing that girl and promenade the ring
Calcutta, I am a taxi driver man

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
Calle-calle-cutta-cutta-cutta-ehyo-ehyo
Calle-calle-cutta-cutta-cutta-ehyo-ehyo
Circle left
Calcutta, I am a taxi driver in
Calcutta swing that girl and promenade in
Calcutta, I am a taxi driver man

TAG
