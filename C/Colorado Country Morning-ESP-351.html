Colorado Country Morning    (ESP 0351)  Tom Miller

OPENER



Grand Square
Looking through the window of a greyhound going down to San Antone
Watching people come and people go and feeling so alone.
Allemande and Weave
To see that Colorado country morning
Swing the little lady and Promenade
I left that Colorado country morning and a Denver woman on my mind.

FIGURE



Heads [sides] Square Thru and get four hands you do, Do Sa Do
Swing Thru and then Boys Run Right, Bend the Line, Right & Left Thru
Pass the Ocean, Recycle now,
Swing the corner lady and Promenade
I left that Colorado country morning and a Denver woman on my mind.

MIDDLE BREAK



Circle Left
I remember standing at the door and looking back as she lay sleeping
Left Allemande that corner, Turn your partner by the right hand
Allemande Left and Weave the Ring
Without a sound I turned around and softly kissed her goodbye
Swing the girl and Promenade
I left that Colorado country morning and a Denver woman on my mind.

CLOSER



Well, four Ladies Promenade in the middle, around you go, run back and Swing
Circle to the left, rolling around you go, Allemande that corner, Weave the ring
To see that Colorado country morning, dusty roads and warm sunshine
I left that Colorado country morning and a Denver woman on my mind.
