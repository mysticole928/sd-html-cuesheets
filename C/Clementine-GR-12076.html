Clementine    (GR 12076)   Earl Johnston

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left, allemande thar, right & left make a star
The men wheel down the line
Let the star to the heavens whirl, right & left to the second girl
She must be Clementine
Shoot the star go right & left grand, go walking round the ring
When you meet that lady promenade
You promenade the ring, take her home again
Oh my darling Clementine

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain across and couples 1 & 3
Dosado go all the way round, make an ocean wave for me
Rock up and back, spin the top, that's all you do
Box the gnat, square thru 3 hands around and you
Find the corner, left allemande, go home and dosado
Your corner swing and promenade
Promenade the ring, take her back home again
Oh my darling Clementine
