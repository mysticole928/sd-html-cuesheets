Charley, My Boy    (MAC 8685)  

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande your corner, come home and you swing
You swing your lady once or twice and promenade the ring
1 & 3 wheel around and star thru you do
Right & left thru, then pass thru, go on to the next
A star thru, right & left thru, you turn with your pet
Star thru, cross trail, left allemande
Come back one and promenade, you promenade the land
Promenade home with your pride and joy
And swing with Charley, my boy

FIGURE



Walk all around your corner, see saw your pet
Head 2 couples forward, come back to the set
Now cross trail, u-turn back, the opposite swing
Face the middle and pass thru and circle half you do
Dive thru and square thru 3/4 round you go
Corner lady swing, you swing her high and low
Allemande left new corner, grand right & left you go
When you meet your lady, promenade her on home
Promenade home with your pride and joy
*And swing, swing Charley, my boy

TAG ENDING (Substitute for last line of figure)



*Swing with Charley, oh, my Charley
Swing with Charley, my boy
