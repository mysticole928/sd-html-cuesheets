Change My Love   (Royal 119 )   Jerry Story

OPENER



Side face, grand square
My love is warmer than the warmest sunshine
Softer than a sigh
My love is deeper than the deepest ocean
Wider than the sky

Four ladies chain across the ring, you chain her home
Take her by the hand and promenade
And there is nothing in this world that could ever
Change my love

Change my love
