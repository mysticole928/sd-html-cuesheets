Cincinnati Ohio    (HH 0358)   Rex Coats

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join hands, circle left about a mile
Then you allemande left in the alamo style
Swing thru, right & left and balance to and fro
A left hand swing, 4 ladies chain you know
Turn the girl and rollaway, you weave the line
In and out around, like the river winds
Promenade this lady, full around you know
Swing in Cincinnati, Ohio

FIGURE



*1 & 3 go up and back, a 1/2 square thru

Wheel & deal, meet a girl, swing her tonight
Promenade this lady, full around you know
