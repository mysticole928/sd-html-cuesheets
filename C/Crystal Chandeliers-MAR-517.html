Crystal Chandeliers   (MarLet 517 )   Grady Humphries

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Circle left
The crystal chandeliers light up the paintings on the wall
The marble statuettes are standing stately in the hall
Left allemande that corner girl, you dosado your own
Men star left around you go
Turn your partner by the right, and go left allemande
Swing and whirl your own little girl, and promenade the land
You promenade this lady and she'll never shed a tear
You swing her 'neath the crystal chandelier

FIGURE



1 & 3 you promenade halfway around you go
Down the middle a right & left thru, turn the girl you know
Flutter wheel in themiddle you go and sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and do a right & left thru
Curlique, scoot back, boys fold and then
Double pass thru, girls turn back and swing
Then promenade this lady and she'll never shed a tear
You swing her 'neath the crystal chandelier
