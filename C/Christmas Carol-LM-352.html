Christmas Carol
(Lou Mac 352) Don Wood
OPENER

Side face grand square
I was playing Santa Claus downtown on Christmas Eve
When a little girl of 3 or 4 climbed up on my knee
I saw she had a question behind those eyes of blue
So I asked her what her name was
Allemande left and weave you do
She said my name is Christmas Carol, I was born on Christmas day
I don't know who my daddy is and mamma's gone away
All I want for Christmas is someone to take me home
Doesn't anybody want a Christmas Carol of their own?

FIGURE

Head pair promenade go halfway round the hall
Down the middle and square thru 4 hands you all
Go all the way around and you do the right & left thru
Veer left and circulate, why don't we go by twos?
Half tag and scoot back, that corner lady swing
Swing and whirl that pretty girl and promenade around the ring
All she wants for Christmas is someone to take her home
Doesn't anybody want a Christmas Carol of their own?

Doesn't anybody want a Christmas Carol of their own?
