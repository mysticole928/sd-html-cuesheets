FIGURE (ORIGINAL)

And then you cross trail thru and
Find your corner - swing her
Left allemande and promenade too

FIGURE (MODERNISED - WITHOUT CROSS TRAIL)

And sides promenade three quarters round

Double pass thru tonight - first left - next right
Do a half square thru - and then trade by

Swing this girl and promenade too
