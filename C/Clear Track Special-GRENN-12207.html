Clear Track Special
(Grenn 12207) Paul Moore
FIGURE (two times through)

All 8 forward and back
Four men turn corner left , partner by the right
Send the ladies in for a left hand star half way around
Turn right hand round to the new partner
Left allemande new corner, back to the new partner
Right hand with the new partner and square your sets that way
4 men star left halfway to original partner
Turn original with the right , send the men back in for a left hand star
4 men star left again halfway
Back to the lady with a right and square your sets that way
4 ladies star left halfway to original partner
Turn original by the right to original corner
Left allemande with the corner, back to partner
All four couples promenade full around
Everybody swing
Join 16 hands and circle left full around to home
