DIXIE DAISY FIGURE No. 1

1st and 3rd right & left thru, two head ladies dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn half by the left, cross again, follow man to left
Around two and go like crazy, head men dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn half by the left, cross again, follow girl to left
Around two and gone again, go down the center, dixie chain
Girl go left, gent go right, allemande left, etc.

DIXIE DAISY FIGURE No. 2

Sides to the right and circle 4, side gents break and line up 4
Forward 8 and back like crazy, bend the line, girls dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn half by the left, cross again, follow man to left
Star by the left in the middle of town, back by the right, go other way round
Girls roll out around one man, allemande left and right & left grand

DIXIE FALL THRU

1 & 3 pass thru, both turn left that's what you do
Gent around two, lady fall thru and dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn the side gent half, cross again, follow man to left
Around two and gone again, go down the center with a dixie chain
Gents go left and ladies go right around one
Into the middle and box the flea, two ladies chain and turn you see
Same couples right & left thru, turn right around and pass thru
Turn alone, go left allemande, partner right, go right & left grand

DIZZY DAISY

2 & 4 half sashay, go forward up and back that way
Out to the right and circle 4, ladies break and line up 4
Pass thru across the floor, lines divide, girls dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn the opposite half, cross again, men dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn half by the left, cross again, girls dixie-chain
On to the next two ladies chain, turn her around, chain back again
Face those two, go right & left thru, turn around and cross trail thru
Find your corner, left allemande, partner right, go right & left grand

CHAIN REACTION

Forward 8 and back like crazy, all four ladies dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn half by the left, cross again, follow man to left
Men reach back with your right hand, catch all eight with the right hand half
Back by the left, go all the way round, four men dixie-daisy
Cross by the right, turn half by the left, cross again, follow gal to left
All four ladies chain across, gents chain back and pass your boss
Find the corner, left allemande, partner right, go right & left grand

EXPLANATION of "Dixie-Daisy":
Designated persons cross set touching right hands, turn opposite with left
fore-arm to face back toward center of set, leaving inactive persons facing
out. Active people once more cross set touching right hands to stand behind
inactive persons single file, waiting for the next call.
