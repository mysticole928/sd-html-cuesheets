Cabaret   (Quadrille 834 )   Lee Main

Four ladies chain and you turn them around
And you chain them home again
Join all your hands and circle to the left
Left allemande and weave the ring
What good is sitting alone in your room
Swing that girl and promenade
Life is a cabaret, ole chum
Come to the cabaret

Ferris wheel and come to the middle and pass thru
Star thru, slide thru and swing
Swing your girl and promenade
Life is a cabaret, ole chum
Come to the cabaret
