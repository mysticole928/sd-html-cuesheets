Christmas Country Style
Statler Brothers 1994
(Omar 114) Corben Geis
OPENER

4 ladies chain
Rollaway 1/2 sashay, circle left
4 ladies rollaway & circle left
Left allemande and weave the ring
We'll have a Christmas country style
You can hear us singing for a country mile
Take off your coats and stay a little while,
We'll have a Christmas country style

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) Square thru 4
Swing thru , girls circulate , boys trade
Boys run right, bend the line , forward & back
Slide thru , square thru 3
Swing your corner and promenade
Take off your coats and stay a little while
We'll have a Christmas country style

MIDDLE BREAK

Circle left
Light the candle on the mantle place
Put another log on the fire
Men star right and turn it one time and then
Left allemande and weave the ring
We'll have a Christmas country style
You can hear us singing for a country mile
Take off your coats and stay a little while
We'll have a Christmas country style

CLOSER

Circle left
We'll pop some popcorn and we'll trim the tree
We'll build a snowman in the yard
Men star right and turn it one time and then
Left allemande and weave the ring
We'll have a Christmas country style
You can hear us singing for a country mile
Take off your coats and stay a little while
We'll have a Christmas country style
