(*Grand Que: From a squared up set and on the command to Grand Que, all 4 couples
face their own partner and do a dosado then curlique. This puts the men facing out
and the girls facing in. Without stopping the girls star left 3/4 as men promenade
the outside 1/4 where they meet their original partner. All dancers are now 1/4
to left of home position and routine is 1/4 finished. Partners dosado and curlique.
This time men star left in middle 3/4, girls promenade right 1/4 around outside.
You're 1/2 way home now. Entire action is repeated until all home again.)
