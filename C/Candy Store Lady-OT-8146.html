Candy Store Lady
(Old Timer 8146) Bill Castner
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Well you bow now to your partner and to your corners all
Allemande left with your left hand and now the old gee-haw
It's a grand right & left around the ring and when you meet your own
Dosado then back right up, get ready for the heel and toe*
It's heel and toe to the center and heel and toe and out
And heel and toe and in and heel and toe and out
Promenade her, serenade her
She's the little girl that runs the candy store

FIGURE

The head 2 couples right & left thru and wheel your girls around
Face the couple on the right and all cross trail thru
U-turn back and then pass thru to a new 2 and do a cross trail thru
Swing the corner up and down and she becomes your own
Then allemande left new corner and allemande right your partner
Go back and swing that corner lady round
Promenade her and serenade her
She's the little girl that runs the candy store

TAG

She's the little girl who sells the candy
She sure sells the candy, at that little
Old candy store

*On the call " get ready for the heel and toe " partners assume semi-closed (open ballroom) position and face the center of the square. Men start with left foot and lady with right foot and all do a heel and toe and then one two-step to the center. Each then faces out from the center (still in semi-closed position) and do a heel and toe and a two-step out. This is repeated in and out and then partners promenade.
