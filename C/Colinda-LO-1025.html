OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Allemande left that corner, then do an allemande thar
Go right & left, men back in and make a right hand star
Shoot that star go down the lane, go right & left again
Four ladies chain across the ring, turn her left around
Four ladies chain across once more, left allemande and then
Grand right & left around that ring, when you meet your girl
Promenade that Cajun girl, go walking hand in hand
Colinda is the sweetest girl in all the bayou land

FIGURE

1 & 3 lead to the right , circle round that track
Head gents break, make a line , go forward eight and back
Pass thru , wheel & deal , the center four star thru
Same ladies chain , turn that girl, then you cross trail thru
Allemande left that corner, walk on by your own
Swing the right hand girl around and promenade her home
Colinda is the sweetest girl in all the bayou land
All the boys who dance with her try to win her hand
