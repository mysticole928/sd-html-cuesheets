Circassian Circle / Thady You Gander
   (Folkraft 1167 side A )   
FORMATION

Single circle of couples facing center, girl on partner's right. All hands joined.

FIGURE

Forward and back
Forward and back again
Girls forward and back
Boys to center, turn left about
Swing your partner with a two-hand swing
Promenade partner around the ring

NOTES

As a mixer, boys walk to corner girl and continue, swinging then promenading this corner who becomes new partner.
