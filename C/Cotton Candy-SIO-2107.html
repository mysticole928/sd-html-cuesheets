Cotton Candy / Square Dance Time
   (Sets In Order 2107 side A )    Lee Helsel
 
FIGURE   

Heads (sides) you bow and then give her a swing
Promenade about a half of the ring
Right & left thru then turn with your girl
Circle 8 well now her head's in a whirl
Gents star right and swing your corner fair
Promenade her home and toss your troubles in the air
Well now then you walk all around your corner, the sweetest in the land
See saw your partner, then do a left allemande
Go right & left grand, now look for your maid
Dosado her then promenade
You're bound to sing a happier tune
If you've got cotton candy and a toy balloon

BREAK   

Walk all around your corner to your partner bow down
Swing your honey on the merry-go-round
Allemande left, turn a right hand round your own
Gents star left but then you walk by your own
A right hand round the next, gents star left across and swing
Ladies promenade inside again, the same gent you swing
Hey why don't you do an allemande your corner, go right & left grand
Walk around the ring, it's a circus land
Your partner box the gnat, girls star left around
Same gent box the gnat and promenade around
You're bound to sing a happier tune
If you've got cotton candy and a toy balloon

(Repeat FIGURE for Sides, then repeat BREAK)
