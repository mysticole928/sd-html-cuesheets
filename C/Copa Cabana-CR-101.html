FIGURE



Head two couples promenade halfway around the ring
Down the middle pass thru, partner trade and then
Square thru 4 hands moving around you go
Right & left thru the outside two, turn your lady go
Swing thru and then swing thru again
Swing the corner lady around and promenade the ring
To the Copa Copa Cabana
The hottest spot north of Havanna
