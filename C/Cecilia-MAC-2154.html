Cecilia
Whispering Jack Smith 1925
   (MacGregor 2154)    Monty Wilson
 
FIGURE    C4, corner progression

All four ladies chain 3/4 round the ring and turn the girl
Heads roll away with a half sashay I sing
In to the middle do a dosado one time no more
Star thru , split the outside two, make a line of four
All eight do a curlique and boys run right
Dosado the outside two, make an ocean wave tonight
Spin chain thru , now girls circulate
Two times around and when you're through boys run , don't be late
Wheel & deal to face those two, then sweep 1/4 more
Star thru , dive thru , you circle up four
Half way around, right & left thru with the pair you found
Pass thru , star thru , right & left thru once more
Square thru four hands go walking round the shade
Trade by , swing that corner lady, promenade
Oh Cecilia, say that you'll be mine

(Sequence four times thru, twice for heads, twice for sides)
