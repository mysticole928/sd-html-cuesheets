Climbing Them Golden Stairs    (RIV 368)  Fred Strang

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All 4 Ladies Promenade one time around that ring.
Gonna get back home and you Swing there with your own.
Join up all your hands and you Circle Left and then.
Left Allemande that corner, Weave the ring.
(Yodel)
Swing the girl and now you Promenade.
(Yodel)
Well, we’re climbing them golden stairs.

FIGURE



Head [side] two, you Promenade Half way around that ring.
Side pair you do the Right and Left Thru.
You’ll Square Thru to four, four hands around and then,
Do Sa Do this corner, Eight Chain Four.
(Yodel)
Swing the corner now, you Promenade.
(Yodel)
Well, we’re climbing them golden stairs.
