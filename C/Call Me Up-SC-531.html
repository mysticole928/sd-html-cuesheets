Call Me Up    (SC 531)   Mac McCullar

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join your hands and make a ring
Circle left and hear me sing
Left allemande come back and promenade
Promenade but don't slow down
Head two couples wheel around
Pass thru, wheel & deal, that's what you do
Star thru, the same two, lead to the right
Left allemande, come back one, dosado, promenade
Promenade the ring, take your lady home and swing
Call me up and I'll go dancing with you

FIGURE



4 little ladies chain, turn your girl and then
1 & 3 lead to the right, circle up 4
Heads gents break and make a line
Forward up and back in time
Right & left thru, turn your girl, barge thru*
With the girl in front of you, go left allemande
Dosado a brand new girl, promenade
Promenade her home you do
Swing her once and when you're through
Call me up and I'll go dancing with you

*Barge thru: 1/2 square thru, centers pass thru, outside two partner trade or California twirl in this figure.
