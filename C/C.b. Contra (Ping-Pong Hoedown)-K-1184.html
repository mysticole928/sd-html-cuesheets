C.B. Contra    (K 1184)   Walt Cole

FORMATION: Couples facing couples, lady on partner's right, in long lines down the hall, an even number of couples is required.



1-8 : - - - - swing the left hand lady
9-16: - - - - ladies lead with a flutter wheel
17-24: - - - - sweep 1/4
25-32: Step to a wave and balance 4, - - swing thru
33-40: - - balance again, - - spin the top
41-48: - - - rear back, - - go right & left thru
49-56: - - - - , - - ladies chain
* (cross at the heads - , - - chain 'em back and cross at the foot)

*The cross over is done every 2nd, 4th, 6th, (even) time through.
