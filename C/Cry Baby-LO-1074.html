Cry Baby
(Lore 1074) Allen Tipton
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Allemande that corner girl, come home and swing
Then join hands and circle round the ring
All 4 men step in, star by the right around the land
Turn it to that corner, allemande , weave the ring
Go winding and weaving around the ring you go
Dosado that lady fair, then promenade from there
I thought I saw a teardrop in your eye
But you don't have to be a baby to cry

FIGURE

Head 2 couples promenade halfway around
Lead to the right , make a right hand star go round
Heads star left in the middle across to the opposite go
With a dosado in time, then circle 4, heads break and make a line
Go forward 8 and back with you, right & left thru , turn the girl
Now cross trail , swing the corner , promenade the world
I thought I saw a teardrop in your eye
But you don't have to be a baby to cry
