Cherokee Boogie
Johnny Horton 1951
   (Cheyenne 2003)    Dean Dederman
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Well, a Cherokee chief as he dances along
He does an Indian boogie to a white man's song
Singin' "Hey-ho-a-lina - Hey-ho-a-lina"
Four ladies all promenade and get you back
And swing with your man and promenade that land
Well "Hey-ho-a-lina - wup-wup a witten-a-yea"

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) square thru four hands and then
Meet your corner honey and you swing thru my friend
Spin your top , slide thru and touch 1/4
Walk & dodge , partner trade
Boy walk across , swing your corner and all promenade
Singin' "Hey-ho-a-lina - wup-wup a witten-a-yea"
