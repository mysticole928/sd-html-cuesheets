Cidade Maravil Hosa (Marvelous City)    (K 1230)   Vaughn Parrish

INTRO



Bow to partner way down low, bow to the corner
Join hands, circle left full around 16 steps
4 ladies chain

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Sides face grand square
4 ladies chain
Heads face, finish the grand square
Promenade
Cidade Maravil hosa
Means beautiful city I love you

FIGURE



You do a left allemande and then a right & left grand
Hand over hand round that ring go
When you meet her bow real low, now swing her twice around
Put the ladies back to back, gents promenade
One time around you go and do a dopaso
Partner left and corner right you know
Turn partner left, take your corner, promenade home
