Clap Your Hands    (MAR 1103)   Cindy Whitaker

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies Promenade that ring there,
Get on back, Swing and whirl your man
Join hands Circle to the left and roll it round the ring
Allemande Left on the corner, Weave the ring
Well clap your hands, snap your fingers,
tap your toes, have a good time
Swing that girl around and Promenade her home
When your favorite line comes in, sing as loud as you can
Clap your hands, Clap your hands!

FIGURE



Heads Square thru and go four hands round you know,
Meet that corner, make a Right hand Star
Well the Heads Star by the Left in the middle, go one time around and then
To the same two you do the Right and Left thru
Swing thru, come down the way, Boy Run to the right
You do a Half Tag, Swing a little girl and Promenade tonight
When your favorite line comes in, sing as loud as you can
Clap your hands, Clap your hands !
