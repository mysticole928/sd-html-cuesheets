Cabin on the Hill   (Crown 102 )   Bob Worley

OPENER



Circle left
Tonight I'm alone without you, my dear
But in my heart there's a longing for you still
Allemande left your corner, dosado and then
Left allemande your corner, weave the ring
Oh, someone has taken you from me
Swing with your girl, promenade
Just to listen to the rain beat on my window pane
In our little cabin home on the hill

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around the ring
Come down the middle square thru go 4
Four hands around, you swing thru and then
Boys run right, ferris wheel
Star thru, pass thru and partner tag and swing
You swing the lady around and promenade
Just to listen to the rain beat on my window pane
In our little cabin home on the hill

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
Tonight I'm alone without you, my dear
But in my heart there's a longing for you still
I just keep it there, so I won't be alone
In our little cabin home on the hill
Circle left
Someone has taken you from me
Left allemande come back and promenade
Just to listen to the rain beat on my window pane
In our little cabin home on the hill

CLOSER



Four ladies chain go straight across the ring
You roll away and circle to the left around
You roll away and circle go moving around the ring
Left allemande your corner, weave the ring
Someone has taken you from me
Swing with your girl and promenade
Just to listen to the rain beat on my window pane
In our little cabin home on the hill
