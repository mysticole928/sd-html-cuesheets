Christmas Medley

OPENER (Rudolph The Red-Nosed Reindeer)    B - SSD Week 1

All four ladies chain across, turn that girl and then
Turn and chain those ladies right back home again
Join hands , circle to the left around the ring you go
Allemande left the corner, weave the ring you know
Then one foggy Christmas Eve, Santa came to say
Do a dosado with the girl you meet, promenade that way
Rudolph the red nose reindeer had a very shiny nose
And if you ever saw it, you would even say it glows

FIGURE (White Christmas)    C4, corner progression

Head two couples square thru four hands around now
Four hands and then a right & left thru
You curlique and then boys run right and then
Partner trade and do a right & left thru
Roll away a half sashay , turn thru and then
Swing that corner girl and promenade
May your days be merry and bright
And may all your Christmas's be white
