Teddy Bear   (TNT 113 )   Al Roberts

BREAK



All four ladies chain across the ring
Turn and chain your lady right on home
Join hands, circle left around you go
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
I wish I was a teddy bear
Do a dosado and promenade her
I wish I was a teddy bear
Not living or loving or going anywhere

FIGURE (twice for head, twice for sides)



Heads promenade halfway
Down the middle, right and left I say
Square thru four hands around you know
Dosado your corner once you go
Slide thru, square thru 3/4 round
Swing the corner promenade her
I wish I was a teddy bear
Not living or loving or going anywhere
