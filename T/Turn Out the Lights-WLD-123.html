Turn Out The Lights    (WLD 1-23)   Larry Jack

Four little ladies chain, turn the girl you know



Join hands, circle go walking around the ring
Left allemande, weave and sing
Turn out the lights the party's over
Dosado then you promenade
Turn out the lights the party's over
And tomorrow night will be the same old thing

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



1 & 3 square thru 4 hands you go
Split the outside, around 1 you know
Move 8 to the middle and back, curlique and then
Boys run right and do a dosado
Swing thru, boys trade, corner you swing
Swing this corner, promenade
Turn out the lights the party's over
And tomorrow night will be the same old thing
