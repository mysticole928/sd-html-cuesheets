The New Freight Train   (Cascade 1007 )   Bill Peterson

BREAK



Four ladies promenade inside the ring
Come back home and give him a swing
Join your hands and circle to the left
Allemande the corner, weave the ring (here it comes)
Freight train, freight train, rollin' on
Dosado and promenade along
I don't care what train I'm on, as long as I keep rollin' on

FIGURE



Heads promenade, go halfway round
Down the center, pass the ocean, extend
Ladies trade and then, recycle now
Veer to the left and a ferris wheel
Pass thru, touch 1/4, scoot back and then
Swing that corner and promenade my friend
I don't care what train I'm on, as long as I keep rollin' on

TAG



Bow to your partner, your corners all, whoa right there

SEQUENCE (Break, figure heads twice, break, figure sides twice, ending, tag)
