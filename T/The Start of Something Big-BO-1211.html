The Start Of Something Big    (BO 1211)   Cal Lambert

BREAK



Walk around your corner, swing your own then
Join hands, circle left around the land
Allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and star
The men back in and make a right hand star
Slip the clutch, left allemande, then dosado your own
Swing that corner girl and then promenade
You promenade this girl, around the ring you whirl
Well, this could be the start of something big

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across and then you turn 'em
Those heads promenade around halfway
Divide, go to the sides, circle one full turn
Dive thru, square thru 3/4 round
Left allemande the corner lady, dosado your own
That corner swing and then promenade
You feel that certain glow, and all at once you know
That this could be the start of something big
