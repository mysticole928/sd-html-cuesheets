This Is My Country
(Solid Gold 1007) Tommy Russell
OPENER

Sides face grand square
*This is my country, land of my birth
*This is my country, grandest on Earth
Circle left
I pledge thee my allegiance
Allemande left , come back an promenade
For this is my country
To have and to hold

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*This is my country, land of my choice
*This is my country, hear my proud voice

FIGURE

Heads square thru , four hands you travel
Meet the sides and make a right hand star
Heads star left one time around now
Meet the sides and do a dosado
Swing thru and then, boys trade turn thru
Allemande left , come back and promenade
For this is my country
To have and to hold

MIDDLE BREAK

Do an allemande left in the alamo style
Right to the partner and balance
Swing thru go two by two
You swing thru two by two, turn your partner right
Allemande left , and weave the ring
I pledge thee my allegiance
Swing your girl and promenade
For this is my country
To have and to hold
