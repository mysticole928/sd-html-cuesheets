Tweedle Dee / Twelfth Street Rag
Bennie Moten 1919
   (MacGregor 7495 side B )    Red Warrick , Fenton Jones
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK    Basic

Dosado round your corner lady
See saw round your taw
Allemande left with the old left hand
And now the old gee-haw (right & left grand)
Go hand over hand around that ring
Now when you meet your gal
Box the gnat , go the other way back
Hand over hand on the wrong way track
It's hand over hand around the ring
And when you meet again
Box the gnat , the gents star left
Go once around and then
Once around on the heel and toe
Swing your honey, go high and low
Swing 'er to that Twelfth Street Rag

FIGURE   

The head gents face your corner girls
You dosado around
Star by the right in the middle of the set
Go once around the town
Skip your own, pick up the next
With the elbow hook and then
The inside out, the outside in
A full turn , you're gone again
The rim turns back , you pass 'em once
But don't you go too far
Catch 'em by the ring
The rim backs up in an arky wrong way thar
Shoot that star with a full turn round
Swing with your own when you come down
Swing 'er to that Twelfth Street Rag
