That Old Gang Of Mine   (Mac Gregor 2132 )   Lloyd "Sparky" Sparks

OPENER - ENDING



All 4 ladies chain across, you turn that girl and then
Join hands and circle left go round that ring
Why ladies in the men sashay and circle left that way
Why ladies in the men sashay and circle left I say
Left allemande that corner, why weave round that ring
When you meet your partner take her hand, promenade and sing
Gee, but I'd give the world to see that old gang of mine

FIGURE



Why the head 2 couples square on thru 4 ladies around that ring
Around that corner lady dosado
You star thru and flutter wheel and roll it round
Why sweep 1/4 then a right & left thru you go
You dive thru and square thru 3/4 around
Why swing that corner lady and promenade round and sing it
Gee, but I'd give the world to see that old gang of mine

SEQUENCE (Opener, figure twice heads, figure twice sides, ending)
