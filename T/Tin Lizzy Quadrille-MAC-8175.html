Tin Lizzy Quadrille / Dance, Dance, Dance
(MacGregor 8175 side A )
FIGURE

Head ladies chain (8 counts)
Heads outside 1/2 promenade (8 counts)
Side ladies chain (8 counts)
Sides outside 1/2 promenade (8 counts)
Face your corner , right & left grand , wrong way around (8 counts)
Turn by the right hand around and promenade all the way back home (24 counts)

MIDDLE BREAK

4 ladies grand chain (8 counts)
4 couples 1/2 promenade (8 counts)
4 ladies grand chain (8 counts)
4 couples 1/2 promenade (8 counts)
Allemande left , grand right & left (16 counts)
Promenade home and swing (16 counts)

(Sequence: figure with heads twice. Middle break. Figure with sides twice.)
