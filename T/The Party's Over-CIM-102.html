The Party's Over   (Cimarron 102 )   Gerald McWhirter
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK

Four ladies chain go across the ring
You roll away and circle left
Four ladies roll away, you circle left again
Left allemande, weave the ring
Turn out the lights the party's over
Dosado let's promenade
Call it a night the party's over
And tomorrow night's the same old thing again

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands, dosado the corner one time
Swing thru, spin the top, boys move up and right & left thru
Pass the ocean, ladies trade, boys cross fold
Swing the corner, promenade
Turn out the lights the party's over
And tomorrow night's the same old thing again

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Heads promenade halfway
Come down the middle, star thru & pass thru, curlique
Split circulate, boys run, right & left thru turn a gal
Pass the ocean, recycle
Swing the corner, promenade
