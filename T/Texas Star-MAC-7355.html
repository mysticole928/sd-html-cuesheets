Texas Star    (MAC 7355)   Fenton Jones

INTRO



Now bow to your partner
Then you allemande left with the old left hand
Partners right and a right & left grand
A right & left, go round the ring
And a hand over hand with the pretty little thing
Now promenade with your lady fair
Take a little walk, go around the square
You promenade

FIGURE



Ladies to the center, back to the bar
Gents to the center with a right hand star
Come back by the left but not too far
Skip your partner, pick up the next and star promenade
The inside out, the outside in
A full turn round and star again
When you get home, everybody sing
