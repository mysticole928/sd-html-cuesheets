The Matador   (Jopat 108 )   Bob Vinyard

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
Everybody holds their breath
As he passes by the horns of death
Then the crowd is on its feet
To roar for the Matador, ole!
Circle left
With his scarlet cape begins the sweep
El Toro whispers from his lips
Promenade
Inside her heart is beating for the Matador

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Heads square thru and go, dosado the corner you know
Swing thru, boys run right, ferris wheel you do
Centers pass thru, swing thru, boys trade, turn thru and go
Allemande left and promenade her back home
Inside her heart is beating for the Matador

MIDDLE BREAK



Circle left
Tonight she'll meet beneath the silver stars, ladies in men sashay
Dance to mandolins and soft guitars, ladies in men sashay
Allemande left, walk by two, turn thru, allemande left and promenade

CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
As he leaves the ring they all cheer
Sombreros fly into the air
She throws the crimson rose she wore
To the Matador

Heads face, grand square
La da da da da da da
La da da da da da da
Inside her heart is beating for the Matador
