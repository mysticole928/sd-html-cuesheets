Lucky Lips / The World Is Waiting For The Sunrise
   (Sets In Order 2115 side B )   
BREAK   

Walk around your corner girl, left hand swing your maid
Ladies center back to back , gents you promenade
Go once around and turn your honey left hand around
A full turn to an allemande thar , gents back down the line
Throw in the clutch , left allemande , let's do a right & left grand
Go right and left around, then promenade the land
Promenade your lady, take her home with you
She's the sunshine of your heart

FIGURE   

1st and 3rd a right & left thru , 2 & 4 do it, too
Join your hands , circle left now go round the ring
Girls whirlaway , star by the right
(Girls whirl once and a 1/4 to a right hand star.)
Gents backtrack you fly
Twice around to the same girl, box the flea and pull her by
Well now it's all eight chain and then you whirlaway
Grand ol' right & left now you'll hear me say
Promenade new lady and take her home with you
The sunshine of your heart

This is the instrumental record. The vocal record is on Sets In Order 1110 .

Note: By using Wheel Around and Back Track in preceding patter call no difficulty will be encountered with this movement. Should be taught as one constant flowing movement.
