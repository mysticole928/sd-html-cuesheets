Texas Rag
(Lore 1174) Curtis Thompson
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
You roll away , circle to the left go movin' round and then
Left allemande the corner, come home with a curlique
Boys run right left allemande , gonna weave around you do
Wind in and out around that ring and when you meet your maid
Dosado and promenade her
Well we did it down in Houston, boy just like we used to
We call it that good old Texas rag

FIGURE

1 & 3 promenade halfway round that ring
Come down the middle , do a right & left thru , then you roll away
You pass thru , then u-turn back , star thru and then
You pass thru , a curlique , a double scoot back
2 times around you swing the girl you found
You swing that girl and promenade her
Well we did it down in Houston, boy just like we used to
They call it that good old Texas rag
