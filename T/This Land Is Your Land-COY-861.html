This Land Is Your Land
Woody Guthrie 1940
(Coyote 861) Don Coy
OPENER

Circle to the left
This land is your land, this land is my land
From California to the New York islands
Men star by the right, one time tonight
Allemande left and weave the ring
This land is your land, this land
Swing and promenade
From California to the New York islands
This land was made for you and me

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) lead to the right and circle to a line
Lines go up and back
Pass thru , wheel & deal
First couple left, next go right
Slide thru , square thru 3
Swing corner and promenade
From California to the New York islands
This land was made for you and me

MIDDLE BREAK

Circle left
As I went walking that ribbon of highway
I saw above me that endless sky way
Men star right one time tonight
Allemande left and weave
This land is your land, this land is my land
Swing and promenade
From California to the New York islands
This land was made for you and me

CLOSER

Circle left
The sun was shining
And the wheat fields waving
And the dust clouds rolling
Men star right one time tonight
Allemande left and weave
This land is your land
Swing partner and promenade
From California to the New York islands
This land was made for you and me
