The Caller's Wife
   (Grenn 16005)    Don Armstrong
FORMATION

Contra lines, 1, 3, 5, etc. couples active and crossed over

FIGURE

With the one below, allemande left, once and a half around
Ladies chain across the set
Those four go forward and back
Circle four, 3/4 round, then pass thru
Dosado
Same girl swing
Half promenade
Right & left thru

(Note: This dance incorporate a double-progression in each sequence of the dance so
it is necessary for the dancers who reach the head (or foot) of the set to cross
over to the other line during the half promenade or the right & left thru actions
of the other couples. There is no "waiting at the head or foot of the set".)
