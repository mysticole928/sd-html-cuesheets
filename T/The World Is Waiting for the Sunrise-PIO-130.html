The World Is Waiting For The Sunrise    (PIO 130)   Charles Ashby

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
Dear one, the world is waiting for the sunrise
Every rose is heavy
Heads face, grand square
The thrush on high, his sleepy mate is calling
And my heart is calling you

FIGURE



Head couples square thru four hands you go
Round the corner lady, dosado
You swing thru tonight, all the boys run to the right
You bend the line, right & left thru and turn the girl
Ladies lead dixie style, make your wave, boys trade
Boys cross run to the right,
swing that corner lady, promenade
Promenade along with you
Because the world is waiting too

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade, come on get a'walkin' round that ring
Gonna run on back and swing that man one time, I sing
Gonna join hands and circle to the left, go moving, walking, go
Allemande with the corner of the hall, and you weave around you know
Weave in and out around the ring until you meet your girl
Swing that lady once and promenade that world
Promenade along with you
Because the world is waiting too
