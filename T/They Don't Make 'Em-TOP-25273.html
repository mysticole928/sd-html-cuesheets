They Don't Make 'Em
(Top 25273) Ed Fraidenburg
BREAK

4 ladies chain across, turn the girls and then
Join hands and circle to the left around my friend
The ladies in the men sashay and circle round the land
The ladies in the men sashay and go left allemande
Weave that ring you go until you meet your maid
Dosado and promenade in time
They don't make 'em like they used to
And a good one now-a-days is hard to find

FIGURE

1 & 3 flutter wheel go straight across you scoot
When you're through sweep 1/4 then you substitute
Pass thru , circle 4 and break to a line you do
Pass thru , wheel & deal and then the center 2
Square thru 3/4 round the ring go
Swing the corner , promenade in time
They don't make 'em like they used to
And a good one now-a-days is hard to find

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

1 & 3 pass thru , cloverleaf and then
2 & 4 pass thru , circle 4 my friend
Halfway round and 1/4 more , then a right & left thru for real
Turn that girl and pass thru and do a wheel & deal
Centers square thru 3/4 round and then
Swing your corner , promenade in time
They don't make 'em like they used to
And a good one now-a-days is hard to find
