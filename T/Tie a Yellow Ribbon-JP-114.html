Tie A Yellow Ribbon    (JP 114)   Bob Vinyard

OPENER - CLOSER



Circle left
I'm coming home I've done my time
I've got to know what is and isn't mine
Allemande left with corner, turn partner right to the corner
Allemande left, weave the ring
Tie a yellow ribbon round the old oak tree
Dosado, swing and promenade for me
If I see a yellow ribbon round the old oak tree
Then I know you still want me

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4 round
Heads (sides) square thru go (4) go all the way
Swing thru the outside two, girls circulate
Boys trade, turn thru
Left allemande, come back, swing your lady, promenade the ring
If I see a yellow ribbon round the old oak tree
Then I know you still want me

BREAK



Sides face, grand square
I'm coming home I've done my time
I've got to know what is and isn't mine
If you receive my letter telling you I'll soon be free
Join hands and circle left
Tie a yellow ribbon round the old oak tree
Allemande left the corner, promenade for me
If I see a yellow ribbon round the old oak tree
Then I know you still want me
