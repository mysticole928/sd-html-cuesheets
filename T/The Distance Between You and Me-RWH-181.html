The Distance Between You And Me    (RWH 181)   Stan Cole

Four ladies chain 3/4 around the ring



Four ladies roll away, circle left that way
Then allemande left that corner, weave the ring
Take a map of the world and measure it with your hands
Swing your own and promenade
Write it down, add it up and you might understand
The distance between you and me

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade travel halfway
Come down the middle and square thru and go
All the way and then right & left thru my friend
You veer left and the couples circulate
Chain 'er down the line, square thru go 3
Swing your corner, promenade
Write it down, add it up and you might understand
The distance between you and me
