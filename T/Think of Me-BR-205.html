Think Of Me    (BR 205)   Glenn Zeno


OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join hands, circle left
All turn back, go single file
The ladies backtrack, find your man
Turn thru, left allemande, weave the ring
And when your eyes disguise the same old loving eyes
You dosado and promenade
And when you have another's arms about you
Do you ever think of me?

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway round the ring you steal
Down the middle, with a flutter wheel
Same two star thru, pass thru, then dosado once around
Swing thru, turn thru, left allemande
Then dosado your partner, corner swing, promenade
And when you say "I cannot live without you
Do you ever think of me?
