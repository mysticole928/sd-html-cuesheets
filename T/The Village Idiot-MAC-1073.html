The Village Idiot    (MAC 1073)   Don Stewart

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Dosado round that corner girl, see saw round your own
Join your hands and circle to the left around you roam
Allemande left go allemande thar, right & left and star
Men back in to the middle now you got the right hand star
Shoot that star, weave by one, box the gnat, change hands
Allemande the corner, and you promenade the land
I hope my friends all know it's you makes me do the things I do
I act like the village idiot when I swing with you

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain the ring, turn a little girl I say
1 & 3 you dosado, make an ocean wave that way
Rock it up, come on back, swing star thru you do
Right & left thru the outside two, turn the girls and then
Dive thru, pass thru, swing the corner one
Allemande the corner, and you promenade the land
I hope my friends all know it's you makes me do the things I do
I act like the village idiot when I swing with you
