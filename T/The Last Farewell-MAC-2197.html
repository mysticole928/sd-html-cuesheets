The Last Farewell    (MAC 2197)   Fred Drouant

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Heads face, grand square
There is a ship lies ready in the harbour
Tomorrow for old England she sails
Far away from your land of endless sunshine
To a land full of rainy skies and gales
And then you allemande left that corner, dosado now
Allemande left, come back and promenade
You are so beautiful and this is the last farewell

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands go
And around that corner girl, dosado
Once around, swing thru down the line, boys run right
Ferris wheel, it's once around tonight
Pass thru and swing that corner girl now
Left allemande, come back and promenade
You are so beautiful, more than words can tell
