OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
From the Ponderosa in Abilene
To the old wooden dance floor down in Gruene
From Fort Worth down to the Luckenbach Dance Hall
Allemande left , weave the ring in time
From the warehouse down on Cheatham Street
To Big Spring and the Old Stampede ( Swing and promenade )
Seems we're always on the dance floor at last call

FIGURE    Basic - SSD Week 9, corner progression

Heads (Sides) promenade now go halfway round
Come down pass the ocean , extend
Swing thru tonight and the boy run right
Bend the line with a right & left thru
Pass the ocean here, then swing thru
Swing the girl and promenade her, too
Seems we're always on the dance floor at last call

FIGURE   

Heads (Sides) promenade now go halfway round
Sides (Heads) do the right & left thru turn the girl
Heads (Sides) square thru 4 hands around then
Dosado the corner girl
Do an eight chain 4 now you know
Swing that girl and promenade her so
Seems we're always on the dance floor at last call
