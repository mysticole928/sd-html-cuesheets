Callers note: until the dancers have established the pattern, the inactives are sometimes caught flat-footed on the call "substitute". The inactive couples are standing out of gear, with the lady on man's left, and on a bias with one another. They should simply close in together and duck under the arch to become active for a star thru and California twirl, etc.
