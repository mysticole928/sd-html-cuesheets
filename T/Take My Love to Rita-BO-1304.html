Take My Love To Rita    (BO 1304)   Otto Dunn

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain straight across the ring
Turn the girl, roll away and circle left
4 ladies roll away, circle left go around that way
Left allemande then you weave around the ring
Take my love to Rita, tell her how I need her
Dosado, promenade her man
Tell her that I love her, always thinking of her
Take Rita home and swing her if you can

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands around the ring you do
Dosado that corner maid
Swing thru go 2 by 2, boys run to the right you do
Bend the line and do the right & left thru, turn a little girl
Flutter wheel full around, slide thru and then
Swing that corner, promenade
Tell her that I miss her, take her home and kiss her
Swing awhile with Rita if you can
