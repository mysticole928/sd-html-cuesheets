FIGURE

As a couple veer left & you ferris wheel now
The centers pass thru & touch 1/4
Scoot back, boys fold & the girls turn back
Swing that man & you promenade
Lord I might as well be talkin' to the moon

BREAK



4 ladies chain across that ring
You rollaway, circle left I sing
4 ladies rollaway, you circle and then
Left allemande and weave the ring
In and out and around you know
Swing this girl & promenade on home
Lord I might as well be talkin' to the moon
