This Thing Called Love    (BSR 115)   Toots Richardson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Bow to your partner, and to your corners all
Walk around your corner lady, see saw your own
Join hands and circle left
Allemande left your corner lady, your own dosado
Back to the corner, allemande left and weave the ring
It can lift you up, never let you down
Take your world, and turn it all around
Dosado and promenade
Can't see it with your eyes, hold it in your hand
But like the wind that covers our land
It rules the heart of every man, this thing called love

FIGURE



Heads promenade about halfway you go
Down the middle, and square thru 4 hands you know
Count to 4, round and then, go right & left thru my friend
Slide thru and square thru again, count 4 hands, trade by
That corner you swing, swing this lady round and promenade
Can't see it with your eyes, hold it in your hand
But like the wind that covers our land
Thank the Lord above we have love
