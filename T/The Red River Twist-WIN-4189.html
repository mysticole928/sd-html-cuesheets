The Red River Twist
(Windsor 4189 side A )
(Starting position: individually facing front of hall)

MEASURE

1 grapevine right, clap
2 grapevine left, clap
3 touch, touch
4 forward two-step, turn
5 back, 2, 3, clap

(Dance starts again)

INSTRUCTIONS IN DETAIL

1 grapevine right, clap (side, behind, side, clap)
Starting with the right foot, step side, then behind with the left, side with right, swing left over in front of right and clap hands

2 grapevine left, clap (side, behind, side, clap)
Starting with the left foot, step side, then behind with the right, side with left, swing right over in front of left and clap hands

3 touch, touch
With weight on left, point right forward two times

4 forward two-step, turn (forward, close, forward, turn)
Right forward, close left to right, forward again on right, with slight hop turn 1/4 turn to the right (a right face)

5 back, 2, 3, clap
Step back left, right, left, swing right over in front of left, clap hands
