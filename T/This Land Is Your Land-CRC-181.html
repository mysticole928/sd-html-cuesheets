Woody Guthrie 1940

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK

Four ladies promenade the inside
Back home and swing with your man
Then join hands circle left go around the ring
Allemande left that corner, weave the ring
This land is your land, this land is my land
Swing and promenade
From the Redwood forest to the gulf-stream waters
This land is made for you and me

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) promenade halfway around the ring
Down the middle right & left thru
Flutter wheel across, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru , right & left thru
Swing thru and then, swing thru again
Swing your lady around and promenade
From the Redwood forest to the gulf-stream waters
This land is made for you and me

CLOSER

Four ladies promenade the inside
Back home and wwing with your man
Then join hands circle left go around the ring
Allemande left that corner, weave the ring
This land is your land, this land is my land
From California, to the New York Island -
From the redwood forest, to the Gulf Stream waters
This land was made for you and me
