Twelfth Of Never    (GR 12166)   Dick Bayer

FIGURE



Heads promeande go halfway round the square
Lead to the right and circle up four and make a line from there
Go forward eight and come on back, touch 1/4
Release the column and when you're through
Double pass thru, track two is what you do
Meet that pair and hinge & flutter and when you're through
Cross trail thru and you go left allemande
Walk right by your own and promenade the land
I'll love you forever, forever my dear
Until the 12th of never and that's a long time

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies chain now straight across the ring
Roll away and circle left go walking round I sing
Four ladies roll away and circle to the left and then
Allemande left the corner and weave around the ring
(musical interlude)
Box the gnat and then four ladies promenade inside
Get on home and swing now, swing there with this gal
Take her by the hand and promenade go walking round the land
