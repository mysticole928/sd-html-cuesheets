Tennessee Waltz    (MAR 528)   Stan Russell

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Circle left
I was waltzing my darling to the Tennessee waltz
When an old friend I happened to see
Do an allemande your corner, come on back a dosado
Four boys star left in the middle of the ring
Back home and you turn thru, and go left allemande
Swing your lady around and promenade
I lost my little darling the night they were playing
The beautiful Tennessee waltz

FIGURE



Head two couples promenade halfway round you go
Come down the middle and do a full square thru
Four hands around in the middle of the ring, and right & left thru you see
Then veer to the left, and ferris wheel in time
Square thru get 3 in the middle of the ring
To the corner lady swing, swing this girl around and promenade
I lost my little darling the night they were playing
The beautiful Tennessee waltz
