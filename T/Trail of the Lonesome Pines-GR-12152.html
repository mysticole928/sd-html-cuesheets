Trail Of The Lonesome Pine    (GR 12152)   Clint McLean

BREAK



4 little ladies promenade inside of the ring
Well you come on home and swing that handsome man
Then join your hands, circle to the left, go walking round the land
Left allemande that corner and weave that ring
Weave in and out, until you meet her my friend
Swing this girl, left allemande and promenade
In the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia
On the Trail of the Lonesome Pine

FIGURE #1



4 little ladies chain across and couples 1 & 3
Promenade outside halfway around the ring
Lead to the right, circle up 4 and make a little line for me
Go forward 8 and you come on back, and slide thru you see
Do an eight chain 3 and then that corner you swing
Swing this girl, left allemande and promenade
In the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia
On the Trail of the Lonesome Pine

FIGURE #2



4 ladies chain across and couples 1 & 3
Promenade outside halfway around the ring
Now square thru in themiddle of the ring
And you get 4 hands you know
4 hands around and then, swing thru you do, boys run
1/2 tag, trade, and you roll
Swing this girl, left allemande and promenade
In the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia
On the Trail of the Lonesome Pine
