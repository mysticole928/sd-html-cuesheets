FIGURE

Around that corner do a dosado
Swing thru and then spin your top
Boys move up and do a right & left thru
Square thru and count 'em three hands go
Swing that corner, promenade and go
And do a little thing called
The Tennessee Bird Walk
(Here we go)

Join hands and circle to the left and go
Allemande that corner , going to weave it and go
In and out around until you meet your own
