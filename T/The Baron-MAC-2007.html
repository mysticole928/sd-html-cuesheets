The Baron
   (MacGregor 2007)    Tommy Stoye
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic, R-H lady progression

All 4 ladies promenade , go once around the land
Then you dosado the partner, turn to your corner man
Allemande left that corner, your own do a dosado
Then bow to her, you weave the ring back home
Move in and out around the ring and do a dosado
Allemande left the corner, come back and promenade
Yes go walking with the Baron, he's big and he's tall
Yes he's the mountain man, the Baron of them all

FIGURE    Basic, no progression

Now 1 & 3 you promenade halfway round the square
Move into the middle , do a right & left thru and turn that lady there then
Swing thru and you balance up and back , now the men trade and star thru
Your corner allemande , now dosado your own and the corner lady swing
Yes swing and whirl the Baron, then promenade the ring
He comes from the mountains where he's the king of them all
Yes he's the real big man, the Baron of them all
