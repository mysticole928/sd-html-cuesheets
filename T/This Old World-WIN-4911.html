This Old World    (WIN 4911)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies promenade go inside of the ring
Get on home and swing your honey, everybody swing
Join your hands and circle left around I sing
Left allemande, weave the ring
Weave 'em in and weave 'em out until you meet your maid
You dosado once around and then you all promenade
What this ol' world needs is a whole lot of lovin'
And a lot less shovin' around

FIGURE



Head 2 couples promenade halfway round will do
Down the middle slide thru and then pass thru
Swing thru this maid and now the boys trade
Box the gnat, change hands, left allemande
Dosado round your partner and your corner lady swing
Swing this lady round and promenade that ol' ring
What this ol' world needs is a whole lot of lovin'
And a lot less shovin' around
