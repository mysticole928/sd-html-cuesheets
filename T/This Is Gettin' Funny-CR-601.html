This Is Gettin' Funny    (CR 601)   Tom Roper

OPENER



Circle left
I came down with a broken heart, I thought I lost my lover
Men star right one time around
Left allemande and weave the ring now
If you don't smile baby I won't know what to say
Dosado and promenade
This is gettin' funny
There ain't nobody laughin' honey
Some folks don't have a sense of humor

FIGURE



1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around
Dosado the corner lady, curlique and then, do a walk & dodge my friend
Do a partner trade, right & left thru, turn the girl
Flutterwheel across, slide thru
Swing the corner, promenade
This is gettin' funny
There ain't nobody laughin' honey
Some folks don't have a sense of humor

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Sides face grand square
4 ladies promenade one time around
Swing your man and promenade now
This is gettin' funny
There ain't nobody laughin' honey
Some folks don't have a sense of humor

TAG



Do an allemande left with your corner
Come home and swing
Some folks don't have a sense of humor

SEQUENCE (Opener, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides, ending, tag)
