Twenty-Five Roses   (Buckskin 1212 )   Larry Cole

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
Give me twenty-five red roses for the sweetest girl in town
and I'll tell you sir a story how she turned my life around

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade go half way
Sides (heads) do the right and left thru
square thru and count to four hands
do-sa-do and eight cahin four
She stood right there beside me
Swing the lady - promenade
Give me twenty-five red roses for the sweetes girl in town

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Four ladies chain across and turn 'em,
you roll away and circle to the left.
Four ladies roll away and circle
left allemande and weave the ring.
She took a man who'd lost everything
Swing your lady promenade
Give me twenty-five red roses cause she means the world to me
