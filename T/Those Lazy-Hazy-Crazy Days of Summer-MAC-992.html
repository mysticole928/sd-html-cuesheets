Those Lazy-Hazy-Crazy Days Of Summer    (MAC 992)   Chuck Raley

FIGURE



Head 2 couples up and back and then you square thru
4 hands around and then you dosado all the way now
Star thru and make a line, go forward up and back
You square thru 3/4 round the track
Allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and star
Gents back up and make a right hand star
Shoot that star full around tot he corner, all eight chain
You'll join hands and circle around the ring
And then you allemande the corner, you'll dosado at home
Swing that same little girl and then you'll promenade
Roll out those lazy-hazy-crazy days of summer
You'll wish that summer could always be here

SEQUENCE: (Figure heads twice, figure sides twice)
