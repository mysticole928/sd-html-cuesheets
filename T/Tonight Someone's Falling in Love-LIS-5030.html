Right- (Left-) Hand Star Chain Circulate: Basic starting position, Box 1-4 (arrived by Heads or Sides square thru 4). Dancers form right hand star and turn star a bit over 1/2, so the girls line up across the center of set in a wave, while 4 boys are on ends or 4 corners. Center girls trade and turn the inactive or end girls 3/4 as in Chain Thru, while the 4 boys circulate 1 position on the outside of set. Ending in a normal two-faced line. The record was recorded before the above figure was changed from Star Chain Thru to Star Chain Circulate.
