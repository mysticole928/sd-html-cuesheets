This Land Is Your Land
Woody Guthrie 1940
(Windsor 5067) Warren Rowles
OPENER

Sides face, grand square
This land is your land, this land is my land
From California to the New York Island
From the redwood forest to the Gulf Stream waters
This land was made for you and me
Left allemande come back a dosado
Left allemande the corner, promenade 'em home
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land was made for you and me

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)

Four ladies chain , across the ring you go
Those heads (sides) pass thru and cloverleaf you know
The sides (heads) pass thru , curlique and then
Scoot back , the boys run right , my friend
Square thru three hands, that corner lady swing
Swing that lady round and promenade the ring
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land was made for you and me

MIDDLE BREAK (ALTERNATE)

Sides face, grand square
As I was walking that ribbon of highway
I saw above me that endless skyway
I saw below me that golden valley
This land was made for you and me
Do an allemande left that corner, come back dosado
Left allemande and promenade her home
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land was made for you and me
