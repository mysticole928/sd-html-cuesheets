Tipperary
John McCormack (1914) 1912
(TNT 133) Jack Cook
FIGURE

All four ladies chain , go straight across the ring
Heads star thru , pass thru , circle four
Head men break, make a line
Dance up to the middle and back
Pass thru , wheel and deal , centers curlique , boys run right
Cloverleaf , others right and left thru and pass thru
Swing thru , spin the top , and when you're through do a
Right and left thru , ladies lead flutter wheel
Reverse the flutter and full promenade
It's a long, long way to Tipperary
But my heart's right there

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)
(in memory of Ed Gilmore, Dance by Ed Gilmore, Yucaipa, CA)

Head two couples promenade , halfway around the square
Chain those girls across the set and turn the lady there
Go to the right , you circle four and make a line for me
Go forward eight and back ,
1 & 3 diagonally right and left thru ,
you turn and pass thru
Hook the sides and turn that line, all the way
Bend the line and chain the ladies
Same two, go forward up and back with you
Now you star thru , your corner swing now
Swing and promenade the ring
