Trixie's Special / Lookin' Back To See
   (MacGregor 763 side A )    Fenton Jones
FIGURE

Dosa round your corner lady, see saw round your pretty little baby
Join your hands and circle round the floor
1 & 3 (2 & 4) right & left thru, 2 & 4 (1 & 3) trail thru, around one, form two lines of 4
Forward eight, eight fall back, opposites right, box the gnat
Box the flea, form two lines again
The center four trail thru, split the ring, pass two
Come down the center, opposites box the gnat
Then you allemande left your corner lady, pass your own, that pretty little baby
Promenade the next gal round the ring
Promenade 'em two by two, take 'em home, here's what you do
Balance out and everybody swing

(Sequence of dance: twice for head couples, twice for side couples.)
