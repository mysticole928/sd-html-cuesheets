The Happy Wanderer
Obernkirchen Children's Choir; Frankie Yankovic; many others 1953
(Coyote 845) Don Coy
OPENER

Sides face grand square
I love to go a wandering along the mountain track
And as I go I love to sing, my knapsack on my back
Allemande and weave
Val-deri, Val-dera
Swing and promenade the ring
Val-deri, Val-dera
My knapsack on my back

FIGURE

Heads (sides) promenade all the way around the ring
Sides (heads) square thru 4
4 hands and then, touch 1/4 , scoot back
Boys fold , girls turn back , that corner swing
Swing and promenade that ring
Val-deri, Val-dera
My knapsack on my back

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face grand square
I love to wander by the stream that dances in the sun
So joyfully it calls to me, come join my happy song
Allemande left and weave
Val-deri, Val-dera
Swing and promenade the ring
Val-deri, Val-dera
My knapsack on my back

CLOSER

Sides face grand square
I wave my hat to all I meet and they wave back to me
And blackbirds call so loud and sweet from every green wood tree
Allemande and weave
Val-deri, Val-dera
Swing and promenade the ring
Val-deri, Val-dera
My knapsack on my back
