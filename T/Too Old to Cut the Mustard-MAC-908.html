Too Old To Cut The Mustard    (MAC 908)   Jerry Helt

BREAK



(Triple allemande)
Allemande left and the ladies star
The gents go round but not too far
Allemande left and the four gents star
The gals go round but not too far
Allemande left with the old left hand
A right to your partner, right & left grand
Hand over hand till you meet your maid
Just take her by the hand and promenade
All sing
She's too old, too old, too old to cut the mustard any more

FIGURE



Ladies to the center and back to the bar
Gents to the center with a right hand star
Turn the opposite gal like an allemande thar
And the boys back up in a right hand star
Throw in the clutch and you put 'er in low
It's twice around that ring you go
Skip your own and on you go
Meet the next for a dopaso
Partners left with a left hand around
Corners all with the right hand around
Partners left with the arm around
You promenade, go round the town
All sing
Too old, too old, too old to cut the mustard any more

(repeat the figure, alternating ladies and gents)
