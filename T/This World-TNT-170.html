This World   (TNT 170 )   Bob Van Antwerp

BREAK



4 ladies chain straight across the ring now
Join hands and circle left and then
Ladies center, men sashay, circle left around the land
Ladies center, men sashay (left allemande)
Curlique and then, boys run to the right
Left allemande that corner girl and promenade tonight
What this world needs is a whole lot of lovin'
And a lot less shovin' around

FIGURE



1 & 3 square thru 4 hands round you go
Round your corner do a dosado
Swing thru and then, the boys run right my friend
Tag the line, face right & boys cross run
Ladies trade and wheel & deal and then pass thru
Left allemande your corner, come back one and promenade
What this world needs is a whole lot of lovin'
And a lot less shovin' around
