This Old Riverboat    (GR 12218)   Johnny Davis

OPENER



Join hands and circle left that Riverboat's awaitin'
All aboard, it's time to go
Allemande left the corner girl, turn a right hand round your partner
Men star left around the town, star promenade that gal around
Girls step out and backtrack, same one dosado and then you
Left allemande, come home and promenade
You promenade her
It's up the river we go...
Go forward eight and come on back

FIGURE ONE



4 little ladies chain now
Go straight across the river
Chain 'em back and watch 'em go
Put the ladies back to back, men go round the outside track
Get back home, dopaso
Don't you hear the whistle blow
With the corner, box the gnat, and then dosado
Left allemande and come home, promenade
You promenade her
As up the river we go
I see that riverboat so
