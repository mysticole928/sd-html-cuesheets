This Can't Be Love
Eddie Albert and Marcy Westcott; many others 1938
(Golden Square 6010) Don Atkins
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Left allemande , come home and box the gnat
Grand right & left the wrong way around
You turn your girl, a right hand round once and a half
The men star left around the town
Come home and dosado , left allemande you know
Come back and swing that pretty little girl and promenade the ring
This can't be love because you feel so well
But how you love to swing with your girl

FIGURE

Four ladies chain 3/4 round with you
Then promenade round the ring
Head (side) couples wheel around and pass on thru
Bend the line , go up and back , pass thru
Fold the girls you do
Pass thru , U-turn back , * star thru , promenade the ring
This can't be love, you get no dizzy spell
But take her home and swing with this maid

(*Note: you can use a dosado instead of star thru as Don calls it on called side.)
