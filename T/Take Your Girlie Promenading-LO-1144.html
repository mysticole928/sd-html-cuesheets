Take Your Girlie Promenading
(Lore 1144) Stan Ruebell
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Now four ladies chain , go straight across that ring
Join hands and circle left I sing
Why don't you allemande left and allemande thar , go right & left and star
Men to the middle, back up boys but not too far
Shoot the star go right & left grand you go
Meet your lady then you promeno
Take your girlie promenading
Down the lane, boys, hand in hand

FIGURE

Heads promenade go half around you know
Into the middle square thru you go
Be sure you count to four and dosado go full around
Swing thru and when you do, swing thru again
Pass thru , trade by , that corner you swing
Swing that corner lady and promenade the ring
Take your girlie promenading
Down the lane, boys, hand in hand
