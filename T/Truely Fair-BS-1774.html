Truely Fair   (Blue Star 1774 )   Marshall Flippo

FIGURE



Why the heads/sides lead out to the right
And circle 1/2 way round
Dive thru, make a right hand star & ride the merry go round
When you meet your corner lady, left allemande & maybe
Promenade your own girl, go walking round the ring
Now heads/sides, you wheel around
Go right & left thru with the two you've found
Star thru, then pass on thru & swing
Swing the little ole girl around & do a left allemande
Dosado, go round your own then weave around the land
Singing truely truely fair
Truely truely fair
When you meet, you dosado & promenade the square
I've got songs to sing her, trinkets to bring her
Flowers for her golden hair
