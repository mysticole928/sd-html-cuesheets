Grande Colonel Spin   (Kalox 1112 )   Harper Smith

OPENER, BREAK, ENDING:



Allemande Left in Alamo Style, Balance There
Swing Thru Two By Two, then Balance There
Swing Thru Two By Two, Turn Partner Right Hand Round
Allemande the Corner Girl, Weave Around the Town
Weave 'Em In, Weave 'Em Out, Partner Do-Sa-Do
Full Around and then Promenade Them Home
Promenade go Round the Bend Take Them Home and Then
When You're Home Everybody Grand Spin - Sides Face Go!

FIGURE OF GRAND SPIN AS FOLLOWS:



Sides will face as in Grand Square, back up 3 steps & turn on #4 step,
go forward then Do-Sa-Do (YOU MUST DO-SA-DO) Then Star Thru, you are now the new Heads,

Heads will Star Thru, then Spin the Top, then Star Thru, to end facing the vacated sides position, then take a good step forward & California Twirl & Face. Heads you are now the new Sides.

Repeat 3 more times and you are home.

The Heads and Sides are working at the same time as in Grand Square.
