The Drummer's Balance
(Balance 114) Ed Gilmore
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

All 4 couples separate, go round the outside track
Pass your partner going, star thru when you get back
Join your hands and circle left , go dancin' round that way
Allemande left the corner girl and weave the ring I say
Oh Solomon Levi, tra-la-la-la-la-la-la
Turn your partner right hand, allemande left with your corner again
Come back and promenade your partner, dance her home again
And when you reach your place in the set all 4 ladies chain

FIGURE

Send the girls across the set , you turn and roll away
Join your hands and circle left , go dancing round that way
Allemande left your corner girl, grand right & left you go
Go right & left around the set, you'll meet again you know
Dosado around her to a double ocean wave*
Star once around and give her a spin
Promenade around the set you dance her home again
When you get back home again all 4 ladies chain

CLOSER

There's plenty of time when you get home
So swing her round and round

TAG

Swing your lady go round and round
Bow to your lady and settle down

*A double ocean wave is two lines crossed in a star position.

The "double ocean wave" is a wrong way thar.
