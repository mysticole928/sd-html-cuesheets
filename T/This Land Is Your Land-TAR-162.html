This Land Is Your Land
Woody Guthrie 1940
   (Tarheel 162)    Ronnie Purser
OPENER    B - SSD Week 2

Four ladies chain now, go across the ring go
You rollaway , circle left and go round the ring go
Four ladies rollaway , and circle left go walking round the ring
Allemande left your corner, weave the ring
This land is your land, this land is my land
From California , swing and promenade her
From the redwood forest to the Gulf Stream waters
This land is made for you and me

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

Now the heads (sides) promenade and go halfway around
Come down the middle go right & left thru and turn the girl around
Flutter wheel across that floor, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and then go right & left thru
Roll this girl a half sashay and then you pass thru you do
Do a U-turn back swing the corner girl and promenade
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land belongs to you and me

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
This land is your land, this land is my land
From California to the New York islands
From the redwood forest, to the Gulf Stream waters ( swing her)
This land was made for you and me
Four ladies promenade one time inside you go
Come back and swing with this man and promenade you go
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land belongs to you and me

CLOSER   

Four boys promenade inside the ring and go
Back home swing your girl round and round and go
Join hands , circle left go walking around you all
Allemande your corner weave the ring
This land is your land, this land is my land
Swing your girl once around and promenade the ring and go
From the redwood forest to the Gulf Stream waters
This land belongs to you and me
