The Ballad Of The Three Amigos
Chevy Chase, Steve Martin and Martin Short 1986
(Omar 152) Corben Geis , Elmer Sheffield, Jr. , Wade Driver
OPENER

Sides face grand square
One for each other and all for one
The three brave Amigos are we
Brother to brother and everyone
A brave amigo , Allemande left and weave
Fighting for justice and liberty
Wherever you find us is where we will be
For the three brave amigos are we

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) promenade go halfway around
Sides (Heads) do the right & left thru
Sides (Heads) square thru four hands you go
Now do an eight chain 4
We are the three aaah-migos!
Swing and then you promenade
We are the 3 ahhhhaaaaa-migos!

MIDDLE BREAK

4 senoritas promenade inside the ring
Throw your hands up high in the air
Back home swing your partner
Left allemande and weave the ring
Fighting for justice and liberty
Wherever you find is where we will be
For the three brave amigos are we

Sides face grand square
Over the mountains across the plains
The three brave amigos are we
Stamping out evil till none remains
The brave amigos Allemande left and weave
Fighting for justice and liberty
Wherever you find is where we will be
And amigos forever we'll be!!!
