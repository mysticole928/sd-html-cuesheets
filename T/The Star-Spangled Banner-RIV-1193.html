The Star-Spangled Banner
many 1773
(Riverboat 1193) Lawrence Johnstone
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Sides face, grand square
*Oh, say, can you see
By the dawn's early light
What so proudly we hailed
At the twilight's last gleaming?
Allemande and weave
Whose broad stripes and bright stars
Through the perilous fight
O'er the ramparts we watched
Were so gallantly streaming?

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) pass the ocean , extend
Swing thru , boys run right
Couples circulate , ferris wheel
Centers sweep 1/4 and back away , the others lead right
Touch 1/4 , scoot back
Swing the corner, promenade her home
O'er the land of the free
And the home of the brave!
