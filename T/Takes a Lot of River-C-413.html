Takes A Lot Of River    (C 413)   Beryl Main

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade go once around the ring
Well come on back and swing your man around
Join all your hands and circle left go walking down the line
Left allemande and weave around the ring
Wind in and out around you go and when you meet your maid
Do sa do around the girl and then you promenade
The Monoga, Hela, Mississippi and the Ohio
Gonna take a lot of river to wash those blues away

FIGURE



Heads promenade take her halfway round the ring
Come down the middle and square thru four hands I sing
Go all the way around and then a right and left thru
Veer to the left and ferriswheel around and then
Square thru three quarters round the corner swing
Swing that corner girl and promenade go round the ring
The Monoga, Hela, Mississippi and the Ohio
Gonna take a lot of river to wash those blues away
