Curl the Line: from an ocean wave. Center 2 drop right hands and swing (left hand) halfway round to face opposite direction, simultaneously the ends walk forward and to the right to end facing the girl who was beside him in the ocean wave line. Curl the Line accomplishes the same thing as a Right & Left Thru.
