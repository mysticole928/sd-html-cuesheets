You turn the girl with an arm around
Join your hands and circle left in style
Rollaway a half sashay

Go right & left to make an allemande thar

Your corner right a wrong way thar
The men back in a left hand star
Back the star around the land
Shoot the star pass one girl , left allemande
Come back one and promenade

That do make it nice
