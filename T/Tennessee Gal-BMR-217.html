Tennessee Gal
(Black Mountain 217) Selmer Hovland
INTRO

Allemande left with your left hand
Partner right a right & left grand
Hand over hand around the ring you go
Meet your honey with a full sashay
Promenade the usual way
The Tennessee Gal that's dressed in calico

FIGURE

Now the gents star left in the middle of the town
Pick up your gal when you come around
Star promenade around the ring you go
The gents swing out the ladies in
Form that star with the pretty points in
Star promenade the other way you know

BRIDGE

You rollaway, the gents star right
You spread that star across the night
The gals duck under to a left allemande
Partner right, a right & left grand
Hand over hand around the ring you go
Meet your honey and pass her by
Pick up Tennessee on the fly
Promenade that gal and don't be slow

(REPEAT FIGURE AND BREAK)

BREAK

The girls turn back around the two
Meet her with a right hand halfway around
Back with a left, now dopaso that girl
Her by the left, corner by the right
Promenade your own if it takes all night
Promenade that little Tennessee Gal

(REPEAT FIGURE, BRIDGE, FIGURE, BRIDGE, THEN USE BREAK FOR CLOSER)

IMPORTANT NOTE: This is a continuous movement dance. Do not stop at home on any promenade but keep moving all the time.
