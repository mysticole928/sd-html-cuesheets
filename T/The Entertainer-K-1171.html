*Grand Parade: 64-beat phrased figure (no time for twirls or fancy flourishes). Heads step to a wave, turn right hand half, centers 3/4 while side lady promenade 1/4 on outside to join head man in promenade position, as the side man courtesy turn head lady to promenade position (8 counts total); all 4 couples promenade exactly halfway to face center of square (8 counts total); repeat for heads; repeat twice for sides. Grand total 64 beats; all dancers back at home.
