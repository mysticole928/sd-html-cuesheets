Time Marches On
   (Cheyenne 2006)    Chuck Veldhuizen
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Sister cries out, from her baby bed
Brother runs in with feathers on his head
Mama's in her room learning how to sew
Daddy's drinking beer listening to the radio
Four ladies chain 'cross the ring
Chain them back now, promenade
Time marches on, time marches on

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

Heads (sides) promenade half around and go
Down the middle , square thru 4 hands and go
Four hands and then do the right & left thru
Veer left and couples circulate one time you know
Wheel & deal , face 'em there
Half square thru , corner swing now, promenade
Time marches on, time marches on

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
The South moves north, the North moves south
A star is born, a star burns out
The only thing that stays the same is everything changes
Four ladies chain 'cross the ring, turn the girls
Chain 'em right back home now, promenade
Time marches on, time marches on

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Sister calls herself a sexy grandma
Brother's on a diet for high cholesterol
Mama's out of touch with reality
Daddy's in the ground
Circle left you see
As the Angles sing an old Hank Williams song
Allemande , promenade
Time marches on, time marches on
