The Man In The Moon    (K 1113)   Vaughn Parrish

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around your corner girl, see saw your taw
Join hands, circle left you go
Left allemande, go weaving round the hall
Take the time to smile at everyone you know
Dosado and then left allemande again
Come back and promenade your bride
The man in the moon may stop smiling
But I'll always be by your side

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4 round
You turn 'em with an arm around
Those heads lead to the right & circle 4
Head men break and make a line,
up to the middle and back in time
Right & left thru you turn once more, slide thru
Swing thru and then, 2 boys trade again
Swing the corner, promenade your your bride
The sun and moon may stop shining
But I'll always be by your side
