Till There Was You   (Grand 403 )   Johnny Preston

OPENER



Four ladies chain, You turn there with that girl
And then you chain her right back home now
Join hands Circle left in a ring
Left allemande your corner and you weave the ring
There were birds, in the sky
Dosado and then promenade
But I never saw them at all, Till there was you

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands around and go
Dosado with your corner, Swing thru
And boy you run right, You ferris wheel
Centres go right and left thru
Centres square thru three quarters
Then swing your corner and promenade her
I never saw them at all, Till there was you

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
There was music and there were wonderful roses they tell me
In sweet - fragrant meadows of dawn - and dew
Four ladies chain, staright across
And you chain her back home and promenade her
[Middle] I never heard it all - Till there was you
[Closer] I never saw it at all - Till there was you - Till there was you
