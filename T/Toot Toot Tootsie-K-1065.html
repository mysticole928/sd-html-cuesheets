Toot Toot Tootsie    (K 1065)   Lee Helsel

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Join hands circle that ring
Go walking all the way back home again
Allemande left, alamo style, balance there and then
Swing thru go right and left and balance once again
Swing thru go right & left grand
Walkin' round the ring hand over hand
Dosado your own, corner allemande
Come back one, promenade go walking round the land
Singin' toot toot Tootsie don't cry
Toot toot Tootsie bye bye

FIGURE



4 little ladies chain across the ring
Side 2 ladies chain back home again
Heads promenade halfway round you do
Star thru, pass thru, go right & left thru
Dive thru, swing thru 3 hands and then
Corner girl left allemande
Dosado your own, corner you swing
Swing that gal around and promenade the ring
Singin' toot toot Tootsie don't cry
Toot toot Tootsie bye bye
