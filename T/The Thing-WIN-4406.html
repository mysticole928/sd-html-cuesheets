S(l)inging Hash / The Thing
(Windsor 4406 side B ) Doc Alumbaugh
OPENER

Everybody swing your honey, swing 'em high and low
Allemande left the corners all and around the ring you go
It's a grand old right & left and when you meet *you do "The Thing"
Oh, cross both hands and ... - ... - ... - ...
And promenade the ring

[*"The Thing": On meeting partners across set, cross and join both hands, right hands on top, and resign yourselves to The Thing! As drums, whistles, horns and other sound effects start the gents crouch first and then rise as the ladies crouch.
Repeat up-and-down motion (like a teeter-totter) as time allows (about three times).
Don't crouch too low or you'll be sorry the next day.]

FIGURE

All four ladies promenade the inside of the ring
Pass right by your partners, give the next old man a swing
Now sashay round your corners and honor to your own
Swing your corner lady around and take her for your own
All four gents you promenade half the inside ring
Dosado that lady there and give her a great big swing
Head two couples pass right thru, the side two couples the same
Now your home and now you swing, swing with might and main

BREAK

Allemande left the corners all and honor to your own
It's a grand ol' right & left, boys, around the ring you roam
Now when you meet on yonder side, *get set to do "The Thing"
Oh, cross both hands and ... - ... - ...
And promenade the ring

(REPEAT FIGURE AND BREAK THREE MORE TIMES)
