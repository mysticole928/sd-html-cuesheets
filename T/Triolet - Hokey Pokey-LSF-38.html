Triolet / Hokey Pokey
(Lloyd Shaw 38) Don Armstrong
FORMATION

Trios facing trios around a circle, alternate trios facing LOD

FIGURE

1-16 Circle six to the left (Join hands and circle left 16 steps)
17-32 Circle to the right (Same six circle right full around with 16 steps)
33-48 Turn the opposites (Similar to "turn contra corners" - Center dancers of each trio turn each other with right elbows just far enough around to turn the right opposite dancer by the left elbow, then center dancers turn each other with right elbows just far enough around to turn the left opposite person with the left elbow and return to own trios. Center dancers only turn dancers from other trio.)
49-56 Trios advance and retire (All dance forward and back in 8 steps ending by turning individually 1/4 right face in place)
57-64 Trios march around and progress to next (Trios march in single file CCW around opposite trio to that trio's place and, by turning again individually 1/4 right face in place, end facing a new trio to begin again)
