This Land Is Your Land   (Grenn 12233 )   Johnny Davis

BREAK



Walk all around that corner girl than see saw your pet
Men star right and turn it once around that set
Turn your partner by the left, the corner box the gnat
Now pull her by, go back home and see saw your pet
Hey corner girl left allemande, go home and then you swing
Swing that girl and then you promenade and sing
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land was made for you and me

FIGURE



Hey now the 1st and 3rd square thru 4 hands 'round
Face the sides, make a right hand star, once around will do
To the same outside two go right & left thru
Hey now you roll away, pass thru
U-turn back and swing, swing that girl them you promenade again
This land is your land, this land is my land
This land was made for you and me

SEQUENCE (Break, Figure twice for heads, Break, Figure twice for sides, Break)
