Trixie's Special
(MacGregor 7635 side A )
FIGURE (4 times through)

Dosado your corner lady
See saw round your pretty little baby
Join your hands and circle round the floor
1 & 3 right & left thru ( backaway )
2 & 4 cross trail thru
Around one, form two lines of 4
Forward 8, 8 fall back
Opposites right, box the gnat
Box the flea , form two lines again
The center 4 cross trail thru
Split the ring, pass two
Come down the center , opposites box the gnat
Then you allemande left your corner lady
Pass your own , that pretty little baby
Promenade the next gal round the ring
Promenade 'em two by two
Take 'em home , here's what you do
Balance out and everybody swing
