The Sun's Gonna Shine   (Kalox 1101 )   Harper Smith

OPENER - BREAK - ENDING



Walk around you corner swing with your partner
Promenade that lady round the ring
Heads (sides) wheel around right & left thru the two you found
Crosstrail allemande left and weave the ring
Well it looks like the sun's gonna shine, shine, shine
Meet partner do-sa-do then promenade
The dark clouds are gone and the blue skies are mine
Yes it looks like the sun's gonna shine

FIGURE



Head (side) couples promenade go half way round the ring
Down the middle do a right and left thru
Square thru four hands with that pretty Jill
Do a sashay thru then wheel and deal
Dive thru and square thru three quarters
Swing that corner girl and promenade
The dark clouds are gone and the blue skies are mine
Yes it looks like the sun's gonna shine

NOTE

Sashay thru starts as a do-sa-do except when the men get back far enough
they slide across face to face & wind up in a two face line. Also try
this record for patter calling, I think you callers will really like it.
