There's A Man In Here
(ESP 1117) Elmer Sheffield, Jr.
OPENER

Circle now
There's a man in here who turned water to wine
And He walked on the water, gave sight to the blind
Left allemande the corner, then box the gnat
Four ladies promenade inside like that
Turn partner right , the corner allemande
Come on back, promenade go round the land
And He lives in my heart and I have no fear
I'm a better man cause there's a man in here

FIGURE

Heads (sides) square thru in the middle
Dosado that corner girl, go round
Swing thru , boys you trade
Boys run right now half tag
You walk & dodge , chase to the right
The corner lady swing , swing and promenade
All my sins are gone and I have no fear
I'm a better man cause there's a man in here

And He lives in my heart and I have no fear
