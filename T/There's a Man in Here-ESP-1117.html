There´s A Man In Here    (ESP 1117)  Elmer Sheffield

OPENER



Circle Left
There’s a man in here, who turned water to wine
He walked on the water, gave sight to the blind
Left allemande, box the gnat and
Four ladies promenade inside like that
Turn her by the right, and find the corner
Left Allemande, come back and promenade that land
He lives in my heart and I have no fear
I’m a better man ‘cause there’s a man in here.

FIGURE



Heads Square Thru count 'em 4 hands round
You make a Right Hand Star
The Heads Star Left, roll it one time and then
Same pair and do a Dosado
Swing Thru and then the Boys Trade
Swing the corner lady round and Promenade
All my sins are gone and I have no fear
I’m a better man ‘cause there’s a man in here.

MIDDLE BREAK



Four Ladies Chain, you turn that girl and then
You Rollaway, Circle Left go round
Four Ladies roll away, Circle Left and go
Left Allemande and Weave The Ring
There’s a man in here who turned water to wine
Swing your girl around and Promenade
He lives in my heart and I have no fear
I’m a better man ‘cause there’s a man in here.

CLOSER



Four Ladies Chain, you turn that girl and then
You Rollaway, Circle Left go round
Four Ladies roll away, Circle Left and go
Left Allemande and Weave The Ring
There’s a man in here who turned water to wine
And he walked on water, gave site to the blind
And in my heart I have no fear
I’m a better man ‘cause there’s a man in here.
