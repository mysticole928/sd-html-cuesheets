The Old Lamplighter   (Keeno 2250 )   Harold Bausch

FIGURE



Well, now the head (side) two couples promenade
Go 1/2 way round there with your maid
Yes, 1/2 around and then 2 ladies chain
Turn the girl and 1/2 sashay, and then star thru across that way
Circle there and make a line of four...
Go forward eight and then fall back
Pass thru, bend the line
Swing the corner girl and promenade
You made the night..a little brighter, wherever he would go
Gents star left around go, star promenade you know
The men back out, all ladies chain, you turn and chain them home
Promenade
That old lamplighter, of long...long ago
