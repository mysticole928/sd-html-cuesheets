This Old World   (Cimarron 112 )   Gerald McWhirter
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK

Four little ladies chain go straight across the ring
Turn and chain them home again
Join hands and curcle left go walking talking round
Left allemande weave and sing
Sind in and out until you meet
Swing promenade and sing
What this old world needs
Is a whole lotta loving and a lot less shoving around

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway, come down the middle star thru
Pass thru, touch 1/4, scoot back
Boys run, right & left thru
Slide thru, pass thru, left allemande
Come back one swing and promenade again
What this old world needs
Is a whole lotta loving and a lot less shoving around

Sides square thru four hands
Right & left thru, swing thru
Spin the top boys move up with a right & left thru
