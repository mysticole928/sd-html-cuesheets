Tie A Yellow Ribbon
Tony Orlando & Dawn 1973
   (Riverboat 549)    Hiromi Kaneko
 
OPENER    Basic

Four ladies chain across the ring
Four ladies chain back home
Join your hands , circle left you go
Left allemande your corner lady, weave the ring
Tie a yellow ribbon 'round the ole oak tree
Swing your little girl, and promenade with me
If I see a yellow ribbon round the ole oak tree
I'll know you still want me

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) square thru four hands you go
Around the corner dosado your girl
Swing thru , two by two, boys run right
Tag the line , face in
Turn thru , ends cross fold
Swing your little girl, promenade with me
If I see a yellow ribbon round the ole oak tree
I'll know you still want me

FIGURE   

Heads (sides) square thru four hands you go
Around the corner dosado your girl
Swing thru , two by two, boys run right
Tag the line , face out
Ends cross fold , touch 1/4 , scoot back
Swing your girl, promenade
If I see a yellow ribbon round the ole oak tree
I'll know you still want me

MIDDLE BREAK    Basic - SSD Week 2

Four ladies chain 3/4
Join your hands , circle left
Ladies rollaway , circle left you go
Left allemande your corner lady, weave the ring
Tie a yellow ribbon 'round the ole oak tree
Swing your little girl and promenade with me
If I see a yellow ribbon round the ole oak tree
I'll know you still want me
