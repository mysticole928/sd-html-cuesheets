Tag-A-Long    (BO 1203)   Cal Lambert

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Left allemande your corner, come back one and then you swing
Then promenade, don't slow down, keep going around the ring
Men turn in, a right hand star, the girls keep going around
Go twice around and turn the partner, left hand around
Then walk around the corner, see saw around your own
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Promenade around that ring, go walking home and swing
Or she'll tag along with me

FIGURE



Head two square thru 4 hands around you go
Go alll the way, face the sides, do a dosado
Pass to the center and square thru 3/4 around the ring
Then dosado that outside 2, all the way around and then
Pass to the center and square thru 3/4 round you go
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Promenade around this minute, just as if you really mean it
Or she'll tag along with me
