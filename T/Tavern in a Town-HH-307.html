Tavern In A Town    (HH 307)   Wayne West

FIGURE



1 & 3 will promenade 3/4 round the town
The side 2 right & left thru, turn your lady round
A double pass thru, face your partner then star thru
Substitute, square thru 3 hands round
Allemande left your corner lady, dosado your own and maybe
All 4 gents will star by the left, go once around the ring
Meet your partner, dosado, corner swing you see
Swing this lady, promenade, sing along with me
I'll hang my harp on a weeping willow tree
And never, never think of thee

MIDDLE BREAK



4 little ladies promenade go once around the town
Pass your partner, swing your corner, swing him twice around
Then promenade go single file, look around that ring and smile
All 4 gents turn back, go twice around
Pass 'em once and don't be late, you meet again and catch all 8
Right hand 1/2, gack by the left, to an allemande thar you know
Shoot that star, go right & left grand around the ring you see
You'll meet your lady, promenade, sing along with me
I'll hang my harp on a weeping willow tree
And never, never think of thee

SEQUENCE: (Figure twice with heads, middle break, figure twice with sides)
