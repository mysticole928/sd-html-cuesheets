The Proposal
   (Blue Ribbon 221)    Gloria Rios-Roth
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Join hands , circle left go round a while
Reverse back , go single file
The girls backtrack , go once around and then
You dosado and weave the ring ( Calypso style )
You weave it in and our around you go ( Try a Limbo )
Dosado that girl and promenade her home
You could have a wonderful life
Please keep that lady for your wife

FIGURE   

(Precede the #1 beat with the cue...)
Sides face grand square [only the Sides (Heads) are working the grand square]
Heads (Sides) curlique , boys you run
Separate and go around one , down the middle and pass thru
And separate and go around one again
In the middle and square thru 3 hands
Allemande left , go home and dosado
Go back, swing the corner, then promenade her home
You could have a happy, happy life
Please, won't you let that gal be your wife?

(Notes: "Calypso" and "Limbo" can be anything to give the dance the Calypso flavor.)
(The Grand Square is a full 32-beat Grand Square.)
