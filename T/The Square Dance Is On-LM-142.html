The Square Dance Is On    (LM 142)   Mac Letson, Tony Oxendine

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



All 4 ladies promenade go once around that ring
Well get back home and swing the man, everybody swing
Join hands and make a ring, you circle to the left and go
Do an allemande left with the corner girl, gonna weave around that ring
Well go winding a weaving in and out, get around that ring and go
Do a dosado when you meet a little girl and promenade her home
I've wined her and dined her, my money it's all gone
Well the gold rush is over and the square dance is on (2, 3, 4...)

FIGURE



Head couples promenade halfway around you go
Come down the middle with a right & left thru and you turn the girl you know
Square thru go 4, go rollin round you know
Swing thru the outside and then the boys run
Girls you trade, tag the line all the way through and then
The girls turn back, and swing, you promenade that ring
I've wined her and dined her, my money it's all gone
Well the gold rush is over and the square dance is on (2, 3, 4...)
