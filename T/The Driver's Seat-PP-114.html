The Driver's Seat    (PP 114)   Toots Richardson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Sides face, grand square
I got one foot on the gas, I got one eye on the law
I got my mind on that good lookin' guy (pretty little gal)
I passed about a mile ago
4 ladies chain across, you turn them and then
You chain them right on back and promenade
Oh with an A-1 long drawn white lined outlaw
Sittin' in the driver's seat

FIGURE



Heads square thru go 4 hands
Oh with the sides make a right hand star
And now the heads star left one time you bet
To the same two pass thru, turn back and then
You touch 1/4, boys run right
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Oh with an A-1 long drawn white lined outlaw
Sittin' in the driver's seat
