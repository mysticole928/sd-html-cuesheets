That Old Gang Of Mine    (GR 12116)   Singin' Sam Mitchell

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left in the alamo style, balance up and back
Swing thru, forward 2 and then box the gnat
Pull her by, left allemande, and then go allemande thar
Right & left, the men swing in, make a back up star
Come on now, shoot that star, grand right & left I sing
When you meet your lady fair, you promenade the ring
Gee, I would give the world to see that old gang of mine

FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain and couples 1 & 3
Promenade her halfway round you go
Lead to the right, circle up 4, make a line you see
Up to the middle and back, slide thru and eight chain 3
Pull 3 ladies by and the 4th little lady swing
Allemande left new corner, come back, promenade and sing
Gee, I would give the world to see that old gang of mine
