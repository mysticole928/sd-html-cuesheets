The Fox   (SIO 142)   Dude Sibley

OPENER, MIDDLE BREAK, CLOSER



One and Three Promenade tonight Half Way round on a chilly night
Lead to the Right, Circle to a Line, Go Up and Back to town-o,
town-o, town-o Pass Thru, Wheel Left, *)
Promenade around-o — One and Three
Wheel **) you do Star Thru, Right and Left Thru, Swing that Girl
that's facing you, Promenade around-o,
round-o, round-o; Like a fox you're sharp tonight
With the cutest little girl in town-o

FIGURE



One and Three Lead to the Right, Circle to a Line on a chilly night
Without a stop Square Thru four hands around-o,
round-o, round-o Swing and Whirl the Girl you meet
then Circle Left around-o Allemande Left,
if you dare Dosado, she's waiting there.
Bow down to the lady fair then Promenade the town-o,
town-o, town-o — Like a fox you're sharp tonight
With the cutest little girl in town-o
