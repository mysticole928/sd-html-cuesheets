Tennessee Border    (TOP 25247)   Emanuel Duming

BREAK



4 little ladies chain 3/4 round and then
All the ladies chain go straight across the ring
4 little ladies chain 3/4 round once more
Join all hands and start to circle left around the floor
Left allemande old corner, come weave around the ring
Pick her up in a pick-up truck, promenade this heart of mine
Her mother said no (no, no, no) she's my only daughter
But we got married on the Tennessee border

FIGURE



Head 2 couples promenade, halfway around and then
Come down the middle, right & left thru, full turn, face out
Cloverleaf, go around one, come into the middle and then
Slide thru, make a right hand star and turn it once my friend
Left allemande old corner, at home a dosado
Back to the corner, swing and whirl, and promenade you know
Her daddy said no (no, no, no) she's my only daughter
But we got married on the Tennessee border

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Head 2 couples promenade, halfway around and then
Come into the middle, pass thru then cloverleaf my friend
Round one to the middle and slide thru, you make a right hand star for me
Roll it once around until you find the corner there
Left allemande old corner, at home a dosado
Back to the corner, swing and whirl, and promenade you know
Her mother said no (no, no, no) she's my only daughter
But we got married on the Tennessee border
