Two Timin' Gal   (Prairie 1153 )   Singin' Sam Mitchell
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Well those four ladies chain 3/4 I sing
Turn that girl and circle round the ring
Roll her away and circle you know
Allemande that corner girl and weave the ring, go round the world
Weave in and out until you meet with your maid
Dosado go once around and then promenade
Promenade home with that two-timin' gal
And she'll keep on a rolling those eyes

FIGURE



Well those heads square thru 4 hands
Round that corner lady dosado
Wink at that girl and do a right & left thru
Dive thru, star thru, go right & left thru
Now all 4 girls make a right hand star
Swing the boy that winked at you and then promenade
Promenade home with that two-timin' gal
And she'll keep on a rolling those eyes
