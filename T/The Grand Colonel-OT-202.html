The Grand Colonel    (OT 202)   Jerry Helt

FIGURE (4 times through)



Sides face, grand square
Walk, two, three, turn
One, two, three, turn
One, two, three, turn
One, two, three, reverse
One, two, three, turn
One, two, three, turn
One, two, three, turn
Home you go
Allemande left the corner
Turn your partner by the right
Men star left, go once around tonight
Pass the partner by, promenade the next one there*
And when you get her home
Sides face, grand square

(Repeat three times then, on the fourth time: *Company halt)
