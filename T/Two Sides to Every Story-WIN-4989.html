Two Sides To Every Story    (WIN 4989)   Tommy Stoye

OPENER



Four little ladies promenade the inside
You get back home, and everybody swing
Join your hands and you make a ring
Circle eight now while I sing
Do an allemande left and weave that old hall
There's always two sides to every story
Work it all out and we'll take time
When you meet that girl promenade the ring
There's always two sides to every story
Work it all out and we'll take time
When you get back there, everybody swing

FIGURE



Now the heads (sides) you promenade and you go halfway
Move into the middle a right & left thru I say
Then rollaway a half sashay, move up to the middle and back
Star thru and then a right & left thru
You dive thru and pass thru, you swing the corner
Swing her go round and round, promenade the ring
There's always two sides to every story
Work it all out and we'll take time
When you get back there, everybody swing
