Traveling Shoes    (RYL 505)   Larry Letson

OPENER



Four ladies chain 3/4 round
Roll-a-way and circle left
I've done some things and I've spread my wings
Left allemande and weave the ring
I'm hanging up my travelling shoes
Swing that girl and promenade
It took some time but I knew
I'm hanging up my travelling shoes

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round
Roll away and star thru
Swing thru the outside pair, spin the top right there
Boys walk up and do a right & left thru
Flutter wheel one time round
Sweep 1/4 more, swing and promenade
It took some time but I knew
I'm hanging up my travelling shoes
