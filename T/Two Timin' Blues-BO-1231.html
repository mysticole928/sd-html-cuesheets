Two Timin' Blues    (BO 1231)   George Leverett

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join all your hands and make a ring, you circle left and sing
I'm singing about those two-timing blues
Allemande left, allemande thar, right and left and make a star
I'm singing blues to my shoes
Shoot that star, 4 ladies chain, you turn that pretty Jane
Roll away and swing somebody new, promenade
The meanest blues I find are those two-timin' kind
I'm singing about those two-timin' blues

FIGURE



Those 4 little ladies chain 3/4
Now turn and chain those ladies straight across
Those heads square thru you count to 4 you do
And with the sides spin chain thru
The girls turn back, as couples circulate
Keep moving, you promenade along
The meanest blues I find are those two-timin' kind
I'm singing about those two-timin' blues
