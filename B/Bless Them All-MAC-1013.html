Bless 'Em All    (MAC 1013)   Bill Ball

OPENER - BREAK



All 4 ladies chain, you turn the girl you know
Now join your hands and make a ring you circle round you go
Allemande left, allemande thar, go right & left and star
The men back up, you've got a right hand star
Well slip the clutch, go twice around that land
The same girl, left allemande, promenade
They stall and they tarry, while we want to marry
But nevertheless bless 'em all

FIGURE



Now heads promenade halfway round
2 & 4 go right & left thru and turn your girl
Heads cross trail go round 1, make a line
Go up and back with you, now pass thru
Wheel & deal, double pass thru
First 2 left, the next 2 right and star thru that way
Left allemande, pass one and promenade I say
*You promenade with Jerry, then Tom, Dick and Harry
But nevertheless bless 'em all
