Blue Hawaii   (Longhorn 137 )   Red Warrick

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain...to Blue Hawaii
Heads (sides) go forward up & back, dosado, go back to back
Star thru, right and left thru
Right and left thru full turn to the outside two
Then do an allemande left, grand right and left you go
Where the ocean breezes blow on the beach at Wakiki
Promenade 'em hand in hand where the moon is on the sand
You swing - and say Aloha ooee

FIGURE



Heads (sides) rollaway, then pass thru, go around two you do
Make a line forward up, come on back, star thru
Center 4 pass thru, circle 1/2 the ring
Dive on thru - pass thru - corner swing
Then do an allemande left, grand right and left you go
Where the ocean breezes blow on the beach at Wakiki
Promenade 'em hand in hand where the moon is on the sand
You swing - and say Aloha ooee
