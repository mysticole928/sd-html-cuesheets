FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands around you go
With the corner do a dosado
Make a right hand star and turn it once around
With the same two go right & left thru
Dive thru, square thru 3 hands around you go
Swing the corner, promenade
Give a blessing to your neighbor
Ring the bells of hope
