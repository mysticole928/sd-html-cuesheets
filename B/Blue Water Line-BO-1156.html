Blue Water Line    (BO 1156)   Charles Drake

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and circle around the ring like that
Left allemande your corner girl, partner box the gnat
Wrong way grand around that ring, hand over hand you roam
Meet your girl, box the gnat, then promenade her home
1st and 3rd wheel around, then do a right & left thru
Cross trail to the corner girl, left allemande you go
Promenade your lady, you dance along in time
Swing your travelin girl on the blue water line

FIGURE



1st and 3rd squrae thru 4 hands around you go
Right & left thru the outside 2, turn the girl you know
Star thru, pass thru, bend the line and then
You roll away, 1/2 sashay to a left allemande
Grand right & left you go, walkin' down that track
Meet your girl dosado, to the corner go
Box the flea, promenade, dance along in time
Swing your travelin girl on the blue water line
