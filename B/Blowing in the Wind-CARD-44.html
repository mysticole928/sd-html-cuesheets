Blowing In The Wind   (Cardinal 42 )   Dr Jerry Routh

OPENER, CLOSER



Circle left
How many roads must a man walk down
Before you can call him a man
Allemande left that corner girl, dosaso and then
Boys star left around the land
Turn your partner by the right, corner allemande
Come on back, swing and promenade
The answer my friend is blowing in the wind
The answer is blowing in the wind

FIGURE



Heads promenade halway around the ring and then
Walk in and do the right and left thru
Flutterwheel straight across, sweep a quarter more
Pass thru, do the right and left thru
Dive thru, square thru, three hands around and then
Swing the corner girl, promenade
The answer my friend is blowing in the wind
The answer is blowing in the wind

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
How many years must a mountain exist
Before it is washed to the sea
How many years must some people exist
Allemande left and weave around the ring
How many times must a man turn his head
Swing that girl around and promenade
The answer my friend is blowing in the wind
The answer is blowing in the wind
