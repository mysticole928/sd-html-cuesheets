By Australian Moonlight
(Top 25257) Wally Cook
BREAK

4 ladies promenade , inside of the ring now
At home box the gnat , then swing her
Join your hands and make a ring, left allemande your corner
Dosado your own, weave the ring now
Go in and out around, until you meet your partner
Dosado and then promenade her
And you sing, sing, sing, yes sing as you swing
In the moonlight

FIGURE

Heads up and back , then go right & left thru ( backaway )
Sides lead right and circle 4 , break and make a line
When you do pass thru , then wheel & deal
Double pass thru , boomerang go
Go all the way around
Swing thru , turn thru ,
swing your corner , promenade her
And you can sing a happy song as you travel along
In the moonlight

Boomerang: Whenever one couple is standing behind another couple (as in a double pass thru setup) on the call Boomerang, the lead couple as individual dancers roll back behind the trailing couple, who in turn step forward and do a partner trade. Movement ends with two couples facing.
