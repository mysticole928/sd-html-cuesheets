FIGURE

All 4 ladies to the left promenade the inside ring
Partners left, box the flea
Your corners you will swing
She'll swing with the bully of the town
Allemande left like a hottentot
You pass your honey to the old red hot
Turn your right hand lady with a right hand round
Your partner lady with the left hand around
Promenade your corner , now you're homeward bound
And she'll swing with the bully of the town

Repeat figure for the gents, using the line All 4 gents to the right...

The FIGURE (for Side B, Powder Your Face) works as follows: Heads promenade FULL; then face the couple on their right (as those couples turn to the left to face the heads); WAIST swing (i.e., highland fling) facing dancer and face the center of the set (notice the outsides are half sashayed, hence the next call); outsides half sashay as centers box the gnat; centers pull by (all are facing original partner); Dopaso; take CORNER and promenade...
