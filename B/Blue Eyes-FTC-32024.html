Blue Eyes
   (FTC 32024)    Paul Hartman
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    C4

All 4 ladies chain across and you turn them
Circle left and around the ring you go
Allemande left , then you do the grand sashay * now
Dosado, right hand and pull her by
See saw round the next one there
Left hand and pull her by
Swing your own and then you promenade
Say, how many long tips have you waited
Just to dance** with this pretty blue-eyed maid

FIGURE    Mainstream - SSD Week 12, corner progression

1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade you go halfway
Then lead right and you circle to a line
Forward 8, come on back , pass the ocean
Two ladies trade , recycle now
Square thru 3 hands, 3/4 round you go
Swing the corner girl and then you promenade
Oh, how many long tips have you waited
Just to dance** with this pretty blue-eyed maid

ALTERNATE LYRICS

**swing, **flirt

*Note: The grand sashay is done by facing your partner (in this case the opposite lady) and doing a dosado around her to wind up facing her. Then with a right hand these two dancers pull each other by. Then see saw around the next person and with left hands joined then pull by.

In this opener/middle break/closer, you actually do ONE HALF of a Grand Sasahy!
