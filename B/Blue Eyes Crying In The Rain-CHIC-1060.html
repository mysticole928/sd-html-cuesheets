Blue Eyes Crying In The Rain
Roy Acuff; Hank Williams Sr.; Willie Nelson; many others 1945
(Chic 1060) Steve Moore
OPENER

Sides face grand square
In the twilight glow I see them
Blue eyes cryin' in the rain
Left allemande and weave
When we kissed good bye and parted
Swing and all promenade
I knew we'd never meet again

FIGURE

Heads promenade go halfway around
walk in and do a right & left thru
Sides square thru four hands you go
And make a right hand star
The sides star left one time around
Your corner swing and all promenade
I knew we'd never meet again

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face grand square
Love is like a dyin' ember
Only memories remain
Allemande left and weave the ring
Through the ages I'll remember
Swing and promenade
Blue eyes crying in the rain

CLOSER

Sides face grand square
Someday when we meet up yonder
We'll stroll hand in hand again
Allemande left and weave the ring
In a land that knows no partin'
Swing and promenade
Blue eyes crying in the rain
