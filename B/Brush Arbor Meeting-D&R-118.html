4 ladies promenade go once inside the ring
come back, swing your man around
let's join hands, circle left, go walkin' talkin' around
left allemande & weave the ring
They call it a brush arbor meeting
swing your lady round & round & promenade

come down the middle & square thru you know

turn the girl, slide thru, square the barge
square thru 4 hands, do a trade by
swing the corner lady round
Praising with a song & shouting victory
there's a brush arbor meeting today
