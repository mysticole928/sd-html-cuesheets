Bundle Of Love   (Blue Star 1835 )   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



Dosi round the corner girl, star thru now with your own
Circle to the right neath stars above
Now rollaway, left allemande & weave the ring around the land
Weave the ring with your love
Why, meet your girl & dosado, to the corner left allemande
Then come on back & swing & whirl & promenade that land
Promenade, go round the ring, take that lady back home & swing
Swing that bundle of joy

FIGURE



Head (side) couples left hand star once around but not too far
Split that corner pair and make a line
Go forward up back with this girl
Then star thru, Ca. twirl
Then make a left hand star one more time
Gents turn around & then you swing, keep her for your own
Left allemande, come back and promenade home
Promenade, go round that ring, take that lady back home and swing
Swing that bundle of joy
