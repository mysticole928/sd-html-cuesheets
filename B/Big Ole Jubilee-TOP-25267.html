Big Ole Jubilee    (TOP 25267)   Chip Hendrickson

BREAK



Allemande left with the corner girl and then go forward 2
With a right & left a full turn round and all 4 ladies chain
Straight across the ring, well turn that girl and then
Join 8 hands and circle to the left, circle to the left you know
Well roll away and weave the ring, it's in and out you go
And when you meet that lady, you do a dosado
Oh promenade the ring with the pretty, pretty thing
Right back home and everybody dance at the big old jubilee

FIGURE



Side 2 ladies chain the ring and couples 1 & 3
Gonna lead to the right and circle 4 and make a line for me
Go forward 8 and back, and then you all pass thru
Wheel & deal and the inside 2 square thru 3 hands
3/4 round, left allemande, walk right by your own
Swing the next young lady and you promenade her home
As easy as can be with my honey and me
Get back home and dance awhile at the big old jubilee
