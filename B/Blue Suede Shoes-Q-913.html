Blue Suede Shoes   (Quadrille 913 )   Shane Greer

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade once around the ring
Get on back and swing your man one time round again
Join hands and make a ring and circle to the left in time
Allemande left, weave around the ring
Blue, blue, blue suede shoes
Blue, blue, blue suede shoes
Swing that lady round, promenade go round the ring
One for the money and two for the show
Three to get ready and go cat go
You can do anything but lay off my blue suede shoes

FIGURE



Heads promenade your lady half around the ring
Walk to the middle, right & left thru, turn your little darlin' there
Flutterwheel around, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and then swing thru the outside two
Spin your top, boys move up, slide thru, corner swing
Swing that lady round, promenade the ring
One for the money and two for the show
Three to get ready and go cat go
You can do anything but lay off my blue suede shoes
