Buicks To The Moon   (CRC 189)   Drew Scearce

OPENER – MIDDLE – CLOSER

Walk around your corner - See Saw your own -

Join hands and Circle to the Left around you roam

Men Star Right one time tonight – Allemande Left that corner -

And Weave the ring,

When a nickel is worth a dollar - and gold ain’t worth a dime -

Swing your lady - and Promenade in time -

When the stars have all been counted - and I stop lovin’ you -

They’ll be drivin’ Buicks to the Moon.

 

FIGURE

Heads/Sides - Square Thru Four - hands around you go - 

And around the corner lady - do a Dosado you know -

Swing Thru – and then Spin the top - my friend -

Boys move up - and do a - Right and Left Thru

Square Thru Three hands - and the corner lady Swing -

Swing your corner lady - and Promenade the ring

When the stars have all been counted, and I stop lovin’ you -

They’ll be drivin’ Buicks to the Moon
