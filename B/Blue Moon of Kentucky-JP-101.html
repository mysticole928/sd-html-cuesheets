Blue Moon of Kentucky   (Jopat 101 )   Bob Vinyard

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande your corner, turn partner by the right
4 ladies promenade, go once inside
You get back home and dosado, once around and then
Allemande left and weave around the ring
Now on this moonlight night, the stars were shining bright
Dosado, allemande left and then promenade
Blue moon of Kentucy, keep on shining
Shine on the one that's gone and made me blue

FIGURE



Heads (sides), you promenade 1/2 way round
Lead right, circle to a line
Do the right and left thru, turn 1/4 more
Couples circulate, then bend the line
Slide thru, square thru 3/4 round
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Blue moon of Kentucy, keep on shining
Shine on the one that's gone and made me blue

TAG



Shine on the one that's gone and made me blue

ALTERNATE BREAK

4 ladies chain, turn 'em left around
Chain those ladies back across the ring
Join all of your hands and circle left around
Left allemande and weave around the ring
Now on this moonlight night with the stars shining bright
Dosado that maid...take her home in a promenade
Blue moon of Kentucy, keep on shining
Shine on the one that's gone and made me blue

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Head (side) two, square thru 4 hands around
With the sides, you make a right hand star
Head two, star left and turn it once around
Pick up the corner one from where you are
Back out and make a ring..circle left and then
Fold that pretty maid..star thru, then promenade
Blue moon of Kentucy, keep on shining
Shine on the one that's gone and been untrue
