Blackboard Of My Heart    (BO 1116)   Billy Lewis

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



1st and 3rd 1/2 sashay, go forward up and back
Right to the opposite, box the gnat, 1/2 square thru on back
Right & left thru with the outside 2 and turn the girl you know
Dive thru and square thru 3/4 round you go
Allemande left your corner, dosado your own
Allemande left just once again, grand right & left you roam
Meet your maid and promenade
Don't dance so far apart
Or tears will wash I love you from the blackboard of my heart

FIGURE



1st and 3rd cross trail thru, u-turn back and then
1/2 square thru, split the sides and around just one my friend
Into the middle and dosado to an ocean wave you go
Balance forward, balance back, cross trail and u-turn back
Cross trail thru to the corner and swing her once for me
Allemande left new corners, grand right & left you see
Take your maid and promenade
Say we'll never part
Or tears will wash I love you from the blackboard of my heart
