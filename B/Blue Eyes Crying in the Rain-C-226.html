Blue Eyes Crying in the Rain   (Chaparral 226 )   Jerry Haag

OPENER



Circle Left
In the twilight glow I see her, Blue eyes crying in the rain
The men star by the right go full around there
Do an allemande left the corner, and weave the ring
When we kissed goodbye and parted, I knew we'd never meet again
(Promenade)
Through the ages I'll remember
Blue eyes crying in the rain

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands, make a right hand star
Heads star left go full around Split the sides
Split the sides, The boy go left - the girl go right
Star thru, California twirl
Swing that corner girl and promenade
Through the ages I'll remember
Blue eyes crying in the rain

MIDDLE - CLOSER



Four ladies chain, rollaway and circle left
Four ladies rollaway and circle
Allemande left and weave the ring
Someday when we meet up yonder
We'll stroll hand in hand (Promenade)
In a land that knows no parting
Blue eyes crying in the rain
