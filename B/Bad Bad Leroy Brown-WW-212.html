Bad, Bad Leroy Brown   (Wagon Wheel 212 )   Jerry Haag
INTRO

Sides face, grand square
Well, the south side of Chicago, is the baddest part of town
Well, if you go down there, you better best beware of a man named Leroy Brown
4 ladies promenade one time round, swing with your man
Allemande and promenade the land
He's badder than ole King Kong, and meaner than a junk yard dog

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru 4 hands around, dosado that maid
Swing thru and the boys you run
Tag the line, face to the right (you wheel and deal and then)
Pass thru and turn back, swing the corner man
Left allemande and promenade the land
He's badder than ole King Kong, and meaner than a junk yard dog

BREAK



Sides face, grand square
Now LeRoy, he's a gambler and he likes his fancy clothes
He likes to wear his diamond ring on everybody's nose..
4 ladies promenade one time round, swing with your man
Allemande and promenade the land
He's badder than ole King Kong, and meaner than a junk yard dog

CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
He got a custom Continental and an Eldorado, too
He got a .32 gun in his pocket for fun, and a razor in his shoe
4 ladies promenade one time round, swing with your man
Allemande and promenade the land
He's badder than ole King Kong, and meaner than a junk yard dog
