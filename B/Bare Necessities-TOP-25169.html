Bare Necessities    (TOP 25169)   Happy Harry Pearcey

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



You allemande left with the corner girl
Do an allemande wee
Go right and left, and then turn back
Go back about 3
Go right, left, right, turn back 1 tonight
Like an allemande thar, the men back in
You've got a right-hand star
Well now you shoot that star
Grand right and left, around that big ole ring
When you meet your lady, take her hand and promenade
To the bare necessities, the simple bare necessities
When you're home, she'll twirl around for you

FIGURE



Now 1 & 3 you promenade
Go halfway round the track
Then whirl a half sashay
Go forward up and back
Pass thru, separate, round just 1 you go
Into the middle and star thru, frontier whirl you know
And then you allemande that corner girl, dosado go round your own
You'll swing that corner lady round, then promenade
To the bare necessities, the simple bare necessities
Twirl your girl, and good things will come to you

SEQUENCE: (Intro, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, ending)
