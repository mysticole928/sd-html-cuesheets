Boogie And Beethoven    (LBR 107)   Richie Burkhart

OPENER



4 ladies promenade inside the ring
Come back and give your man a swing
Join hands, circle left
Left allemande and weave the ring
I said 1, 2, 3, 4 we all started in
Swing that girl, promenade the ring
It was boogie and Beethoven under the Texas moon

FIGURE:



Heads square thru 4 hands around the ring
Round your corner do a dosado
You swing thru tonight, the boys run right
You do a 1/2 tag, scoot back, boys run right
Star thru, pass thru, u-turn back, swing
Swing that girl, promenade around that ring
Boogie woogie and Beethoven under the Texas moon

Sides face grand square

Swing your man around and promenade that ring
