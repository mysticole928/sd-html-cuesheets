Boogie Woogie Rhythm    (MAR 603)   Vern Weese

OPENER



Circle left
Hey rock and roll music sure got a beat
When you hear that rhythm, brings you to your feet
The men star right and roll it around and then
Allemande to the corner, then you weave that ring
Wind in and out around that ring you know

FIGURE



Heads you promenade, it's halfway around
Walk in and square thru 4 around
Go all the way and do the right & left thru
Veer over to the left and now ferris wheel
Come on and square thru and go 3/4 around
Swing that corner and promenade her on down
Baby likes to square dance, but man don't she go
For the boogie woogie rhythm called rock and roll
