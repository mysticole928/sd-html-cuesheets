Because I Love You   (Blue Star 1894 )   Marshall Flippo
SEQUENCE

A-B-B-C-B-B-C-A

FIGURE A



Left allemande and do a grand old right and left
When you meet your partner, promenade around that set
Don't want to be lonely, don't want to be blue
Darlin that's because I love you

FIGURE B



Head (side) 2 couples square thru 4 hands around you go
And around that corner lady dosado
Swing thru, the girls will circulate, the boys will trade
Now turn thru and go left allemande
You'll do dosado and with that corner lady swing
You swing that lady, promenade around that ring
Don't want to be lonely, don't want to be blue
Darlin that's because I love you

FIGURE C



4 ladies chain across, you'll turn 'em where they are
Join 4 hands and circle left and then
Do an allemande left, go allemande thar, go forward 2 & star
You'll make that star & now the boys back in
You shoot that star and dosado around your own
Left allemande, come back & promenade her home
Don't want to be lonely, don't want to be blue
Darlin that's because I love you
