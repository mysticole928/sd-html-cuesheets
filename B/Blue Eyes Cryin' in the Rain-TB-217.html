Blue Eyes Cryin' In The Rain
Roy Acuff; Hank Williams Sr.; Willie Nelson; many others 1945
(Thunderbird 217) Bud Whitten
OPENER

Sides face, grand square
In the twilight I see her
Blue eyes cryin' in the rain
Left allemande , weave the ring
As we kissed goodbye and parted
Dosado and promenade
I knew we'd never meet again

FIGURE

Heads (sides) square thru 4 hands around you go
To the corner dosado
Swing thru and then spin the top for me
Right & left thru , turn the girl
Square thru 3/4 (3 hands around)
Swing the corner lady around and promenade
Blue eyes cryin' in the rain

Sides face, grand square
Love is like a dying ember
Only memories remain
Left allemande , weave the ring
Through the ages I'll remember
Dosado and promenade
Blue eyes cryin' in the rain

CLOSER

Sides face, grand square
Some day when we meet up yonder
We'll stroll hand in hand again
Left allemande , weave the ring
In a land that knows no parting
Dosado and promenade
Blue eyes cryin' in the rain
