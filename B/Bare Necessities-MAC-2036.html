(Daisy Chain: Starting with a right to partner and a left to the next, turn back one for a right hand swing. Then forward again with a left and right and turn back one for a left hand swing; then forward again with a right & left. Turn back one for a right hand swing, then forward again with a left and right to partner. Action is always forward two and back one. In this case instead of giving a right to partner at end, do a dosado with partner.)
