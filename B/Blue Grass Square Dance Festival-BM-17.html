Circle left
There'll be Gilmore and Gotcher
Castner and Zellers, Moody and Osgood
and Big Mac McCullar
Left allemande , well you dosado round your own
Left allemande , then weave the ring you roam
In and out and when you meet you turn thru
Left allemande , come back and promenade you do
We'll call all them squares
In that blue grass style
At that blue grass square dance festival in the sky

FIGURE    A1, no progression

1 & 3 flutter wheel across the ring you go
Same 4 square thru 4 hands you know
Circle 4 with the outside two
And there you make a line
Pass thru , chase right in time
Split circulate , walk & dodge
And partner trade , cross trail thru
Swing the corner, promenade
We'll call all them squares
In that blue grass style
At that blue grass square dance festival in the sky
