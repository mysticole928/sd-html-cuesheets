Betcha My Life    (LH 1042)   Francis Zeller

Four ladies chain go straight across the ring



Sides divide and star thru, heads partner trade
Then join up hands, circle to the left go moving round that way
You allemande left your corner girl, do a dosado your own
Left allemande and then you promenade her home
You promenade for me all the way around you see
And I'll betcha my life I love you

Swing thru two by two, turn thru and then

Swing the corner girl and promenade her home
