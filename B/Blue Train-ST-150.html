Blue Train    (ST 150)   Bob Poyner

OPENER



sides face grand square
blue light flashing down the railroad track
blue smoke falling from the old smoke stack
oh, blue train,...don't leave me behind
4 ladies chain across the ring
you turn & chain 'em back again
roll promenade
Down the heartbreak railroad line

FIGURE



heads/sides touch 1/4, then boys run right
you circle 1/2 to a 2-faced line
wheel & deal, do an 8 chain thru
Don't leave me behind
let me ride till I lose
corner swing & promenade her, too
Oh, blue train,...
of the heartbreak railroad line
