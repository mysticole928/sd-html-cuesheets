Bingo / Bow Belinda
   (Folkraft 1189 side B )   
FORMATION

Column of 3 to 6 couples facing front, girls on partner's right.

STARTING POSITION

Partners facing.

SONG

(1-2) Bow, bow, bow, Belinda
(3-4) Bow, bow, bow, Belinda
(5-6) Bow, bow, bow, Belinda
(7-8) Won't you be my darling?

(1-2) Right hand up, o Belinda
(3-4) Right hand up, o Belinda
(5-6) Right hand up, o Belinda
(7-8) Won't you be my darling?

(1-6) Left hand up, o Belinda (sing three times)
(7-8) Won't you be my darling?

(1-6) Both hands up, o Belinda (sing three times)
(7-8) Won't you be my darling?

(1-6) Shake that big foot, shy all 'round her (sing three times)
(7-8) Won't you be my darling?

(1-6) Promenade all, o Belinda (sing three times)
(7-8) Won't you be my darling?

DANCE

(1-4) First boy and last girl, forward and back. First boy and last girl advance with three steps, bow or curtsey to each other, then return with four steps backward.
(5-8) First girl and last boy do the same.

(1-4) First boy and last girl, right hand swing. First boy and last girl join right hands, swing once around and return to place.
(5-8) First girl and last boy do the same.

(1-4) First boy and last girl, left hand swing.
(5-8) First girl and last boy do the same.

(1-4) First boy and last girl, two-hand swing. First boy and last girl join both hands, swing clockwise once around and return to place.
(5-8) First girl and last boy do the same.

(1-4) First boy and last girl, dosado. Back to back patter. First boy and last girl walk forward passing right shoulders, then, without turning, move to right, passing back to back, and walk backwards to place, passing left shoulders.
(5-8)First girl and last boy do the same.

(1-8) Head couple, lead column to foot of set and form an arch. All face forward and join hands with partner in skater's position, right hands joined over left. Head couple turn left and lead column to foot of set. At foot of set, head couple join and raise both hands to form an arch. Second couple lead others under the arch, advancing to become new head couple.
