Barnacle Bill    (BMV 38)   Brian Hotchkies

FIGURE (6 times, no partner change)



Head two ladies chain across
Same two ladies chain back home
Side two ladies chain across
Same two ladies chain back home
(REFRAIN after each figure)
(everybody)
SWING your partner round and round said Barnacle Bill the sailor
PROMENADE around the ring said Barnacle Bill the sailor
A big one up, a little one down, Ya make ya big feet jar the ground
Home you go around the ring said Barnacle Bill the sailor

MORE FIGURES



Head two couples go right and left thru
Side two couples you do it too
Head two couples go right and left home
Sides go home, go home, go home.

Head two ladies do si do



Head men run into the middle and do si do
Side men run into the middle .. and "just shake hands with each other"

(now are you ready for this?) Two head men will chain across



Two side men will chain across
Two side men will chain back home

Head couples join hands and circle to the left once around inside the set, back out at home



(you ready?) Head ladies chain across, side ladies chain across



Head men chain across, side men chain across
Head men chain back home, side men chain back home
Is everyone back home again?

CLOSER



SWING your partner round and round said Barnacle Bill the sailor
