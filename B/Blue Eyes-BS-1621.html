Blue Eyes    (BS 1621)   Al Brownlee

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join up hands, circle left with your blue eyes
Allemande left, grand old right & left you go
In and out around the square, box the gnat when you get there
Pull her by, allemande, now alamo
Balance up, back again, corners all left allemande
Swing your lady round and promenade
Well how many long years have you waited
Just to swing with this pretty blue eyed maid

FIGURE



1 & 3 go right & left thru, sides cross trail you do
Allemande, swing your lady round and round
Gents star left inside the square, star thru when you get there
Frontier whirl, promenade, don't slow down
1 & 3 wheel around, pass thru to the two you found
Bend the line, cross trail, corners swing
Allemande new corner maid, come back one and promenade
Promenade with that pretty blue eyed Jane
