Back In The U.S.A.
(Thunderbird 199) Bob Bennett
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join hands and circle to the left around you go
Allemande left your corner, turn your partner by the right
Allemande left once again, then weave 'em round tonight
Moscow and Warsaw, they felt fine yesterday
Dosado and then, promenade 'em round I say
Well promenade 'em back, to the good ole U.S.A.

FIGURE

Head (side) couples curlique , boys run to the right
Right & left thru , turn the girl and veer to the left tonight
Ferris wheel you do, now centers pass thru
Swing thru , boys trade and turn thru
Allemande left your corner, then promenade that way
Promenade 'em back, to the good ole U.S.A.

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

Head (side) couples touch 1/4 , boys run to the right
Right & left thru , turn the girl and veer to the left tonight
Ferris wheel you do, now centers pass thru
Swing thru , boys trade and corner girl you swing
Then promenade I say, walk'em home that way
Promenade 'em back, to the good ole U.S.A.
