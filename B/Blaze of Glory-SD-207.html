Blaze Of Glory   (Shakedown 207 )   Dennis Young

OPENER



Circle left
Let's go out in a blaze of glory
All good things must end
Left allemande, run home a dosado
And do a left allemande and weave the ring
Let's go out in a blaze of glory
Swing the lady round and promenade
Like 2 heroes in a story
Let's go out like we came in

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru, 4 hands around you go
Around the corner lady dosado
Swing thru tonite, then the boys run right
You bend the line and do a right and left thru
Flutterwheel across the ring and then slide thru
Swing the lady round and promenade
Like 2 heroes in a story
Let's go out like we came in

BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade go 1 time around
Genna get back home and swing with your man
Join up hands, circle to the left
Go movin' around you go
And do a left allemande and weave the ring
Let's go out in a blaze of glory
Swing the lady round and promenade
Like 2 heroes in a story
Let's go out like we came in
In a blaze of glory

CLOSER



All good things must end
