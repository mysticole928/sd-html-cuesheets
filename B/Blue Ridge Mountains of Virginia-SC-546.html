Blue Ridge Mountains Of Virginia    (SC 546)   Earl Rich

OPENER - ENDING



Left allemande the corner, let's do the grand sashay
Dosado go full around, right hand pull her by
See saw around the next one, give a left to her and then pull by
Dosado the next one, I'll tell you when
Right hand pull by and see saw the next
Left hand pull by, dosado and promenade
In the Blue Ridge mountains of Virginia
On the trail of the lonesome pine

FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) move up and back and do a 1/2 sashay
Turn thru, separate round one, into the middle, circle 4
Go walkin' round you know
Roll away, make a right hand star and to that corner go
Left allemande your corner, dosado around your own
Go back, swing the corner then promenade on home
In the Blue Ridge mountains of Virginia
On the trail of the lonesome pine

MIDDLE BREAK



4 little ladies promenade one time around that ring
Come on back and swing your handsome man
Join hands, circle left go walking round that floor
Left allemande the corner and weave the ring once more
Weave in and out around until you meet your maid
Dosado the pretty little girl, then you promenade
In the Blue Ridge mountains of Virginia
On the trail of the lonesome pine

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Those heads promenade go halfway around the ring
Down the middle and do the right & left thru
Roll away 1/2 sashay, into the middle and back away
Star thru, go right & left thru, turn the girl and then
Dive thru, pass thru, corner lady swing
Swing the corner lady, promenade around the ring
In the Blue Ridge mountains of Virginia
On the trail of the lonesome pine
