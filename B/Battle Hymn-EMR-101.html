OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies promenade go once around the ring
right back home & swing your partner, everybody swing
join hands & circle left go walkin round I sing
left allemande, weave the ring
glory glory hallelujah
swing that girl and promenade her
glory glory hallelujah his truth is marching on

FIGURE



head/side 2 couples promenade go 1/2 around that square
walk in & square thru 4 hands around from there
go all the way around & then you do a right & left thru
veer to the left and couples circulate
ladies trade, bend the line & do a right & left thru in time
you slide thru, swing that corner, promenade
glory glory hallelujah his truth is marching on

CLOSER



sides face grand square
glory glory hallelujah glory glory hallelujah
glory glory hallelujah his truth is marching on
4 ladies promenade go once around the ring
right back home, swing your partner, promenade again
glory glory hallelujah his truth is marching on
