Banjo Picker
   (Grenn 12124)    Earl Johnston
 
OPENER - CLOSER    Basic - SSD Week 1

Four ladies chain you turn them around
You chain them back , turn the cutest girl in town
Join hands and circle , go walkin' round the land
Allemande left your corner, do a right & left grand
A grand old right & left, until you meet her, dosado
Walk all the way around, men star left you know
Men star by the left , come home and swing
You swing your lady, and promenade around the ring
Promenade them go round the hall
To the Banjo Pickers Ball

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

1 & 3 promenade, go halfway around
Down the middle , do a right & left thru , turn the girl ( backaway ) and now
2 & 4 pass thru , separate around two
Around 2 and make a line , move up and back
Box the gnat , then do a right & left thru
All join hands , and circle left with you
Allemande that corner , do a right & left grand
Grand right & left and when you meet the girl, promenade
You promenade her, you go round the hall
To the Banjo Pickers Ball
