Blue Stars And Stripes
(Blue Star 1917) Jerry Helt
FIGURE

Four ladies chain
Heads right & left thru
Four ladies chain
Sides right & left thru
Girls forward 2, 3, shoosh & back
(Girls walk forward four counts and "shoosh" their skirts and back up four counts to place)
Boys forward, right hand star all the way round
Turn partner left , pick up the corner
Promenade halfway round to home

No singing on this release.
