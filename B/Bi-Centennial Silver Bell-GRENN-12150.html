Bi-Centennial Silver Bell

FIGURE (four times through)   

Allemande left with the corner and a balance to your own
Grand right & left around the ring you roam
When you meet your lady, promenade her home
Promenade your pretty Silver Bell
First couple right and circle four hands round
Ladies star by the right, men will walk around
Turn your partner over , shoulder to shoulder
Promenade your pretty Silver Bell
Both couples right and circle six around
Ladies to the center, men walk around the town
Turn your partner over , shoulder to shoulder
Promenade your pretty Silver Bell
All join hands and circle , on the heel and toe
Ladies to the center, men go , man, go
Turn your partner over , shoulder to shoulder
Promenade your pretty Silver Bell

Swing with your pretty Silver Bell

This is a traditional dance with a modern twist that must be taught before calling! It is a progressive visiting couple dance that starts out with only two couples, then three couples, then all four couples. Each time the ladies make a right hand star, the men turn away to their left in a little circle making sure that they come back to the same spot where their partner started the star from. They will curtsy turn their own partner, keeping their arms around her they will put their left shoulders together and promenade home. As more girls are included in the star, the men will be able to make their own circle progressively larger each time. The whole idea of the dance is the timing that the men use always just getting back to curtsy turn their own partner at the precise time! Dance goes through four times. Repeat the dance each time using new couple in rotation to start the entire dance routine.
