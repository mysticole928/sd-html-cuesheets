Banjo Picker's Ball    (FTC 32033)   Ed Fraidenburg

BREAK



4 ladies promenade inside the ring,
get home and swing, everybody swing
Join hands and circle, go walkin' tonight
Allemande your corner, turn partner by the right
4 men star by the left hand, one time and then
Turn thru the partner, and go left allemande
Grand right & left now, go movin' round the land
When you meet you promenade go hand in hand
Promenade your lady go round the hall
To the banjo pickers ball

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands you go
Dosado with the corner you know
Make a wave, ladies trade, swing thru and go
Boys run right, and then couples circulate my friend
Tag the line all the way thru, face to the left you do
Boys trade, boys run, recycle too
Swing the corner girl around and go left allemande
Grand right & left now movin' round the land
When you meet the lady promenade the hall
To the banjo pickers ball
