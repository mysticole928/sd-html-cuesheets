By The Sea   (Grenn 12248 )   Jim Cargill

BREAK



Four ladies chain turn the girl you know
Chain the ladies back, start a do paso
Partner left, corner right, partner left
And then men back up allemande thar
Walk backwards around the ring
Slip the clutch, left allemande
Grand right and left
When you meet your lady take her to a place that's shady
Down by, by the beautiful sea

FIGURE



Head couples square thru four hands you go
Dosado the corner, once around you know
Swing thru 2 by 2, boys run and then
Couples circulate around
Wheel and deal and face those two
Dive thru, pass thru, swing the corner maid
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Promenade this brand new lady
Take her to a place that's shady
Down by, by the beautiful sea

SEQUENCE: Figure twice for heads, Break, Figure twice for sides, Break
