Bayou Baby    (RA 1334)   Bill Wilson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around that corner, see saw round your taw
4 ladies chain, straight across that hall
Chain the ladies home, swing the girls around
Sides face, grand square that hall, go
Oh bayou, my baby on the bayou tonight
To the katydids serenade, my cherie-oh sleep tonight
Reverse
And dream of tomorrow when the fishin' is through
I'll fly o'er that bayou to you

Swing the corner lady, promenade and hold her tight
We'll dream of tomorrow, when the fishin' is through
