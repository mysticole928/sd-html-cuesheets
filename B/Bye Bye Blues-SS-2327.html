Bye Bye Blues    (SS 2327)   Bill Saunders

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All join hands and circle left, you go walkin' hand in hand
You do an allemande left your corner, and you weave around that land
In and out, out and in, smile a bit, in ain't no sin
When you meet, a dosado, swing and whirl the girl you know
Gents star left in the middle and you roll it round the land
A right hand turn your partner, and the corners allemande
Promenade along, go walkin' right on home
Singin' bye, bye blues

FIGURE



Chain 4 ladies cross the ring, and couples 1 & 3
Star thru, pass thru, you dosado for me
All the way round, an ocean wave, all 8 circulate
Balance and you make that wave, all 8 circulate
Do a right & left thru and turn your girl, and then you dive thru
Pass thru, swing and whirl the girl that's facin' you
Promenade along, go walkin' right on home
Singin' bye, bye blues
