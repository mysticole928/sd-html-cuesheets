Black And White

OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face , everybody grand square
The ink is black, the page is white
Together they learn to read and write
A child is black, a child is white
The whole world looks upon the sight
Allemande your corner and weave
And now the child can understand
Swing your girl, promenade hand in hand
Through all the land

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

The heads square thru four hands from right where you are
You meet the sides and make a right hand star
Heads star left and roll it you do
With the outside pair, do the right & left thru
Step to a wave , swing thru two times
Two times through, swing and you whirl with your girl
You keep her close, promenade the world
Through all the land
