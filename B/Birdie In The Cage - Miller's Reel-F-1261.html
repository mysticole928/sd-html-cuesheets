Birdie In The Cage / Miller's Reel
(Folkraft 1261) Frank Kaltman
OPENER

Dosado your corner (back-to-back 4 counts)
Swing partner (8 counts)
Promenade home (with partner)

FIGURE

First lady right-hand swing (with R-H man)
Left-hand swing (with partner)
Right-hand swing (with opposite man)
Left-hand swing (with partner)
Right-hand swing (with L-H man)
Left-hand swing (with partner)

First lady, "the birdie", dance in the middle
(others circle left around her - 4 counts)
First lady join the ring (change places with partner, "the crow")
"Crow" dances in the middle (4 counts)
"Crow" returns and all circle left (4 counts)
Allemande left with corner
Grand right & left, promenade home

(Repeat with 2nd, 3rd, and 4th lady leading in turn)

CLOSER

Circle left halfway round
Circle right to place
Dosado with corner
Swing partner

TAG

Bow to partner
