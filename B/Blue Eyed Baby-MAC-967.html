Blue Eyed Baby
(MacGregor 967) Don Stewart
FIGURE

1st and 3rd go forward and back , then do a 1/2 square thru
Right & left thru the outside two, then turn the girls you do
You dive thru , pass thru , split 2 round 1
And square thru 4 hands around in the middle you run
Go right & left thru the outside two, turn the girls and then
Dive thru , square thru 3/4 around the ring
Allemande the corner and dosado your own
Swing that corner lady, then promenade her home
Promenade your lady right on home and swing
That big blue eyed baby of mine

BREAK

You allemande your corner, go home and you swing
Then join hands , circle left , you circle around the ring
You whirl away a 1/2 sashay , then circle left again
Head 2 gents with a brand new girl , dance into the middle and back
Pass thru and separate and box the flea with the one you meet
Everybody left allemande , a grand ole right & left
When you meet your baby, do a little dosado
Same girl you promenade right on home you know
Promenade your lady right on home and swing
That big blue eyed baby of mine
