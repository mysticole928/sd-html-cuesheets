Blue Moon Of Kentucky   (Blue Star 1719 )   Marshall Flippo

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain, turn 'em left around
Chain those ladies back across the ring
Join all of your hands and circle left around
Left allemande and weave around the ring
Now on this moonlit night with stars shining bright
Dosado that maid, take her home in a promenade
Blue moon of Kentucky keep on shining
Shine on the one that's gone and made me blue

FIGURE



Head 2 square thru 4 hands around
With the sides you make a right hand star
Head 2 star left and turn it once around
Pick up the corner one from where you are
Back out and make a ring, circle left and then
Fold that pretty maid, star thru then promenade
Blue moon of Kentucky keep on shining
Shine on the one that's gone and been untrue
