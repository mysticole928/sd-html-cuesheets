Boogie Grass Band    (D&R 137)   Dale Dockery

OPENER



Circle left
There's a new kind of music going round
It's known as the boogie grass sound
You walk around your corner Sue, see saw your own
Left allemande and weave the ring
I love to hear Bill sing about Kentucky
Dosado that girl and promenade
We put the music all together
So dance to that boogie grass band

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade go halfway around
Come down the middle, star thru and then pass thru
You veer to the left, ferris wheel, centers zoom
New centers pass thru, star thru
You square thru 3 hands to the corner lady, swing
Swing that girl around and promenade
We put the music all together
So dance to that boogie grass band

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies promenade go one time around
Get on home, swing and whirl your man
Join all your hands, circle left go round the ring
Left allemande and weave the ring
I love to hear Bill sing about Kentucky
Dosado that girl and promenade
We put the music all together
So dance to that boogie grass band

CLOSER



Well my daddy played the banjo and sawed the fiddle, too
Taught me how to sing and play for you
Walk around your corner, see saw your own
Left allemande and weave the ring
I love to hear Bill sing about Kentucky
Dosado that girl and promenade
We put the music all together
So dance to that boogie grass band
