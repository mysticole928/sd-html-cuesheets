Blue Eyed Blond    (TRR 107)   Gerald McWhirter

OPENER



Walk around the corner, see saw your own
Then men star right go walking round
Put an arm around your partner, star promenade along
Girls roll back, left allemande, weave the ring
You weave it in and out until you meet your own
Swing her there and promenade
Promenade along and sing a happy song
Swing with your pretty blue eyed blond

FIGURE



1 & 3 square thru 4 hands, all the way
To the corner dosado to an ocean wave
Linear cycle, (single hinge, fold and follow, peel to the right)
Right & left thru, turn your girl
Slide thru, pass to the center
Curlique, box circulate two spots
Swing the corner, promenade
Promenade along and sing a happy song
Swing with your pretty blue eyed blond

ALTERNATE FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway, 2 & 4 square thru 4 hands
All the way, right & left thru, turn your girl
Veer left, ferris wheel
Right & left thru, double pass thru, track II
Swing the corner, promenade
Promenade along and sing a happy song
Swing with your pretty blue eyed blond

Come on home and swing with your man

Gonna weave it in and out until you meet your own
