BRANDY ESP 1171 Elmer Sheffield


Sides Face Grand Square:

There’s a port on a western bay

And it serves a hundred ships a day

Lonely sailors pass the time away

As they talk about their home

Allemande and weave

Oh Brandy, you’re a fine girl

What a good wife you would be

But, my life, my love, and my lady is the sea.


Figure:

Heads square thru, travel 4 hands around

All the way…… Touch ¼ and then

Follow your neighbor and Spred

Fan the top, Boys move up and a right and left thru

Girls lead Dixie style

Trade the wave, swing and promenade

But my life, my love, and my lady is the sea.


Break:

4 ladies chain, turn your girl and then,

You rollaway and circle left and then

You rollaway, again

Left allemande and weave

Oh Brandy, you’re a fine girl

What a good wife you would be

But, my life, my love, and my lady is the sea.


Ending:

Sides Face Grand Square

There’s a girl in this harbor town

And she works, laying whiskey down.

They say Brandy, fetch another round,

She serves them whiskey and wine.

Allemande and weave

Oh Brandy, you’re a fine girl

What a good wife you would be

But, my life, my love, and my lady is the sea.
