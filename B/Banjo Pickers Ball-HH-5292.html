Banjo Pickers Ball    (HH 5292)  Tac Ozaki

OPENER - CLOSER



4 Ladies promenade go walking inside
Back home and Swing - Swing your partner round
Join hands make a big ring and Circle to the left you go
Allemande Left your corner, Weave The Ring you know
There'll be a band strum -min' rag -time all night
Swing your honey babe, oh what a sight
Promenade your lady go walking round the hall
To the Banjo Pickers Ball

FIGURE



Heads Square Thru go four hands inside
You make a Right Hand Star
The Heads Star by the Left, go walking inside
When a Right and Left Thru turn the girl outside
Dive Thru, Square Thru 3 hand in style
Swing your corner, Promenade awhile
Promenade your lady go walking round the hall
To the Banjo Pickers Ball
