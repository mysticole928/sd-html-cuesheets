Brown Eyed Mary
(Old Timer 8051 side B ) Johnny Melton , Gen Melton
POSITION

All couples in promenade position facing counter-clockwise around the hall,
gents on the inside, ladies on the outside, holding hands.

FIGURE

Swing your partner halfway round
(Gent swings his partner a half turn with a right hand swing and faces the lady
in the couple behind him)
Swing your corner lady
(Gent swings his corner lady, whom he is now facing, with a left-hand swing
a full turn.)
Swing your partner all the way around
(Gent swings his partner a full turn around with a right-hand swing and
continues forward, toward the lady of the couple in front of him.)
And promenade the forward lady
(Gent meets the lady in the forward couple with his left hand and swings her
into promenade position.)

(Repeat dance with new partner each time)
