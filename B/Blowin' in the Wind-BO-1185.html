Blowin' In The Wind    (BO 1185)   Cal Lambert

BREAK



Join hands and circle to the left around that ring
4 ladies roll away and then you swing
Promenade and don't slow down, just keep on moving round
1 & 3 wheel around and square thru
Count 4 hands around, then you pull the partner by
Left allemande, come back and swing your own
Promenade around that ring, oh where can you swing
Well the answer is blowing in the wind

FIGURE



1 & 3 go right & left thru with a full turn around
Separate, behind the sides you stand
You'll do a double pass thru, all 4 couples frontier whirl
The center 4 square thru 3/4 round go
Then you allemande left your corner, dosado your own
Swing that corner girl around and then, promenade
Promenade around that ring, oh where can you swing
Well the answer is blowing in the wind
