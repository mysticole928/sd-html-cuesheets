Back In The Saddle Again
Gene Autry 1939
   (Quadrille 871)    Lee Main , Ken Burke , Richard Lane
 
OPENER    Basic - SSD Week 1

Circle left
Back in the saddle again
out where a friend is a friend
Left allemande you know, come back and dosado
Left allemande , weave the ring
Ridin' the range once more
Totin' my old forty four
Where we sleep out every night and the only law is right
Back in the saddle again

FIGURE    Basic - SSD Week 8, corner progression

Head (side) two couples promenade halfway
Walk in and right & left thru , I say
Flutter wheel across the floor, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and then a right & left thru
Swing thru and then swing thru again
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Where we sleep out every night and the only law is right
Back in the saddle again

MIDDLE BREAK    Basic - SSD Week 1

Four ladies chain across that ring
Chain your lady right on home , I sing
All join hands and circle left
Allemande the corner , weave the ring
Ridin' the range once more
Totin' my old forty four
Where we sleep out every night and the only law is right
Back in the saddle again

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
Back in the saddle again
Out where a friend is a friend
Where the longhorn cattle feed on the lowly jimson weed
Back in the saddle again
Circle left
Ridin' the range once more
Swing the girl around and promenade
Where we sleep out every night and the only law is right
Back in the saddle again
