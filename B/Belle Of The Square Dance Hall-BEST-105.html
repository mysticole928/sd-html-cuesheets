Belle Of The Square Dance Hall
(Best 105) John Garrett
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join your hands and start a circle , circle left around that town
Gents star right , go once around that ring
Corners all left allemande , grand ole right & left around
Meet your honey and you promenade that town
1 & 3 wheel around , left square thru with the one you found
Go all the way, do that ole left allemande
Come back one and promenade , take her home and swing and sway
With the belle of the square dance hall tonight

FIGURE

Heads to the middle and back with you
Then lead to the right , a right & left thru *
New head couples trail thru , around just one to a line of four
Pass thru across the world, arch in the middle and dixie twirl
And swing that corner lady round and round
Allemande left new corner lady, turn a right hand there at home
Allemande left just once again, and walk right by your own
Promenade the next one round, take her home and settle down
With the belle of the square dance hall tonight

(*Explanation: On completion of the right & left thru, the alternate couples are in the position of the original working couples, facing the middle of the square.)

Figure, second line, wants (from a Squared Set) Heads with the couple on their right, do a Right & Left Thru, squaring sets that way (i.e., Couples #1 and #2 Right & Left Thru, as Couples #3 and #4 Right & Left Thru, then all Square Your Sets).
