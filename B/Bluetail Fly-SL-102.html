Bluetail Fly    (SL 102)   Melton Luttrell

INTRO



Honor your partner and your corner too
Sashay your partner halfway through
Bow to the girl to the right of you
And resashay, that's what you do
Allemande left with your left hand
A right to your honey, go right & left grand
Every other girl and every other guy
Promenade home with the bluetail fly

CHORUS



Jimmy cracked corn and I don't care
Jimmy cracked corn and I don't care
Swing your little honey when you get there

FIGURE



1st and 3rd bow and swing
Lead right out to the right of the ring
*Lady round the lady and the gent follow
Ladies hook in the middle with a right elbow
Gent around the gent with the lady in tow
Gents hook in the middle with a left elbow
Pick up the girl you call your own
Star promenade till you get back home
Spread that star across the set
Gents back out the girls chain left
Turn her boys you're not through yet
Promenade around with a brand new pet

REPEAT CHORUS



MIDDLE BREAK

Side two couples go right & left thru



Split the sides and separate
And everybody swing your date
Allemande left with your left hand
A right to your honey go right & left grand
Every other girl and every other guy
Promenade home with the bluetail fly

REPEAT CHORUS



ENDING

Honor your partner and your corner miss



Wave at the girl on the old right wall
And swing your little honey cause that's not all
Allemande left with your left hand
A right to your honey go right & left grand
Every other girl and every other guy
Promenade home with the bluetail fly

REPEAT CHORUS



(*Notes on figure: The gent follows his partner through the side couples and around the lady; ladies elbow swing in center and then follow partner through same couple and around gent. Girls stay home as gents turn with a left elbow once in the center.)
