Bringing In The Sheaves
Tennessee Ernie Ford; many others 1874
   (Double M 129)    W.J. "Jay" Morgan
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Sowing the morning, sowing seeds of kindness
Sowing in the noon-tide and the dewy eve
Waiting for the harvest and the time of reaping
We shall come rejoicing bringing in the sheaves
Four girls promenade one time around
Swing your man and promenade the ring
Bringing in the sheaves, bringing in the sheaves
We shall come rejoicing bringing in the sheaves

FIGURE    B - SSD Week 10, corner progression

Heads promenade halfway around you go
Walk in and square thru four hands you know
All the way around and do the right & left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel around you go
Square thru and go 3/4 around and then
Swing your corner girl around and promenade the ring
Bringing in the sheaves, bringing in the sheaves
We shall come rejoicing bringing in the sheaves

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Four girls (boys) promenade one time around
Back home and swing , everybody swing
Join hands and circle to the left around you go
Allemande left your corner and weave around the ring
Bringing in the sheaves, bringing in the sheaves
Swing your girl around and promenade the ring
Bringing in the sheaves, bringing in the sheaves
We shall come rejoicing bringing in the sheaves
