Battle Hymn Of The Republic   (Circle D 270 )   Dean Crowell

OPENER - CLOSER



Sides face grand square
Mine eyes have seen the glory of the coming of the Lord
He has trampled out the vintage where the grapes of wrath are stored
Allemande the corner, do a dosado and then
Allemande the corner, weave the ring
Glory, glory, Hallelujah
(Swing the girl and promenade her)
Glory, glory, Hallelujah
His truth is marching on

FIGURE



Heads promenade go halfway around
Down the middle square thru go 4 hands around
Go all the way and do the right & left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel you do
Square thru and count 'em three hands around
Swing the corner lady and promenade on down
Glory, glory, Hallelujah
His truth is marching on
