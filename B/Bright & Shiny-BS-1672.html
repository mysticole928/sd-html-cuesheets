Bright & Shiny    (BS 1672)   Marshall Flippo

FIGURE



Head couples promenade halfway around
Go right & left thru, turn them around
Pass thru you two, u-turn back and star thru
Left allemande then dosado
Those girls promenade go once around that ring
And dosado, you walk around your own
Those gents star left go once around that ring
And swing twice around with your own
Left allemande, dosado round your own
You swing that corner, promenade her home
Bright and shiny, bright and shiny
Bright and shiny is our love

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain now go cross the floor
You'll chain 'em back, turn 'em left once more
Walk around your corner, see saw your partner
Left allemande, do an allemande thar
Go right & left, gents back up and make that star
You back that old star around the ring
Those gents fold, single file behind that lady
Gents roll out, weave by one, left allemande and swing
You swing that lady, left allemande
You dosado her, promenade again
Bright and shiny, bright and shiny
Bright and shiny is our love
