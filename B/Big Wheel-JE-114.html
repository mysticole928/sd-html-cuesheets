Big Wheel    (J 114)   Jewel O'Brien

OPENER



Join hands circle going somewhere
Left allemande, come on back dosado, then you swing
4 men star by the left, go once around the set
Take your own arm around, star promenade your pet
Oh, this star am a rolling going somewhere
1 & 3 wheel around, dosado, right & left thru
Cross trail to the corners, allemande corners all
Grand ole right & left, go walking around the hall
There's a big wheel a rolling going somewhere
Promenade take her home, then you swing, yes you swing

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway now
Lead to the right, go right & left thru, turn your girls
Join 4 hands, circle
Sides break to a line, go forward up and back
Bend the line, star thru, eight chain thru the track
Oh, this big wheel am a rolling going somewhere
Go all the way over, come on back till you're through
Then square thru 3/4
3/4 round, do allemande corners there
Dosado your own, promenade that corner fair
Oh, big wheel am a rolling going somewhere
And it's carrying my baby back home where you'll swing

TAG



Yes, she'll swing, yes you swing
