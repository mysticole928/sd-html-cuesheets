Old Fashioned Love
(Hi Hat 5011) Jerry Schatzer
OPENER

4 ladies chain about 3/4 round the ring
Roll away , join your hands , circle to the left I sing
I got an old time love, deep down in my soul
Allemande on the corner lady and a weave that ring there beau
Gimme that old fashioned love, keep it sweet and sunny
Swing and whirl with your lady fair, and promenade your honey
You're the sunlight in my sky, I'm an old fashioned guy
Gimme that old fashioned love anytime

FIGURE (Mainstream)

Well the heads promenade outside halfway round the ring
Down the middle , a square thru 4 hands I sing
Dance all the way, and double swing thru I say
You do it once, now do it again, rock on the ocean wave
All 8 circulate , swing on the corner waitin' there
Allemande left new corner lady, and you promenade the square
You're an old fashioned gal, I'm an old fashioned pal
Gimme that old fashioned love anytime

FIGURE (Plus 1)

Head couples square thru 4 hands'll do
Meet with the corner lady, slide thru and pass thru
You do a partner trade , reverse the flutter wheel I say
Now send 'em across a dixie style , you make a little ocean wave
You rock it once, trade the wave and the corner swing

MIDDLE BREAK

Circle to the left
She says "I love you" with her sweet good morning smile
We still hold hands on the front porch, 'cause it never goes outta style
Allemande on the corner lady, and box the gnat at home
Now the 4 little ladies promenade a one time round you go
Turn your partner right a full turn my friend
Allemande left , you run back one, and you promenade again

FIGURE (Plus 2)

Well sides promenade outside halfway round the ring
Lead out to the right and circle up 4 and you make a little line I sing
Dance up to the middle and back , then a right & left thru
Now load the boat (toot, toot) till you meet that two
When you meet her over there square thru 3/4 round
Swing and whirl on the corner girl, and you promenade the town

CLOSER

Circle to the left
We take a picnic in the sand by the sea
I'm a lucky fella 'cause she belongs to me
Allemande on the corner lady, and turn your partner right
Full turn this time, and the 4 little boys promenade tonight
One time around, turn thru when you come back home
Allemande left on the corner lady, and promenade your own
