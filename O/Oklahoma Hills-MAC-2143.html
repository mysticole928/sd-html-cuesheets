Oklahoma Hills   (Mac Gregor 2143 )   Monty Wilson

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



All 4 ladies chain, straight across that ring
Join hands, circle to the left around
Allemande left & allemande thar, go right & left, then star
The men back in, you back that star on down
Shoot that star, touch 1/4, men run to the right you do
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Way down yonder in the Indian nation
I rode my pony on the reservation
The Oklahoma hills where I was born

FIGURE



Heads (sides) you promenade, 1/2 way with that maid
Sides, right and left thru in time
You roll with a 1/2 sashay, then pass thru that way
Separate, round one and you make that line
Up to the middle & back, then star thru, pass thru, trade by
Swing that corner lady, promenade
Way down yonder in the Indian nation
I rode my pony on the reservation
The Oklahoma hills where I was born
