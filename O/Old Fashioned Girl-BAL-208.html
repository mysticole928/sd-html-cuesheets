The Quiet Dance / Old Fashioned Girl
   (Balance 208 side B )    Ed Gilmore
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Dosado that corner girl, come back home and swing and whirl
You swing your pretty little taw
Allemande left with your left hand, a right to you own a right & left grand
Around that ring you go
A right hand round your partner , men make a left hand star
Once around that ring turn your partners where they are
Allemande that corner maid, promenade that pretty little maid
That married dear old dad

FIGURE   

Head couples swing your maid, swing her twice and promenade
Halfway round the ring you go
Now do a right & left thru right down the middle
You turn her twice in time with fiddle
Chain to the right *, the right hand ladies chain
All four ladies chain across the way
Chain 'em right on back again, don't you let 'em stay
Then you promenade that pretty little girl
She's just like the girl that married dear old dad

*Repeat figures for heads but have head ladies chain to the left
Middle break
Repeat figure twice for sides. Side ladies chain right first time and to the left second time
Closer
