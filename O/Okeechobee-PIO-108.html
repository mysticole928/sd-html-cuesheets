Okeechobee
   (Pioneer 108)    Jim Rice
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    B - SSD Week 1

Allemande left that corner, come home and dosado
Join 8 hands and circle to the left
All 4 men star by the right go around to the corner then
Allemande left , then do the right & left grand
Down in southern Florida, little town called Okeechobee
Dosado your lady, promenade go home
Promenade your baby, go all the way to Okeechobee
To a little old shack by the lake

FIGURE   

Head 2 go right & left thru , turn your girl and then
Square thru go 4, sides face grand square
Heads will cloverleaf , come around head position
Cross trail and swing that corner girl
Do allemande left your corner, come home and dosado
Allemande left , go back and promenade
Then promenade your baby, go through Okeechobee
To a little old shack by the lake
