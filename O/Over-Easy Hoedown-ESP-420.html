Over-Easy Hoedown
   (ESP 420)    Bill Harrison
Bow to partner and corner, circle to the left, circle left
Walk around the corner, see saw with the partner
Go back to the corner and star thru, circle left
Dopaso, make an allemande thar
Shoot the star, right & left grand
Meet that girl say whoo!
You're home

Heads square thru 4, touch 1/4, scoot back, girls roll
Peel the top, diamond circulate, boys trade the wave
Cut the diamond, linear cycle, move up and back
Pass thru, wheel * deal, zoom, double pass thru
Leads cloverleaf, others step ahead and do a partner trade
You're home

Sides fan the top, ping pong circulate, extend
Explode and touch 1/4, all circulate, each side peel the top
Grand left swing thru, recycle, walk into the middle and back
Pass thru, wheel & deal, centers pass thru
All do the centers part of load the boat, just the ends load the boat
Four in the middle do a partner trade and pass thru
Touch 1/4, follow your neighbor, just the boys spread
All 8 circulate, centers run, have a line, move up and back
Star thru, double pass thru, track 2, all the boys circulate
Everybody 1/2 circulate, everybody hinge and the girls U-turn back
Clap-clap-clap, you're home

Heads square thru but on the 3rd hand, slide thru
Double pass thru, 1/2 zoom, all 8 circulate, cast off 3/4
Acey deucey, ends cross run, move up and back
Single circle to a wave, boys trade, girls fold, peel the top
Linear cycle and roll, coordinate, everyone 1/2 circulate
Bend the line, roll the boy a half sashay
Home
