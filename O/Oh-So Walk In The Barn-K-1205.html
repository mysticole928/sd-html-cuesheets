Oh-So Walk In The Barn
Tommy Collins 1968
   (Kalox 1205)    Harper Smith
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

Oh so walk in the barn, sides face, heads go
Heads part:
Heads pass the ocean, ah so, walk & dodge, California twirl and face (16 counts)
Sides part:
Face and back up 3 steps and turn on #4 to face, forward dosado and star thru (16 counts)
Repeat the above 3 more times ending at home (64 counts as in grand parade and grand spin)

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Heads square thru out in the center
Around corner, dosado
Curlique and then walk & dodge my friend
Partner trade and a right & left thru you know
Pass the ocean , recycle , swing that corner round
Left allemande , come back and promenade
I can hardly wait til we roll up to the gate
Cause we're having a shindig in the barn

MIDDLE BREAK (for those who do not care to use the "Oh So Walk")    Mainstream

4 ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Roll away and then circle left
Left allemande the corner, dosado your partner
Men star left go round the set
Turn thru your partner, corner allemande
Swing your girl and then you promenade
Tell all the folks you can we're gonna clean it spic and span
Yes we're gonna have a shindig in the barn

(Note: when heads complete the pass the ocean and Ah So as they walk & dodge, have the girls dodge and take a step forward out as they dodge to give more working room for new heads. If sides will take full 16 counts to do your part it works very, very well. However, the sides sometimes get in a hurry. Please don't.)
