One Of Those Songs   (Silver Sounds 101 )   Bruce McCue

OPENER



Four ladies promenade, go walkin' inside
Back home and swing your man 'round and 'round
Join all your hands circle left tonight
Left allemande and a right and left grand
Grand right and left, hey
When you meet your own, dosado promenade
Promenade home with the one you adore, singing
One of those wonderful songs

FIGURE



Heads square thru in the middle
Get me four and ya go to the fiddle
Around the corner lady, dosado her and go
Swing thru tonight, boys run to the right
Couples circulate, and wheel and deal tonight
Pull 'em on around, pass thru, trade by and the corner swing
Swing the corner lady, promenade
You promenade home with the one you adore, singing
One of those wonderful songs

BREAK & CLOSER



Do an allemande left for an allemande thar
Go right, go left and you form a star
Men in the middle like an allemande thar
And you roll it down the line
Shoot that star to the heavens whirl
Right and left to the second girl
Men in the middle like an allemande thar
Roll it, roll it, cadillac car
Shoot that star and a right and left grand
And every other girl with every other hand
When you meet your lady, promenade
Promenade home with the one you adore, singing
One of those wonderful songs
