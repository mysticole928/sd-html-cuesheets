Out On The Texas Plains
Hank Snow; Don Edwards; many others 1958
   (Blue Ribbon 239)    Bobby Lepard
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
I want to drink my coffee from an old tin can
When the moon comes shining down
I want to hear the song of a whippoorwill
I want to hear the coyote's howl
Four ladies chain across, you turn that girl
Chain her back , keep her and promenade
You promenade out where the buffalo roam
Out on the Texas plains

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Well the heads promenade go halfway round the ring
Down the middle square thru 4 hands
Four hands around and then, do a right & left thru my friend
Pass thru , trade by and then touch 1/4
Scoot back and then scoot back again
Star thru and then you promenade
Promenade home out where the buffalo roam
Out on the Texas plains

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

Do an allemande left like an allemande thar , go right & left and star
Men wheel in, make an allemande thar, back it on up and then
Shoot that star go forward two , go right & left, do an allemande thar
Men wheel in, make an allemande thar, back it on up and then
Shoot that star and a right & left grand
Hand over hand go round, meet a little girl and then gonna
Promenade all the way around, promenade out where the buffalo roam
Out on the Texas plains
