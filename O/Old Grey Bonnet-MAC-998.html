Old Grey Bonnet    (MAC 998)   Fenton Jones

INTRO



Allemande left your corner
Dosado your partner
Promenade your lady hear me say
Through the fields of clover
We'll drive up to Dover
On our golden wedding day

FIGURE



1 & 3 you'll promenade halfway round there with your maid
Lead to the right, a right & left thru and there, turn the girls
Star thru, circle up 4, one full turn, the same two ladies chain
The inside arch, the outside under
Square thru, you go like thunder
4 hands round, separate you do,
Around one come back to the middle,
star by the right
Find your corner, swing all night
Swing, hey everybody swing
Join hands, circle left
Allemande left that corner pet
Dosado your own, promenade, everybody sing
Through the fields of clover
We'll drive up to Dover
On our golden wedding day
