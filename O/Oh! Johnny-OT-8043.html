Oh! Johnny
Ella Fitzgerald 1917
(Old Timer 8043 side B )
FIGURE (four times through)

All join hands and you circle to the right
Stop where you are and swing that girlie goodnight
Swing that little girl right there behind you
Go back home , everybody swing and everybody dance
Allemande left with your left hand
And dosi round your own
All promenade with your sweet corner maid
Singing Oh, Johnny, Oh, Johnny, Oh!

BREAK

All join hands and circle to the call
Now take your own and swing at the wall
All around your corner lady
See saw round your pretty little taw
Allemande left and allemande right
Allemande left again
Now you all take your own and run away home
Oh, Johnnie, Oh, Johnnie, Oh
