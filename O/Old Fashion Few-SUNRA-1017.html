Old Fashion Few    (SUNRA 1017)   Whitey Aamot



Four ladies promenade once around that ring



Join your hands, circle to the left and go
Left allemande the corner and weave around the ring
So long to the simple American life
Swing and twirl when you meet your girl and promenade her home
Take off your hat if you still have one on
Let's give a cheer to the old fashioned few

FIGURE



Head (side) couples promenade halfway around the square
Go down the middle, square thru 4 hands around from there
Four hands and then, swing thru the outside two
Boys run to the right and ferris wheel you do
The centers pass thru, square thru and you go 3
Trade by, the corner swing, promenade for me
Here's to the memory of the old general store
Things to remember they're not here any more
