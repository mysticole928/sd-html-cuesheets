Oklahoma Morning    (PP 116)   Toots Richardson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Heads star the route*
Oh there's nothing like an Oklahoma morning
Nothing like it when you're feeling low
Oh it warms up your mind and your body
It puts Oklahoma sunshine in your soul
Circle left
There's nothing like an Oklahoma morning
Left allemande, come back and promenade
It makes the problems of the day seem unimportant
It puts Oklahoma sunshine in your soul

FIGURE



And now the heads square thru and count to 4 now
Around the corner lady dosado
And now you swing thru and then the boys run right
You tag the line, face right, do a wheel & deal
You turn thru and go left allemande new corner girl
Come back, swing and all promenade
Oh it warms up your mind and your body
It puts Oklahoma sunshine in your soul

(*Star the route: From a squared set Heads star right 3/4, left hand star a full turn with their right hand couple, Heads to the middle for a right hand star 1/2 to left hand star with their original left hand couple a full turn to do as per call.)
