Open For Business    (SS 2343)   Cal Brazier

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Dosado the corner, see saw round your own
Men star right in the middle of the ring, all the way around you roam
Allemande left the corner, turn your partner by the right
Make a wrong way thar and the men back down the line
Now shoot that star, do an allemande left and a grand old right & left
When you meet your lady, promenade her
Promenade your lady, go walking through the park
I'm open for business as usual in my heart

FIGURE



Well the head two gents and the corner, go into the middle and back
Square thru in the middle, count 4 hands there, Jack
Now split two, make a line of 4, go up to the middle and back
Star thru and square thru 3/4
Allemande left your corner and dosado your own
Swing the corner girl and promenade her
Promenade this lady, she's your new sweetheart
I'm open for business as usual in my heart
