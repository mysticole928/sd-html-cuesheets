Oktoberfest Is Here!
   (Omar 1)    Corben Geis
 
OPENER - CLOSER

Sides face grand square
It's that time of year again!
Oktoberfest is here again!
Bring your family and your friends
And join in all the fun!
Circle left
Stand and sing 'Ein Prosit'
Left allemande and weave the ring
Stand and sing 'Ein Prosit'
Oktoberfest is here!

FIGURE

Head (Side) couples promenade travel halfway around
Down the middle, square thru go all the way and then
Swing thru my friend, spin the top and right & left thru
Slide thru, pass thru and trade by
Swing your corner lady round and promenade
Stand and sing 'Ein Prosit'
Oktoberfest is here!

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face grand square
Fill your steins with German beer
And raise them high and give a cheer!
Sing it out for all to hear
And drink until it's gone!
Four ladies polka promenade inside the ring
Swing and then you promenade
Stand and sing 'Ein Prosit'
Oktoberfest is here!
