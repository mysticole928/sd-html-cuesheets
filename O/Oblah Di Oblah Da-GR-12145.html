Oblah-di Oblah-da   (Grenn 12145 )   Dick Leger

BREAK



Alemande left with an allemande thar, go forward two my friend
Go right and left and the men hang on, make a back up star and then
Shoot that star a full turn around, with the corner box the gnat
Same little lady dosado, then weave the ring like that
Sing, Oblah dee, oblah da, life goes on, hey
Dosado around and promenade her home
Sing, Oblah dee, oblah da, life goes on, hey
Take that lady home and don'y you roam

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands,in the market place
When you meet your corner lady swing
Join your hands and circle, all around the place
Then you allemande left new corner, weave the ring
Oblah dee, oblah da, life goes on, hey
Dosado around and promenade her home
Oblah dee, oblah da, life goes on, hey
Take that lady home and don'y you roam
