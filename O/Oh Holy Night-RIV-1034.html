Oh Holy Night
   (Riverboat 1034)    Brent Mawdsley , Krista Baird , Nils Trottmann , Jack O'Leary , Kumi Takahashi , Hanna Tenenbaum
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
O Holy Night! The stars are brightly shining
It is the night of our dear Savior's birth
Allemande left and weave
Long lay the world in sin and error pining
Till He appeared and the soul felt its worth

ALTERNATE LYRICS

Fall on your knees! Oh, hear the angel voices!
O night divine, oh night...

Fall on your knees! Oh, hear the angel voices!
O night divine, oh night when Christ was born

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) square thru go 4 ,
You make a right hand star
The heads (sides) star left, one time around, to the corner go
Slide thru , square thru 3 hands
Swing and promenade
Let all within us praise His holy name
