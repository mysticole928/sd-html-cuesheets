Once In A While    (CK 132)   Ken Bower

OPENER



Four ladies chain you turn 'em
Chain 'em go back again
You circle left my friend
Thinkin' of you
Allemande left and weave the ring
You know I'll get by just as long as I
Swing with my girl and promenade
And knowing you think of me
Once in a while

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru go four hands
You meet the sides swing thru and then
Boys run right, you ferris wheel and now
Centers pass thru, make a right hand star and roll it too
One full turn, girls turn back and you swing and all promenade
Knowing you think of me
Once in a while
