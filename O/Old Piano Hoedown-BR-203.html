Old Piano Hoedown
(Blue Ribbon 203) Roger Morris
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join hands and circle , go walkin' round the ring, then
Left allemande and then promenade
Head 2 couples wheel around , a right & left thru you know
Pass thru , partner trade , slide thru you know
Square thru 3/4 , go left allemande , then
Dosado your girl and promenade
Walk around the ring to that old piano hoedown
Oh what a melody

FIGURE

Heads lead to the right , and there you circle, make a line
Dance up to the middle and back ,
pass thru , wheel & deal
Double pass thru ,
first go left, next go right
Square thru you do
Square thru 4 hands, swing that corner lady
Allemande left , come back and promenade
Walk around the ring to that old piano hoedown
