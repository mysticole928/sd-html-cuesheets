One Pair Of Hands
(Royal 1010X) Noah Siegmann
OPENER

Circle left
One pair of hands form the mountains
One pair of hands form the sea
Men star right one time
Left allemande and weave the ring
One pair of hands form the valleys
The oceans, the rivers, the sand
Those hands are so strong, so when life goes wrong
Put your faith into one pair of hands

FIGURE

Heads square thru
Right & left thru and turn
Slide thru , pass the ocean
Scoot back , boys cross run
Chain down the line , slide thru
Swing the corner lady round and promenade
Those hands are so strong, so when life goes wrong
Put your faith into one pair of hands

MIDDLE BREAK

Four ladies chain 3/4
You rollaway , circle left
One pair of hands made the sun and the moon
Left allemande and weave the ring
One pair of hands form the valleys
The oceans, the rivers, the sand
Those hands are so strong, so when life goes wrong
Put your faith into one pair of hands

CLOSER

Sides face grand square
One pair of hands healed the sick
One pair of hands raised the dead
One pair of hands calmed the raging storm
Thousands of people were fed , Allemande and weave
One pair of hands said I love you
And those hands were nailed to a tree
Those hands are so strong, so when life goes wrong
Put your faith into one pair of hands
