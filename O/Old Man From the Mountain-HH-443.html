Old Man From The Mountain    (HH 443)   Dick Houlton

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain 3/4, turn the girl and then
All 4 ladies chain, go straight across my friend
All 8 flutterwheel, go movin' 2 by 2
Join hands and circle left you do
Roll away & weave the ring, it's in & out you go
When you meet, do a dosado & promenade her home
Promenade your lady, around the ring you roam
Cause the old man from the mountain's headin' home

FIGURE



Now 1 & 3 (2 & 4) right & left thru, turn the girl so fine
Flutterwheel, sweep 1/4, move around in time
Pass thru, make a left hand star, once around will do
Head ladies center, flutterwheel, head boys get in there too
When you get there, swing thru, men turn thru
Swing the corner round & promenade her too
Promenade your lady around the ring you roam
Cause the old man from the mountain's comin' home
