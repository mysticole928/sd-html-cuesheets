Heads pass thru , separate round one, got a little line
Lines pass thru , tag the line in
Center four right & left thru , turn the girls
Same ladies chain
Send 'em back dixie style got your wave go
Everybody pass thru , swing your corner there, promenade
It warms up your mind and your body
It puts Oklahoma sunshine in your soul

FIGURE (Plus)    Plus, corner progression

Sides pass thru , separate round one, got your line
Lines pass thru , half tag the line
Single hinge , split circulate
Boys run , pass the ocean
Trade the wave , recycle go now
Swing your corner, promenade
It warms up your mind and your body
It puts Oklahoma sunshine in your soul

Figure contains a Pass Thru from a L-H Wave, an illegal move. The term "Step Thru" is more appropriate. But, since everyone is active, perhaps a directional term such as "all pass by one" would be better?
