One Night Stands    (CBC 533)   Jim Congleton

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies promenade go once inside the ring
Get back home and swing the man you know
Join up all your hands, circle left around the town
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
Move in and out around until you meet her
Dosado and you give that girl a swing
Promenade her then, walk on around my friend
Putting the miles behind me, playing one night stands

FIGURE



Those heads square thru 4 hands you go
Get around the corner, do a little dosado
Swing thru tonight, those boys run to the right
Couples circulate one time and go, wheel & deal
You veer to the left and couples ferris wheel
Centers square thru 3/4 round
Swing that corner girl, promenade go round the world
Putting the miles behind me, playing one night stands

TAG



Just calling old square dances, doing the best I can
Putting the miles behind me, playing one night stands
