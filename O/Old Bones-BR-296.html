Old Bones    (BR 296)   Bill Donahue

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
Old bones inside an old raincoat
Old bones, inside these old shoes
Four ladies chain over and back
I dream that I'm a man much younger than I am
Promenade
I guess by now you think I really don't care

FIGURE



Heads promenade half
But I love life, I'd like to do it again
Lead right and dosado make a wave
Swing thru, men run right, tag the line
Lady go left, gent go right, the corner swing, promenade along
Oh yeah, I'd like to do it again

MIDDLE BREAK



Head ladies center teacup chain
It's time for taking it easy
And it's time for taking it slow
4 ladies chain over and back
These bones don't move so fast
LIke they did once in the past
Promenade
Now if I have to run I simply don't go

PLUS FIGURE



Now the heads pass thru, do a partner trade
Those men reverse your flutter wheel
Same two sweep 1/4 more
A double pass thru, track two and the ladies trade
Swing thru, all 8 circulate
Swing corner, promenade along
Oh yeah, I'd like to do it again

CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
But I love life, I'd like to do it again
Though I might not be much more than I've ever been

Just to have the chance to turn back the hands
And let my life begin
Promenade
Oh yeah, I'd like to do it again
