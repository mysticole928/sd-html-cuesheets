Old Side Of Town   (Blue Star 2108 )   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain go cross that old ring
Join hands, circle left around you go
Do an allemande left in the alamo style and balance
All 8 walk and dodge you know (boys run)
Left allemande that corner, swing your own
Left allemande, go back and promenade
Oh we miss you on the old side of town

FIGURE



The heads (sides) square thru 4 hands around you travel
With that corner lady, do a dosado
You swing thru tonite, let the boys run right
Tag the line and cloverleaf you know
Those girls will zoom, boys left turn thru
Girls sashay, swing the corner, promenade
Oh we miss you on the old side of town


ALTERNATE FIGURE

The heads (sides) square thru 4 hands around you travel
With that corner lady, do a dosado
You swing thru tonite, let the boys run right
Tag the line and cloverleaf you know
Girls square thru 3/4
Swing the corner and promenade
Oh we miss you on the old side of town

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Heads square thru 4 hands you travel
Round the corner, do a dosado
Swing thru tonite, boys run right
Ferris wheel, move it round you do (right & left thru)
Square thru 3/4 round
Swing with the corner and promenade
Oh we miss you on the old side of town

ALTERNATE BREAK

4 ladies chain go cross that old ring
Join hands, circle left
Ladies in, men sashay, circle
Ladies in, men sashay, circle
Allemande left, grand right and left
Promenade
Oh we miss you on the old side of town
