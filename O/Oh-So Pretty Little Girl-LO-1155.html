Ah-So Pretty Little Girl    (LO 1155)   Harold Bausch

PART "A"



4 little ladies promenade, it's once around the ring I say
Right back home and do a dosado
Join up hands a great big ring, circle to the left around I sing
Circle to the left and don't you dare be slow
Ladies center men sashay, circle to the left you're on your way
Ladies center men sashay, circle to the left with a hey, hey, hey
Allemande left do an allemande thar, go right & left and you form a star
Men back in and then shoot the star
Promenade your own little girl, take a little walk around the world
Won't you swing your pretty little girl back home

PART "B"



All around your left hand lady, see saw round your pretty little baby
When you're through, sides face Ah So Walk*
(32 beats)
Won't you swing your pretty little girl back home

(SEQUENCE: A, B, A, B, A)



*Ah So Walk, by Walt Wentworth

Heads' part:



Do an ah so (4 beats)
Now walk & dodge (4 beats)
Step forward into sides position
California twirl and face partner (4 beats)

Sides' part:



Turn and walk to heads position (4 beats)
Dosado with the one met (4 beats)
Star thru to face center of set (4 beats)

When the Heads finish their part, they do the Sides' part.



At this point all have partners, but are across from home.
Repeat all and everyone is home with own partners.
