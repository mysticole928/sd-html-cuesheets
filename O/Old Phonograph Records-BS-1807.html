Old Phonograph Records    (BS 1807)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left the corner, dosado the partner
Then join up hands and circle round that ring
And now those gents star right, go round to the corner Jane
Left allemande, go weavin' round again
I want to play them over and over, and I like to hear them say
You dosado that little girl, then promenade that way
I want some old records, some old square dance records
Like the ones we used to play so long ago

FIGURE



3/4 chain the ladies, now turn 'em left and maybe
Heads promenade 3/4 round you go
And now those side 2 go right & left thru
Turn the girl and pass thru, then dosado to an ocean wave you go
You'll balance, now swing thru and meet a new girl and swing
You swing and whirl and promenade, go walkin' round the ring
I want some old records, some old square dance records
Like the ones we used to play so long ago
