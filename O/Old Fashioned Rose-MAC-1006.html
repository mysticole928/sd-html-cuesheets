Old Fashioned Rose
   (MacGregor 1006)    Bob Van Antwerp
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

4 ladies chain cross the town
Chain 'em on back homeward bound
The girls roll away, make a right hand star
Turn partner left a full turn , an allemande thar
Back that ol' star round the land
Slip the clutch , left allemande , promenade
Promenade home and you swing with your own
Swing with your old fashioned Rose

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER (ALTERNATE)    Basic

4 ladies promenade round
Swing with your partner right now
Allemande your corner and dosado your own
Face your corner, star thru , circle don't roam
Circle left , then whirlaway
Grand right & left hear me say
Promenade home , then you swing with your own
Swing with your old fasioned Rose

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

1 & 3 dosado and make an ocean wave
Balance up and back and then a swing star thru *
Face the sides and circle left and make a line
The line goes forward up and back , a right & left thru
Turn with your gal and cross trail , swing the corner
Swing with this girl and promenade
Promenade home and don't leave her alone
Swing with your old fashioned girl

*Swing star thru: from an ocean wave with the ladies in the center. Swing 1/2 by the right, men in center swing 1/2 by the left, then star thru with the girl they face. As man moves toward lady on end of line he will take his right hand with lady's left hand thus facing the outside couple.
