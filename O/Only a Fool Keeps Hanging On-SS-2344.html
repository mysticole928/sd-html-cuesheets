Only A Fool Keeps Hanging On    (SS 2344)   Ken Finnerty

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Dosado round that corner girl, now see saw round your taw
Join up hands, circle to the left around that hall
You circle to the left around and then left allemande
Weave the ring, it's in and out until you meet again
Only a fool keeps hanging on, on, on
Dosado and promenade
You broke my heart, admit it, now you really did it
Only a fool keeps hanging on

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway round the square
Down the middle, right & left thru and turn that lady fair
Dosado, back to back, make an ocean wave and then
Slide thru and square thru 3/4 round you go
Allemande that corner girl, now dosado your maid
Swing that corner girl and then you promenade
You broke my heart, admit it, now you really did it
Only a fool keeps hanging on
