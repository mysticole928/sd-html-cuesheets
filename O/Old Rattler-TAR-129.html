ALTERNATE LYRICS

**Old Rattler tree'd the other night, I thought he tree'd a coon
When I came to find out he was barking at the moon

***Well Grandma had a yeller hen, we set her as you know
We set her on 3 buzzard eggs, she hatched out one old crow

Star thru , square thru 3/4 round
