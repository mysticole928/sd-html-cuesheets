Old Rocking Chair
(Scope 533) Ed Berryman
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 little ladies promenade inside that big old ring
Get back to that man and give him a great big swing
Now join your hands and circle to the left go round that old hall
Left allemande with the corner girl, and dosado your taw
4 boys star by the left , go once around that set
Turn your partner by the right your corner by the left
Come back dosado then promenade her home
Then swing to the squeaks of that old rockin' chair

FIGURE


1 & 3 (2 & 4) into the middle and come on back you go
Swing thru and rock it to and fro
Spin the top and rock it one time you can
Pass thru , then swing thru there my friend
Now 4 little ladies circulate
The boys will trade and then
Turn thru and the corner lady left allemande
Come back one and dosado
The corner lady swing
Promenade this girl to your old rockin' chair
