Ob La Di, Ob La Da    (GMP 807)   Bronc Wise

OPENER



Circle left
Desmond has a barrow in the market place
Molly is a singer in a band
Desmond says to Molly "Girl, I like your face,"
And Molly says this:
Allemande left and weave the ring
Ob la di, Ob la da life goes on, Bra
Swing your girl and then you promenade
Ob la di, Ob la da life goes on, Bra
La la how the life goes on

BREAK



Circle left
Desmond takes a trolly to the jeweller's store
Buys a twenty carat golden ring
Takes it back to Molly waiting at the door
And as he gives it to her:
Allemande left and weave the ring
Ob la di, Ob la da life goes on, Bra
Swing your girl and then you promenade
Ob la di, Ob la da life goes on, Bra
La la how the life goes on

CLOSER



Circle left
Happy ever after in the market place
Desmond lets the children lend a hand
Molly stays at home and does her pretty face
And in the evening she still sings it:
Allemande left and weave the ring
Ob la di, Ob la da life goes on, Bra
Swing your girl and then you promenade
Ob la di, Ob la da life goes on, Bra
La la how the life goes on
