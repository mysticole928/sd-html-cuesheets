Ode To Joy
(Riverboat 347) Tony Oxendine
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Circle to the left
Come, sing a simple song of joy
Of freedom, tell the story
Left allemande , dosado around your lady
Allemande that corner , weave around that ring
One mighty voice that will bring
A sound that will ring... forever more
Then, sing a song of joy
For love and understanding

FIGURE

Heads (sides) square thru , take it four hands around
Your corner dosado , my friend
Swing thru , boys will trade , boys run right
Bend the line , forward and back
Square thru and go 3/4 around now
Swing that girl, promenade her home
Then sing a song of joy
For love and understanding
