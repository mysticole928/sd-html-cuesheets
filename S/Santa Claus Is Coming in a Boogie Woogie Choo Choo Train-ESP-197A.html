Santa Claus Is Coming In A Boogie Woogie Choo Choo Train   (ESP 197A )   Gary Shoemake

OPENER



Circle left
Santa’s been a checkin’, checkin’ all around that world
Wants to know what’s goin’ on with every single boy and girl.
Left allemande the corner, turn partner by the right
Left allemande the corner then you weave all right
Santa likes to boogie; Santa likes to dance.
Santa likes to rock it, go real fast.
Santa Claus is coming in a boogie woogie choo choo train.

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands you go
Round the corner lady do a dosado
Swing thru tonight, let the boys run right
Bend the line and then you do the right & left thru
Pass the ocean, recycle then
Swing the corner girl and promenade her again
Santa Claus is coming in a boogie woogie choo choo train.

BREAK



Circle left
Hey! Santa’s got his elves workin’, workin’ overtime
He doubled up production on the toy production line.
Left allemande the corner, turn partner by the right
Left allemande the corner then you weave all right
He’s got toys for the boys and girls all over the place.
He’s got ‘em stacked to the ceiling, got ‘em stickin’ in your face.
Santa Claus is coming in a boogie woogie choo choo train.

CLOSER



Circle left
Old Rudolf said, “Wait a minute! What about me?”
“Don’t leave me sittin’ here alone on Christmas Eve.”
Left allemande the corner, turn partner by the right
Left allemande the corner then you weave all right
We’ve got Dasher, and Dancer, and Prancer, and Vixen.
And Comet, and Cupid, and Donner, and Blitzen
They’ll all be a-comin’ in a boogie woogie choo choo train.
