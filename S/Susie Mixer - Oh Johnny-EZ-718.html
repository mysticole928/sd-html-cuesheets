Susie Mixer / Oh Johnny
   (E-Z School Rhythm 718 side A )   
(Begins in Circle of Couples facing Counter-Clockwise, girls on partner's right)

Walk counter clockwise 2-3-4 to face your partner (still holding hands)
Slide (to man's left - step close, step close)
Walk clockwise 2-3-4 to face your partner
Slide (to man's right - step close, step close)
Drop right hands
Star (with left palm in left palm about shoulder high) left turning counter-clockwise
once around and back away from your partner (man to center, lady to outside)
Dosado (the new partner to your right of original partner as you face)
Repeat dance from beginning with a new partner each time
