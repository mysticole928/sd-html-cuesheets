FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain straight across that ring
1 & 3 go right & left thru and turn the girl and then
Star thru and pass thru and pass thru again
Trade by, swing the corner lady one time around and promenade
Straight back home and grand parade

SEQUENCE (Repeat entire dance, chorus and figure, 4 times)
