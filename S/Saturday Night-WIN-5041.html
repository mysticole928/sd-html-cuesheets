Saturday Night
(Windsor 5041) Al Stevens
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join hands all circle left , circle left around you know
Allemande your corner, come home a dosado
Men star by the right , go walkin' round I sing
Left allemande then weave around the ring
Saturday night, Saturday night
Dosado then promenade her for spite
Taker her home and swing her with all of your might
With (name) on a Saturday night

FIGURE

All four ladies chain go across that ring
Heads promenade halfway round I sing
Lead to the right , circle four for me
Make a line , slide thru , do an eight chain 3
Saturday night , the corner you swing
Left allemande your corner, then promenade that ring
You promenade 'em, dance with all of your might
With (name) on a Saturday night

(Note: the caller may use a caller's name,
a club name and the week night may also be changed
to fit the occasion. Example: "With the Star Twirlers
on a Friday night.")
