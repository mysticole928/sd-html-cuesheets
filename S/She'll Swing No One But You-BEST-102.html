She'll Swing No One But You
   (Best 102)    John Garrett
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Walk around your corner girl, see saw round your Jane
Gents to the center with a right hand star , just once around that ring
Turn your partner with a left , your corner right hand round
Partner left , the ladies star 3/4 round that town
And when you see that corner, do that old left allemande
Promenade your partner, walk around that ring so grand
Take her home with you, balance out and swing her too
For she'll swing no one but you

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

Heads (sides) to the middle and back with you, now do a full square thru
Go all the way and star by the right with the outside two
Once around and heads (sides) to the middle, left hand star around
To the same old two, right & left thru and turn that girl around
Then dive to the middle , California twirl and swing that corner girl
Promenade this lady, take a walk go around that world
Promenade her round, take her home and settle down
Then she'll swing no one but you
