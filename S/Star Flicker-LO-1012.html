Star Flicker
   (Lore 1012)    Max Engle
OPENER

Bow to the partner, corners all , circle left around the hall
Walk all around that left hand lady , see saw around that pretty taw
Do an allemande left with the corner maid, come along home and promenade
Gents roll back , a left face whirl, promenade with the corner girl
Then 1 & 3 wheel around and pass thru , on to the next and star thru
Do an allemande left with your left hand, partners meet with a right & left grand

FIGURE

Then 1 & 3 going to swing a little girl
1 & 3 swing , move up to the middle and back
Then forward again and star thru
Two ladies chain , then turn 'em on around, you face that two, pass thru and star thru
Two ladies chain , then you turn 'em on around, face that two, pass thru on to the next and star thru
Two ladies chain , then you turn 'em on around, face that two, dive thru and star thru
Two ladies chain , then you turn 'em on around, face that two and cross trail
*Hunt for the corner, do an allemande left with your left hand, partners right, right & left grand

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

(*Or, Then separate, go around one, come into the middle and box the gnat
Then pull them by , let's square thru with the outside two
Let them by, left and right and find the corner
Do an allemande left with your left hand, partners right, right & left grand )

BREAK

Then 1 & 3 more up to the middle and come on back
Then forward again and star thru , pass thru and star thru
Then cross trail , skip one girl , stick out your hand
Do an allemande left with your left hand, walk right in to a right & left grand

CLOSER

Bow to your partner, corners all
Wave to the girl across the hall
And keeno, that's it, that's all

(SEQUENCE: Opener, Figure - heads , Figure - sides , Break, Figure - heads , Closer)
