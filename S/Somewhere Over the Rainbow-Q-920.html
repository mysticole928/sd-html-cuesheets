Somewhere Over The Rainbow   (Quadrille 920 )   Guy Adams

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade one time round now
Come back and swing your man
Join all your hands, circle left, around the ring you go
Allemande left and weave the ring
Somewhere over the rainbow
Swing your lady, promenade
Birds fly over the rainbow
Why then, oh why can't I?

FIGURE



Head two couples promenade halfway around with the maid
Walk into the middle and now square thru
Go all the way around the ring, right & left thru, turn I sing
Now veer to the left, ferris wheel to the middle of the ring
Square thru 3/4 round there
Swing the corner, promenade
Birds fly over the rainbow
Why then, oh why can't I?
