Solomon Levi    (MAC 2003)   Fenton Jones

INTRO - CLOSER



Everybody swing your honey, you swing her high and low
Allemande left with your old left hand, around the ring you go
Grand old right & left, walk on your heel and toe
Meet your honey and give her a swing, promenade the hall
Singing oh Solomon Levi, tra-la-la-la-la-la
Oh Solomon Levi, tra-la-la-la-la-la

FIGURE



The first* couple separate, go round the outside track
Keep a-goin' around the set and pass her comin' back
Pass right by your partner, salute your corners all
Turn around and swing your own, and promenade the hall
Singing oh Solomon Levi, tra-la-la-la-la-la
Oh Solomon Levi, tra-la-la-la-la-la

*This figure is repeated six times with the second couple, the third couple, the fourth couple, then 1 & 3, 2 & 4, and finally all 4 couples taking their turn leading the figure.
