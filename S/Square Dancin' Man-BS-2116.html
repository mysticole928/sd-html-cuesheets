Square Dancing Man    (BS 2116)   Bob Yerington

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies promenade around the center
Swing your big old handsome man
Join all of your hands and start to circle
Left allemande and weave around the land
Let's get on with the square dance
Swing your girl and promenade her man
Promenade the land, go walking hand in hand
For I'm a square dancing man

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around now
Go right & left thru and turn them there
Square thru 4 hands around, go walking round the town
And make a right hand star with that pair
Heads star left and turn it round the middle
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Promenade the land, go walking hand in hand
For I'm a square dancin' man
