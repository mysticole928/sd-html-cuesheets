Skye Boat Song
Tom Bryce; many others 1884
(BTM 1) Barry Wonson
OPENER

Sides face grand square
Speed bonny boat
Like a bird on a wing
Onward
The sailors cry ( Four ladies chain )
Carry the lad ( Chain 'em back )
Born to be king ( Promenade )
Over the sea to Skye

FIGURE

Heads left square thru
Left touch 1/4 , walk & dodge
Wheel around , dixie style to an ocean wave
Men cross run , recycle
Pass thru , allemande left
Swing your girl and promenade
Over the sea to Skye

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face grand square
Mull was astern
Rum on the port
Eigg on the starboard bow , ( Four ladies chain )
Glory of youth ( Chain 'em back )
Glowed in his soul ( Promenade )
Where is that glory now

CLOSER

Sides face grand square
Loud the winds howl
Loud the waves roar
Thunder...fills the air ( Four ladies chain )
Billow and breeze ( Chain 'em back )
Islands and seas ( Promenade )
Mountains of rain...and sun

Circle left
O-O-Over the sea... to Skye
