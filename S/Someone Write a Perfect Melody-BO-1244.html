Someone Write A Perfect Melody    (BO 1244)   Lem Gravelle

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain, turn a little girl and then
Send 'em back all 8 will flutter wheel
Halfway round you go, then circle left you know
Left allemande and weave the ring
Someone write a perfect melody
Dosado and then you'll promenade her
One note for every time her name runs across my mind
Write it for the girl I love and need

FIGURE



Head ladies chain right, turn a little girl and then
Heads go into the middle and back
1/2 square thru, then go right & left thru
Swing thru, go walking round the floor, boys run
Tag the line and then face right, couples circulate
All 8 back track and promenade her
Someone write a perfect melody
For the girl I love and need
