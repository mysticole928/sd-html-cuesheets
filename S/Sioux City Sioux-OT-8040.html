Arkansas Star / Sioux City Sioux
Dick Thomas; Gene Autrey; many others 1945
   (Old Timer 8040 side B )    Cal Golden
FIGURE

(Part 1)
Swing boys swing, now everybody swing
Promenade around the ring go two by two
Back home again with Sioux City Sue, Sioux City Sue
Everybody swing now, swing Sioux City Sue

(Part 2)
Now first couple right and circle once around
Dosado that opposite gal right shoulder once and around
Dosado your partner and swing her too
Both couples swing, now swing Sioux City Sue

(Couple #1 repeats part 2 with couples 3 & 4. Then repeat Part 1 again,
and then repeat entire sequence for couples #2, 3 & 4.)
