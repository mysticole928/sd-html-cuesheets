Square Dance Fever
   (Blue Ribbon 202)    Francis Zeller
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

All four ladies chain across the way
Heads square thru , just four hands I say
Sides divide , star thru , 1 & 3 California twirl
Join 8 hands , and circle left now
Left allemande , come back and dosado
Left allemande , then promenade your own
A big long walk around the ring, all the way around I sing
Got fever, square dance fever

FIGURE    C1, corner progression

The heads square thru 4 hands around the square
Dosado your corner waiting there
Make a wave , don't be late, then all 8 can circulate
A right & left thru , then dive thru
Substitute , pass on thru and swing
Left allemande new corner, promenade
Promenade around the ring, long walk home again I sing
Got fever, square dance fever
