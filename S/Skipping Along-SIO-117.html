Skipping Along
(Sets In Order 117) Johnny LeClair
FIGURE (4 times through)

Well 1 & 3 (2 & 4) go up and back and star thru right there
Pass thru , a right & left thru with the outside pair
Now duck to the middle and star thru and pass thru once again
Around one go down the middle and square thru and then
Just three hands , split the sides (heads), round one, down the middle
Swing the opposite lady, face the outside two
Right & left thru and turn your girl, dive thru , square thru
Now three hands around, corners all left allemande
Well, come back home and dosado and to the corner go
Swing the corner lady, boys and promenade you know
All the way round the ring, you can skip along in time
Go skipping along until you're home again
