Square Dance Blues    (LIS 508)   Marvin Shilling

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Head two ladies to the right you chain now
New head ladies chain across
Head two couples cross trail thru, go round two
Make a line, go forward up and back
Pass thru, ends cross over, centers turn back
Allemande left, then promenade your girl
Promenade your lady, take her home now
They you swing her to the square dance blues

FIGURE



Head two couples right & left thru and turn 'em
1/2 square thru, then eight chain thru (all the way over and back)
You could search this whole square over
And never find another girl like her
Right & left thru and turn 'em, then you cross trail
U-turn back for an allemande, pass one
You take the next and promenade her home now
She's the sweetest gal in town
