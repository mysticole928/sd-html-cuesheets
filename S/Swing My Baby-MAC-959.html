Swing My Baby
   (MacGregor 959)   
 
FIGURE   

Head (side) two ladies chain you turn 'em around now
All join hands and circle left around
Well allemande that corner , then promenade your own now
1 & 3 (2 & 4) wheel around and make a right hand star
Head (side) two gents, the girl behind star by the left in time
Once around same two go right & left thru and then
You dive thru , square thru 3/4 round the ring then
Swing that corner lady, promenade
You promenade that ring, well take 'em home and swing
Yes you're swinging with your baby, now

MIDDLE BREAK   

All four ladies chain you turn them around now
Chain them back across that ring
Well join hands , circle left , you circle around that ring now
Reverse back in single file
Men turn in a right hand star twice around you go
When you meet your lady fair, do a little dosado
And then you allemande that corner , a grand old right & left now
When you meet your baby promenade
You promenade that ring, you take 'em home and swing
Yes, you're swinging with your baby, now
