Sweet Baby    (RA 1323)   Bill Wilson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Why don't you bow to your corner, that partner you swing
1 & 3 promenade halfway round that ring
Into the middle and square thru 4 hands around that's what you do
Right & left thru the outside two, turn the girl and then
Dive thru, circle 4, it's once around and then
Pass thru and corners all left allemande
Come back one and promenade with your sweet baby
Take her right back home and swing

FIGURE



1 & 3 go up and back and then star thru
Square thru 3/4 round, swing the corner Sue
Promenade go round the town, keep on walkin' this gal around
1 & 3 wheel around, go right & left thru
Cross trail thru, new corners all left allemande
Come back one and promenade go walkin' round that land
Hold her hand and promenade with this sweet baby
Take her right back home and swing
