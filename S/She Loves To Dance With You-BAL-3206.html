4 ladies promenade , keep going till you get back
Turn partner right , turn once and a half
The men turn in stand back to back
Girls to the left go round, don't let your own go by
Ladies promenade clockwise

Allemande the corner lady, grand right & left you go
Grand right & left till you meet your girl & do a dosado

FIGURE   

Head (side) couples swing your maid, you swing then promenade her
Swing twice around , ( do not rush the promenade )
Sides wheel in & follow them, heads wheel in pass thru & stop
( Sides wheel to the left to follow heads, heads promenade 1/2 way round
Then turn in to the center to pass thru & stop facing their own home positions )
Sides keep going round, heads arch & you dive thru
( Sides promenade 3/4 round until #2 is facing #1 and #4 is facing #3
Sides duck thru to center as heads arch over & move to home position
And turn partner under to face center )
You star by the right , in the middle of the set
Your corner waits for you
Allemande the corner lady, you dosado your own
Go back, swing the corner girl you keep her for your own
You promenade this lady now, go dancing 2 by 2
There's a wiggle in her walk & a giggle in her talk
She loves to dance with you
