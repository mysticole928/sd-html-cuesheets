Sweetheart Tree   (Top 25295 )   Skip Smith

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade the inside
Get back home and swing your man around
Join hands, make a ring
Circle left I sing
Left allemande and weave around the ring
They say there's a tree in the forest
Do a dosado and then promenade
Big blossoms of white will burst into sight
When we come to the sweetheart tree

FIGURE



Those heads (sides) promenade, you go 3/4
1 & 3 (2 & 4) will do the right and left thru
Pass thru and then dosado the outside two
Swing thru and then turn thru
Left allemande that corner, walk by one now
Swing the next little lady, promenade
Big blossoms of white will burst into sight
When we come to the sweetheart tree
