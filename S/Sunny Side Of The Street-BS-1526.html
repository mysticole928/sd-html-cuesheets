Sunny Side Of The Street
Tony Bennett; Louis Armstrong and others 1930
   (Blue Star 1526)    Gene McMullen
FIGURE   

Head (side) couples bow and swing , promenade go half around
Sides (heads) go right & left thru , chain four ladies cross and then
Face your corner, all eight chain , join hands and make a ring
Allemande left no thar, right & left , don't star
Shoot it full you know, corner girl dosado
See saw go around your taw, gents walk over, swing your lady
Promenade that gal around, you're as rich as Rockefeller
Gold dust at your feet, on the sunny side of the street

BREAK   

Dosey corner lady around, join hands and make a ring now
Then reverse your feet , to the sunny side of the street
Gents backtrack around that ring, turn your partner with a right hand
Allemande left , go right & left grand
Right & left around you go
Meet your girl pass her by , see saw the next little girl
Box the gnat , pull her by , swing your own , tell you why
Take that lady, promenade , you're as rich as Rockefeller
Gold dust at your feet, on the sunny side of the street
