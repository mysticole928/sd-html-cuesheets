Blue Gold / Spanish Square Dance
Bob Wills and His Texas Playboys 1946
(Sets In Order 1106 side B ) Bob Wright
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

To your partner bow down low, corner girl dosado
Come on home, swing your honey round and round
Join your hands , make a ring , circle left with the pretty thing
Whirlaway all eight make a right hand star
Turn that star round the land, girls roll back pass one man
Allemande left and a grand old right & left
Travel round til you meet your maid, dosado then promenade
The band plays a square dance soft and low

FIGURE

Head ladies chain across the way, turn a new girl, whirlaway
Heads star right 3/4 , ladies lead them through the sides
Both turn left around the lane, down the center , dixie chain
Both turn left , then stop behind the sides
Forward eight and back with you, sides roll back , the heads pass thru
(Sides separate and roll back behind the heads... ZOOM?)
Allemande left and a right hand turn your own
Box the flea with the corner Joe, promenade and around you go
The band plays a square dance soft and low
