Truck Stop / Silver Bells
(Old Timer 8114-78 side B ) Johnny Schultz
(Once this dance starts, do not stop at home, just keep promenading until the following command is given.)

BREAK - CLOSER

You docey round your corner, then form a great big ring
Circle left go halfway round, then give your own a swing,
And now you allemande left your corner, then promenade Ole Nell (partner)
You promenade this gal with silver bells

FIGURE

And now the four little ladies star by the right, go once around the track
(ladies step across in front of partner to a RH star in the center, gents keep promenading)
A left hand turn your partner, to your corner box the gnat
Then do a grand old right and left around, until you meet this Belle
You promenade this gal instead of Nell

And now you allemande left your corner, then walk right by your own
You docey round your right hand girl, go back hip swing your own
And now the gents star left go once around until you meet Ole Nell
You promenade this gal with silver bells
