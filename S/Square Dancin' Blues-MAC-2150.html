Square Dancin' Blues
(MacGregor 2150) Fred Drouant
FIGURE (Heads twice)

Four little ladies chain go straight across that ring
1 & 3 promenade , halfway round I sing
Lead right and circle , make a line for me
Up to the middle, come on back , star thru for me
Do an eight chain 3 , swing that corner girl
Swing that corner girl and promenade that ring
Promenade that lady, take her home and then
Swing her to the square dancing blues

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join hands make a ring , circle left I sing
Walk around your corner , see saw your own
Men star right , one time tonight
Left allemande , weave around that land
It's in and out around, and when you meet your maid
Turn thru , left allemande , we'll all promenade
Promenade this lady, take her home and then
Swing her to the square dancing blues

FIGURE (Sides twice)

2 & 4 square thru four hands round you go
Dosado the outside two, one time you know
Swing thru two by two, then spin the top
Don't stop, right & left thru , turn the girl
Square thru 3/4 round, swing the corner girl
Swing that corner lady round, promenade the world
Promenade that lady, take her home and then
Swing her to the square dancing blues

(Sequence: Figure heads twice, middle break, figure sides twice, closer)
