Star Thru Workshop
(Grenn 12027 side A ) Ron Schneider
TEACHING

Head two couples half sashay, pass thru across the way
Separate, around one, in the middle star thru
Separate, around one, in the middle star thru
Pass thru then crosstrail, corners all left allemande

Sides to the right and circle up 4, side gent break and line up 4
Forward 8 and back again, star thru once, star thru again
Crosstrail thru and find your corner, left allemande

Head 2 couples square thru 4 hands round
And with the sides star thru
Star thru, dive thru
California twirl, left allemande

STAR PLUCKER

Side two couples star thru, pass thru
Star thru, pass thru
U-turn back, star thru, California twirl
Inside arch, dive thru
Pass thru, star thru, California twirl
Face partner, left allemande

FAR FETCH

#1 half sashay, heads go forward, back that way
1/2 square thru, then u-turn back, star thru
Sides divide and star thru
Forward 6, back to the gate, split that couple and separate
Around one, all the way round, circle up 8 like you always do
Reverse back, single file, gents pivot dixie style
Right, left, right, pass the next, left allemande

STAR BRITE

Side ladies chain across the track, first couple stand back to back
With your corner box the gnat
New head couples crosstrail thru, separate, go around two
Hook on the ends and make a line, forward up and back in time
Star thru the opposite girl
Men star left, ladies star right, turn those stars, don't take all night
And gent pick up your maid, rest all follow a star promenade
Head 2 couples wheel around, star thru once, star thru again
Crosstrail thru and find your corner, left allemande

STAR THRU INTRO

Forward 8 and back with you
Face your corner, star thru
California twirl and crosstrail to a left allemande
Skip one girl, right & left grand

Music is Merle's Gallop Grenn 12028
