Steel Guitar Rag    (WIN 4848)   Ruth Stillion

FIGURE - BREAK



4 ladies chain 3/4 chain
Promenade 8 take her walking round the ring
1 & 3 wheel around, right & left thru the town
Chain those ladies back again
Forward 8, back you reel, pass thru to wheel & deal
Box the gnat and do that dixie daisy
Go across the ring and back, the outsides turn back
Left allemande and a grand ole right & left
Go, buddy, go meet your own, dosado
Go back and swing the corner, swing high & low
Promenade home, she'll be your own
While we dance to that old steel guitar rag
