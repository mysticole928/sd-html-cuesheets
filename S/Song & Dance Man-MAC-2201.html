Song & Dance Man    (MAC 2201)   Chuck Acelin

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain 3/4 around
Now join your hands and circle left
Roll away and circle left
Left allemande and weave that ring
Dosado there, now swing this girl and then you promenade
You put a little bit in the kitty, boy
And I'll sing and dance with you

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around
With the corner lady dosado
Swing thru go two by two, boys trade, corner swing
Left allemande new corner, now swing and promenade
You put a little bit in the kitty
And I'll sing and dance with you

Heads star thru, dosado once around



Turn your girl and star thru, square thru and count to 4
Trade by then the corner swing and promenade
You put a little bit in the kitty
And I'll sing and dance with you

Sides promenade halfway around



Go forward up and come on back
Pass thru and tag the line, face in
Star thru, the corner swing
Swing and promenade
You put a little bit in the kitty
And I'll sing and dance with you

Sides square thru and go, go, go



Swing thru, go two by two
Spin the top and when you do, slide thru
Swing the corner lady there, swing and promenade
You put a little bit in the kitty
And I'll sing and dance with you
