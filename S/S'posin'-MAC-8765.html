S'posin'
   (MacGregor 8765)    Bob Van Antwerp
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    A1

Join your hands and circle to the left
S'posin' you will promenade your pet
1 & 3 (2 & 4) will wheel around and square thru 3
3/4 round now, the next a right & left thru
Cross trail and find your corners all, left allemande
Take your own and promenade the land
Promenade , go round the ring, get back home and then you swing
She's not s'posin', she just loves to dance

FIGURE    A1, corner progression

1 & 3 (2 & 4) a half sashay , up to the middle and back
Pass thru , go round one and then the old star thru
Separate around one , then star thru
Then cross trail and swing your corner girl around and round
Allemande new corner girl and dosado your own
Take your lady, promenade , go walking right on home
Promenade , go round the ring, get back home and then you swing
She's not s'posin', she just loves to dance
