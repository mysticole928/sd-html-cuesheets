Summer Winds   (Hi Hat 436 )   Dave Hoffmann

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain, 3/4 round, then circle left
Go allemande left, go allemande thar, 4 men star
(Slip the clutch) Left allemande, come back a dosado
All swing and promenade
Promenade this girl ... To the Summer Winds

FIGURE



Heads square thru, 4 hands you go, then dosado
Touch 1/4, walk and dodge, and California Twirl
(Bend the line)
Flutterwheel you do, and then square thru
Three hands around and swing
Promenade this girl ... To the Summer Winds
