Second Hand Rose   (Kalox 1183 )   Jon Jones

FIGURE



(on up-beat)
And now, the heads promenade you go halfway around
Come down the middle, slide thru and then pass thru
Swing thru the outside 2 and now the boys run right
Bend the line, pass the ocean, make a wave tonight
Recycle around and veer left my friend
Ferris wheel and turn it around and then, pass thru
Slide thru and square thru 3/4 around
Swing your corner lady, promenade 'round the town
Everyone knows she's a second hand rose
From Second Avenue

MIDDLE BREAK



All 4 ladies chain go straight across the ring
Turn and chain the ladies right back home again
Join hands and circle to the left around you go
Left allemande and weave the ring you know
Second hand rose and second hand clothes
Dosado that second hand rose
Men star left in the middle, turn it one time around
Swing your lady, boy, and promenade her on down
Everyone knows she's my second hand rose
From Second Avenue

SEQUENCE (Figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides)
