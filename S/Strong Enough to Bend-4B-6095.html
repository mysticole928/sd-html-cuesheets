Strong Enough To Bend   (4-Bar-B 6095 )   Paul Marcum

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
There's a tree out in the backyard
That never has been broken by the wind
And the reason it's still standing
It was strong enough to bend
Circle to the left
For years we have been together
Left allemande, swing and promenade
Our love will last forever
If we're strong enough to bend

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade 1/2 way and go
Lead to the right, you dosado
Swing thru, boys run
You bend the line & a right and left thru
You flutterwheel, sweep 1/4
You pass thru, swing on the corner and promenade
Our love will last forever
If we're strong enough to bend

BREAK



Sides face, grand square
For years we have been together
As lovers and as friends
What we have will last forever
If we're strong enough to bend
4 ladies chain
You turn the girl & then, chain 'em back again
Turn that girl and promenade
Our love will last forever
If we're strong enough to bend
