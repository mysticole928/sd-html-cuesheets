Sweet Georgia Brown   (Cimarron 113 )   Gerald McWhirter
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK

Four little ladies hit the road go walking talking round
Come on home and swing, swing your man and then
Join hands circle elft go moving round
Left allemande, weave around and sing
Sind it in and out until you meet your doll
Swing the lady there boys and promenade awhile
Promenade that shapely miss, step up and steal a kiss
Georgia named her, Georgia claimed her
Sweet Georgia Brown

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands, dosado
Swing thru, spin the top, boys move up, right & left thru
Pass the ocean, ladies trade, swing thru
Swing the corner, promenade and sing

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Sides promenade halfway, come down the middle and pass the ocean, extend
Swing thru, boys run, tag the line right
Wheel & deal, turn thru
Left allemande, promenade
