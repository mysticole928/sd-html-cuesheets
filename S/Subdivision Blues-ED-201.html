Subdivision Blues    (ED 201)   Don Poling

OPENER



Circle left
Out on the edge of town I bought a two-room brick
I moved in as soon as I got the plumbing all fixed
Left allemande that corner, come back and box the gnat
Four ladies promenade inside the ring like that
Go one time around then swing there with your man
Left allemande and promenade that old land
I've got a million years to pay it off with payments overdue

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around you go
Circle four with the outside two and make your line you know
Walk up to the middle and back, a right & left thru I say
Flutter wheel go full around that way, and now you'll
Slide thru, pass thru, trade by and swing
Left allemande, promenade that old ring
A million years to pay it off with payments overdue
I've got those mean old Subdivision Blues
