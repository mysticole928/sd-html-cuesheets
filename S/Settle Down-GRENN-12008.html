This is a 2-sided singing call with Square Dance Sue on the A side and Settle Down on the B side. Both sides are instrumental. Grenn 12007 is a 2-sided singing call with Ronny Schneider calling the cues on both sides.

Square Dance Sue cue sheet has an unusual Ladies Chain choreography. After the Lines Divide, the formation is a Completed Double Pass Thru with the ladies in front. From here, it's a Ladies Chain + Courtesy Turn Twice.
