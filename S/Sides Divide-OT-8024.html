Half Sashay / Sides Divide
(Old Timer 8024 side B ) Butch Nelson
FIGURE

First two couples forward up and back
Forward again in the same old track
Swing in the center and swing on the sides
Circle four in the middle of the floor
Sides divide and swing some more
Dosado and don't get sore
Sides divide and swing some more
Up the river and around the bend
Sides divide and swing again
Promenade your corners all
Grab that gal, don't let her fall

(Repeat once more for first and third couples, then twice for second and fourth couples.)
