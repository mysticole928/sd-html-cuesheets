Stolen Love
   (Bogan 1152)    Ham Wolfraim
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    C4

Walk around your corner lady, see saw round your pretty baby
Allemande , grand right & left you go
Meet your girl halfway round, do a curlique
Make an ocean wave , you rock it to and fro
Swing by the right , left allemande now
Come on back, dosado the girl you know
You promenade her, serenade her
Take her home, swing the girl you love

FIGURE    A1, unknown progression

2 & 4 go up and back , pass on thru , U-turn back
Head couples right & left thru , turn this girl
Square thru inside you go 4 hands now don't be slow
Split the sides , go round one, line up 4
* Pass on thru , centers hinge , while the ends fold right in
Square thru 3/4 round inside you go
Box the flea with the corner girl, promenade oh what a girl
Take her home and swing your stolen love

(*Note: Ends fold in between centers and square thru 3/4 round,
Centers turn back, face center of set.)

Vic says...the line "*Pass on thru, centers hinge, while the ends fold right in" means to do a Pass Thru, Step & Slide, then U-Turn Back. Alternatively, an "Explode & Roll". A very odd use of the calls Hinge and Fold.
