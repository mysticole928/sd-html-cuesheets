Someday    (BS 1720)   Bob Fisk

BREAK



Well now the 4 ladies chain then you turn 'em
Roll away, you circle round that ring
Go left allemande, then do the right & left grand
Hand over hand then dosado that lady
The gents star left, go round that ring now
Pass this girl, swing the next one round and round
Promenade for now, I'll get along somehow
Then someday, I won't want you

FIGURE



The head couples square thru go 4 hands
Meet the sides do a double swing thru
You expect me to be true, balance you do
Go right & left thru, swing the girl that's facing you
Then do an allemande left new corner lady
Dosado your own then promenade
Promenade for now, I'll get along somehow
Then someday, honey I won't want you
