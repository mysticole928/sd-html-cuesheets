Santo Domingo    (RWH 212)   Jerry Reed

Four ladies (men) promenade inside the ring



Join hands, you circle left
Left allemande and weave the ring
Gay Caballeros with Spanish (large) sombreros
Swing your lady (man) and promenade
*In Santo Domingo my pockets do jingle
Oh, how I wish I were there

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*In Santo Domingo, the girls they are pretty

FIGURE



Head (side) couples square thru four hands around
Find the corner lady dosado
Swing thru and then, spin the top
Boys move up, a right & left thru
Square thru and go 3/4 'round
Swing that corner and promenade
In Santo Domingo my pockets would jingle
Oh, how I'd love to be there
