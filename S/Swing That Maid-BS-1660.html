Swing That Maid
(Blue Star 1660) Marshall Flippo
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join hands and then you circle
Yes you circle to the left around that ring
Gents you star by the right until you see your partner
Turn her left and with your corner lady swing
Swing that girl , then allemande new corner
Pass your own , swing the next and promenade
You promenade back home with your partner
Take her home and swing with that maid

FIGURE

1st and 3rd you lead to the right and circle
Make a line , go forward up and back
Right & left thru and turn and do a dixie style
And you'll balance forward and back
A left hand turn and dixie chain with that lady
She'll turn back , swing that gal and promenade
You promenade back home with your partner
Take her home and you swing with that maid
