She Played Tambourine  (HH 5249)  Tom Perry

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
She played tambourine, with a silver jingle
And she must have known the words to at least a million tunes
But the one most requested by the man she knew as Cowboy
Was the Late Night Benediction At The Y'all Come Back Saloon
4 ladies promenade gonna walk on around the ring
Well gonna get on home swing the man, promenade again
But the one most requested by the man she knew as Cowboy
Was the Late Night Benediction At The Y'all Come Back Saloon

FIGURE



Head (side) 2 you promenade halfway around the ring
Come down the middle with a right & left thru and turn the girl and then
You slide thru and square thru 3/4 go
Dosado with the corner, once around you know
Hey swing thru 2 by 2 and the boys trade in the middle now
Gonna swing on the corner honey, promenade go round the ring
