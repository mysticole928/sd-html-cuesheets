Silver Moon Upon The Golden Sands    (BS 1775)   Al Brownlee

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies chain, you turn 'em left and then
Chain those ladies home and turn 'em left again
Circle to the left beneath that moon above
Left allemande, weave that ring
In and out around you go until you meet your maid
Dosado and promenade that land
Tell her of your love beneath those stars above
While the silver moon is on the golden sand

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around the ring
Down the middle, dosado around that Jane
Swing thru, don't slow down, then spin the top around
Square thru 3/4 round that ring, allemande
Left allemande your corner, dosado your love
Swing the corner, promenade that land
Tell her of your love beneath those stars above
While the silver moon is on the golden sand
