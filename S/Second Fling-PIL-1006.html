Second Fling   (Pilgrim 1006 )   Don Hanhurst

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain the ring, you turn 'em and then
Join hands, circle to the left, go round the ring now
Do an allemande left, just like an allemande thar
Go right and left, the men wheel in, make a right hand star
You shoot the star, dosado round the next one go
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Come on and let your hair down
We're going to tear down
And have ourselves a second fling

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru, 4 hands around you do
Right and left thru with the outside two
Dive thru, star thru, crosstrail thru
Round just one, make a line, go up and back
And then go right and left thru and now slide thru
Swing with the corner, promenade her
Come on and let your hair down
We're going to tear down
And have ourselves a second fling

TAG



Bow to the partner
You've had your second fling
