OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK



Circle Left
Hank Williams sang it. Number three drove it.
*(Chuck Berry twanged it. Tom Petty rocked it.)
The men Star Right inside, come on let’s move it
Allemande your corner, Turn your partner by the right
Allemande Left with the corner of the hall and Weave around the ring tonight
Smooth as the hickory wind that blows out
of Memphis down to Apalachicola
Hi ya’ll! Did yeh eat?
Well, come on in child. I’m sure glad to know yeh.

FIGURE



Heads Square Thru count 'em 4 hands round
You make a Right Hand Star
The Heads Star Left, roll it one time and then
Same pair and do a Dosado
Swing Thru and then the Boys Trade
Swing the corner lady round and Promenade
We’re cicadas makin’ noise with a southern voice.

CLOSER



Grand Square

Rosa Parks rode it. Doctor King paved it.
Bear Bryant won it. Billy Graham saved it.
