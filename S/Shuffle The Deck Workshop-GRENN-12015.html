Shuffle The Deck Workshop
(Grenn 12015 side A ) Ron Schneider
INTRO

Side 2 gents and the corner girl, up to the middle and back you whirl
Right to opposite and box the gnat
1/2 square thru and when you're through
U-turn back and box the gnat, change hands
Left allemande (that's everybody)

AKRON SHUFFLE

Heads to the right and circle up 4
Head gent break and line up four
Forward 8 and back with you, pass thru, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Face your partner, back away, pass thru, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Face your partner, back away, pass thru, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Face your partner, back away, left allemande

IN & OUT SHUFFLE

Sides to the right and circle up 4
Side gent break and line up 4
Forward 8 and back with you, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
1/4 in and back away, bend the line
Forward 8 and back with you, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
1/4 out and step away, bend the line
Forward 8 and back with you, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
1/4 out and step away, California twirl the right-hand girl
Crosstrail thru to a left allemande

ONE BY ONE SHUFFLE

Side ladies chain across the way, heads go right and circle that way
Head gent break and make a line
Forward 8 and back in time
Pass thru, shuffle the deck
Four lines of 2 shuffle the deck one by one
All turn back, pass right shoulders single file
Ladies go left, gents go right, walk by two on you go
Those who can box the gnat and turn back, left allemande

CHICKEN SHUFFLE

1st & 3rd right & left thru, sides go forward, back with you
Dosado that opposite maid, all the way round to ocean wave
Girls turn around, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Same two 1/2 square thru, dosado the outside two
All the way round to ocean wave
Girls turn around, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Inside arch, dive thru, dosado the opposite two
All the way round to ocean wave
Girls turn around, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Pass thru, dosado the outside two
All the way round to ocean wave
Girls turn around, shuffle the deck 2 by 2, left allemande

FEATHER SHUFFLE

#1 couples stand back to back, with your corner box the gnat
New heads go up and back, crosstrail thru go around two
Go around two like birds of a feather, make a line, forward 8 and back in time
Pass thru, U-turn back, shuffle the deck 2 by 2
Double pass thru, first couple left, next one right, circle 4 those in sight
The ladies break and make a line, forward 8 and back in time
Pass thru, U-turn back, shuffle the deck and center two
Square thru 3/4 around, outside 2 half sashay, left allemande
