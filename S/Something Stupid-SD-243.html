Something Stupid   (Shakedown 243 )   Tim Pepper

OPENER



Sides face grand square
I know I'd stand in line until you think you have the time to spend and evening with me
And if we go someplace to dance I know that there's a chance you won't be leaving with me
Allemande and Weave
And afterwards we'd drop into a quiet little palce and have a drink or two
(Promenade)
And then I go and spoil it all by saying something stupid like "I love you"


FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Head square thru four hands around and then you dosado
Swing thru, boys trade, boy run right, bend the line,
Boy walk - girl dodge, Hinge, fan the top, slide thru,
Swing your corner and all promenade
And then I go and spoil it all by saying something stupid like "I love you"

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies chain turn the girl, rollaway and circle left
Four ladies rollaway circle left, allemande and weave
I can see it in your eyes that you despise the same old lies you heard the night before
(Promenade)
And though it's just a line to you for me it's true and never seemed so right before

CLOSER



Sides face grand square
I practice evry day to find some clever lines to say to make the meaning come true
But then I think I'll wait until the evening gets late and I'm alone with you
Allemande and Weave
The time is right, your perfume fills my head, the stars get red and oh the night's so blue
(Promenade)
