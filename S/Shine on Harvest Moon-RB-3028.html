Shine On Harvest Moon
Harry Macdonough & Miss Walton; Ruth Etting 1908
   (Red Boot 3028)    Don Williamson
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    B - SSD Week 1

Circle left
Shine on, shine on harvest moon
Up in the sky
Left allemande , dosado and then the
Men star by the left it's once around the ring you go
Turn partner right , left allemande
You swing and you promenade your pal
So shine on, shine on harvest moon
For me and my gal

FIGURE (1)    Mainstream, corner progression

Well the heads square thru 4 hands around
Meet the sides and make a right hand star
Well the heads star left all the way around
Meet the corner then you touch 1/4 here
Scoot back and then scoot back again
Star thru and promenade your pal
So shine on, shine on harvest moon
For me and my gal

FIGURE (2)    Plus, corner progression

Heads square thru 4 hands around
Meet the sides and do the right & left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel
Everybody double pass thru , track two
All 8 circulate twice around the ring
Swing and promenade your pal
So shine on, shine on harvest moon
For me and my gal

Contains a "blended" movement: Scoot Back followed by Star Thru.
