Sweet Mable Mixer
   (Kalox 1213)    C.O. Guest
 
SETUP FOR MIXER
Couples in Open position with inside hands joined facing LOD (CCW) around hall

FOOTWORK
Opposite, directions for man

DANCE

Walk 4
Back up 4
Walk 4
Back up 4
Face and back away 3 and close
Dosado in 8 steps
Balance forward and back
Turn half by the right
Balance forward and back
Turn by the left 8 short steps
Right & left grand to the 5th girl
Swing the 5th girl 2 times

(Dance goes through 7 times plus ending of Walk 4 and bow to the girl)
