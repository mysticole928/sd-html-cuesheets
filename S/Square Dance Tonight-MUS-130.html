FIGURE



1 & 3 a half square thru, split the outside pair
Round 1 to a line of 4, walk up and back from there
Pass thru, bend the line, box the gnat for me
Do the right & left thru, turn the girl and square thru you see
3/4 round to the corner girl, swing the corner there
Swing the pretty girl and then you promenade that square
Turned up nose and square dance clothes, she'll be all right
Take your pretty baby to a square dance tonight

ALTERNATE FIGURE (for advanced dancers)

Go full around and then, you swing thru you know
Boys run to the right, tag the line and turn to the right
Wheel & deal to face those 2, and then you box the gnat
Right & left thru, turn 'em on around, you dive thru and go
Pass thru, swing the corner girl and promeno
