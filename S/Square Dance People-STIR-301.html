Square Dance People
   (Stirrup 301)    Ivan Midlam
OPENER - CLOSER    Basic

Circle left
There are people you meet everywhere you go
Some bad ones, some sad ones, some are fine
Well walk out around the corner , see saw round your own
Do a left allemande and weave the ring in time
You meet people of all kinds every single day
Do a dosado and promenade along
*Yes, but of the people I meet summer, spring or fall
Square dance people are the greatest ones of all

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*Friendship and love, summer, spring or fall

*It's happiness and good times, summer, spring or fall

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads square thru and get me four hands around
Do a dosado with corner lady there
You swing thru tonight, boys run to the right
Tag the line , do a cloverleaf around
Four girls square thru 3 hands, find the corner, swing
You swing this one and then you promenade
*Prettiest girls, biggest smiles, summer spring or fall
Square dance people are the greatest ones of all

MIDDLE BREAK    B - SSD Week 2

Right now those four little ladies chain and go straight across that ring
Hey join hands circle left I sing
Ladies in the men sashay , circle left and hear me say
Ladies in the men sashay , left allemande , weave the ring
Weave in and out you go, meetin' happy square dance people
Do a dosado and promenade along
*But of the people I meet summer, spring or fall
Square dance people are the greatest ones of all

TAG

Well you bow to partners, corners all
And whoa that's all!
