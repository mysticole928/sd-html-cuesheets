Singing And Swinging
   (Lightning S 901)    Pancho Baird
 
OPENER    Basic

Honor to your partner
All join hands and circle left
4 couples do a dopaso
Corner right from where you are
Partner left and the ladies star
About 3/4 round the ring you go
Allemande left your corner
Grand right & left the circle
Meet and promenade your pretty Sue
If you tell her she's a lady
She'll be no one else's baby
While she's singing and swinging with you

FIGURE    Basic, R-H lady progression

Allemande left your corner, dosado your partner
Head 2 couples make a right hand star once around
Now star left with the corner pair, once around your turn it there
Same head couples center with a right hand star
Allemande left your corner, walk on by your partner
Swing the right hand girl and promenade
Treat her nice and easy but don't you get too squeezy
While she's singing and swinging with you

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

4 ladies chain right over, left hand turn that opposite feller
Head two ladies chain across the ring
Same two couples up and back , dixie chain across the track
Both turn left , the sides pass thru and then allemande left
Grand old right & left you go, meet your gal and meet your beau
Promenade that lady two by two
Treat her like a lady, she's no one else's baby
While she's singing and swinging with you
