Swing Along    (GSR 6003)   Bill Castner

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Head two couples swing and whirl, same two star thru
Right & left thru and turn your girl, and then you substitute
Star thru, right & left thru, turn your girl and then
Star thru, substitute, right & left thru again
Substitute one time now, substitute again
California whirl, left allemande, grand right & left and then
*Grand right & left and promenade, and sing with me this song
If you want to dance and have some fun, you'd better swing along

FIGURE



Head two couples go up and back, same two ladies chain
Turn your girl, lead to the right, circle 'bout halfway
Dive thru, pass thru, split two round one
Star by the right in the middle of the ring, and don't you dare to run
Star by the left with the corner pair, go one full turn you roam
Men turn back and swing the corner and promenade her home
You promenade around the hall and listen to me sing
If you want to dance and have some fun, she's the girl to swing
