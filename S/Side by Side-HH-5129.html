Side By Side   (Hi Hat 5129 )   Ernie Kinney

OPENER



Circle left
Oh! We ain't got a barrel of money
Maybe we're ragged and funny
Left allemande..docey your own
Men star left one time
Turn your partner by the right & go left allemande
Come back, swing your baby, promenade the land
And we travel along..singing our song
Side by side

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade go 1/2 way round
Into the middle, square thru go 4
Go all the way, go right and left thru
Veer to the left & then you ferris wheel you do
Centers square thru & count 3/4 you do
Swing the corner lady and promenade you two
And we travel along..singing our song
Side by side

BREAK



Circle left

Side by side

CLOSER



Circle left
When they've all had their troubles and parted
We'll be the same as we started
Left allemande..docey your own
Men star left one time
Turn your partner by the right & go left allemande
Come back, swing your baby, promenade the land
But we travel the road
Sharin' our load
Side by side
