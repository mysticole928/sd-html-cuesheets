Double The Dose / Spinning Wheel
   (Old Timer 8016 side B )    Johnny Melton
 
FIGURE

First and third bow, and then you swing
Now lead right out to the right of the ring
Circle half, don't you blunder
Inside arch and outside under
Right & left thru and then turn back
Chain the ladies in the center of the floor
Then turn and chain with the outside four
Swing that lady and all promenade
Now promenade single file, lady in the lead and gents run wild
It's a spinning wheel, so roll it along
But turn right back, you're goin' wrong
Gents reach back with your left hand
And docey doe in the promised land
Docey high and docey low, hurry up cowboys, don't be slow
And promenade with a heel and a toe

(Repeat same couple again and then twice for 2 & 4)
