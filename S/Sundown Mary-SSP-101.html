Sundown Mary
(Strato-Sound 101) Jim Wood
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

All four ladies chain now 3/4 round the ring
Then all join hands and circle to the left around I sing
All four ladies roll away and circle to the left and then
Do an allemande left with the corner girl, go weaving round the ring
What have you heard from Sundown Mary, what has she done today?
Do a dosado then allemande left and promenade I sing
Is she in the chips in Texas or stranded broke in Maine?
Do you know if Sundown Mary ever speaks my name?

FIGURE

Now the head two couples promenade halfway around the ring
Down the middle with a dosado full around and then
Star thru and a frontier twirl , then do a right & left thru
Slide thru and square the barge four hands around you do
What have you heard from Sundown Mary, what has she done today?
Swing and whirl with the corner girl and promenade I say
Is she in the chips in Texas or stranded broke in Maine?
Do you know if Sundown Mary ever speaks my name?
