Saguaro Whirl / Inside Arch Outside Under
(Old Timer 8019 side A ) Joe Boykin
FIGURE

Now four gents swing with a right hand swing
Grand chain left across the ring
Meet your opposite turn and whirl half around that pretty little girl
Right hand high that lady there
Grand chain left across the square
Turn and whirl and don't be late
Form a ring and circle eight
First and third break to the right
Form two rings and make it right
Half sashay and around you go
Right & left thru and a docey doe
Partner left, opposite right
Partner left and promenade that pretty girl home

(Repeat three more changes - once for heads and two times for sides)
