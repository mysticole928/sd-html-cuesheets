A Second Fling   (Buckskin 1257 )   Rick Allison

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies promenade, halfway and then
Four ladies chain across you know
You turn the girl and then, chain her right back home you go
Join hands circle to the left 'round that ring
You circle left, left allemande
Swing your girl and promenade
Come on and let your hair down, we'll go up town
And have ourselves a second fling!

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



The heads square thru, four hands around you do
To the outside two touch 1/4
Boys scoot, girls dodge, and a partner trade
A right and left thru, turn that girl
Pass the ocean then, swing thru my friend
Boys trade, swing and promenade
Come on and let your hair down, we'll have a hoedown
And have ourselves a second fling!
