She's A Dancin' Swingin' Rose    (BS 1745)   Larry Faught

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands circle 8 go round
Reverse back single file go round
Girls backtrack that ring 2 times around I sing
Second time gents backtrack behind your own
Girls roll out, corners all left allemande
Grand old right & left then promenade the land
Go walkin' heel and toe, everybody knows
She's a dancin', swingin' rose

FIGURE



Side ladies chain across I say
Head couples promenade halfway
Lead to the right, circle 4, heads break a line of 4
Forward 8 come on back, pass thru
Wheel & deal, substitute, square thru 3
3/4 round then swing the outside 2
Well promenade, you know walk on the heel and toe
She's a dancin', swingin' rose
