FIGURE

Heads to the right and circle like you used to do
Break to a line, go forward eight and then you fall back
Rollaway with a 1/2 sashay, pass through across that track
You turn to the left, go single file, and don'tcha be late
The gals turn back and when you meet, you dosado all eight
Now promenade this lady to the Golden Gate
Catch a painted pony, let the spinning wheels spin
