Shanty In Old Shanty Town   (Top 25193 )   Paul Hartman

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande left your corner, go forward 3 you know
Go right, left, right, then turn back 1 & allemande left & go
Forward 3 once again, go right, left, right, my friend
Then turn back one, left allemande and weave around the land
Go in and out around until you meet your lady fair
Dosado your partner and promenade the square
There's a queen waiting there with a silvery crown
Let's swing her in ol' Shantytown

FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain, 3/4 'round you go
And 1 & 3 (2 & 4) lead to the right & circle 4 you know
Yes, you circle to a line, go up and back in time
You do a right & left thru, turn the girl and slide thru
Dosado, go once around, now swing thru and then
Boys trade, girls turn back and promenade the land
There's a queen waiting there with a silvery crown
Let's swing her in ol' Shantytown

ALTERNATE BREAK

4 ladies chain, go across the ring
Join your hands and circle left I sing
Ladies center, the men sashay
Circle left till you hear me say
Ladies center, the men sashay
Left allemande, weave the ring
Go in and out around until you meet your lady fair
Dosado your partner and promenade the square
There's a queen waiting there with a silvery crown
Let's swing her in ol' Shantytown

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Heads promenade 3/4 round
Sides will do a right and left thru
Pass thru and circle 4, 1/2 way round and 1/4 more
Go right and left thru, turn the girl and then cross trail
Allemande left that corner maid, walk by the one you know
Swing the right hand lady once & promenade her home
There's a queen waiting there with a silvery crown
Let's swing her in ol' Shantytown
