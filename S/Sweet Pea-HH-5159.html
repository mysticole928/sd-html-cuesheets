FIGURE



Head two you promenade and go halfway
Side two go right & left thru and turn the girl I say
Square thru and travel, go four hands around
Dosado that corner, do an eight chain four
Singin' oh, Sweet Pea, won't you dance with me?
Swing that corner, promenade
Oh, Sweet Pea, won't you be my girl
Won't you, won't you, won't you be my girl?

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade inside that old ring
Back home and swing your man around
You join hands, circle to the left, go round the ring
Left allemande, come on, weave the ring
Singin' oh, Sweet Pea, won't you dance with me?
Swing that girl and promenade
Oh, Sweet Pea, won't you be my girl
Won't you, won't you, won't you be my girl?
