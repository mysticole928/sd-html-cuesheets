Steel Guitar Rag
(Keeno 2220) Harold Bausch
FIGURE (four times through)

Everybody bow , you give 'em a swing
(Well) Now a left hand round your corner
And go twice around that pretty little thing
Dosado your partner
Heads (sides) step to the middle of the set
Star thru , same lady swing
Swing, you're not through yet
Now face the sides , and a right & left thru
Turn your gal and dive thru
Square thru 3/4 round
Swing your corner lady round
Promenade come a two by two
Take that lady home with you
(And) Swing 'em to that old Steel Guitar Rag

Now those 4 little ladies chain across
You turn that opposite man
Star right back across the set
Turn your honey again
Dosado that corner gal
Seesaw round your own
* And listen to that old Steel Guitar Rag
