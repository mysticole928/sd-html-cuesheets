San Antonio Rose
Bob Wills and His Playboys; Merle Haggard; Johnny Cash; John Denver; many others 1938
   (Folkraft 1272)    Floyd Parker
VISITING COUPLES FIGURE

INTRO

Oh you swing them boys, swing them high and low
Allemande left the old left hand, around the ring you go
A grand old right and left boys, now take your lady home
And swing with your Rose of San Antone

PATTERN

First couple out, circle four and smile
Dosado your partner, in that good old *mountain style
Two-hand swing your opposite till you find your own
Then swing with your Rose of San Antone

On to the next, circle four and smile
Dosado your partner, in that good old *mountain style
Two-hand swing your opposite till you find your own
Then swing with your Rose of San Antone

On to the last, circle four and smile
Dosado your partner, in that good old *mountain style
Two-hand swing your opposite till you find your own
Then swing with your Rose of San Antone

All swing your corner, the lady on the left
Right back home and swing the girl you love the best
Allemande left on the corner, dosado your own
Balance to your honey and you weave that ring back home

Weave that ring, in and out you go
Dosado your partner, your partner dosado
Weave that ring until you get back home
Then swing with your Rose of San Antone

(Repeat the entire PATTERN, with #2, #3, and #4 leading in turn.)

(Note: "Mountain Style": Man retaining hold of partner's left hand,
stands or kneels in place as he leads his partner in front and around
him, raising the joined hands overhead as she walks once around.)
