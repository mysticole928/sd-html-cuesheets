Sugar Babe    (MAC 952)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



1 & 3 go forward up and come on back with you
Turn opposite right a right hand round, then partner left you do
Same 2 go right & left thru, turn the girl, it's up to you
Then 2 & 4 cross trail, find the corner girl
Allemande your corner and a grand ol' right & left
Keep the beat a-movin' on until you find your pet
*Promenade around the hall, walking with your square dance doll
That's honey, sugar, babe

FIGURE



Head 2 couples square thru 3/4 with your girl
When you're facing out, stop, then California twirl
Right & left thru across the set, turn your girl, you're not through yet
Cross trail and swing that corner Sue
Allemande your corner, and a grand ol' right & left
When you meet your sugar babe, promenade the set
*Promenade that Georgia peach dancing with a Dixie beat
That's honey, sugar, babe
