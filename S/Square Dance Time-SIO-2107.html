Cotton Candy / Square Dance Time
   (Sets In Order 2107 side B )    Lee Helsel
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

Allemande your corner, walk on by your own
Right hand round the right hand girl, left hand round at home
Gents star right in the middle once and hear me sing
Allemande left your corner, then you weave the ring
In and out around the circle till you meet your maid
Dosado your partner, then you promenade
Promenade the circle, promenade the ring
Take her home and settle down, it's square dance time again

FIGURE   

Head (side) two couples bow, go forward up and back
Pass thru go round one in the middle, box the gnat
Face in the middle, right & left thru, turn back half, square thru
Face the sides (heads), right & left thru, turn that girl around
Dive thru, pass thru, allemande the girl you see
Right hand round your partner, the corner box the flea
Promenade this girl around, promenade the ring
Take her home and settle down, it's square dance time again
