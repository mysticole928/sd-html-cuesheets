Starry Eyes    (WW 129)   Don Franklin

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain, turn a little girl and then
Roll away and circle left and go walking around my friend
Ladies roll away and circle left and now
Left allemande and weave the ring somehow
In and out around and when you meet your beau
Swing your girl and promenade
Promenade this girl, take her home and don't be shy
But beware of the girl with starry eyes

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway around
Down the center, square thru 4 hands around
All the way around and then you circle 4 halfway
Veer to the left and the couples circulate one time
Wheel & deal and now, dive thru and square thru 3/4 around
And swing your corner there, promenade this girl
Take her home and don't be shy
But beware of the girl with starry eyes
