Spring Fever
   (MacGregor 301)    Don Ward
 
INTRO: (16 counts, "join hands in long lines")
1-8 Long lines forward and back
9-16 Left hand lady swing (End the swing facing across the set with left hand lady on man's right side.)
17-24 Men star left 1 1/2 (end star facing your partner.)
24-32 Partner swing (End the swing facing across the set.)
33-40 With couple across, circle left
41-48 Left hand star
49-56 Partner half promenade (to man's original line.)
57-64 Ladies chain across (Courtesy turn to long lines.)
Repeat the dance
