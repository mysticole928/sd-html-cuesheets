Slip Sliding Away    (RYL 144)   Jerry Story

OPENER



Circle left
Slip sliding away, slip sliding away
Allemande your corner, turn your partner by the right
Left allemande and weave the ring
Slip sliding away
Swing your partner, promenade
You know the nearer your destination
The more you slip sliding away

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway
Walk in and square thru 4
Go all the way and do a right & left thru
Veer left, ferris wheel
Square thru 3
Swing the corner and promenade
You know the nearer your destination
The more you slip sliding away
