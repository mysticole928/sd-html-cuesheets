Square Dance Saturday Night
   (Red Boot Star 1289)    Red Bates
OPENER    Basic

Hey, you walk around that corner , come back a dopaso
Her by the left, and corner by the right, partner left
Well men wheel in like an allemande thar you back on down
Slip the clutch , left allemande and weave that old ring
Oh, square dance Saturday night
Dosado and you promenade around tonight
I got a dollar in my pocket and a lady on the right
It's a square dance Saturday night

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)    Plus, corner progression

Head (side) two couples square thru and get me 4 you know
Without a stop a right & left thru turn the girl and go
Pass thru and trade by , swing thru right there
Boys run right , and couples circulate
Well do a half tag , trade and roll and the corner lady swing
Swing the lady around, you promenade around that ring
I got a dollar in my pocket and a lady on the right
It's a square dance Saturday night
