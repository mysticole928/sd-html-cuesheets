Sioux City Sue
Dick Thomas; Gene Autrey; many others 1945
   (Dance Ranch 667)    Al Stevens
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
I drove a herd of cattle down from old Nebraska way
That's how I got to be in the state of Iowa ("Ioway")
I met a girl from Iowa, she had eyes of blue
I asked her what her name was
Circle to the left you do
Sioux City Sue...Sioux City Sioux (or yodel during the repeat)
Do a dosado (partner) and then, promenade we're gone again
Sioux City Sue...Sioux City Sue (or yodel)
There ain't no gal as true as my sweet Sioux City Sioux

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) you promenade and go halfway the ring
Two and four go right & left thru , turn the girl I sing
Square thru and you get 4 hands around the ring you know
You do a dosado , then do the eight chain 4
Sioux City Sioux (yodel)
Swing that corner girl, allemande left and promenade
Sioux City Sioux (yodel)
There ain't no gal as true as my sweet Sioux City Sioux

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

4 ladies promenade go inside the ring
Get back home and then you give your man a swing
Join up your hands and you circle left for me
Left allemande , hey weave the ring you see
Sioux City Sioux (yodel)
Dosado and then promenade we're gone again
Sioux City Sioux (yodel)
There ain't no gal as true as my sweet Sioux City Sioux
