Shindig In The Barn    (WW 921)   Bob Ruff

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



all 4 ladies chain, you chain across the ring
turn & chain them back again
join all of your hands & circle to the left
allemande left your corner, grand right and left
right & left you go until you meet her
dosado your gal and promenade
promenade the ring, lift your heads up and sing
We're going to have a shindig in the barn

FIGURE



1&3 (2&4) lead to the right and circle 4
heads break & make a line, go up & back
do the right & left thru, turn the girls and then
same two right and left thru again
allemande left with the corner, your partner dosado
take your corner lady, promenade
promenade the ring, lift your heads up and sing
We're going to have a shindig in the barn

TAG



allemande left your corner, come back & bow to the partner
We're going to have a shindig in the barn
