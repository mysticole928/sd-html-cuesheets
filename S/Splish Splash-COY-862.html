Splish Splash
Bobby Darin 1958
(Coyote 862) Mike Seastrom
OPENER

Sides face grand square
Splish Splash, I was taking a bath
Long about a Saturday night
A rub dub, just relaxing in the tub
Thinking everything was right , Allemande and you weave
I stepped out the tub, put my feet on the floor
Wrapped the towel , you swing and promenade , opened the door
Splish Splash, I jumped back in the bath
How was I to know there was a party going on?

FIGURE

Heads promenade you travel half-way in time
Lead right and circle four , make a line
Go forward up and back and do the right & left thru
Now flutter wheel across, sweep 1/4 too
Pass thru and trade by and go allemande
Walk back one and promenade you walk hand in hand
*Splish Splash, I jumped back in the bath
How was I to know there was a party going on?

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*Splish Splash, I forgot about the bath
I went and put my dancing shoes on

MIDDLE BREAK

Heads face grand square
Bing Bang, I saw the whole gang
Dancing on my living room floor
Flip Flop, they was doing the bop
They were all dancing there , Allemande and weave the ring
There was Lollipop with a Peggy Sue
Good Golly Miss Molly , Swing and promenade her too
Splish Splash, I forgot about the bath
I went and put my dancing shoes on

CLOSER

4 boys you promenade go inside the ring
Back home and swing Miss Molly go round
Join your hands and circle to the left round I sing
Left allemande come on and weave round the ring
There was Lollipop with a Peggy Sue
Good Golly Miss Molly , Swing and promenade her, too
Splish Splash, I forgot about the bath
I went and put my dancing shoes on

TAG
