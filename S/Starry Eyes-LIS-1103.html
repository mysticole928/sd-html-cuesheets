Starry Eyes
   (Lightning S 1103)    Don Franklin
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

Allemande left your corner, go back and promenade
1 & 3 you wheel around , and dosado this maid
All the way round, make an ocean wave , balance up and back
Arch in the middle, ends duck thru , men turn back
Snap-a-roo *, then square thru 3/4 round you go
Allemande left your corner, come back and dosado
Promenade this girl, take her home and don't be shy
But beware of the girl with "starry eyes"

FIGURE    A1, corner progression

1 & 3 go up and back and do a right & left thru
Cross trail and go to your corner , do a snap-a-roo *
Pass thru , wheel & deal , then double pass thru
First couple left, the next go right , then do a right & left thru
Turn this girl, then snap-a-roo *, now swing that corner one
Allemande left new corner, promenade the one you swung
Promenade this girl, now hurry don't be shy
But beware of the girl with "starry eyes"

*Snap-a-roo: (also known as Star Thru)
