Slippin' Around    (RA 1354)   Virgil Troxell

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain across, you turn the girl and then
Join hands and circle left go walkin' round my friend
Do an allemande left, go forward two again
Left and right, turn back one, left allemande
Dosado your partner now, promenade that ring
Promenade your lady, go walkin' round for me
And I won't have to slip around to have your company

FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) you square thru 4 hands around you go
To the corner, dosado go full around you know
Swing thru, girls circulate, boys you trade and then
Run to the right and bend the line, go up and back again
Put a right hand up and star the square*, go walkin' round
Swing the corner lady now, promenade the ring
You promenade this lady, go walkin' round for me
And I won't have to slip around to have your company

*Star the Square: Right hand up, star halfway around, drop hands, girls turn back, 1/2 square thru.



Square thru can be substituted for Star the Square.
