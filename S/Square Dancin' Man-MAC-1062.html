Square Dancin' Man    (MAC 1062)   Chuck Raley

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies circle in the center
Full around and swing with your man
Join all your hands and then you circle

Come on and let's get on with the square dance

FIGURE



Heads lead right and you circle to a line
Without a stop you do a right & left thru
Go forward up and back, star thru across the track
Dosado to an ocean wave and rock it up and back
Eight chain 5 across the ring now
Pull the lady by and then you swing
Promenade go hand in hand
Cause I'm a square dancing man
