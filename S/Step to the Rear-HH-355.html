Step To The Rear    (HH 355)   Dick Houlton

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain you turn 'em and then
Join your hands, circle left around you go
Allemande your corner, do an allemande thar
Go right, go left, men back up with a right hand star
Shoot that star, turn thru, left allemande
Come back one, dosado, promenade
Well everyone here, kindly step to the rear
And let a winner lead the way

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway round with your maid
4 ladies chain 3/4 around
1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around you go
Meet a girl, dosado full around the girl you know
Swing thru, turn thru, find a corner girl
Left allemande, come back, promenade
Well everyone here, kindly step to the rear
And let a winner lead the way
