Star & Promenade Contra    (TNT 201)   Al Brundage

FORMATION: DUPLE (Odds Improper: 1st, 3rd, 5th, and every other couple cross over)



1. Face your corner, dosado

2. Same four right hand star (full around)



3. Gents move up and promenade

4. Wheel around and promenade back



5. Flutter wheel

6. Sweep 1/4 more, pass thru, star thru



7. Circle four (to the left, full around)

8. Circle right (full around)
