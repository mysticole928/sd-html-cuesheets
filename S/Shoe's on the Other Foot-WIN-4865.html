Shoe's On The Other Foot
(Windsor 4865) Chuck Raley
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join hands and circle go left around the ring
Left allemande and then go forward 2
Turn back one and box the gnat , men star left
Dosado and weave around the ring
Wind in and out around until you meet your own
Dosado and promenade her home
Tonight I'll throw my heartaches out of sight
For the shoe goes on the other foot tonight

FIGURE

Heads (sides) lead to the right & circle to a line
Without a stop, do a right and left thru
Go forward, back you reel
Pass thru and wheel and deal
Centers dosado , go full around, spin the top
When you meet this girl, star thru
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Tonight I'll throw my heartaches out of sight
For the shoe goes on the other foot tonight

Contains a "blended" movement: Spin The Top followed by Star Thru.
