Square Dance Jubilee    (TOP 25048)   Chip Hendrickson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left with the corner girl, and then go forward 2
With a right & left and turn back 1, and now you box the gnat
Dosado that girl, all the gentlemen left hand star
Just once around, meet her again and dosado once more
Allemande left your corner, well come on home and swing
Hey swing your partner round 'n' round and take a walk again
Oh promenade with me, as happy as can be
Roll it man, and home you go, we'll have a jubilee

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across the set, now turn 'em one by one
Oh chain 'em back and turn them, turn them when you're done
Dosado the corner, come home and then see saw
Gents star right across the set and turn that opposite left
Like ye allemande thar, well you back on in, and roll along awhile
Now shoot the star and swing the next young lady, and you smile
Oh promenade 'em all, go rollin' round the hall
Take your lady home again, and have a jubilee
