Sweet Georgia Quadrille    (SIO 121)   Bob Osgood

FIGURE (Repeat 4 times thru)



One and three right and left thru
Head ladies chain
Two and four right and left thru
Side ladies chain
All eight, circle to the left
Turn the corner lady by the left
Wheel promenade, keep her...go all the way

TAG



Bow to your partner...bow to your corner
