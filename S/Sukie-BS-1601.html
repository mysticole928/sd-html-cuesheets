Sukie    (BS 1601)   Buford Evans

FIGURE



Allemande your corner, go home and you swing
Swing, then 4 ladies promenade that old ring
Pass your man, corner swing one time around
Allemande left have a little fun, go right & left, you turn back one
Go back to that corner, catch all 8, don't be late
Go back by the left, 4 ladies 3/4 chain
Turn this gal and promenade, don't you slow down
We're going to Chinatown
Heads wheel around, star thru and then
Right & left thru, turn 'em, star thru again
Right & left thru, turn 'em, go forward up and back
Cross trail thru across that set, allemande that corner pet
Dosado your partner, the corner you swing
Left allemande, promenade them again
*Take little Sukie to the square dance hall
Swing with that China doll

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*Take little Sukie, go round that old ring
Swing with your gal from Beijing
