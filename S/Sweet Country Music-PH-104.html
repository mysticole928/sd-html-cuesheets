Sweet Country Music    (PH 104)   Jim Snyder

OPENER -- CLOSER



Sides face, grand spin
Singing sweet country music
Breathing clean mountain air
Swinging sweet country music
Makes me wish that I was there

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around, lead to the right
Circle 4 and make a line, curlique and then
Coordinate my friend, men move up and bend your line
Star thru, pass thru, trade by, swing your lady
Allemande left and promenade
Singing sweet country music
Makes me wish that I was there

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
Will the circle be unbroken, by and by Lord, by and by
There's a better world a-waiting in the sky, Lord, in the sky
4 ladies promenade, swing that lady round and promenade
Singing sweet country music
Makes me wish that I was there

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway go round
Side pair do a right & left thru
Squrae thru and go 4 hands you know
Dosado and do an eight chain 4
Singing sweet country music
Swing the corner, promenade
Singing sweet country music
Makes me wish that I was there
