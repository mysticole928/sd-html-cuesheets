Sleepy Time Gal    (SIO 110)   Bob Page

FIGURE



Four ladies chain, you'll turn them
Chain them again, heads go forward and dosado, too
Right 7 left thru and turn them you do
Sides cross trail
All eight chain
Join your hands, circle left, circle left, circle left
Walk all around that corner girl, turn your own by the left
The men star right, that corner's waiting there
Left allemande, dosado your honey and then
Men star left, star left and pass by your maid
Pick up the next girl, star promenade
Full turn you wheel on around
Four ladies chain (straight across), promenade all the way round
Promenade that play at home, stay at home, eight o'clock
Sleepy time gal

ALTERNATE FIGURE (first four lines only)



Four ladies chain, you'll turn them
Chain them again, heads balance and
Then you swing, promenade the ring
Halfway and then, sides cross trail

TAG



Yes, stay at home, play at home, eight o'clock
Sleepy time gal
