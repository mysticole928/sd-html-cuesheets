Summer Sounds
Robert Goulet 1965
(Bogan 1189) Cal Lambert
BREAK

Join hands and circle left around you go
Walk around the corner , turn the partner dopaso
Turn the corner by the right, the partner left hand swing
4 ladies star right 3/4 round that ring, then
Left allemande and dosado your own
Left allemande , come back, promenade
Summer sounds are all around
The summer sounds I love

FIGURE

4 ladies chain across and then the heads promenade
Halfway around while the sides square thru
4 hands around, then do a dosado
Make that ocean wave , all 8 circulate , then
Go right & left thru and that corner swing
Then promenade around that old ring
Summer sounds are all around
The summer sounds I love
