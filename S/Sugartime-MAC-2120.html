Sugartime
The McGuire Sisters 1957
   (MacGregor 2120)    Mike Hull
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    C4

All four ladies flutter wheel go full around I say
Join hands and circle left , go walking round that way
Do an allemande left in the alamo style , and swing thru and then
You curlique with your partner, boys run right my friend
Do an allemande left with the corner, come back and weave the ring
Weave in and out around that square and promenade and sing
Well, sugar in the morning, sugar in the evening, sugar at suppertime
You be my little honey and love me all the time

FIGURE   

1 & 3 go forward and back and square thru I say
You find the corner lady and curl to a wave
Boys trade and boys run , bend the line for me
You flutter wheel , across there, make lines of four you see
You square thru 3/4 , find the corner, ladies swing
You swing and whirl the corner girl and promenade the ring
Well, sugar in the morning, sugar in the evening, sugar at suppertime
You be my little honey and love me all the time
