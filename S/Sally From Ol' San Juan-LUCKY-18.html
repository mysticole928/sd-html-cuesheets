Sally From Ol' San Juan    (LUCKY 18)   John Shallow

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade inside that old ring
Box the gnat and do a little dosado
Join your hands and circle round that old ring
Left allemande the corner girl and weave around I sing
Go in and out and when you meet a dosado
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Promenade her along, she will dance until dawn
Cause she's Sally from ol' San Juan

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around that old ring
Get to the corner girl and squash the bug* (clap 4)
Single circle to an ocean wave, boys run to the right
Bend the line, go up and back and then star thru
You dive thru and square thru 3/4 round
Swing the corner girl and promenade
She will sing us a song, promenading along
Cause she's Sally from ol' San Juan

*Note: Squash the bug is from facing couples. Clap own hands twice, pat hands twice with the person you face and circle 1/2, walk ahead to form right hand wave (boys in them iddle as used above).
