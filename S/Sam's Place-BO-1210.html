Sam's Place    (BO 1210)   Cal Lambert

BREAK



Join up hands, circle left around to Sam's place
Left allemande the corner, then go forward two
Turn back one and box the gnat, you box it back and then
Pull by, left allemande, and weave that ring
*There's old shimmy-shakin' Tina, she hails from Pasadena
She's always got a big smile on her face
Promenade
There's old hootchie-kootchie Hattie, she comes from Cincinatti
Just to swing tonight at Sam's place

FIGURE



Head 2 go right & left thru and turn 'em
Sides cross trail around just one, 4 in line,
go up and back, then do the right & left thru
Star thru, then do an eight chain 4
*There's old slippin'-slidin' Lucy, she comes from Opelousas
Swing the corner girl and promenade
There's old two-timin' Tammie, she comes here from Miami
Just to swing tonight at Sam's place
