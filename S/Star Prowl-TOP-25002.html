Star Prowl
John Collinson and Russell Callow; Slim Dusty 1895
(Top 25002) Lloyd Litman
A

Sashay round your corner maid, come back one and promenade
Promenade Matilda , but don't you dare slow down
Head 2 couples wheel around , right & left thru with those you found
Full turn round to the next old two and cross trail thru
Allemande left your corner, allemande right your partner
Box the flea with the corner, with her you promenade
Promenade home you go, balance out and dosado
Sides back to back and all star prowl* (go)

B

1, 2, 3, turn; 1, 2, 3, turn
1, 2, 3, turn; 1, 2, 3, turn
1, 2, 3, turn; 1, 2, 3, turn
1, 2, 3, turn; 1, 2, 3, turn

C

Allemande left , 4 ladies star , gents run around the outside bar
Allemande left the gentlemen star , Matildas ( ladies ) promenade
Allemande left same corner maid, take your own and promenade
Promenade Matilda, come dancing with me

(Dance sequence: ABC, ABC, ABC, A)

*Star Prowl: a movement like grand prowl, except when dancers meet in the center of the square they do a star thru, then back away. Callers could substitute Grand Square or Grand Prowl.
