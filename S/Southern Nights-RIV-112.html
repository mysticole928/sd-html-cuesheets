Southern Nights    (RIV 112)   Keith Gylfe

OPENER



Circle left
Southern nights - have you ever felt southern nights?
Left allemande, now partner dosado
Men star left, once around tonight
Turn thru and then, left allemande
Swing, promenade
Free as a breeze not to mention the trees
Whistling tunes that you know and love so

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands around
And now dosado
Curlique and then cast-off 3/4 round
Girls trade and do a right & left thru, turn 'em there
Flutter wheel and now, swing the corner
Left allemande, promenade
It's precious beauty lies just beyond the eye
Goes running through the soul like the stories told of old
