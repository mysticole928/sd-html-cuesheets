Sweet Georgia Brown    (MAC 986)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - BREAK



Join your hands and make a ring, circle left you know
Move it all the way around until you get back home
Allemande your corner girl and dosado your own
Men star by the ol' left hand, once inside the land
Dosado, go round your own, your corner allemande
Come back home and swing Miss Brown and promenade her home
Promenade this shapely miss, step right up and steal a kiss
Turned up nose and rolled down hose, that's sweet Georgia Brown

FIGURE



1 & 3 you promenade go halfway round the ring
Lead to the right and circle left just halfway round again
Dive thru and dosado to an ocean wave you know
Rock it forward up and back, swing thru you know
When you've balanced up and back, box the gnat, pull by
Swing that corner lady round and promenade the ring
Promenade this pretty miss, step right up and steal a kiss
Georgia named her, Georgia claimed her, sweet Georgia Brown
