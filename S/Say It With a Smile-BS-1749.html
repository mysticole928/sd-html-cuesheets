Say It With A Smile    (BS 1749)   Curly Custer

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk around that corner girl, see saw round your taw
Men star right one time round
Allemande that corner girl, weave that old ring now
In and out around that ring you go
Dosado with the partner, corners all left allemande
Come home and swing awhile, promenade that land
Promenade your livin' doll, get back home and swing awhile
You swing her with a smile

FIGURE



1 & 3 1/2 square thru, then dosado the outside 2
All the way make an ocean wave, and you rock it up and back
Then a right & left thru turn Sue, dive thru
Square thru 5 hands around that ring you go
Allemande left the corner, gonna walk on by the partner
Your right hand lady swing and whirl, promenade that ring
Promenade about a mile, get back home and swing awhile
You swing her with a smile
