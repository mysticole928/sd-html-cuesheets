So Long Dearie   (Chinook 085 )   Dan Nordbye

OPENER



Four ladies chain go cross the ring now
You roll away and circle left
Four ladies roll away, circle round now
Left allemande and you weave that ring
I was all dolled up and singing that song
Swing your girl and you promenade
Take your hat in hand and whisper, so long dearie
Dearie should have said so long, so long ago

FIGURE



Head couples promenade halfway around now
Into the middle and do the right & left thru
Square thru and count 'em four hands around now
Dosado that outside 2, star thru, pass thru, chase right and then
Swing your corner and promenade
Take your hat in hand and whisper, so long dearie
Dearie should have said so long, so long ago
