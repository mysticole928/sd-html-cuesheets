FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru and get 4 hands
Gonna meet your corner, touch 1/4
Follow your neighbor and spread, explode the wave
Bend the line, load the boat
Swing the corner, promenade, you promenade for me
You got me singing the blues

ALTERNATE FIGURE #1



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru, 4 hands around & a right & left thru
Veer to the left, ferris wheel, centers right & left thru
Veer to the left and then crossfire
Do a walk & dodge, swing the corner, you promenade her home
You got me singing the blues

ALTERNATE FIGURE #2



1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade go 1/2 around, into the middle and star thru
Pass thru & then dosado to waves, relay the deucey
Swing the corner, promenade, promenade her home
You got me singing the blues

ALTERNATE FIGURE #3



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru, 4 hands around and then
Touch 1/4, follow your neighbor and spread
Ladies trade, recycle, veer left
Couples circulate, ferris wheel & then zoom
Square thru 3/4 round, swing & ten you promenade her home
You got me singing the blues
