Pistol Packin' Mama / Spanish Cavalero
The Sons Of The Pioneers; Riley Puckett; many others 1881
   (Old Timer 8055 side B )    Cal Golden
 
OPENER    Basic - SSD Week 1

It's allemande left with the lady on the left
It's a grand right & left all the way around
When you meet your own, promenade her home
You promenade that gay cavalero

FIGURE   

It's first couple divide and circle three hands around
Now swing with that opposite girlie
And after you have swung, go back where you begun
Now swing with that gay cavalero
It's on to the next and circle four hand round
Now swing with the opposite girlie
Then after you have swung go back to where you begun
And swing with your own senorita
And there they divide and circle three hands round
Now swing with that opposite lady
After you have swung, go back where you begun
Everybody swing their Spanish cavalero

(Repeat OPENER and FIGURE for 2, 3, 4th couple.)
