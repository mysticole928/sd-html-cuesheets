Square Dance Honeymoon    (RA 1329)   Bill Wilson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left the corner girl, dosado your pet
4 ladies promenade go once around that set
Home you go with a dosado, your corner allemande
Come back one and promenade, go walking around that land
1 & 3 wheel around and do a right & left thru
Cross trail, find the corner, left allemande
Come back one and promenade in the merry month of June
Take her home and swing her on a square dance honeymoon

FIGURE



1 & 3 go up and back, then roll a 1/2 sashay
Box the gnat across, go right & left thru I say
Square thru 3 across from you, 3/4 around and then
Separate go round one, come into the middle again
Star thru, go right & left thru and turn that pretty maid
Cross trail, swing the corner, then you promenade
Promenade this gal around in the merry month of June
Take her home and swing her on a square dance honeymoon
