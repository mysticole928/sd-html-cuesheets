Split Your Corner
(Old Timer 8139 side A )
OPENER

You bow to your partner, corners all
Now circle to the left go around the hall
Circle to the left and around you go
Go into the center and make a little nest
Then rare right back and bust your vest
Circle to the left one more time
Allemande left with your left hand
Your partner by the right, a right & left grand
Hand over hand with every little maid
Gonna meet your partner and promenade
Promenade home with the pretty little dears
And whisper sweet things in her ears
Get back home and swing your baby
Go around and around with the pretty little lady

FIGURE

The 1st and 3rd go forward and back
Now forward again and split your corners and leave your taw
The ladies go gee (right) and the gents go haw (left)
Now sashay round your pretty little baby
Right elbow swing your corner lady
It's a left hand around your own little maid
Now pick up your corners and all promenade
Promenade go round the ring
While the geese fly over go high on the wing
Get back home and swing your girl
Go round and around with the pretty little girl

MIDDLE BREAK

Swing your corner go round and around
Now set 'em on your right and form a ring
Circle to the left like everything
Into the center and make a little nest
Rare right back and circle to the left
One more time and around you go
Now break it up like a dopaso
Your partner by the left
And your corners with a right
Now your partners left and hang on tight
Roll promenade, don't take all night
You promenade go round the ring
Get back home and give her a swing
Swing your baby go round and around

CLOSER

Swing your corners go round and around
Now set 'em on your right and form a ring
Circle to the left like everything
Now turn right back and circle to the south
And let a little moonshine out of your mouth
Go into the center and make a little nest
Then rare right back
Walk all around your left hand lady
See saw your pretty little taw
Now face your corners and allemande left with your left hand
Your partner by the right, a right & left grand
Hand over hand with every little maid
Gonna meet your partner and swing all 8
Swing your baby go round and around
Then promenade go around the town
Get back home with the pretty little dears
And whisper sweet things in her ears
Get back home and bow to your partners, your corners too
Now wave to the kids right across from you
And I think you kids 'cause we're all through
