Smile In Your Style
   (MacGregor 933)    Jerry Helt
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK    B - SSD Week 8

Allemande left in the alamo style
Right to partner, balance awhile
Balance in, balance out
Swing by the right hand half about
Balance out, balance in
Swing by the left hand half again
Balance in, balance out
Swing by the right hand half about
Balance out, balance in
Swing by the left hand half again
Balance forward and back you go
Partners all dosado
Promenade your partners back home you know
And dance with a smile in your style

FIGURE   

First and third , take your maid up in middle and back
Right & left thru , turn 'em around, cross trail back
Go round one into the middle , make a left hand star
Go to your corner, turn her right twice around but not too far
All four men star by the left once around the land
Same girl, right & left grand
Meet this lovin' maid, take her hand and promenade
And dance with a smile in your style

(Sequence: OPENER, FIGURE with heads, FIGURE with sides, MIDDLE BREAK,
FIGURE with sides, FIGURE with heads)
