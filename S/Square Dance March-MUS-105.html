Square Dance March    (MUS 105)   Snooky Brasher

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join hands and circle left, you circle round the ring
Allemande your corner, that partner you swing
Promenade, go single file, ladies in the lead
Girls step out and backtrack, go twice around that ring
Meet your partner, turn her by the right, allemande corners all
Dosado your own, promenade that hall
Promenade go round the ring, take your lady home again
And dance to the square dance march

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right and circle round that track
Head gents break and make a line, go marching up and back
Star thru and dive thru, a left square thru you go
3/4 round and star thru, circle (eight hands) round you go
Allemande left your corner girl, march right by your own
Swing and whirl with the right hand girl and promenade her home
Promenade go round the ring, take that lady home again
And dance to the square dance march
