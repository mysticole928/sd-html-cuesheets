Stand Up For Jesus
1858
(Double M 359) Glenn Wilson
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK

Circle left
Stand up, stand up for Jesus
Ye soldiers of the cross
The men star right one time tonight
Left allemande, weave the ring
From victory unto victory
Swing your lady there and promenade her
Let courage rise with danger
And strength to strength oppose

FIGURE

Heads square thru count four hands now
All the way around the ring and then
Swing thru, the boys run right
Couples circulate, chain down the line
Pass the ocean, all eight circulate
Boys run right, promenade her
And with the king of glory
Shall reign eternally

CLOSER

Circle left
Stand up, stand up for Jesus
Stand in his strength alone
The men star right one time tonight
Left allemande, weave the ring
From victory unto victory
His armies shall he lead
Till every foe is vanquished
And Christ is Lord indeed
