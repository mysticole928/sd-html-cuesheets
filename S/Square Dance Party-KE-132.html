Square Dance Party    (KE 132)   Harold Bausch

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 little ladies promenade inside that big old ring
Then dosado your partner, that corner girl you swing
Come on back to your own little girl, swing her there for me
What a square dance party this will be!

FIGURE



Head 2 couples square thru 3/4 round you go
Separate go round one, in the center dosado
Same two couples square thru 3, again 3/4 round
Circle 1/2 on the outside of town
And then you dive thru and pass thru, that corner girl you swing
Promenade this lady, go once around that ring
Oh, she thinks you're handsome, and you thrill her so
Take her home and swing her, make it so
Oh, yes! What a square dance party this will be!
