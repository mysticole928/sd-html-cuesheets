Square Dance Doll
(MIBS Recordings 5002) Bob & Zella Lewis
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join your hands and circle left around the ring for me
Swing that corner lady round and round
Allemande left , allemande thar , right and left and star
Four men back in and make a star
Shoot that star full turn you go, corners all dosado
Take your own and then promenade
Promenade around with your sweetheart then
Swing a real square dance doll

FIGURE

Head two couples star 3/4 round that track
Lead 'em to a line , go up and back
Pass thru , wheel & deal , double pass thru
First two left, next go right and cross trail thru
Now U-turn back and do a half square thru
Go right & left, that corner lady swing
Promenade around with this sweetheart
Cause she's a real square dance doll

Note: Lead to a line - same as Head Couples lead to the right,
Head Gents break to a line of four.

Callers Note: The following two lines can be substituted for
first two lines in figure.
Head two couples lead to the right, circle round the track
Head Gents break, make a line, go walking up and back

Vic says: Initial part of figure choreography is non-standard. Designated dancers actually star less than 3/4 (the boy goes 1/2). It's as if the dancers did a boy walk girl dodge, followed by 1/2 scoot back, step forward and a (fuzzy) 'circle to a line'.
