Square Dancing Man    (CBC 0803)  David Cox

OPENER



Circle Left
He wears a timex watch and a Rhinestone suit
Silver buckles and rattlesnake boots
Walk around your corner, see saw your own
Left allemande and weave it you know
He lives in a shack way down by the sea
Turn thru, allemande left and promenade with me
He ain’t got money
He’s a square dancing man – yeah!

FIGURE



One & three promenade, halfway I say
Walk in, pass the ocean, extend & the ladies trade
Swing thru, come two by two, the boys run right you see
Bend the line do the right and left thru
Turn ‘em on around and the ladies lead dixie style
Make a wave and the men will trade, recycle,
Veer to the right, promenade
He ain’t got money
He’s a square dancing man - yeah!

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies promenade roll it inside the set
Gonna meet your man, slide thru, join up hands, circle left
Men star right you roll it round you will go
Allemande left and weave the ring
He calls hot stuff and yet he looks so cool
Swing and promenade, he’s no fool
He smells of sweet success
He’s a square dancing man

CLOSER



Grand Square
You know ladies and gentlemen, when I became a square dancer caller
I thought it was going to be a case of big bucks and fast women
But it just hasn’t happened – in fact business has been so bad that
Even the tax office is sending me sympathy cards
(Four ladies promenade)
Roll it and go inside and then
You swing that man, promenade that ring
He smells of sweet success
He’s a square dancing man
