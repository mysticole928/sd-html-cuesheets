Say You'll Be Mine    (SD 314)   Pat Carnathan

OPENER



Circle to the left
Say you'll be mine, say you'll be mine until the sun shines
Left allemande and then dosado
Men star left inside the ring
Turn your partner by the right hand and go
And do a left allemande, swing and promenade
Say you'll be mine, and bring me the dream of a lifetime

FIGURE (Twice for heads, twice for sides)



Heads (Sides) touch 1/4, spread, sides (heads) pass the ocean
Everybody flip the diamond, centers trade
Girls trade, well you pass thru with a wheel & deal
In the middle, you pass thru, touch 1/4, scoot back
Swing a little girl around, all promenade the ring
Say you'll be mine, and bring me the dream of a lifetime

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
I just can't hide the truth no more
I gotta get my feet back down on the floor
It don't seem right, I should be alone tonight
Four ladies promenade go girls, inside the ring
Swing your partner, all promenade the ring
Say you'll be mine, and bring me the dream of a lifetime

CLOSER



Four boys promenade boys, inside the ring
Get back, swing your baby round and round
Join hands circle to the left you know
Left allemande, weave the ring
(Won't you) Say you'll be mine
Say you'll be mine until the sun shines
Say you'll be mine and bring me the dream of a lifetime
