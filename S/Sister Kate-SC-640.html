Sister Kate
(Scope 640)
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Four ladies chain go straight across that old ring
Rollaway and circle left I sing
Rollaway and circle go 'round
You allemande left and (buddy) weave the ring
Weave in and out till you meet your date
Dosado and promenade
Promenade your lady, it would be so great
If only she could shimmy like her sister Kate

FIGURE

Heads flutter wheel one time and then
Touch 1/4 , walk & dodge my friend
Swing thru and the men run right
Tag the line , face right , wheel & deal
Then turn thru and do a left allemande
Swing a new girl, promenade that land
Promenade your lady, it would be so great
If only she could shimmy like her sister Kate

TAG

Allemande left now bow to your date
