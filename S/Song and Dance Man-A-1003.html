OPENER    Basic - SSD Week 1

Four ladies promenade go rolling round the ring
Get on home and swing there with your beau
Join hands and circle left go rolling round you see
Allemande left and weave the ring for me
I'm just a song and dance man going from town to town
Swing your girl and promenade her home
I'm just a song and dance man, living on a smile
I'll share your laughter anywhere I can
