Song And Dance Man   (Aussie Tempos 1003 )   Kevin Kelly

OPENER



Four ladies circle left go rolling round the ring
Get on home and swing with your beau
Join you hands and circle left go rolling round you see
Allemande left and weave the ring for me
I'm just a song and dance man going from town to town
Swing your doll round and you promenade
I'm just a song and dance man living on a smile
I'll share your laughter anywhere I can (go)

FIGURE



Heads you promenade halfway round you see
Down the centre pass the ocean, extend the tag for me
Do a single hinge, scoot back and when you get straight
Single hinge and fan the top right there
Recycle two by two and then slide thru
Swing the corner girl and Promenade
I'm just a song and dance man living on a smile
I'll share your laughter anywhere I can (go)

MIDDLE (Musical Break)



Four ladies promenade get a walking round the ring
Gonna get on home and swing there with your partner
You join hands circle left, go rolling with her
Allemande the corner and weave the ring
I'm just a song and dance man going from town to town
Swing your doll round and you promenade
I'm just a song and dance man living on a smile
I'll share your laughter anywhere I can (go)
