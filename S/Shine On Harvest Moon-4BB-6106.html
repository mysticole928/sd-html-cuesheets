Shine On Harvest Moon
Harry Macdonough & Miss Walton; Ruth Etting 1908
   (4-Bar-B 6106)    Bill Volner
OPENER - CLOSER    B - SSD Week 11

Circle left
Shine on, shine on harvest moon up in the sky
Left allemande the corner, box the gnat at home
4 ladies star by the left , go round that old track
Box the gnat , go right & left grand , meet that girl
Swing awhile and then promenade home
Because I ain't had no lovin' since..
January, February, June or July

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) box the gnat & then you square thru 4
Sides you rollaway
Touch 1/4 then, scoot back , go girl
When you're thru, do a walk & dodge , partner trade
Reverse the flutter wheel , go a little bit more
Keep that girl, promenade her home
Because I ain't had no lovin' since..
January, February, June or July

MIDDLE BREAK   

4 ladies promenade , get back home
Swing awhile with this man
With the ladies back to back , boys promenade outside
Go all the way around, same girl, do a grand yellow rock
Left allemande , come back, swing awhile & promenade
Because I ain't had no lovin' since..
January, February, June or July
