Someday Soon    (PP 103)   Toots Richardson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Sides face, grand square
There's a young boy that I know
He just turned twenty-one
He comes from down in Colorado
4 ladies chain, go straight across
You turn and chain 'em back and promenade

Boys run right and promenade
