Send Me The Pillow    (FTC 32016)   Bob Cone

INTRO - ENDING



4 ladies chain across the ring there
Back right out and circle left I sing
Allemande left like an allemande thar, go right & left
Men back in, allemande thar
Shoot that star, curlique, men run right you do
Left allemande, then promenade
*Send me the pillow that you dream on
And darling I will dream on it, too

FIGURE



Head (side) two you promenade halfway round the ring
Down the middle, square thru I sing
4 hands around, then dosado there
Swing thru, boys run right you do
1/2 tag, trade and roll, pass thru
Trade by and swing your corner, promenade
*Darling I've been lonely since you left me
Send me your pillow and I'll dream

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain across the ring there
Back right out and circle left I sing
Ladies center, men sashay and circle
Ladies center, men sashay and circle again
You do an allemande left your corner lady
Weave around that ring, it's in and out around
Meet your lady, promenade
*Darling I've been lonely since you left me
Send me your pillow and I'll dream

ALTERNATE LYRICS
