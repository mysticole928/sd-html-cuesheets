Grand Parade: Heads step forward to wave, turn 1/2 right, boys turn 3/4 left.
Side girls move forward to head man, side man courtesy turn girl in front of
him, everybody promenade 1/2. Repeat again with heads going first. Repeat
twice with sides going first. This should take 64 beats and everyone should
be home with their original partner.
