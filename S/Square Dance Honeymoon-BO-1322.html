Square Dance Honeymoon
   (Bogan 1322)    Tim Ploch
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    B - SSD Week 1

4 ladies promenade go walking round that ring
Get back and swing there, swing your man I sing
Join your hands and circle left go walking round you know
Left allemande there, then weave the ring you know
Weave it in and out and when you meet your lady fair
Dosado and then you promenade the square
Promenade your lady round, take a walk and sing
Take your lady dancing on a square dance honeymoon

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Heads square thru and count me 4 and around you go
Round that corner you'll do a dosado
Do a curlique and then follow your neighbor , don't spread
Boys you cross run , recycle there
Slide thru , and square thru 3/4 go
Swing that corner girl and promenade you know
Promenade your lady there and take a walk and sing
Take your lady dancing on a square dance honeymoon
