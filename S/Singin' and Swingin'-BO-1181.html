corner by the right a wrong way thar

Face those sides (heads) make a right hand star ( once around )
Heads star left in the middle of the square once around you turn it there
Face that same ol' 2, go right & left thru
Dive thru , California twirl , swing awhile with the corner girl
Swing her twice around , then promenade
Treat her nice and easy but don't you get too squeezy
While she's singin' and swingin' with you
