Wonderful Feeling / She Loves To Dance With You
   (Balance 106 side B )    Ed Gilmore
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

Four ladies promenade, keep going till you get back
Turn partner right, turn once and a half, the men turn in stand back to back
Girls to the left go round, don't let your own go by
When you get home just dosado and catch your corner's eye
Allemande the corner lady, grand right & left you go
Grand right & left till you meet your girl and do a dosado
Then allemande left on the corner, come back and promenade
There's a rose in her hair and a gleam in her eye
She loves to dance with you

FIGURE   

Head (side) couples swing your maid, you swing then promenade her
Sides/heads wheel in and follow them, heads/sides wheel in, pass thru and stop
Sides/heads keep going round, heads/sides arch and you dive thru
You star by the right in the middle of the set, your corner waits for you
Allemande the corner lady, you dosado your own
Go back, swing the corner girl, you keep her for your own
You promenade this lady now, go dancing two by two
There's a wiggle in her walk and a giggle in her talk
She loves to dance with you
