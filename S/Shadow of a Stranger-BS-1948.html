Shadow Of A Stranger
   (Blue Star 1948)    Al "Tex" Brownlee
 
OPENER    Basic, corner progression

Circle left
She was just a total stranger when we first met that day
Although she couldn't linger, her shadow seemed to stay
Reverse back in single file , go walking round you know
Boys roll back , left allemande and weave the ring you go
This shadow of a stranger is driving me insane
Dosado this lady and promenade the ring
She must have known I loved her, though it could never be
This shadow of a stranger keeps coming back to me

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Down the middle and square thru four hands I sing
Four hands around and then do the right & left thru you know

This shadow of a stranger is driving me insane
Swing the corner lady and promenade the ring
She must have known I loved her, though it could never be
This shadow of a stranger keeps coming back to me
