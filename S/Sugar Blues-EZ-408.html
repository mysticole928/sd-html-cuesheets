Sugar Blues    (EZ 408)   Dick Leger, Pat Phillips

Head (side) gents with the corner go forward and back



Side (head) gents with the corner go forward and back
Forward again and dosado
All join hands and circle left
With the corner allemande left
Grand right & left
Dosado your own

FIGURE



Head (side) gents take your corner forward and back
Same four circle left once around the inside track
A left hand star the other way back
All dosado your own partner
All swing your corner girl
All go forward and back
Promenade the girl you swung

CLOSER



Ladies promenade the inside
Swing your own girl
All circle left
Allemande left the corner
Weave the ring
Dosado your own
Promenade
