Swingin' Man
(Lore 1071) Johnny Creel
FIGURE

1 & 3 go right & left thru , turn that girl and then
Same two ladies chain across, turn that girl again
Star thru , California twirl , circle 4 and then
Head gents break, make a line , go up and back and then
Star thru , then square thru 3/4 round that ring
Swing that corner girl and promenade
4 little ladies backtrack , go twice around and then
The second time you dosado , promenade again
You know you're dancing with a swingin' man
The swingingest man of them all

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 little ladies chain across, turn those girls around
Join hands and circle left , circle round that town
Allemande left , go allemande thar , go forward two and star
Men back in a right hand star, walk along not too far
Shoot that star , go right & left grand around that big old ring
When you meet that little girl you box the gnat
Pull by , left allemande , come back one and swing
Swing and then you promenade , go walking round that ring
You know you're dancing with a swingin' man
The swingingest man of them all
