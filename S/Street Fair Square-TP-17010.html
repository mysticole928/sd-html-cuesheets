Street Fair Square
   (Tanz 17010)    Gerhard Kamm
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    A2

Join up all your hands and circle left around you know
Reverse trail , ladies in the lead, single file you go
Men turn in , make a right hand star and the girls keep walkin' round
Allemande left that corner girl, and weave around the town
Well, you join us at the fair
Dosado and promenade from there
Can't you hear the sound of the merry-go-round
Well, join us at the fair!

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

Head (side) two couples right & left thru with a half sashay
Star thru and circle four 3/4 round I say
When you're there, two ladies chain , turn this girl and then
Star thru , pass thru , do a U-turn back and then
Dopaso , partner left and the corner right
Partner left, keep this girl and promenade
Can't you hear the sound of the merry-go-round
Well, join us at the fair!
