Square Dance Time in Texas   (Quadrille 853 )   Bob Huff

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Walk around the corner lady, see saw your own
Join all your hands circle left
All four boys star by the right, turn it round you go
Allemande left your corner, weave it round the set
Wind in and our around and when you meet your own
Swing that girl and promenade
Cause when it's square dance time down in Texas
That's where I want to be

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round you go
2 and 4 do the right and left thru
Sides square thru four hands around you go
Dosado that corner do an 8 chain 4
Dancing with your lady underneath that Texas moon
Swing that girl and promenade
Cause when it's square dance time down in Texas
That's where I want to be
