Sadie The Cleaning Lady    (SS 2345)   Ed Fraidenburg

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Head couples bow and swing, promenade the outside ring
Halfway around the sides a right & left thru I sing
Join hands and circle left go walking round the set
Allemande left the corner, turn a right hand around your pet
Men star left once around, meet old Sadie
Dosado and promenade this lady
Can't afford to get bored, dear old Sadie
Looks as though you'll always be a cleaning lady

FIGURE



Heads go up to the middle and back
Square thru go round that track
Count 4 hands around and then you swing thru
Those boys trade and then turn thru, left allemande
Come back and dosado and weave around the land
Scrub your floors, do your chores, dear old Sadie
And when you meet you dosado this lady
Promenade home and you swing with old Sadie
Looks as though she'll always be a cleaning lady
