Saints Go Marchin' In    (CT 107)   Bill Kramer

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain across that ring
You join your hands and circle left I sing
Allemande left, allemande thar,
Forward two and the four men star
When the saints go marching in
Now shoot that star, dosado,
Left allemande and then promenade
Well, your heart will hum with that number
When the saints go marching in

FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru four hands you go
Around that corner lady dosado
Swing thru 2 by 2, spin the top
And when you're through pass thru and a partner trade
Square thru 3 hands around
Swing that corner lady round and promenade
Well your heart will hum with that number
When the saints go marching in

ALTERNATE FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru four hands you go
Around that corner lady dosado
Swing thru 2 by 2, boys trade, turn thru
Left allemande and weave the ring
Strike up the band, start the parade
Dosado and then promenade
Well your heart will hum with that number
When the saints go marching in
