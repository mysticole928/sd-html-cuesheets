Sweetheart Of Venezuela    (GR 12225)   Dick Leger

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around si senor
With the outside 2 go right & left thru si senor
Dosado, make an ocean wave si senor
Cross trail and U-turn back, swing the corner, Walita
Promenade Walita, the sweetheart of Venezuela
Heads wheel around, go right & left thru si senor
Pass thru, do a right & left thru si senor
Cross trail, do an allemande left si senor
Come back and swing Walita, promenade to Venezuela
I love my Walita, sweetheart of Venezuela

BREAK



4 ladies chain across the way si senor
Chain them back, don't let them stay si senor
Join your hands and circle left si senor
Allemande left your corner, dosado Walita
4 men star left now, once around the ring
Dosado then allemande left si senor
Grand right & left around the set si senor
When you meet your lady, promenade si senor
Promenade with Walita, sweetheart of Venezuela
I love my Walita, sweetheart of Venezuela
