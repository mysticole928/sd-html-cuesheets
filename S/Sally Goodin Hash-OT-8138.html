Sally Goodin / Sally Goodin Hash
   (Old Timer 8138 side B )    Johnny Schultz
OPENER

You bow to your partners and corners all, now wave to the kids right across the hall
Walk all around your left hand lady, see saw your pretty little taw
Now face your corners, allemande left with your left hand
Your partner by the right, a right & left grand
Hand over hand and don't look back, gonna meet your partner and box the gnat
Now pull her on by and find your corner, left allemande, here we go
Go right & left grand, hand over hand go around the ring
Now meet your honey and give her a swing
Give her a swing and give her a twirl
Then settle down with the pretty little girl

FIGURE

Now the 1st and 3rd couples turn halfway around with a right hand swing
And halfway back with your left wing
Now two hands around like a highland fling
The head gents lead out to the right, and
Turn Sally Goodin with a right hand around
Then right back and turn your taw (left)
Now straight across to Arkansaw (right)
Now turn Sally Goodin on the way home (left)
Then right back and turn your taw (right)
Now don't forget your old grandma (left)
Home you go and everybody swing your baby

Now set 'em on your right and form a ring
And circle to the left like everything

Turn that star go once about, then docey around your partner there
The gents star right in the middle of the square
It's once around, and home you go
Meet your partner left like a dopaso
Her by the left, and your corner by the right
Now back to your partner with a left
Now the gents star right, and turn the opposite lady with a left hand around
The gents star right on a heel and a toe
Back to your partner with a left elbow
Now the gents star right 3/4 around, to the right hand lady with a left hand around
Now the gents star right and across you go, there's your corners
Left allemande, here we go
Right & left grand, hand over hand with every little maid
Gonna meet your mama and promenade
Promenade right down the line and get along home, you're doing fine
Get back home it's up to you, just swing your baby, that's what you do
Swing your baby go round and around
