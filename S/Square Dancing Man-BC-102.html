Square Dancing Man

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    B - SSD Week 1

Four little ladies promenade the ring go
Get on home and swing with your man
Join up all your hands , circle left now
Left allemande that corner, weave the ring
Let's get on with the square dance
Gonna dosado and promenade that hall
Forget about your troubles, have a good time
Cause I'm a square dancing man

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Heads you promenade , you go halfway now
Come down the middle and curlique and then, boys run
Swing thru , get two by two, boys run to the right
Bend the line and do a right & left thru
Swing thru , you star thru and trade by
Swing your corner and promenade that hall
Forget about your troubles, let your hair down
Cause I'm a square dancing man

Contains a "blended" movement: Swing Thru followed by Star Thru.
