Smile Darn You Smile    (BS 1611)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Dosado round that corner Jane
See saw round your own and then 4 ladies chain
Join your hands and then
You circle 8, you circle 8 go round the ring
Then allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and star
Hook an elbow, gents back up and make a right hand star
Shoot it, promenade your own awhile
Come on and smile darn you smile

FIGURE



Head ladies chain, heads go single file
Around just two, that's when you'll do the dixie style, balance
Those sides single file halfway round
4 gents star right, the girls go twice around
You turn your partner full around and find that corner maid
You swing this girl around and then you promenade
Take her home, swing awhile
Come on and smile darn you smile
