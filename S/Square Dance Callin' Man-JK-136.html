Square Dance Callin' Man (or Girl)   (Jay-Bar-Kay 136 )   Singin' Sam Mitchell

OPENER



All 4 ladies chain, you turn 'em and send them back a flutterwheel
Here we go -----
Join hands, circle, go walking, go walking on a heel and toe ----
Allemande the corner, come back dosado
Dosado and promenade the world
There's a square dance club in Reno, I'll call them on the phone
I hear they need a square dance callin' man (girl)


FIGURE



One and three promenade 3/4 round the outside ring (you go)
Two and four a right & left thru you know,
Pass thru, dosado go once around that outside two
And then, swing thru, turn thru and then
Left allemande your corner, walk on by your own
Swing the right
