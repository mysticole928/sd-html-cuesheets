Pennsylvania Polka Square    (SS 2313)   Bob Wright

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join hands and circle left half around will do
Heads forward and back, now sides a right & left thru
Heads half square thru, then split the outside two
Go around one and make a line, go forward up and back
Forward again, box the gnat, then pull that lady by
Allemande left that corner, grand right and left you fly
Go right and left that set, when you meet your pet
Promenade that girl from Pennsylvania

FIGURE



Head couples forward and then back with you
*Now star thru, pass thru, star by the right with the outside two
Heads to the center with a left hand star, turn it once and a half
Go once and a half around till your corner comes around
Then swing with your corner, yes swing her round and round
**Then promenade this girl, promenade around the town
Everybody has the mania to do the polka from Pennsylvania

ALTERNATE FIGURE



*If your group does not care for the Star Thru then Pass Thru,
this can be substituted:

Forward again half square thru, star right with the outside two



ALTERNATE LYRICS

**You've got a brand new baby, so promenade this lady



**Promenade the floor with the girl you adore
