OPENER - BREAK - ENDING



Well, all four ladies promenade, go once inside the ring
When you get back home, everybody swing
Join all your hands and circle, to the left around the town
Allemande left, and weave the ring around
Wind in and out you go, and when you meet that maid
Swing and whirl that pretty little girl, all promenade
She wore pigtails and ribbons and a petticoat of blue
She said that she would only dance with you

FIGURE



The head two couples flutter wheel, the inside of the set
Square thru, four hands around you bet
You make a right hand star, you turn it once around
The heads star left, one time around
Go to the same two and swing thru, turn thru and then
Swing that corner lady, promenade a brand new friend
Promenade this lady, she's young and sweet and fair
She wore pigtails and ribbons in her hair
