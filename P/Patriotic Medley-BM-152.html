Patriotic Medley    (BM 152)   Mac McCullar

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four little ladies promenade inside the ring you do
Get on home and swing with your man
Join hands and circle to the left around you go
Left allemande the corner, do a little dosado
Boys star left around the ring you know
Turn thru, left allemande and promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot (1st, 2nd, and 7th time through)
Keep your eye on the grand old flag

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru four hands around you go
Dosado the corner one time you know
Swing thru go two by two, boys run to the right
Bend the line and do the right & left thru
Pass the ocean and then, recycle my friend
Swing the corner lady and promenade again
Should old acquaintance be forgot
Keep your eye on the grand old flag

ALTERNATE LYRICS



Glory glory hallelujah (3rd and 4th time through)
His truth is marching on

Yankee doodle keep it up, Yankee doodle dandy (5th and 6th time through)
