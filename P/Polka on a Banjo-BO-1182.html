Polka On A Banjo    (BO 1182)   Joe Robertson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain across that set, turn the girls and circle to the left
And keep those banjos high
Do an allemande left and allemande thar
Forward two and make a star, and give it all you've got
Now shoot that star, and weave the ring, go in and out till you meet again
This lady with a smile and promenade full around
Hey promenade this lady, this polka dancin' baby
To the polka on the old banjo

FIGURE



Now 1 & 3 you promenade go round the ring about halfway
Come down the middle and do a right & left thru, and turn the girl
Now star thru, do a dosado, back to back and around you go
Ocean wave and rock it up and back, square thru 3
3/4 round, left allemande, walk right by the one you know
Swing the right hand girl and promenade
Hey promenade this lady, this polka dancin' baby
To the polka on the old banjo
