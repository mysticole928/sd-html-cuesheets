Piano Roll Blues   (Red Boot 297 )   Mac McCall

OPENER - CLOSER



Four ladies chain about 3/4 you go
You roll way and circle left you know
Sittin' by the fire my sweetie and me
Allemande left the corner and you weave the ring
You weave it in and out around and when you meet that girl
Dosado and promenade
I want to hear it again, I want to hear it again
The old piano roll blues

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Heads square thru and get me four hands you go
Get all the way to the corner lady and you dosado
Swing thru and then all the boys you run right
You ferris wheel, in the center pass thru
*Touch 1/4, scoot back, scoot back again
Swing that corner promenade
I want to hear it again, I want to hear it again
The old piano roll blues

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies promenade go rollin' round the ring now
You get on back and swing that man, swing them now and then
Join up all your hands and you circle to the left
Allemande left that corner and weave the set
Come on and weave it in and out and when you meet again
Dosado and promenade
I want to hear it again, I want to hear it again
The old piano roll blues
