Star thru and pass thru , circle 1/2 way around
Veer to the left , make me a column * all the way on down
Trade and you roll , pass thru , wheel & deal
Square thru 3/4 , swing corner girl
Promenade go walking round the world
Walking with your lady move on down the line
Promise land is calling and the poor boy is on the line

(* Make me a column : From two faced lines, ladies facing in start
walking forward. Man falls in behind her. Couple facing out turn
right 1/4, Single file on around behind them to form a column
Takes 4-6 counts.)
