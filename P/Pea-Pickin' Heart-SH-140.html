The Grand Sashay: Dosado partner all way round. Catch her by right hand and bow. Pull her by, go onto the next and see saw all the way around. Catch her by left hand, bow. Pull her by and go onto next, dosado. Catch her by right, bow. Pull her by and go onto next, see saw. Catch her by left, bow. Pull her by and you are in front of your new partner for next call. (Grand Sashay should be executed at fast tempo.)
