Patriotic Medley    (BS 2155)   Johnnie Wykoff

OPENER



Circle Left
Yankee Doodle went to town a riding on a pony
Stuck a feather in his hair and call it macaroni
Allemande left your corner, partner box the gnat
All 4 ladies promenade, once inside the set
Turn your partner by the right, full turn around you go
Allemande left with the corner, promenade
Yankee Doodle keep it up, Yankee Doodle dandy
Mind the music and the step and with the girls be handy

FIGURE 1 & 2



1 & 3 promenade, go halfway round the floor
2 & 4 pass thru, round one make a line of 4
Go forward 8 to the middle and back
Star thru, all double pass thru
Lead couples partner trade, make a right hand star
Go once around, girls turn back
Swing and promenade
*Promenade the lady fair, walking talking singing there
Yes we'll all be glad when Johnny comes marching home

*We are proud to claim the title of United States Marines



MIDDLE BREAK

Circle



Allemande left your corner, partner box the gnat
All 4 ladies promenade, once inside the set
Turn you partner by the right, full turn around you go
Allemande left with the corner promenade
Oh, Yankee Doodle came to town a riding on his pony
I am a Yankee Doodle boy

FIGURE 3 & 4



2 & 4 promenade, go half way round the floor
1 & 3 pass thru, round one make a line of four
Go forward 8 to the middle and back
Star thru, all double pass thru
Lead couples partner trade, make a right hand star
Go once around, girls turn back
Swing and promenade
*We'll look up with hearts that are free
And from afar we'll see the stars and stripes forever

*Should auld acquaintance be forgot



CLOSER



Circle
Oh beautiful for spacious skies, for amber waves of grain
Allemande left your corner, partner box the gnat
All 4 ladies promenade, once inside the set
Turn you partner by the right, full turn around you go
Allemande left with the corner promenade
And crown thy good with brotherhood
From sea to shining sea
