Circle left



Her whistle blowing steam at every turn
Allemande left and then dosado my friend
Left allemande again and weave the ring
Paddlewheeler, paddlewheeler
Dosado and promenade her home
Paddlewheeler, paddlewheeler
I hear the capt'n calling "All aboard!"

FIGURE



Four ladies chain 3/4 around the ring
Turn the girl and heads (sides) go up and back, square thru
Four hands you go, then do a little dosado, rock an ocean wave
Then you spin chain thru

Dosado and promenade her home
