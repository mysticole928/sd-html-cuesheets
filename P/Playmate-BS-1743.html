Playmate    (BS 1743)   Sal Fanara

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Circle left my playmate, come out and play with me
My dolly's got the flu, left allemande you do
Weave the ring my playmate, slide down my cellar door
Dosado and we'll be friends for ever more
Gents star right, it's once you know, you pass right by your own
Then allemande left your corner maid, come back and promenade
Ain't got no rain barrel, ain't got no cellar door
But we'll be jolly friends for ever more

FIGURE



4 ladies chain now, turn the girls and make a ring
Circle left around with me, climb up my apple tree
Head couples square thru 5 hands around the land
Side two divide, star thru, left allemande
Dosado your lady fair, swing the corner waiting there
Swing that lady round and then you promenade and sing
Ain't got no rain barrel, ain't got no cellar door
But we'll be jolly friends for ever more
