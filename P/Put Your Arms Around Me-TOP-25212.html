Put Your Arms Around Me   (Top 25212 )   Jim Cargill

BREAK



Four ladies chain across, turn her by the left and then
Join 8 hands circle left walking around the ring
Ladies roll a half sashay, circle left you go
Ladies roll a half sashay, circle left and then
Allemande left your corner, grand old right & left
When you meet your lady promenade, go around the ring
Oh, oh, I never knew any girl like you

FIGURE



1 & 3 up and back, square thru you go
4 hands around to the corner girl, star thru you know
Pass thru, partner trade, star thru, slide thru
Square thru 3/4 round the ring
Allemande left with the corner, dosado your own
Back to the corner, box the flea and promenade her home, singing
Oh, oh, I never knew any girl like you

SEQUENCE (Break, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, break)
