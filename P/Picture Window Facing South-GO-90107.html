Picture Window Facing South    (GO 107)   Red Warrick

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left the corner then swing
Join hands and circle left and make a ring
Allemande left the corner, let's do that allemande thar
Go right & left, the men back in, make a back-up star
Shoot that star full turn, pull corner by
Allemande left new corner, promenade
Promenade that lady, take her home and swing
In that picture window facing south

FIGURE



Well now 1 & 3 you promenade halfway round the ring
Into the middle, star thru, go right & left thru and then
Square thru 3/4, corner swing
Put her on the right and make a ring, circle left, you're gone again
Allemande left the corner, turn a right hand turn your own
Allemande left the corner, promenade
Promenade that lady, oh what a queen
In that picture window facing south
