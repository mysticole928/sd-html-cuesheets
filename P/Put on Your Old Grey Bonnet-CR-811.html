Put On Your Old Grey Bonnet    (CR 811)   Don Coy

Circle left



While I hitch old Dobbin to the shay
Left allemande the corner, dosado
Allemande left and weave the ring
Put on that old grey bonnet, with that blue ribbon on it
Swing and promenade
And through the fields of clover, ride on to Dover
On our golden wedding day

ALTERNATE LYRICS



* On the farm house veranda, there sat Silas and Miranda,
Thinking of the days gone by

* The birds were sweetly singing, and the same old bells were ringing



FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade halfway round that ring now
Down the middle pass the ocean, extend, swing thru
Spin the top, and when you're thru do the right & left thru
Square thru 3 hands, get me 3 hands around there
Swing the corner lady, promenade
And thru the fields of clover, ride on to Dover
On our golden wedding day
