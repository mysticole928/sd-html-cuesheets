Pocket Full Of Dreams    (BO 1112)   Nathan Hale

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Dosado your corners all, turn a left hand round your taw
4 ladies chain across that big old ring
Turn and chain 'em back, when she gets there, turn her Jack
Then join hands and make a great big ring
You allemande left your corner, grand right & left we'll go
Meet your girl on the other side, then we'll promeno
Promenade the town, take her home, we'll settle down
And swing this pocket full of dreams

FIGURE



Head couples 1/2 sashay, box the gnat across the way
Then cross trail, travel round just one
To the center dosado, to an ocean wave you know
Balance forward, balance back, then right hand swing
You allemande left your corner, walk on by your own
Swing the right hand lady, then promenade her home
Promenade that street, you'll want to make it neat
When you swing this pocket full of dreams
