Pied Piper    (RIV 279)  

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies chain, you turn ‘em around and chain them back home
Join up hands and you circle around you will fly
Allemande that corner girl and weave that by
Follow me, come on girl,
Swing with your lady & Promenade them home,
I’m the pied piper
I’ll show you where it’s at.

FIGURE



One and three [2 and 4] Square Thru
Four hands around the ring you will travel
Right hand star, you turn it around
Heads star left, full turn around, now
Right and Left Thru, Half Sashay
Swing with that corner & Promenade her that way
I’m the pied piper
I’ll show you where it’s at.

CLOSER



Four ladies chain, you turn ‘em around and chain them back home
Join up hands and you circle around the ring you will fly
Allemande that corner girl and weave around that ring
Come on girl, follow me, I’m the pied piper
Can’t you see. I’m the pied piper
I’ll show you where it’s at
