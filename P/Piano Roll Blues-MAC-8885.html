Sequence of the dance: OPENER; FIGURE twice for head couples; BREAK; FIGURE twice for side couples

Slip The Clutch explanation

While men are backing up in allemande thar position, on the command of "Slip the Clutch", they will release ladies' arm hold, retaining their right hand star and walk forward. Ladies will also walk forward, passing partner, who they will pass two times and left allemande corner.
