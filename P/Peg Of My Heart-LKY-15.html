Peg Of My Heart
Charles Harrison; Harmonicats; Pat Boone 1913
   (Lucky 15)    John Shallow
 
BREAK    Mainstream

4 ladies promenade once around , then you get home and swing
Swing the lady, join all your hands , circle the ring
Allemande left your corner girl, you dosado your own and then
The gents you star left once around ,
then you turn thru at home
Allemande , then promenade home
Swing with that Peg o' my heart

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Head couples square thru 4 hands around you will go
Dosado go once around and then
Swing thru my friend, spin the top , go two by two
And when you're through go right & left thru
And then you square thru 3 hands and then you will swing
Swing the lady, promenade home
Swing with that Peg o' my heart
