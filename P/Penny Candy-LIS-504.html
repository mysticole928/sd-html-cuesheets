(*Old time docey-do, or Colorado style do-si-do, originated by Lloyd Shaw.
This figure is done with two couples only, from a circle of four. Gents roll
your corner across in front of you like a half sashay, this puts your original
partner in front of you, take her by the left and finish the figure like a
do-paso making certain to finish up with your original partner in home position.)
