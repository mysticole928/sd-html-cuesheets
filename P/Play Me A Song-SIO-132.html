Play Me A Song

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

(You) allemande your corner, dosado your partner
Go back and swing that corner baby, mine
Join hands and circle , yes circle to the left then
Rollaway and weave along the line
Play me a song on that old time piano
(Now) dosado , then promenade along
You know that whenever this whole gang gets together
We'll dance to that piano all night long

One and three (2 & 4) you promenade, go half way around and then
Square thru in the middle 3 hands round (Take your time)
Both turn right and then, round one , down the center
Dixie style you ocean wave in line
Cross trail thru and swing , (You) swing the corner lady
Go twice around and promenade along
You know that whenever this whole gang gets together
We'll dance to that piano all night long
