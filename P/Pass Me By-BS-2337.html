Pass Me By   (Blue Star 2337 )   Lem Smith

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies promenade go walking round the ring
Get on home and swing, everybody swing
Join hands and circle, go go walking round that land
Allemande that corner, weave around the ring
Weave that ring until you meet your girl
Turn her by the right full turn, promenade
Promenade the ring take the ladies home I sing
If you don't happen to like it pass me by

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Head two couples promenade halfway round the ring
Down the middle star thru, double pass thru
Peel off, make lines, dance up to the middle and back
Star thru, cloverleaf, once around you do
Centers pass thru, swing that corner waiting there
Swing that girl and promenade
Promenade the ring take the ladies home I sing
If you don't happen to like it pass me by
