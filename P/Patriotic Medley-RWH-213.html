Patriotic Medley    (RWH 213)   Tom Rudebock

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSERr



sides face grand square
oh! beautiful for spacious skies
for amber waves of grain
for purple mountain majesties
above the fruited plain
circle left
America--America
God shed his grace on thee
allemande left, promenade
and crown thy good with brotherhood
from sea to shining sea!

FIGURE



heads/sides promenade 1/2 way around
sides/heads square thru go 4
4 hands around, then a right & left thru
8 chain 8 across the floor
you're a grand old flag--you're a high flying flag
swing the corner girl, promenade
should old acquaintance be forgot
keep your eye on that grand old flag

PLUS FIGURE



heads/sides promenade 1/2 way around
sides/heads do a right and left thru
square thru 4, move around the floor
relay the deucey and wave that flag
you're a grand old flag--you're a high flying flag
swing the corner girl, promenade
should old acquaintance be forgot
keep your eye on that grand old flag
