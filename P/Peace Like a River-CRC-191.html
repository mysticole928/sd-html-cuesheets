Peace Like A River    (CRC 191)   Matt Worley, Elbert Harrison



Four ladies promenade go once inside the ring



Join hands, circle to left around the ring
Allemande left your corner, weave the ring
I've got peace like a river
Peace like a river
Swing that girl around and promenade
I've got peace like a river
Peace like a river
Peace like a river in my soul

FIGURE



Heads (Sides) promenade and travel halfway around
Down the middle and do the right & left thru
Flutter wheel across, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and do the right & left thru
Swing thru and then, swing thru again
Swing the corner girl and promenade
I've got peace like a river
Peace like a river
Peace like a river in my soul

CLOSER



Four boys promenade go once inside the ring
Back home swing and whirl your girl
Join hands, circle to left around the ring
Allemande left your corner, weave the ring
I've got peace like a river
Love like an ocean
Joy like a fountain in my soul
I've got peace like a river
Peace like a river
Peace like a river in my soul
