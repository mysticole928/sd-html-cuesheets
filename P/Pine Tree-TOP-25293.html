Pine Tree
   (Top 25293)    Chip Hendrickson
 
OPENER - CLOSER (Head gents intro, side gents ending)   

Head gents (with partner and corner)
Take two girls , go forward up and back
Pass thru and do a u-turn back

Side gents (with partner and corner)
Take two girls , forward and back
And then you pass thru , do a u-turn back
And everybody left allemande

Weave around the ring until you meet again
Do a dosado around that girl, promenade
Go walkin' hand-in-hand, just listen to the band
Playin' underneath the old pine tree

FIGURE (twice heads, twice sides)    Basic, corner progression

Head couples do a right and left thru and turn your girl
Then a 1/2 square thru and split the sides
' Round just one and make a line , you go forward 8 and back
Now box the gnat and do a right pull by and ... left allemande
Dosado your own, well to the corner swing
High and low and then you promenade
Go 'round that big old room, just like a bride and groom
Dancin' underneath the old pine tree

MIDDLE BREAK    A1

Four ladies chain across the ring
And all four couples promenade halfway around the ring you go
Four ladies chain across again
And couples 1 and 3 do a right and left thru and turn your girl
Side couples cross trail thru and you go left allemande
Swing your honey high and low and then promenade
Walkin' round the hall, man you should'a seen it fall
When we chopped down that old pine tree!
