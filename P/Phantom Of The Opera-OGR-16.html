Phantom Of The Opera
(Omar 16) Tony Simmons
OPENER

Sides face grand square
In sleep you sang to me, in dreams you came
That voice which calls to me
And speaks my name
Allemande left and weave the ring
And do I dream again
For now I find
The phantom of the opera is there
Inside my mind

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) square thru 4 hands
And make a right hand star
Heads (Sides) star left once around now
Do a right & left thru
Swing thru and then swing thru again
Swing the corner, promenade
The phantom of the opera is there
Inside my mind
