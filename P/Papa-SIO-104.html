Papa
   (Sets In Order 104)    Bob Ruff
FIGURE    Basic, R-H lady progression

(And now the) Heads lead right and you circle to a line
A right & left thru and turn 'em in time
Go up and back , and then you bend the line
Now pass thru and partner swing
(Hey now you) join your hands and you circle the land
(Your) corner left allemande and a right & left grand
(You'll) meet your honey with a right hand swing
Gents star left and travel round the ring, your partner dosado once around this maid
(Then) pass her by , take the next , promenade
She'll say "Swing me, hold me tight, hey
Papa won't you please swing me"

MIDDLE BREAK    A1

(And now the) Heads pass thru travel round just one
Then pass on thru , round one , don't run
Cross trail , then U turn back
Now right hand star once around
Your corner left allemande and a right & left grand
Round the ring, go hand over hand
Meet your honey with a right hand swing
(The) gents star left , full round the ring
Your partner right hand swing
Corners allemande left , come back and promenade go round the set
She'll say "Swing me, hold me tight, hey
Papa won't you please swing me"

TAG (On last time through omit last line of figure and use the following)

Papa won't you please swing, Papa won't you please swing
Papa won't you please swing me"

(Sequence: Figure twice for heads, middle break, figure twice for sides, tag)
