Pins and Needles    (PP 118)   Toots Richardson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain 3/4 round
Catch 'em left and promenade the town
Why 1 & 3 (2 & 4) wheel around and
Do the right & left thru, slide thru
Allemande left and weave that old ring
Pin and needles, needles and pins
Dosado and promenade
Well, I think I'm getting over you within
Pin and needles, needles and pins

FIGURE (Heads twice / Sides twice)



Heads square thru 4, 4 hands and go
With the sides make a right hand star
Heads star left one time you bet, to that same
Two slide thru, then do a right & left thru
Boys cross fold, swing and promenade
Well, I think I'm getting over you within
Pin and needles, needles and pins
