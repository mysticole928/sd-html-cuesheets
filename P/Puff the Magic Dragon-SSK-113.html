OPENER



Sides face grand square
Puff the magic dragon lived by the sea
And frolicked in the autumn mist in a land called Honalee
Allemande left and weave
Little Jackie Paper loved that rascal puff
Promenade
And brought him strings and sealing wax and other fancy stuff

FIGURE



Heads promenade three quarters
Sides do the right and left thru
Pass thru and then Swing thru my friend
Boys run right you bend the line
Touch a quarter, circulate, boys run right
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Pirate ships would lower their flags when Puff roared out his name

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face grand square
A dragon lives forever but not so little boys
Painted wings and giant's rings make way for other toys
Allemande left and weave
One grey night it happened Jackie Paper came no more
Promenade
And Puff that magic dragon he ceased his fearless roar

CLOSER



Sides face grand square
His head was bent in sorrow green scales fell like rain
Puff no longer went to play along the Cherry Lane
Allemande left and weave
Without his life long friend Puff could not be brave
Promenade
So Puff the magic dragon sadly slipped into his cave
