Perfect Fool
Debby Boone 1980
(Rawhide 1068) Buddy Weaver
OPENER

Circle left
Regrets, I have enough
To fill a book or two on love
Allemande left that corner, turn your partner by the right
Allemande left and weave around tonight
I was a perfect fool to believe you
A perfect fool to think your love was true
For once in my life, I did something right
I was a perfect fool for you

FIGURE

Heads promenade halfway round
Walk in , square thru four hands around
Meet the side pair touch 1/4 then
Scoot back , boy run right around
Pass the ocean , circulate and swing the corner girl
Take her hand and promenade away
For once in my life, I did something right
I was a perfect fool for you

MIDDLE BREAK

Four ladies promenade one time round the ring
Back home swing that girl, I say
Join your hands , circle left and round today
Allemande left and weave the ring
I was a perfect fool to believe you
A perfect fool to think your love was true
For once in my life, I did something right
I was a perfect fool for you
