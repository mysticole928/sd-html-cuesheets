Put On Your Dancing Shoes    (BS 2149)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Now 4 little ladies chain about 3/4
Join up hands and circle left you go
Now roll away and circle to the left now
Left allemande, now weave the ring
Wind 'em in and out, when you meet your own
Swing and whirl that girl, promenade her home
Put on your dancing feet, slip into my arms so neat
We'll promenade that floor, we'll dance some more

FIGURE



Now the head 2 couples promenade go halfway
Come down the middle and do the right & left thru
Flutter wheel and now, you'll sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and do the right & left thru
Veer left and chain down the line, you turn that girl so fine
You'll slide thru and swing, promenade that ring
Put on your dancing feet, slip into my arms so neat
We'll promenade that floor, we'll dance some more
