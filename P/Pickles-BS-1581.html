Pickles    (BS 1581)   Vaughn Parrish

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Head 2 couples right & left thru, side 2 ladies chain
Head 2 couples right & left back, side ladies chain again
Per her on your right & make a ring, circle to the left you know
Walk all around your corner, turn your partner left elbow
It's partner left then corner right, and partner left hand swing
Gents star right, girls promenade round that big old ring
Then allemande left your corner, promenade your honey
Wow, gee, oh golly, you oughta see Pickles now

FIGURE



You all join hands and circle to the left and wear a great big smile
Then allemande left your corner, weave around that ring awhile
It's in and out and out and in, when you meet do a dosado
Go back to back then corner swing, swing a brand new beau
Then you all go right in single file, go walkin' round the floor
The girls roll back and pass one man, dosado once more
Go back to the corner, promenade, gonna walk her home and
Wow, gee, oh golly, you oughta see Pickles now

SEQUENCE: (Opener, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, ending)
