Pave Your Way Into Tomorrow    (BO 1243)   Lem Gravelle

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Join hands and circle left and then
4 ladies roll away, circle left around I say
Left allemande and weave the ring
You've got to pave your way into tomorrow
Dosado and promenade
I say just pave your way into tomorrow
And let your love of livin' cast its spell

FIGURE



Those heads square thru count 'em 4 hands and then
Split 2 round one get 4 in line
Move into the middle and back, do a turn & left thru
Ladies lead a flutter wheel so fine
When you get there just sweep 1/4, pass thru and then
Left allemande, get back and promenade
I say just pave your way into tomorrow
And let your love of livin' cast its spell
