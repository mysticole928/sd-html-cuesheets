Prairie Moon
Fred Latham with Jack Jackson and His Orchestra; Al Bowlly; Gracie Fields; others 1934
   (Go 102)    Mack McCoslin
 
FIGURE    A1, R-H lady progression

1 & 3 (2 & 4) right & left thru , turn that girl, half square thru
Dosado the outside two, make an ocean wave
Balance forward up and back , go right & left thru , turn this girl
Dive on thru , half square thru , separate
You walk around just one, down the middle star thru
Go right & left thru and turn the girls
Then do a cross trail thru
Allemande left the corners all, dosado with your taw
Join your hands , circle left around that ring
Your corners dosado , weave by your own
Swing the next little lady then you promenade on home
Promenade this pretty girl, take a walk around the world
Swing and whirl neath that old prairie moon

CLOSER    Basic

Bow and swing with your girl
Circle left go around the world
Circle left neath that old prairie moon
Sashay around the corners all, dosado with your taw
Allemande left then go weave that old ring
Go in and out you know till you meet your own
Dosado go once around, step right up and swing
Promenade this pretty girl, promenade go around the world
Swing and whirl neath that old prairie moon
