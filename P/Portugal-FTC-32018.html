Portugal    (FTC 32018)   Harold Bausch

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies chain, go straight across the ring
Join hands you circle to the left and then
Left allemande, do an allemande thar, go forward two and star
Men back in you've got a right hand star
Now shoot the star, curlique, men run right
Allemande left, come back promenade her
Promenade around the ring, take the lady home I sing
Take your girl to Portugal

FIGURE



Head (side) couples flutter wheel, then sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and then swing thru
Those men run to the right, couples circulate tonight
Wheel & deal and then pass thru, trade by
Left allemande your corner, turn thru at home
Swing corner girl, promenade her
Promenade around the ring, take the lady home I sing
Take your girl to Portugal
