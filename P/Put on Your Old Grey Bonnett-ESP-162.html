Put On Your Old Gray Bonnet   (ESP 162 )   Elmer Sheffield, Jr.

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



Well 4 ladies promenade go walking round
Get on back and swing your man around
Join hands and circle left, go moving round
Allemande left and weave the ring
Put on your old gray bonnet
With the blue ribbon on it
While I swing this girl and promenade
And thru the fields of clover
We'll ride on to Dover
On our golden wedding day

FIGURE



Well now the heads (sides) square thru, go walkin' round you do
Meet the sides and make a right hand star
Heads star left in the middle, go walkin' around you know
With the same two, do the right & left thru
Swing thru tonite, let the boys run right
1/2 tag, swing and you promenade
And thru the fields of clover
We'll ride on to Dover
On our golden wedding day
