New England March

Four ladies promenade the ring
Back home and give your man a swing
Join your hands and circle left go walking round the set
Left allemande and weave the ring
We're from New England, yes we are
Swing your lady round and promenade
Summer, Winter, Spring or Fall, we welcome one and all
To New England, U.S.A.

In New England, U.S.A.

*Calling neighbors by their name, from Connecticut to Maine
In New England, U.S.A.
