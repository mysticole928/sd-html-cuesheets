Nelly Bly    (TOP 25172)   Ralph Sweet

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands, circle left around the ring you go
Turn partner left a dopaso, corner right hand round
Partner left, back to the corner, make a wrong way thar
Men back in a left hand star, slip the clutch and then
Box the gnat, pull on by, left allemande
Grand old right & left around, now meet your girl
Promenade your lady, take her home and swing
Ho Nelly, Hi Nelly, Ho Nelly Bly

FIGURE



The 2 head couples separate go round the outside track
Star thru when you meet your girl, do a 1/2 square thru
Circle up 4 with the outside 2, heads break to a line
Without a stop 2 ladies chain, turn the girl and then
Star thru, dive thru, pass thru, and swing
Swing that corner lady, then promenade the ring
Ho Nelly, Hi Nelly, listen love to me
I'll sing for you, play for you, a dulcem melody
