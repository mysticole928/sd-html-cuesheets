Never Ending Song Of Love   (Chaparral 219 )   Jerry Haag

OPENER



All 4 ladies promenade inside the ring we go
Well, get back home, swing your man around & 'round we go
Join hands, circle left and then
We'll do an allemande left, your corner weave the ring
I've got a never ending love for you
Swing your girl and then promenade
From the first time we met I knew
I'd have a never ending love for you

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru in the middle & get 4 hands go
Find that corner girl and do a little dosado
Swing thru and let the boys run right
Bend the line and do the right and left thru
Pass the ocean, recycle then
Swing that corner girl and promenade
From the first time we met I knew
I'd have a never ending love for you

BREAK - CLOSER



Sides face, grand square (or teacup chain)
All this time of being alone
We can love one another feel for each other
From now on....
Feels so good I can hardly stand it
4 boys promenade inside
Swing with your own and promenade
From the first time we met I knew
I'd have a never ending love for you
