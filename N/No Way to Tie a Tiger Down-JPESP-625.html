No Way To Tie A Tiger Down    (JP 625)   Allen Tipton

OPENER



Circle left
Is it women's intuition
Somehow you seem to know I'll be going

Now the urge is strong for going
And in the morning you'll be knowing
That there ain't no way to tie a tiger down
