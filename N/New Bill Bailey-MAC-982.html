Now you allemande left the corner and grand old right & left

FIGURE



Now 1st and 3rd, you lead to the right and circle to a line
Go forward up and back with you
You square thru 4 hands, 4 hands and don't you roam
Find the corner and dosado, turn a right hand around your own
Then allemande the corner and walk on by your own
Swing the next little girl and promenade
You promenade that ring and get on home and swing
Bill Bailey, won't you please come home
