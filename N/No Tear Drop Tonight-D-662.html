No Teardrops Tonight    (D 662)   Ron Schneider

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain 3/4 round there
Join hands, your circle 8
4 ladies rollaway, come on back and weave the ring
You weave it out, you weave it in, dosado, promenade again
Walk along until tomorrow
For there'll be no teardrops tonight

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway round the ring
Down the middle and a right & left thru
Come on you square thru in the middle and get 4 hands you go
Slide thru and do a right & left thru
You flutter wheel, reverse flutter
Oh keep that maid and promenade
Walk along until tomorrow
For there'll be no teardrops tonight
