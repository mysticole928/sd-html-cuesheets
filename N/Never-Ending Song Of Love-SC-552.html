*Star Prowl: From a squared up set, sides start back to back, walk 3 steps turning on the 4th to face the head positions, walk, 2, 3, star thru with the person you meet.
At the same time that the sides start back to back, the heads will walk to the center and star thru with the person they meet, back out, 2, 3, turn back to back. Repeat this movement until you wind up back home with your original partner.
