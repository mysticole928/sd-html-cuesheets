Nickelodeon Rag
(Prairie 1066) Renny Mann
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 ladies promenade go one time arund
Get back home swing your man
Join hands circle left move it round the set
Allemande left and weave the ring
Weave in and out around, when you meet your own
Then dosado and then promenade
Tex and I are olean, he's a Californian
He loves a nickelodeon rag

FIGURE

Heads (sides) promenade go 1/2 way round
Walk into the middle , square thru 4 hands
You make a wave , ladies trade , swing thru
Turn and left thru and then
Square thru 3 hands, trade by , swing
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Tex and I are olean, he's a Californian
He loves a nickelodeon rag
