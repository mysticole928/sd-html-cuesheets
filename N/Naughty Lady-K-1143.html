Naughty Lady    (K 1143)   Bill Castner

FIGURE



Head 2 couples dosado and then you pair off
Face your corner, swing thru you do
Boys run, couples circulate around you know
Wheel & deal, get ready for the heel and toe, here we go
(Take opposite lady in butterfly position, hands joined)
Heel and toe and out you go, heel and toe and in
Heel and toe and out you go, heel and toe and in
Star thru and flutter wheel, then sweep 1/4 more
Dive thru, pass thru, swing the corner girl
Left allemande, go right & left grand
Go round the ring, go hand over hand
Promenade around the floor, promenade the girl you adore
Promenade that lady down the lane

BREAK



All join hands and circle, you circle round the ring
All the way around, you circle down the lane
Walk all around the corner, see saw round your taw
Men star by the right around the big old hall
Meet your partner with a dopaso, dopaso you know
Her by the left, the corner right, and partner left you know
Allemande thar, the men back in, the men back in and star
Hold it now and allemande thar
Slip the clutch, left allemande, grand right & left
When you meet your lady, you promenade your pet
Promenade the girl you adore, take a walk go round the floor
Promenade your lady down the lane
