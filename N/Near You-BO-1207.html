Near You    (BO 1207)   Keith Thomsen

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies chain 3/4 around that ring
Join your hands and circle, you circle left and then
Allemande left, go allemande thar, right & left and star
Men back in the right hand star, back up boys and then
Now slip the clutch, left allemande, go home and dosado
You promenade that lady around you go
You promenade that ring, take a lady home and swing

FIGURE



The head ladies chain it's straight across you go
Now star thru, you pass thru, the right & left thru you go
Dive thru, star thru, but roll 1/2 sashay
Dosado, put the men in the middle, make the ocean wave
You spin the top, go two by two, and pass thru and swing
Swing that corner gal and promenade
You promenade that ring, take a lady home again
Cause she likes to be here near you
