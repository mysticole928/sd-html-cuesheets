Naughty Lady
The Ames Brothers 1954
   (Aqua 119)    Phil Booker
 
FIGURE   

Heads forward and back , square thru 3/4 round
You both turn left round just one
Dixie chain across, men go left, girls right
Four in line , forward up and back you run
Pass thru , u-turn back , box the gnat
Join hands circle to the left go round the ring
Walk all around your corner and pass the next one by
To a right & left grand around the ring
Promenade the Lady of Shady Lane - she's got the town in a whirl
Balance out, dosado then the men star left
Go once around and then you swing your girl at home
Swing your girl around
(8 beat musical tag while couples are swing ing after each figure)

MIDDLE BREAK    C4

Your corner dosado , see saw round your own
The men star right go once around
Turn your partner left a full turn round you go
Face your corner, all eight chain as you come down
Girls star right you know, a full turn round you go
Turn them round , circle eight, go round the ring
Walk behind your corner , swing the next one
And then left allemande , grand right & left the ring
She's the naughty lady of Shady Lane , dosado when you meet
Left allemande your corner, promenade your own
Balance out at home and then you swing her round so neat
Your corner dosado , back to back, then get on home
Sides get set to go
(no musical tag in break)
