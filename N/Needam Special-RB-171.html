Needam Special / Diagonal Contra
   (Red Boot 171 side A )    Stan Burdick
Needam Special

FIGURE (as many times through as necessary to use up the music)

In your lines go forward and back
Forward then you back away
With the left hand lady swing and sway
Then down the floor, 4 in line
California twirl when you get below
Now bend the line, and the ladies chain
Chain them back on the same track
Then everyone pass thru
Walk right over and turn alone
Those who can right & left thru
And in your lines get ready, go
