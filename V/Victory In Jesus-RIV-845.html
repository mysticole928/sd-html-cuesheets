Victory In Jesus
The Don Marsh Chorus and Orchestra; others 1939
(Riverboat 845) Mitchell Osawa , Koji Harai , Ko Iwata , Kumi Takahashi
OPENER

Circle left
Oh victory in Jesus, my Savior, forever
Allemande left the corner, dosado your partner
Allemande left the corner, weave the ring
He loved me ere I knew Him
Swing and promenade
He plunged me to victory beneath the cleansing flood

FIGURE (1 & 3)

Four ladies chain 3/4
Heads (Sides) square thru 4
Right & left thru , veer left , couples circulate
Chain down the line , star thru , pass thru
Swing and promenade
And somehow Jesus came and brought to me the victory

FIGURE (2 & 4)

Heads (Sides) square thru 4 , right & left thru
Veer left , couples circulate , chain down the line
Star thru , pass thru , trade by , swing and promenade
And some sweet day I'll sing up there the song of victory

MIDDLE BREAK

Circle left
Oh victory in Jesus, my Savior, forever
Allemande left the corner, dosado your partner
Four boys star left one time around
Turn the partner by the right , allemande left the corner
Swing and promenade
He plunged me to victory beneath the cleansing flood

CLOSER

Sides face grand square
Oh victory in Jesus, my Savior, forever
He sought me and bought me with His redeeming blood
Allemande and weave
He loved me ere I knew Him, and all my love is due Him
He plunged me to victory beneath the cleansing flood
