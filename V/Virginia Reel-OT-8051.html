Virginia Reel
(Old Timer 8051 side A ) Johnny Melton , Gen Melton
FORMATION: Six couples standing in two lines facing partners. Ladies in one line and gents in other. Head couple at one end and foot couple at the other.

FIGURE

Everybody forward and bow (bow to partner and return to place)
Right hand swing with your pretty gal (right hand swing partners)
Left hand back with the same old pal (left hand swing partners)
Two hands up and around you go (two hand swing partners)
Dosado and don't be slow (back to back around partners)
Head lady go out and bow (head lady and foot gent bow to each other)
Head gent and you know how (head gent and foot lady bow to each other)
Head lady with a right hand swing (head lady and foot gent)
Head gent that same old thing (head gent and foot lady)
Head lady with a left hand twirl (head lady and foot gent left hand swing)
Head gent that same old girl (head gent and foot lady)
Head lady two hands round (head lady and foot gent)
Head gent go round and round (head gent and foot lady)
Head lady dosado back to back (Head lady and foot gent)
Head gent dosado the same old track (head gent and foot lady)
Head couple join hands and sashay down the center and sashay back
Right elbow swing and reel through the set
(Head couple swings with right elbow once and a half around, then
lady swings second gent and gent swings second lady with left elbows,
then each other in center, then next two in lines, then back to center, etc.
to end of line.)
Right to your partner and left to the side
Right to the buggy and left to the wheel
Swing them all on the ball of your heel
Hand over hand and heel over heel
The harder you dance the better you feel
Sashay up the center and around the bend
(head couple meet at the foot of the set, sashay back to the head of set)
Lady go gee and the gent go haw (lady goes out and around the outside to
the foot and the ladies all follow, the gents do the same)
Form a bridge at the end of the hall (head couple forms a two hand arch at foot
of set)
Next couple under and don't you fall (each couple meet and go under the arch)
All the way down two by two (the #2 couple goes up and becomes new head couple)
All the way down as you always do (#1 couple becomes foot couple)
(Repeat for each couple)

(Note: this record is for the first three couples. Replay the record for the last three couples.)
