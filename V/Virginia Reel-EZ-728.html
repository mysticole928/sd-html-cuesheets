Virginia Reel
   (E-Z School Rhythm 728)    Johnnie Wykoff
 
FORMATION

Two lines of dancers, four ladies in one line on the caller's left, four boys in the other line on the caller's right. Partners face each other, standing about six feet apart.

FIGURE

All go forward and bow and back
All go forward and do it again
Turn your partner right hand round
Turn your partner left hand round
Turn your partner both hands round (clockwise)
All go forward and dosado
*First couple sashay down
Re-sashay come back to place
**First couple reel the set
Same couple sashay to the head
#March to the foot and arch
##Lines sashay to the head of the set

*Number one couple only side-skips down the center of the set with both hands joined, and then returns to head of set

**Using right elbows, couple one only go forward and turn once and a half and then left with next below, the man reeling partner and then the next lady each time, the lady reeling partner and a man each time until they reach the foot of the set where they turn by the right elbow once and a half

#All face the head of the hall, number one man turns left, number one lady turns right, each leads their line down the outside to the foot, where couple number one makes an arch

##The original number two couple leads the lines under the arch, all side-skip toward the head until the number two couple is at the head, all fall back into lines to start the dance again with the original number two couple now the new number one couple.
