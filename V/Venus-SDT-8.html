Venus
Frankie Avalon 1959
   (Square Dancetime 8)    Jack Murtha
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Venus, if you will, please
Send a little girl for me to thrill
A girl who wants my kisses and my arms
A girl with all the charms of you
Circle left
Circle round the ring until you get her home again
Allemande left your corner lady there
And promenade on home

FIGURE    Basic, R-H lady progression

Four ladies chain 3/4 round that ol' ring and then
Four ladies chain straight across and go
Join your hands and circle left ( bow to your partner )
Weave the ring , weave in and out around
Promenade then home , it's home you go out there

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Venus, make her fair
A lovely girl with sunlight in her hair.
And take the brightest stars up in the skies
And place them in her eyes for me
Circle left
Circle round the ring until you get her home again
Allemande left your corner lady there
And promenade on home

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Venus if you do
I promise that I always will be true
I'll give her all the love I have to give
As long as we both shall live
Circle left
Circle round the ring until you get her home again
