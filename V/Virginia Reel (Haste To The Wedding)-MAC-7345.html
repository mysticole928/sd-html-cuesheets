Virginia Reel (Haste To The Wedding)
   (MacGregor 7345)    Fenton Jones
 
DANCER SETUP

Line of girls on left, boys on right, nearest two to stage are heads

FIGURE

Bow to partner
All forward and back
All right hand swing
All left hand swing
All two hand swing
All dosado
Heads sashay down and back (between the line of girls and boys)
Heads reel 1 1/2 to far end of line
Same 2 sashay back
Girl leads girls to the right, boys go left, make arch
All sashay under arch and start over again
