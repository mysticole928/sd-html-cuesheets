Vacation
   (Blue Star 1703)    Marshall Flippo
FIGURE    C1, corner progression

Four ladies chain 3/4 around, those heads go forward up and back
And then you cross trail thru , behind the sides star thru
Substitute , then right & left thru you do
Now pass thru , star thru and do a dosado
Make an ocean wave and balance to and fro
And then you cross trail thru and do a left allemande
Box the gnat and then swing
And now those gents star left 3/4 round the set
And then you swing that corner, round and round
Four ladies promenade , you'll go around the ring
Box the gnat and promenade her home again
Va-ca-tion...we're gonna have a ball
