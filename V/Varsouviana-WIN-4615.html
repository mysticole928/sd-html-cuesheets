Teton Mountain Stomp / Varsouviana
   (Windsor 4615 side B )   
 
STARTING POSITION: PARTNERS IN "VARSOUVIANNA" POSITION, BOTH FACING IN
LOD, W ON M'S R SIDE, M'S R ARM ACROSS IN BACK OF W'S SHOULDERS WITH R HANDS
JOINED OUT TO R OF AND SLIGHTLY ABOVE W'S R SHOULDER, L HANDS JOINED IN
FRONT OF M'S L SHOULDER, M SLIGHTLY IN BACK OF W, M'S R ARM SHOULD NOT REST
ON W'S SHOULDERS.

FOOTWORK: SAME FOOTWORK FOR M AND W THROUGHOUT THE DANCE.

"LONG PHRASES"

MEAS.
1-4
SWEEP, GLIDE, CLOSE; SWEEP, GLIDE, CLOSE; SWEEP, GLIDE, CROSS; STEP,
POINT,-;
SWEEP L FOOT OVER ACROSS R INSTEP, STEP FWD IN LOD ON L FOOT IN A GLIDING
STEP, CLOSE R FOOT BESIDE L TAKING WEIGHT ON R; REPEAT THIS ACTION; THEN
SWEEP L FOOT OVER R INSTEP, GLIDE FWD ON L FOOT, WHILE STEPPING ON R FOOT W
CROSS5S OVER IN FROM OF M TO HIS L SIDE; STEP FWD IN LOD ON L FOOT; POINT R
TOE LIGHTLY TO FLOOR' IN FRONT, HOLD ONE COUNT;

5-8
SWEEP, GLIDE, CLOSE; SWEEP, GLIDE, CLOSE; SWEEP, GLIDE, CROSS; STEP,
POINT, -;
REPEAT THE ACTION OF MEAS. 1-4 ABOVE ONLY STARTING ON R FOOT AND ENDING
WITH W ON M'S R SIDE,

"SHORT PHRASES"

9-12
SWEEP, GLIDE, CROSS; STEP, POINT, -; SWEEP, GLIDE, CROSS; STEP,POINT, -;
SWEEP L FOOT OVER R INSTEP, GLIDE FWD ON L FOOT, WHILE STEPPING ON R FOOT
W CROSS5S OVER IN FRONT OF M TO HIS L SIDE; STEP FWD IN LOD ON L FOOT, POINT
R TOE TO FLOOR IN FRONT, HOLD ONE COUNT. REPEAT THIS ACTION STARTING ON R
FOOT AND ENDING WITH W ON M'S R SIDE.

13-16
SWEEP, GLIDE, CROSS; STEP, POINT, -; SWEEP, GLIDE, CROSS; STEP, POINT, -;
REPEAT ACTION OF MEAS. 9-12 ABOVE,

REPEAT ENTIRE SEQUENCE TO END OF MUSIC

NOTE: THERE ARE MANY DELIGHTFUL VARIATIONS OF THE "BASIC" VARSOUVIANNA
SEQUENCE DESCRIBED ABOVE AND DANCERS ARE URGED TO LEARN AND DANCE THE
VARIATIONS POPULAR IN THEIR AREA. SEVERAL SQUARE AND ROUND DANCE
PUBLICATIONS AND PERIODICALS HAVE PUBLISHED NUMEROUS VARIATIONS OF THE
VARSOUVIANNA, ALL OF WHICH ARE INTERESTING AND ENJOYABLE.
