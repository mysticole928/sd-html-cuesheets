Virginia Reel
(Folkraft 1342) Frank Kaltman
FORMATION

Column of four, five or six couples, facing forward, girl on partner's right

STARTING POSITION

Partners facing

FIGURE

Forward and back, forward and back again
Right elbow swing once around with partner
Left elbow swing once around, returning to place
Head couple sashay down
Head couple sashay back
Head couple elbow reel down the set (swing partner with right elbow,
then first one in opposite line with left. Continue through to foot of set,
alternately reeling partner with right and next one in opposite line with left,
finishing on own side at foot of set)
Head couple sashay up the set to place
Head couple cast off and form an arch (Head boy turn left, Head girl turn right
and each lead line to foot of set. Head couple at foot of set join and raise
both hands to form an arch. Second couple lead others under the arch, advancing
to become new Head couple.)

(Repeat entire dance with each new Head couple. Play record twice for a set of
six couples.)
