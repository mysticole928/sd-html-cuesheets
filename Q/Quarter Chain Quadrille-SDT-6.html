Quarter Chain Quadrille
   (Square Dancetime 6)    Jack Murtha
 
OPENER

_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ _ _ _ One and three promenade half

FIGURE

_ _ _ _ Down the middle right and left thru
_ _ _ _ Two and four promenade half
_ _ _ _ Down the middle right and left thru
_ _ _ _ _ _ Four ladies chain
_ _ _ _ _ Four ladies chain one quarter
_ _ Courtesy turn _ _ promenade
_ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ __ _ _ One and three promenade half

Repeat once more for the heads...then twice for the sides
