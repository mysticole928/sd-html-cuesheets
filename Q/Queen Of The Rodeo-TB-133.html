The 4 ladies chain , go straight across the square
Join hands , circle left you know
Left allemande the corner, go forward 2 and then
Meet the partner , then weave around you go
You wind in and out around the ring you go
Dosado and promenade this lady around the ring you go
The queen of the big rodeo

FIGURE

The heads (sides) promenade travel halfway and then
Come down the center , do a dosado , star thru , pass thru
Dosado the outside two, go full around and then
You swing thru , cast off 3/4 round
Walk & dodge and then
Boys fold , star thru and promenade
You promenade this lady around the ring you go
The queen of the big rodeo
