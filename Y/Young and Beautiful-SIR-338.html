Young and Beautiful    (SIR 338)   Doug Bennett

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
You're so young and beautiful and I love you so
Your lips so rare, your eyes so bright, shame the stars that glow
Allemande left and weave
So fill these lonely arms of mine, then kiss me tenderly
And you'll be forever young and beautiful to me

FIGURE



Heads reverse flutterwheel, sweep 1/4 more
Veer right, veer left, everybody veer to the left
All the girls circulate, you'll tag the line, face to the right
And the girls circulate, boys scoot back
You wheel around and then
Half-sashay and promenade you see
Then you'll be forever young and beautiful to me

TAG



Allemande left and swing your girl
Then you'll be forever young and beautiful to me
