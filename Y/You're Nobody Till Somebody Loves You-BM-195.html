California twirl, do a half square thru
Everybody California twirl and then swing the corner
Swing this girl and then you go left allemande
You promenade your lady, but don't slow down
Back right out and circle, you circle to the left
Left allemande, turn partner by the right hand
Gents star left, one time around and then
Star promenade my friend
Girls turn back twice around that track
Second time you meet dosado, promenade
You're nobody till somebody loves you

SEQUENCE

Dance goes through four times, twice for heads, twice for sides.
