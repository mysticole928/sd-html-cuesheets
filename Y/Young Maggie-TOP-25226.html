Young Maggie    (TOP 25226)   Joe Prystupa

BREAK



4 ladies chain go straight across the ring now
Join hands, circle left I sing
Allemande left, allemande thar, go forward 2 and star now
The men swing in, you've got a right hand in
Shoot that star, grand right & left, go around the ring now
Meet young Maggie, promenade
I walked through the halls where we danced, Maggie
When you and I were young

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around the ring now
All the way around, dosado I say
Make an ocean wave and balance
Scoot back (walk in, thru thru, rejoin 'em)
Men trade, men run
Going to wheel & deal, swing young Maggie
Left allemande, come back and promenade
*We square danced till dawn many times, Maggie
When you and I were young

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around the ring now
Down the middle, right & left thru I say
Roll away, go forward and come on back
Star thru, right & left thru, turn that girl and then
Dive thru, pass thru, allemande
Dosado your partner, swing corner Maggie, promenade
*We didn't have this beat to move our feet, Maggie
When you and I were young
