Y'all Come Back Saloon    (D&R 122)   Ron Hunter

OPENER



Four ladies promenade once inside the ring
Gonna get back home, swing with your man
Join all your hands, circle eight
Left allemande that corner girl and weave the ring
And she played tambourine with a silver jingle
Dosado and promenade
And the song most requested by the man she knew as "Cowboy"
Was the Midnight Benediction at the Y'all Come Back saloon

FIGURE



Heads rollaway, zip up to the middle and back
Star thru and do the right & left thru
Pass thru and trade by
Dosado make a wave, girls trade,
Recycle once around you go
Dive thru, square thru 3/4 round
Swing the corner girl, promenade
And the song most requested by the man she knew as "Cowboy"
Was the Midnight Benediction at the Y'all Come Back saloon
