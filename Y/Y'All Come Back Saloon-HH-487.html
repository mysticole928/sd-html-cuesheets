Y'All Come Back Saloon    (HH 487)   Ernie Kinney

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain go straight across that ring
Join hands circle left go walkin' 'round and then
Ladies in the men sashay then circle left I say
Ladies in the men sashay, left allemande and weave
She played tambourine with a silver jingle
Dosado your girl and then you promenade on home
The tune most requested by the one she knew as "cowboy"
Was a late night benediction at the Y'all Come Back saloon

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade halfway around
The side (head) two go right & left thru and turn the girls around
Touch 1/4, boys run right, touch 1/4 more
Scoot back and roll to face and do an eight chain four
She played tambourine with a silver jingle
Swing the next little girl and then you promenade along
The tune most requested by the one she knew as "cowboy"
Was a late night benediction at the Y'all Come Back saloon

ALTERNATE CLOSER



Four ladies chain go straight across that ring
Take your time, chain 'em back to a dopaso and then
Turn partner left, corner right, partner left again
Head ladies to the center for a tea-cup chain go round the ring my friend
She played tambourine with a silver jingle
And she must have known the words to at least a million tunes
But the tune most requested by the one she knew as "cowboy"
Was a late night benediction at the Y'all Come Back saloon
