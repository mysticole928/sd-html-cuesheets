You Little Sweet Little You    (BO 1147)   Charles Drake

Allemande left in the alamo style, balance out and in



Swing by the left, gents star right, straight across the square
Turn her by the left, go allemande thar, and you back in right there
Shoot that star, go right & left grand, round the ring you go
Meet your little lady, dosado, promeno
Walk along and whisper, well I sure love to swing
You little sweet little you

FIGURE



1 & 3 you promenade halfway around the square
2 & 4 with a right & left thru, and turn your lady fair
Heads divide to the sides, circle halfway round the town
Dive thru, square thru 3/4 around
Left hand round the corner, pass by your own
Swing the right hand lady around then promenade her home
Walk along and tell her, say I just like to swing
You little sweet little you
