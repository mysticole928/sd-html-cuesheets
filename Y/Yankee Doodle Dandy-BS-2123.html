Yankee Doodle Dandy    (BS 2123)   Johnnie Wykoff

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Circle left
Oh, I'm a yankee doodle dandy
Yankee doodle do or die
Allemande left your corner, your partner box the gnat
4 ladies promenade one time you fly
Turn your partner by the right hand, a full turn around
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Yankee doodle went to town just to ride the ponies
I am that yankee doodle boy

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands around you go
You make a right hand star once around
Heads to the middle, star left one time will do
To the same outside pair do the right & left thru
You roll away and now you turn thru
Left allemande, come back and promenade
Yankee doodle went to town riding on a pony
I am that yankee doodle boy
