You Are My Sunshine    (TOP 25170)   Chip Hendrickson

OPENER - CLOSER



head couples circle 3/4 round and
then you pass thru & circle to a line (head men break)
go forward 8 & back, and now you crosstrail
left allemande, come back & weave the ring
You are my sunshine, my only sunshine
you dosado and then promenade
You'll never know dear how much I love you

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4, 3/4 round
head couples crosstrail thru around one in the middle,
box the gnat & then you square thru 3/4 round &
go left allemande, go forward 2, then turn back one
swing the girl you meet and promenade
You'll never know dear how much I love you
please don't take my sunshine away

MIDDLE BREAK



left allemande, then dosado partner
join 8 hands & circle in a ring
4 ladies rollaway, you circle left I say
with a brand new partner, dopaso
You are my sunshine, my only sunshine
swing the corner girl and then you promenade
You'll never know dear how much I love you
please don't take my sunshine away
