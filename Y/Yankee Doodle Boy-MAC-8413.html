Yankee Doodle Boy / New Heart Of My Heart
George M. Cohan; Billy Murray 1904
   (MacGregor 8413 side A )   
 
BREAK "A"

Hey swing your Yankee Doodle sweetheart
Left allemande , four ladies star around
Then gents step in behind your lady , walk it right along
The gals roll back , pass one man
Corners all left allemande
A right & left around that ring
When you meet your own, promenade
She's looking mighty coy
Now, step to the center, tap your foot
With a one, two, three, four
Swing with your Yankee doodle boy

FIGURE   

1 & 3 lead to the right , you circle half the town
Dive thru , right & left thru , a full turn round
Star with the sides , a right hand star
Back by the left and then, head ladies lead a dixie grand
Down the middle hand over hand, all turn left , go single file
Hurry don't be late
The gents roll in , pass one by
Next gal promenade , promenade a girl and boy
Take her home , we'll swing with joy
Swing with your Yankee Doodle boy

BREAK "B"

Allemande left the corner
Turn a right hand round your own
Gents star left , go once around and then
Gals step in behind your partner , hurry don't be late
Gents reach back with the old right hand
Box the gnat , go right & left grand
A right & left around the set
Now when you meet your pet, promenade
She's looking mighty coy
Step to the center, tap your foot
With a one, two, three, four
Swing with your Yankee doodle boy
