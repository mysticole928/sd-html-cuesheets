Loose Talk / Yellow Rose Of Texas
Mitch Miller (1955) 1836
   (MacGregor 7475 side B )   
BREAK    B - SSD Week 1

You go to the left with a left allemande
A right & left around
Go hand over hand with each little maid
Now boys, you're going to town
Promenade your lady fair
You promenade the floor
And the yellow rose of Texas
Shall be yours forever more

FIGURE   

(part one)
Now allemande left , the ladies star
The gents go round the ring
Reverse the star, reverse the ring
Pass your own again
The gals slide out, the boys slide in
Star once around the floor
Swing your yellow rose of Texas
She's the gal that you adore

(part two)
Now allemande left your corner
Pass right by your own
Right hand swing old Sally Goodin'
A left hand swing at home
Gents star right out in the center
You pass the gal you know
Swing the corner rose of Texas
She's the gal in calico
