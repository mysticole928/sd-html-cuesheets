Yes Sir She's My Baby   (Roofers 129 )   Gerald McWhirter
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING

Four ladies hit the road walkin' & talkin' round the ring
Come home and swing your man
Join hands circle left
Allemande left and weave around and sing
Wind it in and out until you meet your doll
Swing and promenade
Promenade with little Kitty, she's from Oklahoma City
Yes, sir she's my baby now

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands, dosado
Swing thru, boys run, tag the line
Cloverleaf follow the girls, girls square thru 3/4
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Little Sally she's the gal from down in the valley

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Heads promenade halfway, down the middle star thru
The pass thru, swing thru, boys run, ferris wheel
Center four only double pass thru
Left allemande come back one and promenade
