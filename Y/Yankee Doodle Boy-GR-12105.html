4 ladies promenade go once around the ring
Come on back box the gnat and swing
Now join your hands circle left around you go
Allemande left your corner, weave the ring
In and out around and when you meet your maid
Dosado and promenade
Promenade your lady fair, right on home I sing
Dancing with a Yankee Doodle Boy

Swing thru the outside two, ladies trade you know
Boys trade, boys run, wheel & deal and go
Dive thru, pass thru, corner lady swing
Swing that girl and promenade
Promenade your lady fair, right on home I sing
