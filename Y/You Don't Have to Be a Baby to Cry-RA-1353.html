You Don't Have To Be A Baby To Cry    (RA 1353)   Earl Wright

INTRO



4 ladies chain across you go
Turn and chain the ladies home you know
Join hands and circle left I sing
Left allemande and weave around the ring
When you leave me, my golden rainbow disappears
Dosado and promenade
These aren't raindrops in my eyes, why should I lie
You don't have to be a baby to cry

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around you go
Down the middle with a right & left thru and then
Square thru go walking around that floor
Dosado and then an eight chain 4
When you leave me, my golden rainbow disappears
Dosado and promenade
These aren't raindrops in my eyes, why should I lie
You don't have to be a baby to cry

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join all your hands and circle left around you go
Left allemande your corner, come home a dosado
Men star left and turn it once around and then
Turn thru, left allemande and weave the ring
When you leave me, my golden rainbow disappears
Dosado and promenade
These aren't raindrops in my eyes, why should I lie
You don't have to be a baby to cry
