You're A Grand Old Flag / Hello, Hello, Hello

INTRO

Keep your eye on the grand ole flag

OPENER

Allemande left with your left hand partner, right, a right & left grand
Around that ring, go hand over hand, every gal with every other hand
You promenade and don't be slow, get back home and dosado
Side gents face your lady fair, then all get set for the grand square
1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 reverse
1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 home
Head ladies chain across and back (16 beats)
Head ladies chain to the right and back (16 beats)
Side ladies chain across and back (16 beats)
Side ladies chain to the right and back (16 beats)

BREAK

All around your left hand lady , my, oh, my, what a baby
See saw round your lady fair and the four gents star in the middle of the square
All the way round in the middle of the land and turn your corner with a left allemande
Dosado your lady fair, then all get set for the grand square
1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 reverse
1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 face, 1-2-3 home
Head couples right & left thru and back (16 beats)
Heads to the right , a right & left thru and back (16 beats)
Side couples right & left thru and back (16 beats)
Sides to the right , a right & left thru and back (16 beats)

BREAK

All around your left hand lady , my, oh, my, what a baby
See saw round your lady fair and the four gents star in the middle of the square

CLOSER

Allemande left in the alamo style , right to your honey and balance awhile
Balance in and balance out , swing with the right hand , half about
Balance out and balance in , swing with the left hand, half again
Balance forward, balance back , swing with the right to the outside track
Balance again but don't just stand, swing with the left to a right & left grand
Around that ring, go hand over hand, every gal with every other hand
Promenade your lady fair, take a walk, go round the square
That's the finish of this ole' square
