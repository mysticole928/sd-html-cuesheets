Your Cheatin' Heart   (Gold Star 714 )   Cal Golden
OPEN - BREAK - CLOSE

Head (side) couples star the route
Your cheatin' heart...
Will make you weep...
You'll cry and cry...
And try to sleep...
Circle left
But sleep won't come...
The whole night thru
Allemande left and promenade
Your cheatin' heart...will tell on you...

FIGURE



Head (sides) square thru and go...
Around the cornor do a do sa do...
Spin chain thru and then girls circulate
Left allemande your corner walk by your own
Swing the next girl and promenade her home
Your cheatin' heart...will tell on you...

TAG



Swing your partner twice around
Your cheatin' heart...will tell on you

NOTE TO CALLER

Grand square can be used instead of star the route

STAR THE ROUTE

From a static square those designated (heads or sides)

Example: The heads will right hand star 3/4 to meet
the couple on their right with a left hand star one full turn....
Heads will meet back in the center of the set to star right 1/2 way to meet
the others sides with a left hand star one full turn....
Heads meet in the center for a right hand 3/4 star back to home position.

It takes 32 beats to execute star the route, same as grand squre.

Note: Tell the head men they are leading the star all the way through the route.
Tell the head ladies to follow their partner
