The Pine Ridge Boys; The Rice Brothers Gang; many others 1939

FIGURE (1)   

Now 1 & 3 with a 1/2 sashay
Go up to the middle and back that way
Pass thru , turn left , around one
Come down the center , dixie chain
Through the sides , turn right again
Star left in the middle once around
You walk all around your left hand lady , oh boy, what a baby
See saw round your little taw
Right to your corner, chain all 8
Put 'er in the lead, star all 8
Star right in the middle once around
Ladies roll back , pass one man
Allemande left with the old left hand, a right and left around you go
Promenade this big old ring
Now everybody sing
