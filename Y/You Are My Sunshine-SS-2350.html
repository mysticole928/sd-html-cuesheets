You Are My Sunshine    (SS 2350)   Harry Tucciarone, Jr.

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain about 3/4 round now
Then join your hands, circle left around the ring
4 ladies roll away, you circle left and then
You allemande left and weave around the ring (everybody sing)
You are my sunshine, my only sunshine
You dosado and then you promenade
You'll never know dear, how much I miss you
Please don't take my sunshine away

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands around now
With the corner dosado, go all the way
Why don't you swing thru, boys you run to the right
Wheel & deal, with a right & left thru, turn the girl
Why don't you dive thru, square thru 3/4
Your corner swing and then you promenade
You'll never know dear, how much I miss you
Please don't take my sunshine away
