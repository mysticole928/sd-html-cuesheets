You Don't Care   (Kalox 1330)   Al Stevens, Jon Jones

INTRO



Well all 4 ladies chain you turn them left
Then chain the lady right back home again
Join up all of your hands, circle left around that town
Left allemande and weave the ring
Wind in and out around until you meet your girl
Dosado and promenade
Darlin' I love you so it's kind of hard to know
That you don't care what happens to me

FIGURE #1



Heads pass the ocean and all 4 boys extend
All recycle, now all veer to the left, the center ladies trade
You 4 tag the line, face left and now swing thru in time
Boys you trade, turn thru you go
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Darlin' I love you so it's kind of hard to know
That you don't care what happens to me

FIGURE #2



Heads touch 1/4 and let the boys run right
Right & left thru and turn your girl
Half sashay, swing thru, and now ladies trade
Those ladies run, ferris wheel, swing thru in the middle
Everybody scoot back, swing the corner girl and promenade
Darlin' I love you so it's kind of hard to know
That you don't care what happens to me
