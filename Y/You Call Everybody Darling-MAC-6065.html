San Antonio Rose / You Call Everybody Darling
The Andrews Sisters 1948
   (MacGregor 6065 side B )   
OPENER

Honor your corner, and swing her short and sweet
Now run back to your darlin' and swing her when you meet
Allemande left with the old left hand
Partner by the right and a right & left grand
Round and round the ring you go
And hurry, don't be slow
Promenade the girl you call your darlin'
Swing that gal around when you get home
Step back and look her in the eye
Now swing again and you'll know why
Nobody calls her darlin' any more

FIGURE

Two head couples lead to the right and circle
Round and round in a pretty little ring you go
Now break at the heads and form a line
It's forward and back, you're doin' fine
Right & left thru and over you go
And hurry, don't be slow
It's right & left back with your darlin'
Chain those ladies once right down the line
Then promenade around
With that new girl you've found
She'll always call you darlin' evermore

BREAK

All around your corner, she's the gal from Arkansas
See saw your pretty little taw
Allemande left with the old left hand
Partner by the right and a right & left grand
Hand over hand around the ring
Right & left with the pretty little things
Promenade the girl you call your darlin'
Swing that gal around when you get home
Step back and look her in the eye
Swing again and you'll know why
Nobody calls her darlin' any more

Repeat Figure and Break
