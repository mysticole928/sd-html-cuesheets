Y'All Come    (CC 113)   Dave Sutter

OPENER



Walk around the corner, see saw your own
Join hands, circle left go round
Men star right, once around
Allemande left and weave the ring
Y'all come, y'all come
Swing and promenade (yodel)
Y'all come to see me when you can

FIGURE



Heads/sides pass thru, partner trade
Star thru, pass thru, star thru again
Up to the middle and back,
Right and left thru, ladies lead dixie style to an ocean wave,
Girls circulate, boys trade,
Allemande left, promenade (yodel)
Y'all come to see me when you can

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 girls/boys promenade inside, get home swing
Join hands and circle left,
Allemande left, weave the ring
Y'all come, y'all come
Swing the girl and promenade (yodel)
Y'all come to see me when you can
