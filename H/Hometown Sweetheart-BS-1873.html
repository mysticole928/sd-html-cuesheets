Hometown Sweetheart    (BS 1873)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



You circle left around that ring with that hometown sweetheart
Hometown sweetheart of mine
California twirl, do a partner trade and then
All 4 gents star left around, go once around that ring
Dosado that partner, left allemande that corner
Come back and promenade her home this time
You promenade that ring with that dear little sweetheart
That hometown sweetheart of mine

FIGURE



Now 4 little ladies chain and you turn with that lady
Those heads promenade about halfway
Come down that middle with a right & left thru
Turn that girl and then you slide thru, then pass thru
Go right & left thru again
Now pass thru, do a u-turn back, and swing with that lady
Left allemande then promenade that line
You promenade back home with that dear little sweetheart
That hometown sweetheart of mine
