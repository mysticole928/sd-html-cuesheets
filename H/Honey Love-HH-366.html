And now the sides (heads) square thru

Then you balance too, swing thru
Boys run , wheel and deal , meet a couple, star thru
Then square thru three hands
Swing the corner lady, left allemande new corner
Promenade , you do
Honey, now that I've found you, I'll treat you good
