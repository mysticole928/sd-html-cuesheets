Hurry Hurry Hurry   (Top 25090 )   Dick Leger
SEQUENCE

Figure twice, break, figure twice

FIGURE



Head (side) two ladies chain across and
Couples 1 & 3 lead to the right & circle 4
Make a line you see
Go forward up and back with you and then pass thru
Ca. twirl and the same 2 ladies chain (straight across)
Flutterwheel, ladies walk across
A brand new corner swing
Join your hands & circle left
We've got a brand new ring
Ladies in, men sashay, by that corner go
Ladies in, men sashay, walk on your heel & toe
Allemande left your corner girl, grand right & left the ring
When you meet your lady fair, dosado and then
Take your corner lady and you promenade her home
You'd better hurry, hurry, hurry, hurry home

BREAK



4 little ladies chain across & turn them left around
Join your hands & circle left, go walkin' round the town
Ladies in, men sashay, walk on your heel & toe
Ladies in, men sashay, you go, man, go
Allemande left your corner girl, grand right & left the ring
When you meet your corner lady
Well, there isn't time to swing
Promenade your honey, go walkin' right back home
You'd better hurry, hurry, hurry, hurry home
