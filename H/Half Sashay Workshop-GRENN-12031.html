Half Sashay Workshop
(Grenn 12031 side A ) Johnny Davis
INTRO

All around the left hand lady, see saw your pretty taw
Face your corner, all eight chain, circle up 8 with might and main
Ladies to the right half sashay, men to the left half sashay
Ladies go right, left allemande

1. HEADS ALLE. THAR:

Heads to the middle, right hand star, turn corner left, allemande thar
Heads back up a right hand star, shoot the star all the way round
Heads star right across the town, opposite one allemande thar
Heads back up a right hand star, 8 rollaway a half sashay, heads back up same way
Shoot the star, pass one and swing the next, promenade
Heads wheel around, right & left thru, rollaway half sashay
Half square thru, right & left grand

2. HALF SHOT:

Allemande left, allemande thar, right & left, form a star, men back up a right hand star
Shoot the star, you're on your way, right & left half sashay
Head gent re-sashay, go all the way round
Star by the left across the town, opposite lady right hand round
To the right hand lady left hand round, put 'er on the right and circle up 8
Allemande left you're on your way, right & left half sashay
Side gent re-sashay, go all the way round
Star by the left across the town, opposite lady right hand round
To the right hand lady, left hand half
Back by the right go all the way round, corner all left allemande

3. ROLLING BENT SQUARES

Head two ladies chain across, whirlaway with a half sashay
Side two couples right & left thru, same two ladies chain
#1 couple down the center, split that pair and line up four
Forward four, back in time, up to the middle, bend the line
Square thru while the sides divide and box the gnat, face inside
Dosado the one you meet, all the way round to an ocean wave
Rock up and back, 8 rollaway a half sashay
Balance again same ole way, swing by the left, left allemande
