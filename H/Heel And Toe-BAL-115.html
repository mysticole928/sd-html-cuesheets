Heel And Toe
   (Balance 115)    Ed Gilmore
FIGURE

Allemande left, alamo style
Grand right & left, promenade home and swing
Heads go forward up and back, square thru
Dosado to an ocean wave
Right & left thru, dive thru, star thru
Cross trail thru, dosado, right & left thru
Dive thru, California twirl, allemande left
Grand right & left, promenade home

2 & 4 forward up and back
Square thru, dosado to an ocean wave
Ladies face for a heel and toe
Same ladies chain, chain back
Right & left thru, pass thru, forward out and back
With the lady on the right, left allemande
Right & left grand, promenade home, swing

Heads forward and back, square thru
Dosado to an ocean wave
Ladies face for a heel and toe
Same ladies chain, chain back
Square thru 3/4, allemande left
Promenade home, bow to partner and corner

Patter call borrowed from a quadrille by Van Van der Walker, San Diego, CA.
