Hasta Manana    (D 655)   Frank Lane

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



All 4 ladies chain go straight across that ring
You roll away and circle left
4 ladies roll away, you circle left around I say
Left allemande and now let's weave the ring, go
Hasta manana, until we meet again
Do a dosado and promenade
Don't forget to send me a letter, say you forgive me the sooner the better
Hasta manana until then

FIGURE #1



Oh 1st and 3rd you square thru go 4 hands
And around that corner do a dosado
Let's curlique, and a walk & dodge and a partner trade
Pass thru and now let's do a wheel & deal
Do a double pass thru and go track two
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Don't forget to send me a letter, say you forgive me the sooner the better
Hasta manana until then

FIGURE #2



1st and 3rd you promenade go halfway
While 2 & 4 you do a right & left thru
Flutterwheel and then, sweep 1/4 more
You pass thru, do a dosado, then eight chain 4
Well darling, our love is much too strong to die
Swing that corner, promenade
Don't forget to send me a letter, say you forgive me the sooner the better
Hasta manana until then
