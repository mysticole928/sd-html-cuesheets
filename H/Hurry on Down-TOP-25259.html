Hurry On Down
(Top 25259) Jim Cargill
BREAK

4 ladies chain across, turn them left and then
Join hands , circle left go walking around the ring
Ladies roll a 1/2 sashay , circle left around I say
4 ladies roll away , weave that old ring
Weave in and out around you go, meet partner dosado
Allemande your corner girl, come back and promenade
Promenade 2 by 2, take that lady back home with you
Hurry down to the square dance baby and swing tonight

FIGURE

1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around
Dosado your corner lady one time around
Swing thru 2 by 2, boys run to the right you do
Wheel & deal , face those 2 and do a right & left thru
Now dive thru , square thru 3/4 around
Swing that corner lady boys 2 times around
Promenade 2 by 2, take that lady back home with you
Hurry down to the square dance baby, we will swing tonight
