Happy Birthday Medley
   (Scope 630)    Don Pfister
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
Happy birthday to you, happy birthday to you
Happy birthday (insert name), happy birthday to you
Four ladies chain , you turn the girl and then
Send 'em back in a right hand star
Catch 'em by the left and then, roll promenade my friend
You promenade this lady go walking full around the ring
Many long years ago

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Heads square thru four hands
Gonna meet that corner lady there, curlique , scoot back
Go two by two and then, boys run to the right my friend
Reverse the flutter wheel go walking full around
Now sweep 1/4 more and curlique again you do
Scoot back there one more time, swing the corner girl
You promenade this lady, go walking round the ring I said
Many long years ago

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Circle left
Happy birthday to you, happy birthday to you
Allemande left on the corner lady then do a dosado
The men star left around the ring
Now turn thru with the partner and you go left allemande again
Come back and swing your own and then you promenade
You promenade this lady go walking round the ring and then
Many long years ago

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Happy birthday to you, happy birthday to you
Happy birthday everybody, happy birthday
Left allemande and weave
The old gray mare she ain't what she used to be
Ain't what she used to be
Dosado and promenade
The old gray mare she ain't what she used to be
Many long years ago
