Join hands , circle left ... The curfew must not ring
Left hand round the corner girl , your partner right hand swing
Your corner box the gnat , 4 ladies chain across the dell
Join hands , circle left .
We must not ring the bell - hang on the bell
Now the partner dosado
Go back, swing the corner girl and promenade her home
As you sway to the left and you sway to the right
Remember that curfew must never ring tonite

FIGURE

Then 1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade 1/2 way round you do
You pass thru - both turn right - sides pass thru too
Two right hand stars outside the set - you turn them once around
Girls star left - men promenade the wrong way round the town
Twice round the ring - now box the gnat so neat
Then pull her by - swing the next girl so sweet
You allemande new corner - promenade the girl in sight
Remember that curfew must never ring tonite
