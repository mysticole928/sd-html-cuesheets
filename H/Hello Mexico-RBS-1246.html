(Grand weave: From a static square. 4 ladies split their corner: i.e., run
right in front and fold behind corners to form 4 single file columns of 2 at
right angles, all weave through the center by walking straight in front of man
on right, man on right passes in front of girl on the left, girl on left passes
in front of girl on the right: on the other side man makes a right-face turn
back to star thru with the trailing girl; the above completes 1/4 of a Grand
Weave. It may be called 1/4, 1/2, or 3/4 or simply Grand Weave as in this
record. 64 beats.)
