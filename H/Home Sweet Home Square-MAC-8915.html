SEQUENCE

Break, figure twice, break, figure twice

BREAK

You'll circle 1/2 the town
Dive thru..right & left thru
Turn 'em full around
Through the sides crosstrail
Around just one and then
Star by the right..your corners allemande
It's a right & left around the ring
When you meet your own
Give 'er a twirl..promenade
Sing it as you roam
Be it ever so humble

Take the next girl..promenade
We'll sing it as you roam
Be it ever so humble
There's no place like home
