Hawaiian Christmas Song
(Omar 113) Corben Geis
OPENER - CLOSER

Sides face grand square
Mele Kalikimaka is the thing to say on a bright Hawaiian Christmas day
That's the island greeting that we send to you
From the land where palm trees sway , Allemande and weave
Here we know that Christmas will be green and bright
The sun will shine by day and all the stars at night
Mele Kalikimaka is the Hawaiian way to say Merry Christmas to you

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) Square thru 4 , then you make a right hand star
Then star by the left in the middle and urn it one time
Back to the same two and do the right & left thru (make your waves)
Swing thru and then, come on and do that again
Swing your corner and promenade
Mele Kalikimaka is the Hawaiian way to say Merry Christmas to you

MIDDLE BREAK

4 ladies chain , chain them back
Join hands circle left
Do an allemande left your corner
Now weave the ring
Here we know that Christmas will be green and bright
The sun will shine by day and all the stars at night
Mele Kalikimaka is the Hawaiian way to say Merry Christmas to you
