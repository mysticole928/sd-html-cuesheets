Hangin' Around   (Lou Mac 175 )   Randy Dougherty

INTRO



Four ladies chain across the ring
Hey, roll away and circle to the left
Four ladies roll away, circle to the left go round
Left allemande and you weave the ring
What am I doing hangin' round?
Swing the little girl and promenade
I should be riding on that train to Mexico
What am I doing hangin' round?

FIGURE



Heads pass thru and promenade back home
Sides square thru 4 hands
You promenade all the way around
Slide thru, reverse flutter wheel one time
Ladies lead dixie style, make your wave
Boys turn back and you promenade
I should be riding on that train to Mexico
What am I doing hangin' round?
