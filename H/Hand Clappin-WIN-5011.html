Hand Clappin'   (Windsor 5011 )   Larry Jack

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



1 & 3 dosado and then star thru
Pass thru, circle 4 halfway around you do
Dive thru, right & left thru, a full turn around
You star thru with the corner and then square thru 3/4 around the ring
Swing the corner maid, left allemande new corner, weave the ring
You clap hands and holler, we're havin' a ball
Dosado and promenade that old hall
We're raisin' the roof and a-havin' a ball
It's a hand clappin', foot stompin' call

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies promenade, now men clap hands
Get back home and swing her, everybody you swing
Join hands and circle to the left around the ring
Walk around the corner, see saw around your own
Now men star right, it's once around you roam
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
You clap hands and holler, we're havin' a ball
Dosado and promenade that old hall
We're raisin' the roof and a-havin' a ball
It's a hand clappin', foot stompin' call
