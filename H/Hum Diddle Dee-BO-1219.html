Hum Diddle Dee    (BO 1219)  Al Eblen

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Walk all around your corner, bow down to your partner
4 little ladies chain across that ring, you turn and circle left
You circle to the left, roll away with a half sashay, circle left in the same old way
Allemande left with your corner, pass your partner
Swing that right hand girl around, promenade
You promenade her home and swing her for your own
Singing hum diddle diddle dee, hum diddle diddle dee aye
