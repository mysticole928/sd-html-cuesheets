Hang On The Bell    (D 608)   Frank Lane

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Oh join your hands and circle, the curfew must not ring
Allemande left the corner, turn your own with a right hand swing
Now corner swat the flea, and all 4 ladies chain
Straight across and turn the girl and circle left again
Oh hang on the bell Nellie, hang on the bell
Swing the corner lady and promenade the hall
It swings to the left, and it swings to the right
Remember Nell, the curfew bell must never ring tonight

FIGURE



Oh 1st and 3rd you promenade just half around you do
You pass thru and you both turn right, sides pass thru
And you make two right hand stars right there, you're doing fine
Girls star left in the middle and boys run around this time
The second time you see your girl, box the gnat
Then pull her by, swing the next little lady round like that
Promenade back home with the cutest girl in sight
Remember Nell, the curfew bell must never ring tonight
