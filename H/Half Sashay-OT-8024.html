Half Sashay
(Old Timer 8024 side A ) Butch Nelson
FIGURE

First couple out to the couple on the right
Circle four with all your might
Sashay halfway round her
Resashay and all the way round her
Circle four and around you go
Two little ladies dosado
Dosi high and dosi low
One more change and circle four
Circle four until you get fixed
Pick up two and we'll make it six
Sashay halfway round her
Resashay and all the way round her
Circle six and around you go
Three little ladies dosado
Doe, doe, and a little more doe
One more change and on you go
Circle six and don't be late
Pick up two and make it eight
Sashay halfway round her
Resashay and all the way round her
Circle eight and around you go
Four little ladies dosado
Dosado and a little more doe
One more change and home you go

(Repeat entire call for second couple.)
