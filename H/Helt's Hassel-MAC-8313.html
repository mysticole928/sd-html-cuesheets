Helt's Hassel / Smile In Your Style
   (MacGregor 8313 side A )   
 
SUNNY MONDAY

Ladies center, back to the bar, gents center, a right hand star
Turn the opposite left and square the set
Head men with corner girls go forward and back
Circle four 3/4 round on the inside track
Face the girl on the right of you, pass thru
Circle 3/4 with the outside two, face the girl on the right of you
Back out in lines of 4, forward 8 and back like that
Ends go forward, box the gnat, 1/4 face out separate, go round two
Into the middle, a right hand star
One full turn in the middle of the land, corner's left allemande, etc.

TRIPLE TROUBLE (BREAK)

Allemande left, head ladies star (right hand star)
The others go round the outside bar (single file)
Allemande left, side ladies star (allemande same corners)
The others go round the outside bar, allemande left, head men star
The others go round the outside bar, allemande left, side men star
The others go round the outside bar, allemande left, etc.

SLOW DOWNER (BREAK)

Promenade, don't slow down, 1 & 3 wheel around
Right & left thru with a full turn around
Pass thru the next two, face your own
Square thru right, left, right, left, cross trail the next two
Box the gnat with the folks you've met
Allemande left with the lady on the left

DOUBLE SQUARE THRU (BREAK)

Heads forward, back with you, forward again, square thru twice you do
Sides divide with a square thru
Round the outside go
(The heads square thru twice in the middle while the sides do a
single square thru around the heads. Both heads and sides are
working independently of each other at the same time ending facing
corner with right hand free.)

ARKIE THAR BREAK

Corners right a wrong way thar
The side couples back up in a left hand star
(Side men with original side ladies back up in a left hand,
wrong way thar with the original head men with original head
ladies go forward on the rim of the wrong way thar.)
Side couples back up, but not too much
Eight roll away and throw out the clutch
(The eight roll away is done by turning half around with the
right forearm, then push forward with the right hand releasing
your forearm to do a half right face individual turn then
everyone progresses forward as in throw out the clutch.)
With the same girl you've met (original corner)
Allemande left, etc.

CRAZIE DAZIE BREAK

Allemande left with the corner Mack
Right, left, first man with the left turn back (#1 man)
Right, left, second man by the left turn back
Right, left, third man by the left turn back
Right, left, fourth man by the left turn back
(When an indicated man is called to turn back by the left,
he reverses his direction, also the person he is turning
by the left forearm, and continue the grand right and left.)
The crazie dazie is repeated once more to put you
back into a normal right & left grand
