Holly Jolly Christmas    (RYL 1203)   Patty Greene

OPENER



Have a holly, jolly Christmas,
It's the best time of the year
Men star right, one time tonight
Allemande left and weave the ring
Have a holly, jolly Christmas
Swing your partner and promenade
Oh by golly have a holly jolly Christmas this year

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru four hands you go
You make a right-hand star
Heads star left one time around
With your corner do a right & left thru
Swing thru tonight
And now the boys run right
Half tag, swing your corner promenade
Oh by golly have a holly jolly Christmas this year
