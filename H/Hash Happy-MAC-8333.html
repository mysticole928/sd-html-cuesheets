It's You I Love / Hash Happy
   (MacGregor 8333 side B )   
OPENER

Honor your partners, corners all, circle left around the hall
Corners roll a half sashay, circle left same old way
Corners roll away sashay, allemande left, come back one promenade home
(The men now have opposite ladies ready to start the figure.)

(Air Hammer)

Heads go forward and back
Head ladies chain, turn twice with all your might
Head couples lead to the right, circle four around you roam
Head men drop off at home, forward six and back that way
Six to the right, 1/4 sashay, forward eight and back again
Four men dixie chain, first man right, second man left
Forward eight and back with you, pass thru face the girl beside you
Dosado all the way around, to an ocean wave at the side of town
Swing by the right to a left allemande, etc.

(Hot Ham 'n Eggs)

Allemande left just like that, partners all box the gnat
Girls star left 3/4 there
Twirl 'em out, heads promenade half the square
Head ladies chain across, turn 'em boy don't get lost
Opposite swing in the middle of the ring, face the sides
Circle four 3/4 around then no more
Pass thru, face your own
Outside arch, inside under, around one
Turn the left hand lady under, u-turn back
Center people forward, opposite box the gnat, 1/4 face in, pass thru
Left allemande, etc.

(Divided Ends)

Heads go forward and back that way, roll away with a half sashay
Lead to the right, circle four, around you roam
Head men go home alone, forward six and back to the land
Ends divide go around the lone man, forward six and back now
Ends divide go around the lone gal, forward six and back like that
Ends go forward, box the gnat
Star by the right in the middle of the town
Pick up your own with an arm around
Slide the star wide, turn your partner under
Reverse the star, don't you blunder
The rims backtrack around the land, turn your partner with a right hand
Allemande left, a right & left grand
