Hard Times Roll    (AK 106)   King Caldwell

OPENER



Circle left
Sometimes it's hard to see the silver lining
When the storms of life seem like they'll never end
Men star right one time you go now
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
Wo-o-o-o, let the hard times roll
Swing that little girl and promenade
Wo-o-o-o, let the hard times roll
Let them roll on by, let the good times roll in

FIGURE



Heads promenade and go about halfway
Come down the middle and do the right & left thru
Square thru 4 hands around the ring now
With the sides you make a right hand star
Heads star left in the middle and turn it around you go
Swing your corner, promenade
Wo-o-o-o, let the hard times roll
Let them roll on by, let the good times roll in

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade go inside
Get on back, swing there with your man
Join hands, circle left go moving
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
Wo-o-o-o, let the hard times roll
Swing the little girl and promenade
Wo-o-o-o, let the hard times roll
Let them roll on by, let the good times roll in
