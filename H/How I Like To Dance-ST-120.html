How I Like To Dance

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic, R-H lady progression

Join your hands and circle left , go walking around the ring
Left allemande and then go forward two
Turn back one , now box the gnat , gents star left from there
Dosado , then weave the square
Weave in and out around until you meet your own
Dosado this lady and then promenade her home
Promenade, you walk them two by two
Oh how I like to dance with you

1 & 3 (2 & 4) you lead to the right , circle four , make a line
Without a stop, do a right & left thru
Forward back you reel, pass thru , wheel & deal
Centers dosado full around, spin the top
When you meet this girl then star thru
Swing and whirl the corner maid, now promenade
Promenade go walking two by two
Oh, but I like to dance with you
