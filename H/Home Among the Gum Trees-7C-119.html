Home Among The Gum Trees
Captain Rock; John Williamson 1974
(Seven C's 119) Ian Mitchem
OPENER

All 4 ladies chain , now turn the girl around
Roll away , circle to the left and now
Ladies roll away , well circle left I say
Allemande left the corner, weave the ring (sing it with me)
Give me a home amongst the gum trees
With lots of plum trees, a sheep or two and a kangaroo
And a clothes line out the back, verandah out the front
And an old rocking chair

FIGURE

Heads square thru go 4 hands around
Round the corner lady, dosado and now
Swing thru and then spin the top you do
Boys move up and then go right & left thru
Square thru 3 hands, walking round the ring
Swing the corner girl, let's promenade I sing
Clothes line out the back and verandah out the front
And an old rocking chair
