Hark The Herald Angels Sing
Waltham Abbey Choir; many others 1739
   (Royal 339)    Jerry Story , Tony Oxendine
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Hark the herald angels sing
Glory to the newborn King
Peace on earth and mercy mild
God and sinners reconciled
Four ladies chain across that ring
Chain her right back and promenade I sing
With the angelic host proclaim
Christ is born in Bethlehem

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads promenade half around you know
Sides square thru four hands you go
All the way and then a right & left thru
Eight chain six across you do
Joyful all you nations rise
Swing your corner , promenade tonight
With the angelic host proclaim
Christ is born in Bethlehem
