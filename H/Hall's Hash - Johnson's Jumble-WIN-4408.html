Hall's Hash / Johnson's Jumble
   (Windsor 4408 side A )    Bob Hall , Bruce Johnson
FIGURE ("Riptide")

Well, honor your partner, and honor your corner
And first and third go forward and back, then star by the right on the inside track
And turn your corner with a left hand swing and box the gnat in the middle of the ring
Pass right back to the sides of the town, with a dosado go full around
Like an ocean wave you hang on tight, go forward and back, then swing by the right
Same couple star with the old left wing, to the opposite shore with a right hand swing
Back to the middle and box the flea, pass right back to the sides of the sea
See saw round 'em, all the way around, and you make that wave as you come down
Go forward and back and swing by the left, same couples star in center of the set
Turn your corner with a left elbow, it's partners all with a dosado

FIGURE ("The Backtracker")

Now two and four do a half sashay, go up to the middle and back that way
Take the opposite lady and box the gnat, come right & left thru the other way back
Turn right back and pass right thru, you split that ring and around just two
And by your corners stand
Forward eight and eight fall back, go forward again and box the gnat
Come right & left thru the other way back
Then turn right back and pass right thru, and then you stop!
Swing the gal behind you, pop!
Now the center couples you form a ring, and circle half with the dear little thing
Sashay by your partners two, re-sashay then a right & left thru
And turn 'em around like you always do, and swing that gal in front of you
Swing her on out to the edge of the ring, and all join hands and form a ring
Walk all around that ol' left hand lady, see saw round your pretty little baby

FIGURE ("The Free Wheeler")

Now allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and form a star
You back 'em up, boys, but not too far
The gents wheel out, the ladies star, turn the opposite gent from where you are
Go once and a half and the gents star in, turn the opposite lady with a left hand spin
A right to the corner like a wrong way thar, back 'em, boys, in a left hand star
You throw out the clutch and put 'er in low, it's twice around that ring you go
Skip that gal as you come down and turn the next with the right hand around
And the right hand lady with the left hand around, partner right with a full turn around
A left to the corner like an allemande thar, you back 'em up, boys, in a right hand star
Shoot this star with a full turn around, turn the left hand lady with the right hand round
Go all the way round...

FIGURE ("Shooting Star")

Do an allemande left and a right to your dears, it's a wagon wheel but strip those gears
You catch her by the left with a dopaso, it's corners right and around you go
Then a left hand round your pretty little maid, the ladies star, the gents promenade
Go twice around to your pretty little girl, then shoot that star with a left hand whirl
It's once and a half and the gents star in, the girls run around on the outside rim
Wave to your gal, we're off to the races, pass her again and then trade places
Gents step out and the girls star in, go twice around 'til you meet her again
Now box the gnat and the gents star back, the gals run around on the outside track
It's twice around to your pretty little date, shoot that star with a catch all eight
By the right hand half, back by the left hand all the way around
And promenade the corner as she comes down
Yes, promenade, and you walk with pride, you've got mamma by your side
Now honor your partner, corners all, and you wave to the gal across the hall
Do an allemande left, don't go too far, and bow to your honey and there you are!
