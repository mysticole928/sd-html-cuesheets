Hoop Dee Doo Polka    (SH 168)   Max Normando

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain, turn your girl
Join your hands and circle left, go 'round the world,
Left allemande, allemande thar
Go right & left then now the four gents make that star
Shoot that star, dosado
Swing your girl, then promenade,
Promenade, go 'round the ring, go back home, swing and sing,
Hoop de doin it tonight

FIGURE



1 & 3 right & left thru, 2 & 4 cross trail thru,
Go round one, ladies lead a dixie chain
Gals go left, gents go right round one into the middle,
Box the gnat, half square thru (outside two)
Right & left thru, turn your girl, then cross trail thru,
U turn back, swing your corner too,
Swing that corner gal around, promenade, go 'round the town,
And you'll hoop de do it tonight
