OPENER



circle left
I'm walking in the moonlight beneath the old gum tree
dreaming of a sheila who's been so good to me
do an allemande left your corner, come back & dosado
left allemande your corner & weave the ring you know
weave it in & weave it out until you meet that maid
swing & whirl that pretty little girl & then you promenade
*Walking in the moonlight beneath the old gum tree

*yodel or Dreaming of ___ who's been so good to me



FIGURE

head/side 2 couples rollaway, go up to the middle & back



now swing thru 2 by 2, the boys run right and then
you ferris wheel & the center 2 will zoom back my friend
new centers square thru 3 hands around you go now
swing & whirl that corner girl & promenade her home now
Walking in the moonlight beneath the old gum trees
with a wallaby stew and a damper too
(dreaming of a Sheila who's been so good to me)
that's where I long to be

MIDDLE BREAK



4 little ladies chain across gents,
turn 'em around and don't get lost
you join up hands & make a little ring &
you circle left like any old thing
do an allemande left, go allemande thar,
it's right & left and a little old star
the men swing in with a right hand then
you've got that back up star
shoot that star & weave the ring and
The Dingos hows and the Bonyips sing
and you promenade just like that
*With the back-jumping maggie and the leather-bellied pat
walking in the moonlight beneath the old gum trees

*yodel or Dreaming of ___ who's been so good to me
