Hoop De Do Polka    (BM 167)   Mac McCullar

Four ladies chain, turn your girl



Left allemande, allemande thar
Go right & left, four men you star
Shoot that star, dosado
Swing your lady, promenade
*Promenade go around the ring, go back home, swing
And sing hoop de do-in' it tonight

CLOSER



*Promenade go around the ring, take her home swing and
Hoop de do, hoop de do, hoop de do alright (tonight)

FIGURE



Heads square thru four hands in the middle you do
Around to the corner one dosado
Swing thru go two by two, boys run to the right
Bend the line and do the right & left thru
Pass the ocean there, recycle around
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Promenade go around the ring, take her home
Swing and sing hoop de do-in' it tonight
