Hot Pepper
(Grenn 12006 side B )
SPLIT SQUARE THRU NO. 1

Heads go forward, back with you, same two split square thru
All the way round the outside track, inside 2 box the gnat
Then square thru 4 hands round and 1/4 more
Outside two California twirl, corners all left allemande

SPLIT SQUARE THRU NO. 2

2 & 4 half sashay, up to the middle and back that way
Same two split square thru all the way round, that's what you do
Inside two 1/2 square thru, separate around one into the middle
Square thru 3/4 round, outside 2 California twirl, left allemande

CRAZY CORNER

Heads to the right, circle up 4, head gent break, line up 4
Forward 4 and back again, pass thru, lines pair in
Inside two right & left thru, same two face your partner
Box the gnat, split square thru other way back
All the way round and 1/4 more, lines of four go out and back
Ladies to the right half sashay, arch in the middle, ends turn in
Pass thru, split two, around one, here comes your corner, left allemande

SPLIT THRU KINDERGARDEN

Side 2 couples split square thru, all the way round, that's what you do
Outside two separate around one
In the middle split square thru, all the way round, that's what you do
Outside two separate around one, crosstrail thru, u-turn back
Same 2 split square thru, all the way round, that's what you do
Outside two separate around one
In the middle split square thru, all the way round, that's what you do
Outside 2 separate around one, crosstrail thru to a left allemande

SPLIT SQUARE PAIRS

Heads to the right, circle up 4, break to a line, you're doing fine
Forward 8 and back with you, box the gnat across from you
Inside two right & left thru, same two split square thru
3/4 around to line up 4, forward out and back once more

LINES PAIR IN & DOUBLE PASS THRU

First couple left, next one right, circle up 4 those in sight
Head gent break and make it 8, circle up 8 til you get straight
Four ladies rollaway to a catch all 8
Back by the left, left allemande, gents to the right, right & left grand
