Hurry, Hurry, Hurry
Don Reid; The Andrews Sisters 1949
   (Grenn 12223)    Dick Leger
 
FIGURE    A1, no progression

Head two ladies chain across and couples 1 & 3 (2 & 4)
Lead to the right and circle four , make a line you see
Go forward up and back with you and then pass thru
California twirl and the same two ladies chain straight across
The same four cross trail , a brand new corner swing
Join your hands and circle left , we've got a brand new ring
Ladies in, men sashay , by the corner go
Ladies in, men sashay , walk on your heel and tow
Allemande left your corner girl, grand right & left the ring
When you meet your lady fair dosado and then
Take your corner lady and you promenade her home
You'd better hurry, hurry, hurry, hurry home

MIDDLE BREAK    Basic - SSD Week 2

Four little ladies chain across and turn them left around
Join your hands and circle left , go walkin' round the town
Ladies in, men sashay walk on your heel and tow
Ladies in, men sashay , you go man go!
Allemande left your corner girl, grand right & left the ring
When you meet your corner lady, well there isn't time to swing
Promenade your honey, go walkin' right back home
You'd better hurry, hurry, hurry, hurry home

(FIGURE twice heads, MIDDLE BREAK, FIGURE twice sides)
