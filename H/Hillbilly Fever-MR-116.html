Hillbilly Fever
   (Mountain 116)    Kim Hohnholt
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    C1

allemande left & then do an allemande thar
all 4 men swing right in & make a back up star
remake your thar
slip the clutch & you weave around the ring
that hillbilly fever's going round
dosado your lady and promenade on down
promenade your lady
her fever's running wild
take her right on home in that hillbilly style

FIGURE    Plus, corner progression

heads (sides) lead to the right , circle 4 on around the track
break & make 2 lines of 4 , up to the middle & back
pass thru & a wheel & deal , double pass thru
track 2 and move it round you do
recycle round to face
pass thru , swing the corner & promenade the hall
promenade your lady
her fever's running wild
take her right on home in that hillbilly style

The "slip the clutch" is really a cross by (or step thru).
