Bunny Hop / Hinkey Dinkey Parley Vous
   (Old Timer 8161 side B )    Henry Knight
 
OPENER

It's everybody go forward and back, parley vous
Forward again and stand right there
All shake hands across the square
Then swing your partners round and round
And when you're through just settle down
Hinky dinky parley vous

FIGURE

The head two ladies go forward and back, parley vous
Forward again and dosado, parley vous
Now dosado round your corners all
Come back and swing with your partners all
Hinky dinky parley vous
The side two ladies go forward and back, parley vous
Forward again and dosado, parley vous
Now dosado round your corners all
Come back and swing with your partners all
Hinky dinky parley vous
Keep swinging
Swing our partners round and round, swing them around
Promenade around the tow, promenade
Promenade on your toes, all the belles and all the beaus
Hinky dinky parley vous

MIDDLE BREAK

All join hands and circle left, parley vous
The other way back, you circle right, parley vous
Swing your partners round and round
And when you're through, just settle down
Hinky dinky parley vous

FIGURE

(as above with head man, then with side man)

CLOSER

All join hands and circle left, parley vous
The other way back, you circle right, parley vous
Swing your partners round and round
And when you're through, just settle down
Hinky dinky parley vous
