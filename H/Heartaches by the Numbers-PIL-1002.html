Circle left
Heartache number one is when you circle

Heartache number two is roll away a half sashay
Join eight hands and circle to the left around that way (and now)
You allemande left with the corner, grand right & left and then
When you meet your girl, dosado, promenade around the ring
I've got heartaches by the number, troubles by the score

FIGURE



And now head two couples promenade, halfway around you go
Down the middle a right & left thru and then turn the girl
Square thru four hands round and when you meet the outside two
Dosado all the way around an ocean wave and then, swing thru
The boys trade, turn thru, left allemande and then
Dosado the partner, promenade around my friend
I've got heartaches by the number, a love that I can't win
But the day that I stop counting, that's the day my world would end
