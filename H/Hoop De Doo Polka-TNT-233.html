Hoop De Doo    (TNT 233)   Al Brundage

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade hoop de doo
Swing the girl when she gets home to you
Join hands & circle, circle the ring
Left allemande & then you weave the ring
Hoop dee doo, hoop dee doo
Dosado and promenade, promenade
*Along with me, c'mon home and I can see
We're hoop de do'in it tonight

FIGURE



Heads/sides promenade & you go 1/2 way around
Down the middle & do a right & left thru
Slide thru, square thru 3/4 and then
Make a left hand star, with the outside 2 (lady lead)
Heads/sides star right & turn it once around
Swing the corner lady, promenade, promenade
*And hold me tight, take me home we'll do alright
A hoop de do'in it tonight

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Heads promenade 1/2,
Square thru, single circle to a wave
Boys trade, boys run, ferris wheel
Square thru 3/4, swing and promenade

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*Around the set, dance me home & you can bet

*Around with me, we'll be as happy as can be
