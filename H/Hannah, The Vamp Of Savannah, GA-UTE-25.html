Hannah, The Vamp Of Savannah, GA
Ella Fitzgerald 1924
   (Ute 25)    Bob Main
OPENER - CLOSER    Basic, corner progression

Join all your hands , circle left and then
Reverse in single-file promenade
Men turn in and star right , once around tonight
Left allemande and weave the ring
Wind it in and out until you meet your own
Swing with Hannah, promenade her home
She's hard-hearted Hannah, the vamp of Savannah, GA.

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Four ladies chain 3/4 around that ring
Heads promenade 3/4 go
Those sides dosado full around in the middle you go
Pass thru and then go right & left thru
Why don't you dive thru , swing thru , turn thru
Left allemande , come on back swing with Hannah, promenade
She's hard-hearted Hannah, the vamp of Savannah, GA.

MIDDLE BREAK    B - SSD Week 1

Join all your hands , circle left and go
Left allemande , come on back and dosado
Those Men star by the right , once around tonight
Left allemande and weave around that ring
Move it in and out until you meet your own
Swing with Hannah, promenade her home
She's hard-hearted Hannah, the vamp of Savannah, GA.
