Humpty Dumpty Heart    (SS 2363)   Rocky Strickland

OPENER



Circle to the left now
Humpty Dumpty sat on the wall, Humpty Dumpty had a big fall
All the king's horses, all the king's men, couldn't put him together again
You do an allemande left ole corner, come back a dosado
A left allemande, then weave the ring
I've got a Humpty Dumpty heart, you dropped it and broke it apart
Dosado go all the way around and promenade around that town
I gave to you my heart, you said that we must part
It was my doom, my heart went boom, I've got a Humpty Dumpty heart

FIGURE



Head couples promenade 3/4 around you go
2 & 4 go right & left thru, turn the girl around you do
A double pass thru, first left, the next two right
Go right & left thru and turn that girl, star thru
Pass thru and swing a left allemande
Come back a dosado and then promenade around that land
I gave to you my heart, you said that we must part
You got it on a platter, and let it shatter, my Humpty Dumpty heart

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain across, turn the girl around
Join hands, circle to the left go walking around the ring and then
Roll away a 1/2 sashay, circle left and then
You roll away and weave the ring
I've got a Humpty Dumpty heart, you dropped it and broke it apart
Dosado go all the way around and promenade around that town
I gave to you my heart, you said that we must part
It was my doom, my heart went boom, I've got a Humpty Dumpty heart
