FIGURE    A1, corner progression

Head (side) ladies chain you do

Cross trail , u-turn back
Pass on thru
Dosado to an ocean wave
Balance forward and back

Cross trail , u-turn back and swing
Promenade home with the girl you know

*Daisy Chain: After the allemande left starting with a right to the partner and
a left to the next turn back one for a right hand swing. Then forward again with
a left and right and turn back one for a left hand swing, then forward again with
a right and left. Turn back one for a right hand swing, then forward again with
a left hand, right to partner and promenade.
