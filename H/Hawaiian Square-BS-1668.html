Hawaiian Square
   (Blue Star 1668)    Vaughn Parrish
OPENER    A1

Head ladies chain , sides go right & left thru
Four ladies chain you know
Heads a right & left thru , side
ladies chain
Head couples you do a dosado all the way around
Cross trail thru , behind the sides , star thru
Double pass thru then a frontier whirl you do
Centers just pass thru , left allemande and then
Go home, swing your partner again

FIGURE    A1, corner progression

Four ladies chain across the ring you know
Heads (sides) go right , circle four , make a
line , don't stop
Star thru , dive thru , pass thru then star thru
Cross trail thru and swing the
corner girl
Then allemande new corner, docey your own
You promenade her, take her right back home
Swing like you want her alone
