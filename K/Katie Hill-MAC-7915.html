Katie Hill / Goin' Up A Little Creek
(MacGregor 7915 side A )
FIGURE (1)

The first couple stand back to back
Right to your corner, box the gnat
The heads go forward up and back with you
Forward again, trail thru
Around the outside, around two, form two lines of 4
Forward 8, come back like that
Forward again, box the gnat, pass thru, u-turn back
Circle up 8 on the same old track
Now one little lady roll away with a 1/2 sashay
Circle up 8 in the same old way, now two little ladies roll away
Circle up 8 in the same old way, now three little ladies roll away
Circle up 8 in the same old way
Swing on the corner like swinging on a vine
Promenade, go down the line, promenade 'em two by two
When you get back home, she'll swing with you
Everybody swing

FIGURE (2)

1 & 3 go forward up and back with you
Forward again, right & left thru
The same two ladies chain
#2 stand back to back, right to your corner, box the gnat
The sides go forward up and back with you
Forward again, trail thru
Go round that outside, around two, form two lines of 4
Forward again, pass thru
Don't look back, two circles of 4 on the same old track
The ladies star right, the boys star left
Walk right along, you're not through yet
The gents step in behind your gal
Walk right along with the pretty little pal
The gals roll back, pass one man
Allemande left with the old left hand
Here we go, right & left grand
Hand over hand on the heel and toe
When you meet your partner, promeno

Note: When sides lead with the right & left thru, either of the head couples can stand back to back for the balance of the figure.
