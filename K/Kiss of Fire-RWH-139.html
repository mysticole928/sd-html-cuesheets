GYPSY FLING DEFINITION:

From two facing dancers.
Lead to the left (2 steps);
back up to the right to end facing (2 steps);
lead to the left (2 steps);
back up to the right to end facing (2 steps).
Ends as facing dancers where you started.
Have dancers clap their hands over their heads.
