Kitatinny Kontra
(Grenn 16015) Walt Cole
FORMATION

Improper Duple; 1, 3, 5, etc. all odd numbered couples crossed over with partners.
Double progression. To set up from squares have side ladies chain; heads face your
partners and back away to lines. Align lines up in long lines down the hall.

FIGURE

With the left hand lady swing (ladies face left, gents face right and do a normal
8-count swing)
Promenade half
Ladies lead dixie style to an ocean wave and balance
Men turn half by the right, with the ladies turn half by the left
Ladies turn half by the right, gents courtesy turn the ladies
Ladies lead in a flutter wheel
Cross trail thru
Trade by and box to the next
