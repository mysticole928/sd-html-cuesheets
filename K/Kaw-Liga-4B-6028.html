Kaw-Liga   (4-Bar-B 6028 )   Bill Owsley

OPENER



Circle left
Kaw-Liga was a wooden Indian standin' over by the door
Fell in love with a Chocktaw maid over at George's store, Kaw-Liga,
Left allemande that corner, weave the ring
Poor old Kaw-Liga he never let it show
Dosado, promenade back home
Is it any wonder that his face is red,
Kaw-Liga that poor old wooden head

FIGURE



Heads (sides) lead right, circle to a line, move up come back, Touch 1/4
All 8 circulate, boys run, right and left thru, turn that girl,
Square thru, but on the third hand swing thru, boys trade, promenade
Is it any wonder that his face is red,
Kaw-Liga that poor old wooden head

BREAK - CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
He always wore his Sunday feathers,
And carried that old black hawk, maiden wore her beads and braids,
And hoped someday he'd talk, Kaw-Liga, standin' there and never show a sign
4 ladies chain, 4 ladies chain back,
Roll, promenade
Is it any wonder that his face is red,
Kaw-Liga that poor old wooden head
