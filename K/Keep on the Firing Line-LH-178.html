Keep On The Firing Line    (LH 178)   Bob Bennett

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain now, chain across that ring
Join hands and circle left, go walking round I sing
Ladies center, men sashay, circle left again
Ladies center, men sashay, circle left and then
Left allemande your corner, your partner dosado
Swing your partner lady, and promenade you know
Promenade big Jim, walk him round the rim
Keep on the firing line

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade halfway round the ring
Come down the middle, do a right & left thru, turn that pretty thing
Heads lead to the right and circle round the track
Head gents break and make a line, go forward & back
Star thru, square thru 3/4 round the square
Swing your corner girl, promenade her there
Promenade big Jim, walk him round the rim
Keep on the firing line

SEQUENCE: (Opener, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides, ending)
