OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and circle left you do
Allemande left, come back and curlique
Boys run right, allemande left, turn partner by the right
Men star left inside the ring
Turn thru with partner to the corner allemande
Come back, swing that girl and promenade
I saw them one evening when they thought they were hid
You should have seen the things that he and Katy did

FIGURE



1 & 3 you promenade go halfway
Lead to the right and circle to a line
Go forward 8 to the middle and back
A right & left thru and then
You flutterwheel and sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and trade by and you go left allemande
Come back a dosado and promenade
I saw them one evening when they thought they were hid
You should have seen the things that he and Katy did

SEQUENCE: (Opener, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides, ending)
