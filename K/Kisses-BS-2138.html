Kisses    (BS 2138)   Bob Yerington

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join all your hands and circle round you go
Left allemande the corner, swing your own
The men star left one time around I say
Turn thru, left allemande, weave the ring that way
Wind in and out around until you meet that girl
Take her by the hand and promenade
I loved you from the start, but still you broke my heart
Your kisses are not kisses any more

FIGURE



The heads promenade go halfway around
Down the middle and do the right & left thru
2 ladies chain across, turn and send her back
Dixie style to a wave, trade the wave and then
Square thru 3/4 round the ring you go
Swing the corner lady, promenade
I loved you from the start, but still you broke my heart
Your kisses are not kisses any more
