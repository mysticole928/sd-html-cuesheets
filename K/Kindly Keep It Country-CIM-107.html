Kindly Keep It Country    (CIM 107)   Gerald McWhirter

Four ladies chain, go straight across the ring



Four ladies roll away, you circle left that way
Allemande left, weave the ring
Kindly keep it country, don't give me no rock and roll
Swing the doll go round and round, promenade home
Don't hit them fancy licks, stick to the melody
Kindly keep it country, is good enough for me

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway, sides right & left thru
Same four square thru 4 hands, dosado
Eight chain 4
Kindly keep it country, don't give me no rock & roll
Swing the doll go round and round, promenade home and sing
Don't be hittin' those fancy licks, stick to the melody
Kindly keep it country, is good enough for me
