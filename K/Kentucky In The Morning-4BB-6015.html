OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Walk around your corner girl, come home and see saw
The men star right , turn it one time you go
Turn your corner by the left , your partner by the right
The girls star left one time
Turn your partner right , left allemande that ole corner
Come back and promenade round that land
Kentucky in the morning, shades of green and blue
I'll be coming back to you

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads promenade , travel halfway round
Come into the middle , star thru and pass thru
And now do a right & left thru , turn 'em
Then square thru on the 3rd hand swing thru
Boys run right , couples ferris wheel
Centers pass thru , corner swing , promenade
Kentucky in the morning, shades of green and blue
I'll be coming back to you

Cue sheet has been modified, in the Opener to change "turn thru" to "turn your partner right" since a full 360° turn is required.
