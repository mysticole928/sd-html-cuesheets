When I'm under that Kimberley moon

The Kimberley's is a region in the North Western part of Australia. It is probably the most isolated and sparsely populated area in Australia. It has been described as the most dangerous and inhospitable, and yet one of the most beautiful regions in the world. There is such a variety in scenery in the Kimberley's that in some areas there are steep cliffs and in others there are rolling plains, sandy beaches and mangrove flat areas. The great thing about the Kimberley's region is that it is virtually untouched by man, due to its rugged nature and inaccessibility.
