Keep Your Sunny Side Up   (Grenn 12046 )   Earl Johnston

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain, go cross the ring
Rollaway, circle left around
Rollaway, circle left around that old land
Allemande left your corner, you go right and left grand
When you meet your lady, do a dosado
That's back to back, then you promeno
Stand upon your legs, be like 2 fried eggs
Promenade you do, take her home with you

FIGURE



One and three (two and four) go up and back, then promenade
1/2 way round, into the middle star thru
Pass thru, split two, go round one and then
Make a right hand star you go round the land
Allemande left that corner, turn a right hand round your own
Come back, swing, promenade well
Stand upon your legs, be like 2 fried eggs
Keep your sunny side up
