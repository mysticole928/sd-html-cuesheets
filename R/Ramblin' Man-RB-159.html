Ramblin' Man    (RB 159)   Jim Coppinger

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All 4 couples star by the right around now
4 girls backtrack go walking round
Men step in behind your girl, go single file
Men roll in, left allemande and weave around awhile
Lord, I was born a ramblin' man
Do a dosado and promenade that land
And when it's time for leaving, I hope you'll understand
That I was born a ramblin' man

FIGURE



Head 2 couples square thru 4 hands now
Meet your corner do the curlique, scoot back
When you're through you cast off 3/4 round and then
Scoot back go once again and then a right & left thru
Dive thru and square thru 3/4 round
Swing the corner girl and promenade
And when it's time for leaving, I hope you'll understand
That I was born a ramblin' man

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway
Lead right, break out to a line
Right & left thru, square thru
Swing corner twice
Left allemande and promenade
