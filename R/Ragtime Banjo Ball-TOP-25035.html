Ragtime Banjo Ball    (TOP 25035)   Paul Moore

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Allemande the corner, walk by your own
Turn a right hand round the right hand girl and a left hand round at home
Men star by the right, one time around
Turn the partner by the left and then a full turn, 4 ladies chain
Chain the girls across, turn on around, chain back
Chain 'em back and turn your own, your corner dosado
Back to back and around, come on back and promenade
Promenade your honey, take a walk now with your maid
Hey you're walkin' with your baby, go home and swing

FIGURE



Head couples forward, come back with a half sashay
Pass thru, turn to the right, behind the sides you stay
Go forward up and back, do a double pass thru
1st couple go right and the next one left, do a right & left thru with 2 you've met
Turn your girl and make a line, go walkin' up and back
Square thru 4 hand around this track
Find the corner, swing her, you swing her round and round
Promenade this cutie, take a walk go round the town
Hey, you're walkin' with your baby, go home and swing
At the ragime banjo ball

SEQUENCE: (Intro, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides
