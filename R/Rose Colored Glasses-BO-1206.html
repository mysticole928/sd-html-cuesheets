Rose Colored Glasses    (BO 1206)   Cal Lambert

BREAK



Allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and star now
Men back in a right hand star
Shoot that star and on you go, right & left a dopaso
Partner left and corner right hand round
Partner left, make an allemande thar,
the men back in a right hand star, then
Shoot that star, go grand right & left around that ring
Hand over hand, aroiund then dosado your lady
Promenade, go 2 by 2 around
You're looking at the world through rose colored glasses
And everything is rosy now

FIGURE



Head 2 girls to the right you chain, turn 'em left around now
New head ladies chain across that ring
Those heads promenade halfway around you go
Sides pass thru, u-turn back you know
Join 8 hands, make a ring, circle left you do
4 men go up and back, do a full square thru
Count 4 hands around, step up, swing the corner
Promenade, 2 by 2 around
You're looking at the world through rose colored glasses
And everything is rosy now
