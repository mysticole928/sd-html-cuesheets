Rosa Rio
(Lamplight 1001) Jim Diffey
OPENER

Sides face, grand square
My heart is in Rosa Rio, under the Argentine skies
There with the beautiful lady, with the dark and sparkling eyes
Four ladies promenade one time around then you
Swing with your own
Swing with that girl and promenade
There with the beautiful lady
I'll love till the day that I die

FIGURE

The heads (sides) square thru four hands around now
Meet the sides (heads) make a right hand star
Heads (sides) star left and turn it one time around
Same two and do a right & left thru
You pass to the center and square thru 3 now
Your corner swing and promenade
There with the beautiful lady
With the dark and sparkling eyes

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Circle left
My heart is in Rosa Rio, Vo-sen-ti-mente Aye Aye
Allemande your corner, come back with a dosado
The men star left around you go
You turn your partner right and go left allemande
Well swing with your own and promenade
There with the beautiful lady
I'll love till the day that I die
