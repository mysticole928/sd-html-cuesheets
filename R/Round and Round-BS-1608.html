Round And Round
Perry Como 1956
   (Blue Star 1608)    Marshall Flippo
 
FIGURE (4 times through)   

Heads promenade , you travel halfway round
To the center dosado , one time around
1/2 square thru , a right & left thru will do
Two ladies chain and turn those girls around
Make a right hand star , and turn it round the town
Head gents lead and circle 8 hands round
Left allemande and promenade your own
Keep on walking around the ring you roam
Well now those heads you wheel around , cross trail
Left allemande , come back, promenade your gal
Just like a wheel you walk 'em round, round, round
And when you're home you swing that girl around
