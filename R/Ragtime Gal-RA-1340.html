Ragtime Gal    (RA 1340)   Bill Wilson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around that corner girl, come home and swing your girl
Join hands, circle left go round
Allemande left go right & left and star
Gents back in, you've got an allemande thar
Now slip the clutch, skip one girl, left allemande that corner girl
Grand right & left around the ring you go
You promenade her home and tell her she's your own
She'll swing nobody but you

FIGURE



Head ladies chain across you do, turn the girl and slide thru
Square thru 4 hands around you go
Separate around just one you know, star thru
Two ladies chain across from you
Right & left thru, turn this girl, star thru, California twirl
Now swing the corner lady round and round
You promenade her home and tell her she's your own
She'll swing nobody but you
