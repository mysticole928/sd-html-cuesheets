Rocky Top   (Jewel 146 )   Harold Kelley

FIGURE



Four Ladies Chain turn old Rocky Top
Chain the ladies back home for me
The Heads (Sides) promenade travel halfway around
Side (Head) ladies chain across you see
Now star thru and pass thru circle to a line
Walk up to the middle and come back you know
Star thru go right and left thru turn the girl in time
Square thru three quarters round you go
Swing the corner lady around you go
Swing and then you promenade
Good old Rocky Top
Rocky Top Tennessee - Rocky Top Tennessee

BREAK AND CLOSE (NO OPENNER)



Four Ladies promenade go full around old Rocky Top
Back home and swing the partner man
Join sixteen hands circle left around the ring
Allemande left your corner weave the ring
Move in and out around that ring until you meet your own
Dosado one time around for me
The men star left get it full around old Rocky Top
Find the partner turn thru you see
Allemande left the corner lady and Swing with your own
Swing and then you promenade
Good old Rocky Top
Rocky Top Tennessee - Rocky Top Tennessee
