Rumble Seats & Running Boards    (WIN 4972)   Earl "Foxie" Fox

Join hands, circle left go walking round the ring



1 & 3 wheel around, right & left thru, turn your girl
Star thru, pass thru, go left allemande
Bow real low, weave the ring, go in and out I say
Meet a little girl, do a dosado, promenade around that way
Good old days, Model A's, radios, rodeo-doe
Rah rah, rumble seats and running boards, those were the good old days

FIGURE



1 & 3 (2 & 4) square thru 4 hands around you go
Dosado the outside two one time around you know
Swing thru, two by two, boys run, when you're through
Wheel & deal, face that two, do the right & left thru
Dive thru, square thru 3/4 round that ring
Swing that corner lady, promenade around I sing
*Good old days, Model A's, radios, rodeo-doe
Rah rah, rumble seats and running boards, those were the good old days
