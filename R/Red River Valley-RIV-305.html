Red River Valley
Arlo Guthrie 1896
(Riverboat 305) Ken Bower
OPENER

Sides face grand square
From this valley they say you are going
We will miss your bright eyes and sweet smile
For they say you are taking the sunshine
That has brightened our path...
Allemande left and weave the ring
Come and sit by my side if you love me
Do not hasten to bid me adieu
But remember the Red River Valley
And the cowboy who loved you so true

FIGURE

Head (side) couples square thru and get 4 hands around now
When you meet the sides you make a right hand star
Heads come in reverse the flutter wheel around now
Then sweep 1/4 more until you're home
Side (head) pair touch 1/4 , that boy run to the right
Swing your corner lady around and promenade
But remember the Red River Valley
And the cowboy who loved you so true

Sides face grand square

And the grief you are causing...

Come and sit by my side if you love me
Do not hasten to bid me adieu
But remember the Red River Valley
And the cowboy who loved you so true

CLOSER

Sides face grand square

May you never forget those sweet hours
That we spent in the Red River Valley
And the love we exchanged...

Come and sit by my side if you love me
Do not hasten to bid me adieu
