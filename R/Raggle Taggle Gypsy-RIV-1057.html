Raggle Taggle Gypsy
   (Riverboat 1057)    Jack O'Leary , Nelda Eaton
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
*There were three old gypsies came to our door
They came brave and boldly-o
And one sang high and the other sang low
And the other sang a raggle taggle
Allemande left and weave
**It was upstairs downstairs the lady went
Put on her suit of leather-o
And there was a cry from around the door
She's away wi' the raggle taggle gypsy-o

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*It was late that night when the lord came in
Enquiring for his lady-o
And the servant girl - she said to the lord
She's away wi' the raggle taggle
...
**Then saddle for me my milk white steed
My big horse is not speedy-o
And I will ride till I seek my bride
She's away wi' the raggle taggle gypsy-o

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*Now, he rode east, and he rode west
He rode north and south also
Until he came to a wide open place
It was there that he spied
...
**Well, how could you leave your house and your land?
How could you leave your money-o?
How could you leave your only wedded lord
All for a raggle taggle gypsy-o
