Ring Of Fire    (PR 1156)   Chuck Donahue

Four ladies chain across I say



Roll away with a half sashay, circle left
Allemande left that corner and weave the ring
Wind it in and out until you meet
Dosado and promenade
It burns, burns, burns like a ring of fire
Like a ring of fire

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru four hands you go
Dosado one time you go
Swing thru go two by two, boys run right
Couples circulate, do a wheel & deal
Pass thru, trade by, the corner swing
Allemande new corner and promenade the ring
It burns, burns, burns like a ring of fire
Like a ring of fire
