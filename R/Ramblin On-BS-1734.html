Ramblin On    (BS 1734)   Larry Faught

BREAK



Heads square thru round the ring
Go right & left thru, turn your Jane
Dive thru and star thru, two ladies chain too
Turn this new Sue, lead to the right
Left allemande, go right & left grand
Meet your girl now promenade the land
Yes promenade her, and serenade her
There's no one fairer than your Ramblin Rose

FIGURE



Four ladies chain across the ring
And you chain them home again
Head two you star thru, go right & left thru
And then you pass thru, your corner swing
Left allemande, grand eight you run
When your ramblin days are done
Yes promenade her, and serenade her
And then you swing her, this Ramblin Rose
