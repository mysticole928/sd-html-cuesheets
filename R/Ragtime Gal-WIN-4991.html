Ragtime Gal
   (Windsor 4991)    Don Gibson
 
(Figure twice for Heads, Middle Break, Figure twice for Sides)

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

1 & 3 (2 & 4) go up and back and then you flutter wheel
Sweep 1/4 to your left, then a right & left thru I say
Full turn to the outside two, let's make a right hand star
Heads (sides) to the middle with a left hand star it's once around and then
Back to the same two, you pass thru and trade by and then
Pass thru and trade by , right & left thru my friend
Dive thru , square thru 3/4 round and then
Swing that corner lady and promenade her home
Promenade this lady, go walking around
Swing with your ragtime gal

MIDDLE BREAK    Basic - SSD Week 2

Four ladies chain , you turn by the left and then
Join hands circle to the left I say
Ladies in, the men sashay
Circle to the left that way
Ladies in, the men sashay
Circle to the left and then
Allemande left your corner, dosado your own
Allemande left your corner, and weave around the ring
In and out around and when you meet, dosado and promenade her home
Promenade this lady, go walking around and
Swing with your ragtime gal
