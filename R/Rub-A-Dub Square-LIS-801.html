Rub-A-Dub Square    (LIS 801)   Al "Tex" Brownlee

Allemande left like a daisy chain, forward 2 you go



Her by the left and a corner by the right
Her by the left hand round
Roll promenade with the pretty little maid, now you're homeward bound
With rub-a-dub-dub three men in a tub, sailing on the ocean blue
*Roll like a hub singing rub-a-dub-dub as she swings at home with you

FIGURE



Heads square thru 3/4 round, a right left right you do
Separate around just one and do a left square thru
Left and a right, four hands around and then 1/4 more
Swing that corner round and round and you promenade that floor
Promenade with the pretty little maid around that ocean blue
Roll like a hub singing rub-a-dub-dub as she sails along with you

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*When you steal a kiss from a pretty little miss, as she sails along with you
