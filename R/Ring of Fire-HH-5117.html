Ring Of Fire    (HH 5117)   Tom Perry

OPENER



Sides face grand square
Love is a burnin' flame, and it makes a fiery ring
Bound by wild desire I fell in to a ring of fire
Circle
I fell in to a burning ring of fire
Allemande left with the corner, swing and promenade 'em higher
And it burns, burns, burns
The ring of fire, the ring of fire

FIGURE



Heads promenade 3/4 round
2 & 4 do the right & left thru
Pass thru, touch 1/4 then, walk & dodge
Bend the line, go up and back
Star thru, square thru 3/4 round the ring
Swing on the corner girl and then you promenade again
And it burns, burns, burns
The ring of fire, the ring of fire

MIDDLE BREAK



4 little ladies chain I say
Roll away and circle left
Ladies roll away and then
Allemande left with the corner, weave the ring
I fell in to a burning ring of fire
Swing the lady round, then you promenade 'er higher
And it burns, burns, burns
The ring of fire, the ring of fire

CLOSER



Sides face grand square
The taste of love is sweet, when hearts like ours meet
I fell for you like a child, ah! but the fire is wild
Circle
I fell in to a burning ring of fire
Allemande left with the corner, swing and promenade 'em higher
And it burns, burns, burns
The ring of fire, the ring of fire
