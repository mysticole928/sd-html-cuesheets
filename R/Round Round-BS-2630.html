BS 2630

“ROUND, ROUND”

Opener (Circle Left)

Find a wheel and it goes round, round, round

As it spins along with a happy sound

Men Star Right … you go round, round, round

Left Allemande and you Weave that ring

And your love will hold you round, round, round

As your hearts a song with a happy sound

And your head goes spinning round, round, round

Cause you've found what you've been dreaming of


Figure

Heads Promenade go halfway round, round, round

Now Flutter Wheel go once around

Sweep a Quarter and then Pass Thru

Do the Right & Left Thru – Half Sashay

Pass Thru – Trade By – Touch a Quarter then

Boys Run – Slide Thru – Swing and Promenade

And the ball of snow (sun) in the day

Makes them all want to say


Break and Close

Four Ladies Promenade go round, round, round

Get back you Swing you man go once around that town

Join your hands you Circle round, round, round

Left Allemande and Weave that ring

* Find a song and sing it round, round, round

And with ties so strong, your hearts are bound

Old and near, the ones you've found, found, found

For you know that this is really love


* And your love, you'll hold her round, round, round

And your hearts a song with a brand-new sound

And your head goes spinning round, round, round

Cause you've found what you've been dreaming of
