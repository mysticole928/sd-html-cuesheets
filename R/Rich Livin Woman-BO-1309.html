Rich Livin Woman    (BO 1309)   Sleepy Browning

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies promenade go once around that ring
Get home and swing, everybody swing
Join hands and circle to the left go walking around that ring
Allemande left your corner and weave around that ring
I've got a rich livin' woman who loves livin' high
Dosado go round your partner, promenade I'll tell you why
You've gotta make you a fortune or you'll just be a pal
To that sweet caressing, fancy dressing, rich livin' gal

1 & 3 you promenade halfway round that ring
Down the middle and square thru 4 hands around I sing
4 hands around, do the right & left thru and then
Veer to the left and ferris wheel and centers square thru 3
And count me 3 hands around, your corner lady swing
Allemande new corner, promenade go round that ring
You've got to make you a fortune or you'll just be a pal
To that sweet caressing, fancy dressing, rich livin' gal
