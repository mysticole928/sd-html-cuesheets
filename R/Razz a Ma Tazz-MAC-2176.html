Razz-A-Ma-Tazz    (MAC 2176)   Monty Wilson

OPENER - CLOSER



4 men make a right hand star with a razz-a-ma-tazz
allemande left that corner while they play that old jazz
swing the partner round & round 2 times & then
4 men you promenade til you get back home again
home you go with a dosado, full around and now
allemande left that corner come back, promenade somehow
I don't know why it has what it has
a razz-a-razz-a-razz-a-ma tazz

FIGURE



heads/sides right & left thru, come back & pass thru
separate go round 1 make a line of 4 & everybody
star thru & double pass thru, centers in
cast off 3/4 round, go up & back again
star thru & square thru 3 hands around
swing that cornr lady there then promenade the town
I don't know why it has what it has
a razz-a-razz-a-razz-a-ma tazz

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies star by the right with a razz-a-ma-tazz
turn partner by the left hand while they play that old jazz
walk all around that corner girl, seesaw round your own
4 ladies promenade til you get back home
home you go with a dosado, full around and now
allemande left that corner come back promenade somehow
I don't know why it has what it has
a razz-a-razz-a-razz-a-ma tazz
