Riverboat Blues
   (Riverboat 340)    Elmer Sheffield, Jr.
OPENER    B - SSD Week 2

* 4 ladies chain , turn the girl around, boy
Rollaway , then you circle , go around now
Roll her away and you circle the ring
Left allemande , come on and weave that ring
A little jig, a little jive, dancin' holes in your shoes
Meet the little girl, promenade home , you do
Went down to the river, took a little cruise
We danced all night to the Riverboat Blues

*ALTERNATE LINE

4 ladies chain , [listen to that piano!]

FIGURE    Plus, corner progression

Come on the heads (sides) square thru , 4 hands around
You make a right hand star and go around now
The heads (sides) star left , inside, go!
Same two, both hands, single circle , you know
Rear back, star thru and roll
Swing the corner girl around, promenade on home
A little rock, a little roll, dance with who you choose
But dance all night to the Riverboat Blues
