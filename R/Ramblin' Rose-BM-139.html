Ramblin' Rose    (BM 139)   Jeanne Briscoe

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



1 & 3 you promenade halfway and then
Now the side two do a right & left thru
Allemande left, allemande thar, forward two and star
Boys back in you got a right hand star
Slip the clutch, pass one, left allemande
Dosado and promenade
You promenade home with your sweet ramblin' rose
And swing, swing, yes you swing

FIGURE



Heads ladies chain right, turn the girl and then
Send 'em back, dixie style, make an ocean wave
Girls circulate, swing the corner date
Left allemande and weave that ring
Weave it in and out until you meet her
Dosado and promenade
You promenade home with your sweet ramblin' rose
And swing, swing, yes you swing
