Just sing a la la for you and a dee dee for me
You put them both together get a la la dee dee
Sing a la la, dee dee and you add a little do

FIGURE



Now heads/sides go right & left thru once more
Sides/heads half square thru and circle 4
Well, you break & make a line, forward up & back in time
You'll pass thru, then wheel & deal you see, centers zoom
Now square thru 3 hands, you go movin' round the ring
Swing the corner lady & you promenade again
Sing a la la, dee dee and you add a little do
We'll both sing this rotten little song for me and for you

GRAND PARADE DIRECTIONS

Heads step to a wave, turn 1/2 right
Center 2 boys turn 3/4
Side men courtesy turn head lady from center
While side lady promenades 1/4 (alone)
Head men pick up a side lady & all prom 1/2 & face in
Repeat for heads once more, then do it twice with the
Sides going in, ends at home for 64 beats.
