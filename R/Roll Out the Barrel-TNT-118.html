Roll Out The Barrel   (TNT 118 )   Singin' Sam Mitchell

BREAK



Circle to the left
You roll out the barrel, we'll have a barrel of fun
Go allemande left that corner, turn partner by the right
Now men star left go round you run
YOu box the gnat and girl star left go around the ring
Once around and box the gnat I sing, promenade
Now the time to roll out the barrel
Because the gang's all here

FIGURE



One and three go right & left thru and then you roll away
Star thru and do a little dosado
Go right and left thru, turn the girl and square thru 3 hands
YOu box the gnat and pull her by and go left allemande
Come back dosado and promenade
Now the time to roll out the barrel
Because the gang's all here
