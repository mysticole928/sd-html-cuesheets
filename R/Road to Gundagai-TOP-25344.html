SEQUENCE (Intro, figure heads, figure sides, middle break, figure sides, figure heads, closer)

Grand Slide: From a squared set, the side couples face their partner. All dancers will remain facing this direction for the next 32 beats of music (heads face across at opposite, sides face partner). The dancers will move either forward, backward or slide to the side but will not turn as in Grand Square, each dancer moves as a mirror image of the person facing them. First 4 beats: sides walk back, heads walk forward Second 4 beats: sides slide toward opposite, heads slide out away from partner (two slide steps, each taking 2 beats). Third 4 beats: sides walk forward, heads walk back Fourth 4 beats: sides slide out to home, heads slide together to home position The action is now reversed with the sides sliding in while the heads slide apart, etc.
