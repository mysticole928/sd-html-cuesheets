Raggin' A Call    (WIN 4856)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade inside of the ring
Let's box the gnat & give your man a swing
All join hands & circle left you know
An allemande left & weave around you go
Weave in & out around the ring until you meet your maid

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru, but not too far
And with the sides, a right hand star
Heads star left, the center once around you go
Your corner dosado & it's once around you know
Swing thru & then, men trade
Swing that corner lady and you promenade the ring
You gotta dance your lady to a square dance call
Or you might as well not dance 'er at all
