Red Red Robin    (GR 12115)   Singin' Sam Mitchell

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left and star
Men swing in a right hand in, back along but not too far
Shoot it, go forward 2, make a brand new thar and then
Men back in a right hand in, back right along, you're gone again
Shoot it, grand right & left around the ring you go
When you meet your lady fair just promenade her home
Promenade 8, promenade all, sing me a song
As the red red robin goes bob-bob-bobbin' along

FIGURE



1 & 3 you promenade halfway round the square
Down the middle, right & left thru and then
Dive thru, pass thru, and go left allemande
Dosado your partner, go back to the corner, swing
Swing that lady round and round and promenade the ring
Promenade 8, promenade all, sing me a song
As the red red robin goes bob-bob-bobbin' along
