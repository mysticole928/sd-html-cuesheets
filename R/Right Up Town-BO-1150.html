Right Up Town
   (Bogan 1150)    Allie Morvent
 
BREAK    Basic

Walk all around that corner girl and swing your partner
Join up hands , circle left go round the town
Allemande left your corner, then weave around that ring
Go in and out till you meet your girl, swing and swing
And then the men star left you turn it once around
Dosado all the way round, promenade that girl around
Promenade there's no harm, take that girlie off the farm
And you swing her right up town

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

1 & 3 lead to the right you circle round the track
Head gent break make a line , go up and back , star thru
Right & left thru , turn the girl and then you square thru
Count 4 hands is what you do, move on to the next ole two
Right & left thru , turn the girl and then you star thru
Pass thru , swing that corner round and round, promenade
Promenade that pretty pet, she hasn't seen the bright lights yet
And you swing her right up town
