Ragtime Cowboy Joe    (GR 12055)   Earl Johnston

INTRO



1 & 3 promenade you go halfway around
Whirl away with a 1/2 sashay, star thru across the way
Do a right & left thru with the outside 2
Now dive thru, square thru 3/4 round inside the land
Here we go left allemande
Dosado your own, left allemande again
Run back home and promenade
He's a rootin', tootin', high falutin'
Son of a gun from Arizona
Ragtime cowboy Joe

FIGURE



Left allemande, dosado around your own
The men star left, once around the ring you roam
Box the gnat, change hands, now turn her left and then
4 ladies chain straight across I say
Turn 'em round, don't let 'em stay
You chain them right on back, then whirl away
Swing somebody new and promenade
He's a rootin', tootin', high falutin'
Son of a gun from Arizona
Ragtime cowboy Joe
