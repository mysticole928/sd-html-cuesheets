Razz-A-Ma-Tazz
   (MacGregor 2176)    Monty Wilson
 
OPENER - CLOSER    Basic - SSD Week 1

4 men make a right hand star with a razz-a-ma-tazz
Allemande left that corner while they play that old jazz
Swing the partner round and round 2 times and then
4 men you promenade til you get back home again
Home you go with a dosado , full around and now
Allemande left that corner come back, promenade somehow
I don't know why it has what it has
A razz-a-razz-a-razz-a-ma tazz

FIGURE    Mainstream - SSD Week 10, corner progression

Heads (sides) right & left thru , come back and pass thru
Separate go round one make a line of 4 and everybody
Star thru and double pass thru , centers in
Cast off 3/4 round, go up and back again
Star thru and square thru 3 hands around
Swing that corner lady there then promenade the town
I don't know why it has what it has
A razz-a-razz-a-razz-a-ma tazz

MIDDLE BREAK   

4 ladies star by the right with a razz-a-ma-tazz
Turn partner by the left hand while they play that old jazz
Walk all around that corner girl, see saw round your own
4 ladies promenade til you get back home
Home you go with a dosado , full around and now
Allemande left that corner come back promenade somehow
I don't know why it has what it has
A razz-a-razz-a-razz-a-ma tazz
