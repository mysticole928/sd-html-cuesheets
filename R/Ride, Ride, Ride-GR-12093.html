Ride, Ride, Ride   (Grenn 12093)   Johnny Davis

OPENER



Walk all around the corner gal, see saw round your pet
Men star by the right and turn it once around the set
Allemande left the corner, walk on by your own
Right hand round the right hand gal, and a left hand around your own
Four little ladies chain now, go straight across the land
You chain 'em right on back and promenade again
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride

FIGURE 1



First and third you Promenade go half-way round the ring
Down the middle swing the opposite lady round again
Face to the middle pass thru thru, split the outside go round one
Make a right hand star in the middle of the ring it's once around you run
Allemande left the corner, go home and dosado
Back to the corner swing and whirl and promenade you know
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride

FIGURE 2



First and third you lead to the right and circle round the track
Head men break and you make a line go up to the middle and back
Pass thru, wheel and deal, double pass thru
First couple go left, next couple go right, and do a right and left thru
Star thru, and pass thru, corner lady swing
Swing that gal go up and down and promenade and sing
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride

MIDDLE BREAK



Four little ladies chain now, go straight across the ring
You chain 'em right on back and turn your own again
Walk all around the corner, see saw round your own
Men star by the right and turn it once around you roam
Allemande left the corner, come home and dosado
Left allemande the corner, then promenade your own
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride

FIGURE 3



Two and four you Promenade go half-way round the ring
Down the middle, swing the opposite lady round again
Face to the outside two, go right and left thru, turn that gal and then
Dive thru, pass thru, go right and left thru again
Rollaway, pass thru, U-turn back and swing
Swing that gal go up and down and promenade and sing
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride

FIGURE 4



Side two couples right and left thru, turn your gal and then
Pass thru, seperate, go round one and then
In the middle do a dosado go all the way and go-man-go
Box the gnat make a right hand star it's once around you know
Allemande left the corner, go home box the gnat
Pull 'er by and swing the corner, promenade the track
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride

ENDING



Walk all around the corner gal, see saw round your own
Men star by the right and turn it once around you roam
Left allemande the corner, go home and box the gnat
Four little ladies promenade go once around the track
Get back home do a dosado go all the way round and then
Left allemande and come back home, you promenade again
If you don't want me honey, if you're not satisfied
If you don't care, get on your horse and ride, ride, ride
