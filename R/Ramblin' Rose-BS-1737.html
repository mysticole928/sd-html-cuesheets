Ramblin' Rose
Perry Como; Gordon MacRae; George Paxton and His Orchestra; many others 1947
(Blue Star 1737) Al "Tex" Brownlee
FIGURE

Heads a right & left thru , turn 'em you do
1/2 square thru then dosado
Star thru and then, right & left thru again
Star thru , then swing you know
Do a left allemande pass by one , weave the ring
Meet each lady with a smile
Promenade with this girl, stop at home swing and whirl
Swing that clinging vine awhile

Now those four ladies chain 3/4 round that old ring
Those heads lead right you know, circle 4
Those heads make a line , forward eight back in time
Star thru then dosado
Square thru 3/4 , do a left allemande
Dosado the next in line, promenade
From this day hence there'll be a picket fence
Around that rambling Rose of mine
