Rocky Mt. Dew
Grandpa Jones; Glen Campbell; Willie Nelson; many others 1928
   (Lightning S 6001)    Chuck Bryant
 
OPENER    Basic

Well allemande left your corner, box the gnat with your partner
4 ladies star by the left hand go round
You turn your partner right , keep her , promenade tonight
And you feed her on good old mountain dew

FIGURE   

1 & 3 (2 & 4) move up and back , forward up and box the gnat
Pull her by , turn your partner left hand round
Turn your corners all with a right hand round
Back to your partner like an allemande thar
All 4 men going to make a back up star
Shoot that star, go right & left grand
Every other girl in every other hand
Meet a new girl and do a dosado
You promenade her home and keep her for your own
And feed her on good old mountain dew

All 4 men going to make a back up star
Shoot that star and here we go
Right & left dopaso , you're going to keep on doing a dopaso
*My uncle Bill owns a still on the hill
Where he runs off a gallon or two
The buzzards in the sky get so dizzy they can't fly
From smelling that good old mountain dew
Partner left go all the way round to the right hand lady
With a right & left grand , grand right & left go round the world
You promenade her home and keep her for your own
And feed her on good old mountain dew
