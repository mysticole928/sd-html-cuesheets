FIGURES #1 - 10

Gents reach back , pull 'em thru , promenade like sin

Promenade the circle and wear a nasty grin

Promenade your partner, lady lead single file
Swing the girl behind you paw, everybody smile

Resashay go all the way , now promenade your own

Promenade with Rosie, don't you dare slow down
Gents roll back , promenade the corner gal around

All join hands , circle left around the ring
Ladies roll across , give the new girl a swing
Promenade 'ol Rosie, she's everybody's kin

All around your corner , left hand swing your taw
Box the gnat with the corner, promenade the hall
Promenade with Rose, she's a man's best friend

Allemande left your corner, give your own a swing
Swing her round and round, promenade the ring

All join hands , circle left around the land
Swing a girl, any girl , on either hand

Promenade right to her seat, 'cause Rosie's gone again

Note: This dance may be used as a simple square dance or a circle mixer. There are ten figure variations; the caller may choose any one or all as a circle mixer or square dance.
