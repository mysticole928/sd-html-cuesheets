Ragtime Call    (LUCKY 19)   Jon Hed

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade inside of the ring
Get back home and swing with your man
Join all your hands and circle to the left
Left allemande and then weave that ring, go
Weave in and out until you meet with your maid
You dosado and then you promenade
Let's promenade her around that ol' hall
While dancing to a rag time call

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands round you go
Dosado a little corner one time you know
Swing thru, girls circulate, boys trade
Boys run right, and then bend the line
Slide thru and square thru 3/4 round
Swing that corner and you promenade her
Promenade her around that ol' hall
While dancing to a rag time call
