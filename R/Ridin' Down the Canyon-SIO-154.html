Ridin' Down The Canyon   (Sets In Order 154 )   Johnny Le Clair

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



Those heads (sides) promenade go 1/2 around the ring
Come down the middle, square thru 3/4 round
You separate around one, the line go forward up
Come back, pass thru, the ends cross fold
Allemande left old corner, then a grand old right & left
Hand over hand around, then promenade the set
Oh, promenade your lady
Go dancin' down that trail
To watch that evenin' sun go down

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across and couples 1 & 3 (2 & 4)
Rollaway, star thru to an 8 chain 4
Heel and toe and out you go, heel and toe and come on in
Heel and toe and out you go, heel and toe, star thru
Pass thru and bend the line
And then you circle up 8
Allemande left new corner, come back and promenade
Ah! You promenade this lady
Go dancin' down that trail
To watch that evenin' sun go down
