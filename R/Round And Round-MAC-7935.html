Round And Round
Perry Como 1956
(MacGregor 7935 side A )
FIGURE

Swing your girl, you swing her round and round
To an allemande left with a happy bound
Grand right & left , you move around the town
Promenade that gal and don't slow down
Girls turn back , go twice around the track
When you meet your partner , box the gnat
Corner left, you turn her twice around
Partner right, box the gnat , girls star left , gents stand pat
Once around , same girl you box the gnat
Gents star left around, the girls stand pat
Pass your girl, the next you star promenade
Take her home and swing around this maid
