Opener

Sides Face Grand Square I've been walkin' these streets so long Singing the same old song. I know every crack in these dirty sidewalks of Broadway

Allemande Left and weave Where hustle's the name of game

Swing and promenade (and nice guys get washed away) like the snow and rain

 

Figure (twice for heads, twice for sides)

Heads (side) Square through count four, with the corner Dosido.  Swing through, spin the top with a right and left through now.  Square through to three swing the corner and promenade to home and offers coming over the phone

 

Middle break

Sides Face Grand Square There's a load of compromising On the road to my horizon But I'm going to be where the lights are shinning on me

Allemande left and weave Like a rhinestone cowboy

Swing and promenade with (getting cards and letters from) people I don't even know and offers coming over the phone


Closer

Sides Face Grand Square Like a rhinestone cowboy Riding out on a horse in a star-spangled rodeo Like a rhinestone cowboy allemande left and weave
Getting cards and letters from (Swing and promenade with) people I don't even know and offers coming over the phone
