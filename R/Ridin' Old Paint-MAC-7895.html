Ridin' Old Paint / Chantez-Chantez
   (MacGregor 7895 side A )   
 
BREAK

Go to the left with a left allemande
Right to your partner, right & left grand
Hand over hand, around the ring you go
Promenade eight, promenade all
Take a little walk, go around the hall
Ridin' Old Pait, ridin' Old Paint
And leading Old Bald

FIGURE

Walk all around your left hand lady
See saw round your pretty little baby
Join your hands, let's circle round the floor
The first old couple rip and snort
Go down the center, tie 'em up short
Break at the foot, form two lines of four
Forward eight, come back with you
Bend your line, square thru
Do a right, a left, a right, a left
Face your corners, promenade
You've got yourselves a brand new maid
Ridin' Old Paint, ridin' Old Paint
And leading Old Bald

(Repeat the figure three more times with a break between each figure.)

Note: The square thru is a brand new movement originated by Bill Hansen of Los Angeles, CA and can be executed in many various patterns.
