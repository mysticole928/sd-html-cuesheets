Join hands and circle with a lassie by your side
Walk all round your corner, see saw round your taw
Men star by the right on once around tonight
Allemande left with your left hand and weave around and sing
Just a roamin' in the gloamin' with a lassie by your side

FIGURE



The head 2 couples promenade halfway round you go
Come down the middle a right & left thru and turn the girl you know
Square thru 4 hands around the ring you do
Right & left thru with the outside 2, and then dive thru
Square thru 3/4 to the corner, highland swing
It's a highland swing, swing and whirl and promenade the ring
When the sun has gone to rest
That's the place that I like best
It's lovely just roamin' in the gloamin'
