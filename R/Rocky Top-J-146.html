Rocky Top

FIGURE

4 ladies chain turn old Rocky Top
Chain the ladies back home for me
The heads promenade travel halfway round
Side ladies chain across you see
Now star thru and pass thru , circle to a line
Walk up to the middle and come back you know
Star thru go right & left thru , turn the girl in time
Square thru 3/4 round you go
Swing the corner lady, do a left allemande
Promenade her home
Good old Rocky Top

All 4 ladies promenade go full around ole Rocky Top
Back home and swing the partner man

Allemande left your corner, weave the ring
Move in and out around that ring until you meet your own
Dosado one time around for me
The men star left go full around old Rocky Top
Find the partner turn thru you see
Allemande left the corner lady and swing with your own
Swing and then you promenade
