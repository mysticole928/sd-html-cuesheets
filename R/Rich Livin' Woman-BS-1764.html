Rich Livin' Woman
(Blue Star 1764) Marshall Flippo
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Join up hands , circle to the left , go walking round the ring
Gents star left go once around and then
Dosado the partner, do a left allemande
Bow real low to the partner then weave round the land
I've got a rich livin' woman who loves living high
Dosado round that partner, promenade , I'll tell you why
You've gotta make you a fortune or you'll just be a pal
To that sweet carressing, fancy dressing, rich livin' gal

FIGURE

Head two couples right & left thru and turn the girl and then
* 1/2 square thru , then face the sides & circle 1/2 again
* Dive thru & square thru 3/4 round you go
* Left allemande the corner then weave the ring you know
I've got a rich livin' woman who loves living high
Dosado round that partner, swing that corner on the sly
You've gotta promenade this lady or you'll just be a pal
To that sweet carressing, fancy dressing, rich livin' gal

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

* 1/2 square thru then face the sides swing thru once again
*Now boys run around just one & then you wheel & deal
* Left allemande the corner, go weaving round you reel

ENDING LYRICS

To that sugar speaking, treasure seeking
Turtle dovin', money lovin'
Tantalizing, enterprising, rich living gal
Oh what a gal
