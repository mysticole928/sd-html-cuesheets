Rocky Mountain Fling
   (Wagon Wheel 101)    Don Franklin
 
FIGURE (4 times through)    C4, R-H lady progression

Allemande left, allemande thar , go right & left and star
The men back up in a right hand star
Shoot the star, go all the way around , corner all eight chain
Go right & left, turn the girl, and then 4 ladies chain
You turn her left, roll promenade , keep walking round you go
Now 1 & 3 wheel around , right & left thru you know
You turn the girl and cross trail , find the corner beau
Allemande left , double grand , *while I do a little yo-do

YODEL OR LYRICS

A little girl who gets a kiss and runs and tells her mother
Does a very foolish thing and seldom gets another
A little boy who gets a kiss and runs and tells his brother
Does a very useful thing and brother gets another

Young girls are nice, here's some advice, choose one a little older
When they're afraid of being old maids, they get a little bolder
Get in the swing and have a fling, before it is too late
If you delay, soon they'll say, she's someone else's mate

When a boy meets a girl, she's a sweet young miss
He works to get his courage up and ask her for a kiss
And if it's nice, he tries it twice, she thinks it necessary
To let him know (but not too bold!), she's old enough to marry!

Way up on a mountain, beneath a giant tree
A darling young maiden is waiting for me
She promised me we'd marry, she'll be my loving wife
I'll do the Rocky Mountain Fling, the rest of my life

There is another Wagon Wheel #101 called Bo Sa Nova Patter No. 1 / Bo Sa Nova Patter No. 2 . A double grand is done by meeting your partner in a right & left grand, but pass by and go around again.
