Rally Round    (TOP 25220)   Ed Fraidenburg

BREAK



Allemande left in the alamo style, balance out and in
Swing thru go forward 2 and balance once again
Swing thru go right & left, turn thru and then
Allemande left that corner, weave the ring again
Rally round your love, boys, rally round your love
Dosado and promenade beneath the stars above
Keep the home fires burnin', give her plenty love
Swing the lady round & round and rally round your love

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right and circle round the track



Right & left thru, turn the girl, star thru and then
Right & left thru, turn again, square thru 3 hands
Get to the corner, allemande and you walk right by your own
Swing the right hand lady there and promenade her home
Walk that narrow line, boys, when you're on the town
Swing and whirl that lady fair, swing and rally round

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Head couples promenade go halfway round the square
Down the middle, right & left thru and turn that lady fair
Star thru, pass thru and do a dosado, all the way round and
Square the barge 3/4 round you go
Swing that corner and you go left allemande
Come back 1 and promenade go walkin' round the land
Walk that narrow line, boys, when you're on the town
Swing and whirl that lady fair, swing and rally round
