OPENER    Basic

Bow and swing your partner, boys, then make a great big ring
You circle to the left now and go round that big old ring
Allemande left your corner girl and dosado your own
Allemande left just once again, grand right and left you roam
Go right and left around that ring, you meet and dosado
Then promenade your lady home, walk on your heel and toe
