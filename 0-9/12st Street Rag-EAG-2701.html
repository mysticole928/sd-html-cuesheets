12st Street Rag    (EAG 2701)  Chuck Peel

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain around the ring
Join hands and circle left, go walking round I sing
Four ladies roll away, now circle left you go
Allemande left that corner and weave
Weave in and out around until you meet your own
Dosado and promenade around the ring
Promenade her down the line go walking, moving around in time
Dancing to the 12th Street rag

FIGURE



Heads (sides) promenade halfway around I sing
Down the middle, right & left thru, turn that girl you know
Pass the ocean, extend and linear cycle when you're through
Square thru four hands around the ring, trade by and now
Swing the corner girl and promenade around the ring
Promenade her down the line, go walking moving round in time
Dancing to the 12th Street rag
