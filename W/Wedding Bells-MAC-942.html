Wedding Bells    (MAC 942)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Dosado round your corner maid, see saw round your taw
Join your hands and circle left, go round that square dance hall
The girls you whirl a 1/2 sashay, star right inside the ring
Go back and turn your partner left, your corner right hand swing
Your partner left, an allemande thar, the boys back up and then
Back it, now then, slip the clutch, your corner allemande
You promenade your lady, just dance along in time
Those wedding bells keep breaking up that ol' gang of mine

FIGURE



Heads go forward up and back and then a 1/2 square thru
Do an eight chain 6 across the set, count 6 hands, you do
Across the set, go 2 by 2, until you all get straight
Then star thru, pass thru, bend the line and then
Go forward up and back, square thru 3/4, then
Swing that corner beside you there and promenade the square
Promenade your lady, keep her off your mind
Those wedding bells keep breaking up that ol' gang of mine
