Wake Me Up Early In The Morning    (BS 1936)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Now 4 little ladies chain 3/4 round that ring
Then join up hands, circle left you know
You roll away then circle to the left go round that ring
Left allemande go weaving round you know
Wake me up early in the morning
Dosado and then you'll promenade
Wake me up early in the morning
So I can dream of you each hour of the day

FIGURE



Now 1 & 3 you pass thru and walk around just one
You'll make a line, go forward up and back
Star thru, a double pass thru, then cloverleaf
The inside two you do the right & left thru
Now pass thru, then eight chain 4, go straight across that ring
Meet the next little gal and then you swing, promenade
Oh, wake me up early in the morning
So I can dream of you each hour of the day
