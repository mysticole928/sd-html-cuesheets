Wish Me A Rainbow    (BS 1808)   Andy Andrus

BREAK



4 ladies chain now and then you roll away
Join hands and circle left around the ring that way
Allemande left that corner and do an allemande thar
Go forward 2, the men back up and make a right hand star
Slip the clutch, left allemande, a grand right & left
And when you meet that lady, promenade
All my tomorrow depend on your love
So wish me a rainbow above

FIGURE



Head 2 go forward, come back then square thru 4
4 hands around there and do a right & left thru
Turn the girl and star thru, pass thru, wheel & deal
A double pass thru, the first go left, the next go right
Slide thru, left allemande, then dosado your maid
Swing the corner lady, promenade
Wish me a rainbow and wish me a star
All this you can give me whereever you are

ALTERNATE LYRICS



Sweet dreams for my pillow, that only you can give
So wish me a rainbow as long as I live
