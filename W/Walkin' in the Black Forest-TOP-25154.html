Walkin' In The Black Forest    (TOP 25154)   Chip Hendrickson

BREAK



4 ladies chain, chain the ladies cross the set
Turn the girls and chain 'em straight on back
Take your own and promenade 2 by 2 and hand in hand
Go walking, talking round the land
It's right back home, sides face, grand slide
Walk 2, 3, 4; Slide --, Slide --
Walk 2, 3, 4; Slide --, Slide back
Slide --, Slide --; walk 2, 3, 4
Slide --, Slide --; walk 2, heads square thru

FIGURE



Heads square thru 3/4 round
Then take your partner promenade, you go halfway
Side couples square thru 3/4 round
Then leave your partner, separate go halfway
All join hands, circle left
Swing your corner lady round and round
And then you better promenade 2 by 2
You walk this maid and sing a little serenade back home
And when you're there (heads square thru or 4 ladies chain)

ENDING



Bow to your partner and your corner girl

SEQUENCE: (Break, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, break, ending)



CALLER'S NOTE: (This dance is arranged so the dancers are able to start each movement on the first count of the musical phrases. All the movements take 8 steps with the exception of the promenade (16 steps) and the grand slide (32 steps). If you would like to cue the whole dance and let the dancers enjoy a maximum of music, use the following calls:)

BREAK



(Give first command at the end of the musical introduction [i.e., wait 6 counts])
4 ladies chain (wait 6)
Chain 'em back (wait 6)
Promenade the track (wait 12)
Sides face, grand slide (wait 14)
Slide back (wait 12

FIGURE



Heads square thru 3/4 round (wait 4)
Then promenade, go halfway round (wait 4)
Sides square thru 3/4 round (wait 4)
Separate, go halfway round (wait 4)
All join hands, circle left (wait 6)
Swing your corner (wait 6)
Now promenade (wait 12 if calling heads or sides next, wait 14 if calling 4 ladies chain next)
