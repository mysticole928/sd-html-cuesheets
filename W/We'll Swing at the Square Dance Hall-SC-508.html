We'll Swing At The Square Dance Hall
   (Scope 508)    Johnny LeClair
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Allemande the corner lady, dosado your own
Join up hands circle left , go around the land
Allemande left , allemande thar , forward 2 now make a star
Now the men back down the line
Shoot the star full around , pull the corner lady by
Left allemande , come back and promenade
You promenade that lady, go walkin' around you all
We'll meet at the square dance hall

FIGURE    Mainstream, no progression

Heads square thru 4 hands around will do,
Now turn thru that outside two
Left turn thru in the middle you do,
Now cloverleaf all 8 of you
Center 4 turn thru , one more time, allemande
Now dosado your own, to the corner lady swing
Swing and then promenade ,
You promenade that lady, go walkin' around you all
We'll meet at the square dance hall
