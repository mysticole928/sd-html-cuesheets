We'll Square Dance Again Tonight    (TOP 25339)   Dana Blood

BREAK



Those 4 ladies chain, go straight across the ring
Join hands and circle left, go walking round I sing
Ladies in, men sashay, circle left that way
Ladies in, men sashay, circle left I say
Allemande left that corner and weave around the ring
All the way around till you meet your own and promenade
You promenade, go walking with that maid
We'll square dance again tonight

FIGURE



1 & 3 promenade, go 1/2 around that ring
Down the middle and square thru 4 hands around I sing
Go all the way around and then, swing thru my friend
Boys run to the right, 1/2 tag, trade & roll
Face that girl, pass thru, trade by and swing
Swing that girl around and promenade that ring
You promenade, go walking with this maid
We'll square dance again tonight

TAG



Swing her boys
