Wall To Wall Love    (MAC 974)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - BREAK



Walk all around your corner, turn a left hand round your maid
Ladies star by the right inside, the men you promenade
Second time you meet her, catch her left and then
An allemande thar, a right hand star, the men back up again
Slip the clutch, left allemande, grand right & left you go
When you meet your lady, then you promeno
*Then for something extra, I added to the call
I added wall to wall love

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain across, then roll a 1/2 sashay
Circle 8 around that way, circle left I say
4 little ladies pass thru, both turn left and then
First around 2, next around 1, circle left again
Allemande the corner, your own a dosado
Swing the corner lady, and then you promeno
*The rug was torn and tattered from kitchen to the hall
So I added wall to wall love
