Four little ladies chain, turn a girl and then
Join hands and circle round the ring
Roll away a half sashay, circle left I say
Now roll away a half sashay, circle left that way
Allemande left a corner, weave that ol' ring
Weave in and out then promenade, when you meet again
I'm sittin' in a tavern playin' the juke box
And waitin' for my baby to show

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Head 2 ladies chain, sides promenade 3/4 round for me
2 & 4 do a right & left thru, turn a girl
Sides squeeze in and go 8 to the middle, come right back
Slide thru you know, centers right & left thru, turn and then
Square thru 3 hands, swing the corner lady, promenade the land
I'm sittin' in a tavern playin' the juke box
And waitin' for my baby to show
