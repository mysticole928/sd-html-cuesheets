Whispering   (Balance 403 )   Ed Gilmore
OPEN - BREAK - CLOSE

Allemande left on the corner, weave by your own,
A left hand round the right hand girl, a right hand round your own,
Four ladies promenade around the ring you go, come on home swing and whirl
Around and around with the pretty little girl.
Four men promenade inside the ring, come home swing your partner, everybody swing,
Promenade this lady, you dance 'em home for me,
Whispering I love to dance with you

FIGURE



Now one and three (2 & 4 ) promenade half way round the floor,
Same two ladies chain across and turn one quarther more, move to the sides (heads)
Girls hook elbows turn the line for me, exactly once around you do,
Wheel and deal, face those tow, dive thru pass thru swing thru and then,
Men you trade swing that corner round and then, you promenade her, dance her
Home for me
Whispering I love to dance with you
