Who's World   (Blue Star 1832 )   Al Brownlee
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING

Well the 4 little ladies chain across you go
Now chain back home, do a dopaso
Corner right and partner left around
Now boys star by the right go round the town
Well pass your own and go left allemande
Dosado, then you promenade that land
You leave...and then come home again
Well who's world am I a-living in?

FIGURE



Those heads a right & left thru and turn you know
Then slide thru and do the dosado
You'll swing thru, the boys will trade and then
Well turn thru and go left allemande
Well dosado, that corner lady swing
You swing then promenade that ring
You leave...and then come home again
Well who's world am I a-living in?
