Walkin' On New Grass   (Chinook 018 )   Daryl Clendenin
OPEN - BREAK - CLOSE

Four ladies chain across that ring you know,
join up hands and circle left you go,
you'll do and allemande left, go forward two,
go right and left, turn backward one,
right hand around the corner, go foward two,
go left and right, turn back one,
allemande left come back and promenade.
'Cause I'll be walkin' on new grass, singin' a new song.
The next town down the road keeps callin' me.

FIGURE



The heads(sides) promenade half way you go,
now lead to the right and do a dosado.
Swing thru and then boys run right,
you bend the line & do the right and left thru,
Why don't you star thru, slide thru, star thru, pass thru,
swing the corner girl and promenade.
'Cause I'll be walkin' on new grass, singin' a new song.
The next town down the road keeeps callin' me.
