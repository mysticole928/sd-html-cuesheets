OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK

FIGURE

With the sides (heads) do a right & left thru, then you dive on thru
**And do a California twirl, swing with your corner girl
Then you promenade the square, just like you're walking on air
Out under a western moon

CLOSER

A grand ole right and left, 'til your own little ole doll you see
