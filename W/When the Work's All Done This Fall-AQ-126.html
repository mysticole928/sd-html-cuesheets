When The Work's All Done This Fall
   (Aqua 126)    Vernon Hutton
 
OPENER    Basic - SSD Week 1

Allemande left your corner girl, your own you dosado
Gents star right across the set to the opposite girl you go
Swing her once or twice boys, she's small and you're so tall
Star back home and swing your own
'Til the work's all done this fall

FIGURE (A)    Basic - SSD Week 11, corner progression

First and third you promenade about half-way round the town
Right and left thru right down the middle and turn those girls around
Then allemande left your corner girl and allemande right your own
Box the flea with the corner girl and promenade her home .
(yodel)

FIGURE (B)    Basic - SSD Week 11, corner progression

Four little ladies promenade go inside of the ring
Swing your own when you get back home,
You swing and swing and swing
Then allemande left your corner girl, and allemande right your own
Box the flea with the corner girl and promenade her home .
(yodel)

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic - SSD Week 1

Allemande left your corner girl, your own you dosado
Gents star right across the set to the opposite girl you go
Swing her once or twice boys, she's small and you're so tall
Star back home and swing your own , then promenade you all.
(yodel)

SEQUENCE

Opener, Fig. A and B for Heads, Middle Break, Fig. A and B for Sides, Closer

Note: If caller does not care to yodel, just let music play or add own patter.
