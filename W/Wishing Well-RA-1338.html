Wishing Well    (RA 1338)   Bill Wilson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around that corner, see saw round your partner
4 little ladies promenade, go once around that ring
Home you go with a dosado, with the corner star thru
Join hands, circle to the left around the ring will do
Whirlaway, right & left grand until you meet your maid
Dosado around your partner, and then you promenade
Promenade go full around until you get back home and then
Swing that sweet gal down by the wishing well
