Wild Colonial Boy
(Top 25337) Wally Cook
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Allemande left your corner, forward 2 you go
Like an allemande thar , boys swing in
Star right you know, shoot that star , alamo style
Balance for a while
Swing thru 2 by 2, boys run right
Wheel & deal , left allemande
Dosado your own
Left allemande , come back home and promenade the land
Winter's over, spring is here
Time to shout for joy
Go back home and swing with that wild colonial boy

FIGURE

Head couples square thru 4 hands round the square
Meet the sides, right & left thru , turn your lady there
Dive thru and (centers) touch , then recall *
Everybody on the move, travelling round the world
Centers pass thru and swing your corner round
Left allemande new corner, come back, promenade the town
Time to jump for joy
Go back home and swing with that wild colonial boy

(*Recall: from an ocean wave between facing couples, wave dancers do a
long recycle, new centers move forward, touch and recycle. About 8 counts.)

Opener-MiddleBreak-Closer: Non-standard use of Wheel & Deal. The desired choreography is "with your (original) partner, Reverse Wheel Around.
