When My Baby Swings With Me
Ted Lewis; Benny Goodman and His Orchestra; Billy Murray and Gladys Rice; others 1920
(Rockin' A 1316) Bruce Welsh
BREAK

4 ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Join your hands and circle left around that ring
Left allemande and do an allemande thar
Go right and left, boys back in a right-hand star
Now shoot that star full around and slip the clutch
Left allemande , dosado your girl and promenade that land
Don't cry, don't sigh, you'll get a glimpse of heaven
When your baby swings with you

FIGURE

Head ladies chain across and turn 'em full around
Head couples promenade 3/4 round that town
Now 2 & 4 star thru then square thru 4 hands
Around and you swing that corner Sue
Why don't the men star left go once around
Dosado that pretty Jane and promenade that ring
Don't cry, don't sigh, you'll get a glimpse of heaven
When your baby swings with you
