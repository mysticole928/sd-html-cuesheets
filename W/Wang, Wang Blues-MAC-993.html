Wang, Wang Blues    (MAC 993)   Bob Van Antwerp

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande the corner girl, your partner box the gnat
Girls star left inside the set, it's once around like that
Dosado when you get home, once around and don't be slow
Then allemande your corner girl and a grand ol' right & left
Move it round the circle, and then you dosado
Allemande the corner, come on back and promeno
Promenade around the set, dancing with a rhythm step
To the Wang, Wang Blues

FIGURE



Heads go right & circle 4, make a line right there
Forward up and back with you and then, pass thru
Wheel & deal and 1/4 more, step forward out and back once more
Wheel & deal and 1/4 more, step forward out and back
Bend the line, cross trail, swing the corner maid
Allemande new corner girl, come back and promenade
Promenade just halfway round, swing the gal from way up town
To the Wang, Wang Blues

SEQUENCE: (Intro, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, closer)
