Waltz Quadrille / Silver Bells
   (Old Timer 8008-78)   
Now you waltz down the center and there you divide
(First couple waltzes, ballroom position, across the set and between the opposite couple)
The lady go right and the gent the other side
(Lady turns right around the outside of the square, back to home position, and the gent does the same going left around the square)
You honor your partner, and then make a trade
(As you meet at home position, bow, and all gents turn and take ballroom dance position with their corner lady [left hand lady].)
And you waltz with your corner to the Waltz Promenade
(All couples waltz ballroom position around the square. Each gent has new partner.)
(Repeat for couples #2, #2, and #4. Then you have your original partner back again.)

Side A (Waltz Quadrille) the music is Peakaboo Waltz. Side B (Silver Bells) is a round dance.
