Wabash Cannon Ball
The Carter Family; Roy Acuff; many others 1882
   (Crown 196)    Bob Worley
OPENER    B - SSD Week 1

Circle left
From the great Atlantic ocean to the wide Pacific shore
Men star by the right hand and rolling round the floor
Allemande left your corner girl, turn the partner by the right
Allemande left your corner, weave around the ring
Listen to the jingle, the rumble and the roar
Swing your lady round and promenade the floor
She's mighty tall and handsome
And she's known well by all
She's the combination called the Wabash Cannon Ball

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) promenade halfway around you go
Come down the middle , pass thru , partner trade you know
Square thru 4 hands, go moving around the ring
Right & left thru with the outside and turn that pretty Jane
Star thru , slide thru , corner lady swing
Swing and whirl the corner girl and promenade the ring
She's mighty tall and handsome
And she's known well by all
She's the combination called the Wabash Cannon Ball

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

4 ladies promenade , go one time around
Back home and swing , swing your man around
Join hands circle to the left , around the ring
Allemande left your corner girl and weave around the ring
Oh listen to the jingle, the rumble and the roar
Swing your lady around and, promenade the floor
She's mighty tall and handsome
And she's known well by all
She's the combination called the Wabash Cannon Ball
