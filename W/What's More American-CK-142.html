What's More American    (CK 142)   Daryl Clendenin

OPENER



Sides face, grand square
What's more American than corn flakes
The Fourth of July, or Uncle Sam?
What's more American than baseball?
I am! I am!
4 ladies chain, chain 'em back
Then promenade that land
What's more American than corn flakes?
I am! I am! I am!

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru 4 hands you do
Meet the sides (heads) and do the right & left thru, veer left
Couples circulate, chain down the line, turn the girl, go, wup, 2, 3, 4
Star thru, pass thru, and trade by
Swing the corner and promenade that land
What's more American than popcorn?
I am! I am! I am!

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Sides face, grand square
Hup, 2, 3, 4, hup, 2, 3, 4
Hup, 2, 3, 4, hup, 2, 3, reverse
Hup, 2, 3, 4, hup, 2, 3, 4
Hup, 2, 3, 4
4 ladies chain, go
Chain 'em back and promenade that land
What's more American than hot dogs?
I am! I am! I am!

ALTERNATE MATERIAL
