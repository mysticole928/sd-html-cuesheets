Wedding Bells   (TNT 164 )   Al Brundage
INTRO

All four ladies promenade, once around the ring
Gonna get back home and swing your own, everybody swing
Allemande left your corner, dosado your own
Men star left inside the ring, once around you roam
Come back home, turn thru, your corners allemande
Meet your own and promenade, once around the land
Gee, I get a lonesome feeling when I hear the church bells chime
Those wedding bells are breaking up that old gang of mine

FIGURE



Head two couples promenade halfway round the floor
Come in the middle and square thru, it's full around go 4
Get to the outside pair, dosado one time and then
Make an ocean wave, girls trade, recycle once and then
Pass thru, trade by, then your corner swing
Promenade this new little maid, it's once around the ring
Gee, I get a lonesome feeling when I hear the church bells chime
Those wedding bells are breaking up that old gang of mine
