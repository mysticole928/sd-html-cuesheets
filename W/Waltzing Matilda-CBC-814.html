Waltzing Matilda
John Collinson and Russell Callow; Slim Dusty 1895
   (C Bar C 814)    David Cox
 
OPENER    Basic - SSD Week 1

All 4 ladies promenade , once around that billabong
Come back home and swing , you swing him with glee
Join hands , circle to the left around that billabong
Allemande left your corner girl and weave that ring
Waltzing Matilda, waltzing Matilda
Swing and promenade 'neath the coolabah tree
He sang as he watched and waited 'till his Billy boiled
You'll come a waltzing Matilda with me

FIGURE    Plus, corner progression

Head two couples promenade halfway round that billabong
Walk right in, square thru 4 hands you see
Touch 1/4 , then follow your neighbor round the ring
Hang on, trade the wave , all 8 circulate
Explode and square thru 3/4 round now
Swing and promenade 'neath the coolabah tree
He sang as he watched and waited 'till his Billy boiled
You'll come a waltzing Matilda with me
