When The Roses Bloom Again    (OT 8191)   Joe Boykin

BREAK



Corner dosado, turn partner left you know
Men star right, turn the girl straight across
Full around to left hand lady, turn her right around
Partner left hang on tight, the allemande thar
Shoot the star, skip one girl, then go right & left grand
When you meet again, promenade, promenade here and there
I'll see you round the square
Or maybe when the roses bloom again

FIGURE



Sides go right & left thru, head gents new corner star thru across
Circle 4 with the outside 2, one time around
Ladies break, make that line
Forward 8 and back, pass thru, wheel & deal
Double pass thru, centers in and then
Cast off 3/4 and swing the corner girl
Promenade 'em here and there, I'll see you round the square
Or maybe when the roses bloom again
