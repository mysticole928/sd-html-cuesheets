Wheel And Deal Workshop
(Grenn 12027 side B ) Ron Schneider
TEACHING

Heads 2 couples swing and sway
Side ladies chain across the way
Heads go right and circle four
Head gent break and line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Inside two square thru 3/4, don't stand
Corners all left allemande

1st & 3rd right & left thru, rollaway with a half sashay
Pass thru while you're that way, turn left single file
Lady round two, gent round one, line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Dixie chain on a double track
Lady go left, gent go right, left allemande

FAST DEAL

Heads to the right and circle four, head gent break and line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Double pass thru, keep in time, face partner, bend the line
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Double pass thru, keep in time, face partner, bend the line
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Double pass thru, keep in time, face partner, bend the line
Allemande left

WHEEL & PIVOT

Head two couples right & left thru
New #1 stand back to back, with your corner box the gnat
New heads crosstrail thru, separate, go around two
Four in line go up and back, pass thru, wheel & deal
Center two square thru 3/4, with the outside, left square thru 3/4 around
All turn back, eight chain thru (patter)
When you're through, face partner and circle up 8
Reverse back single file, gents pivot, dixie style
Right, left, right, left allemande

PROMENADE BREAK FOR W & D

Promenade, don't slow down, head 2 couples wheel around
Tow lines of four, go forward and back, wheel & deal two by two
First couple right, next one left, crosstrail thru to a left allemande

Music is Merle's Gallop Grenn 12028
