World Of Our Own    (ER 1044)   Bob Baier

OPENER



Circle left
Close the door, light the lights, we're staying home tonight
Far away from the bustle and the bright city lights
Walk around your corner, see saw your own
Men star right around that ring
Find the corner girl, do an allemande left and then
Swing your own and promenade again
And I know you will find there'll be peace of mind
When we live in a world of our own

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around
Down the middle, pass the ocean, extend
Hinge, split circulate, cast off 3/4
Boys meet, boys trade, boys run right, bend the line
Right & left thru, turn the girl and then
Slide thru, swing that corner girl, promenade
And I know you will find there'll be peace of mind
When we live in a world of our own

MIDDLE BREAK



Four ladies chain, go cross the ring
You roll away, circle to the left, go round I sing
Boys center, girls sashay, circle to the left
Allemande left and weave the ring
We'll build a world of our own, that no one else can share
Swing that girl and promenade the square
And I know you will find there'll be peace of mind
When we live in a world of our own

CLOSER



Four boys promenade one time around
Back home and swing the lady, round and around and around
You join hands, circle to the left, go movin' around the ring
Allemande left and weave the ring
We'll build a world of our own, that no one else can share
All our troubles we'll leave far behind us there
And I know you will find there'll be peace of mind
When we live in a world of our own
