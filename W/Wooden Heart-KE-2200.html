Wooden Heart   (Keeno 2200 )   Harold Bausch
SEQUENCE

Figure twice, break, figure twice

FIGURE



Allemande your corner, Joe, come back home a dosado
Promenade and sing Oh, I don't have a wooden heart
Back right out, make a ring, circle left around that ring
Corner I would swing, cause I don't have a wooden heart
(Promenade)
Promenade the town, you don't slow down
Heads (sides) wheel around and do a right and left thru
Star thru and you swing, promenade, you know you should
I'm not made of wood and I don't have a wooden heart

BREAK



Allemande left and allemande thar, right and left, you form a star
Boys back in and sing Oh, I don't have a wooden heart
Shoot that star, heavens whirl, right and left the second girl
Boys back in again, I was yours from the very start
Shoot that star, grand right and left you go
When you meet your lady, do a dosado
Promenade, treat her good, swing her like you really should
She's not made of wood and she don't have a wooden heart
