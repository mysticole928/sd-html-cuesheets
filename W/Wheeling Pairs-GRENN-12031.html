Wheeling Pairs
(Grenn 12031 side B ) Johnny Davis
INTRO

Allemande left, allemande that, right & left and form a star
Men back up a right hand star
Shoot that star, you're on your way, right & left half sashay
(Face the center of the set)
Lady on left, left allemande (original corner)

1. WHEELING PAIRS

Heads to the right and circle four, head gent break and line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Double pass thru, centers in, cast off 3/4 around
Pass thru, wheel & deal, double pass thru, centers in, cast off 3/4 around
Pass thru, wheel & deal, double pass thru, centers in, cast off 3/4 around
Allemande left

2. BACKTRACK DEAL

Sides to the right and circle four, side gent break and line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Backtrack, separate walk past two, line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel & deal
Backtrack, separate walk past two, line up four
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru, wheel *& deal
Backtrack, separate walk past two, line up four, allemande left
