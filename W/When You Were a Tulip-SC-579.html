When You Wore A Tulip   (Scope 579 )   Ted Wegener
OPEN - BREAK - CLOSE

Allemande left your corner, turn you partner right,
Men star left around you go.
Swing your partner once around, four ladies promenade,
Inside around the big old ring you go (turn thru)
Allemande left your corner, grand old right and left,
Grand old right and left around you go (promenade)
Lips sweeter than julip, oh, she wore a tulip,
and you wore a big red rose

FIGURE



Heads square on thru, four hands around yo go,
Corner lady curlique you know (walk and dodge)
Partner trade, and the two ladies chain my firned,
Turn the girls, it's up and back you go (slide thru)
Allemande that corner, do sa do your partner,
Swing the corner lady, promenade.
You made life cheerie, when you called me dearie,
down where the blue grass grows.

ALTERANTE BREAK

Head two gents and the corner move up to the middle and back,
Right to the opposite, box the gnat you go (right and left thru)
Back right out, and make a ring, circle left I sing, Circle left around the you go.
Those who can, left allemande, the rest rollaway, go right and left grang,
Grankd old right and left you know (promenade)
Promenade your deary, for she makes life cheerie,
down whre the blue grass grows.
