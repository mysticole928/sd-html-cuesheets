Who's Cheating' Who    (LM 138)   Larry Letson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies circle left, go once inside the ring
At home the boys join in, everybody circle to the left
All the boys star right go one time around
Do an allemande the corner, weave the ring
Well, who's cheating who? Who's being true?
Dosado and you promenade
Who's doing right with someone tonight
And whose car is parked next door?

FIGURE



Head 2 you square thru, you pull out 4 good hands to your corner
Dosado, swing thru tonight, well let the boys run right
Couples circulate, wheel & deal, eight chain 2
Do a curlique, scoot back, swing with corner, promenade
Who's doing right with someone tonight
And whose car is parked next door?

ENDING



Come on 4 little ladies promenade once around inside
Run back, swing your man around
Join up all of your hands, circle left get down the land
Do an allemande the corner, weave the ring
Singing, who's cheating who, well who's being true?
Come on dosado, all promenade
Who's doing right with someone tonight
And whose car is parked next door?

TAG



Well, whose car is parked next door?
