Witch Doctor / Fit On A Fiddle
David Seville And The Chipmunks 1958
   (J-Bar-L 4122)   
INTRO

My friend the witch doctor said swing your lady fair
He said the gents star left and leave her standing there
When you're home you dosado and promenade the square
With an ooo e, ooo ah ah, ting tang, walla walla, bing bang
Ooo e, ooo ah ah, ting tang, walla walla, bing bang

FIGURE   

Head 2 couples cross trail , travel round just one
Forward 8 and back , lot'sa time, four in line
Right & left thru and then two little ladies chain across the ring
Then chain two ladies down the line
My friend the witch doctor said single file for you
He said the gents reach back and pull that lady through
Go right & left grand around the ring and then here's what you do
Ooo e, ooo ah ah, ting tang, walla walla, bing bang
Ooo e, ooo ah ah, ting tang, walla walla, bing bang

MIDDLE BREAK    Basic

4 little ladies chain across, turn the girls around
Head 2 ladies chain , just the two
Head 2 couples promenade 3/4 round for you
Side 2 go right & left thru with a full turn around to the outside two
Allemande left your corner, go home and swing 'em there
He said the gents star left and leave them standing there
And when you're home dosado and promenade the square
With an ooo e, ooo ah ah, ting tang, walla walla, bing bang
Ooo e, ooo ah ah, ting tang, walla walla, bing bang
