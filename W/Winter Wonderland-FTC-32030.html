* TO * NOTE



* to * in the breaks
This entire sequence is known as a Triple Allemande Left and requires everyone to do an Allemande Left
Then the LADIES move into the center and make a Right Hand Star
while the MEN Promenade single file on the outer perimeter of the square.
Everyone will meet the corner after about three or four steps and do an Allemande Left again
This time the MEN move into the center and make a RIGHT HAND STAR, while the LADIES Promenade single file outside
Upon meeting the corner for the THIRD TIME, everyone will do an Allemande Left (and the next call will take them from that point
