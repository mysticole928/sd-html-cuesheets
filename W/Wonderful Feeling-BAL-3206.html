Wonderful Feeling
James Baskett; Johnny Mercer; many others 1946
(Balance 3206 side A )
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Allemande your corner, come home swing your maid
Men star by the left , once around the ring you go
Pick up your lady, arm around, star promenade go round the town
4 ladies backtrack , turn partner by the right
Left allemande , home you go and swing her
You swing and you sway
What a wonderful feeling, square dancing this way

FIGURE

Head (side) couples forward & back ,
promenade 1/2 way round the ring you do
Come down the middle , do a right & left thru , turn that girl
Pass thru , separate around one into the middle & square thru 3/4
Swing that corner & promenade around that old ring
What a wonderful feeling, square dancing this way
