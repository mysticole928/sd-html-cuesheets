Puddy Tat Square / Whirlpool Square
   (Old Timer 8080 side B )    Johnny Schultz
INTRO

It's eight to the center, and back to the square
Now swing your own, she's standing there
Then you all get ready for the Whirlpool Square

FIGURE

The ladies to the center and back to the bar
Now the 4 gents center with a right hand star
Now back with a left, don't be afraid
And take your honey for a star promenade
It's a star promenade and around you go
Now the gents back out and the ladies swing in
It's a turn and a half and you're gone again
And the ladies star in with a right hand spin
The heads roll out like the rising tide
And come back in behind the sides
Now the sides roll out a boy and a girl
And come back in, make the waters whirl
Now the heads roll out just one more time
And you come back in, you're doin' fine
The sides roll out just one time more
And come back in like you did before
The ladies rollaway with a half sashay
And the gents star in that same old way
Now the ladies turn back to a left allemande
And your partner with a right and a right & left grand
It's a hand over hand all around the ring
Now meet your partner and you give her a swing
And promenade with the dear little thing

CLOSER

The ladies roll back to a left allemande and away you go
It's a right and a left, then docey-doe that gal you know
It's your corner with a right and back to the bar
And the four gents center like an allemande thar
Now shoot that star with a full turn around
And promenade your corner as she comes round
You promenade here and you promenade there
Now take her out and find a chair
That's all there is to the Whirlpool Square
