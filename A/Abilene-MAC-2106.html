Abilene   (Mac Gregor 2106 )   Tommy Stoye

OPENER



Circle to the left
Now Abilene, Abilene, prettiest town I ever seen
Your corner allemande, you dosado your own
And the men star left one time
Turn your partner by the right, your corner allemande
Swing your own and promenade that old ring
When in there in Abilene, my Abilene

FIGURE

in Abilene, my Abilene
