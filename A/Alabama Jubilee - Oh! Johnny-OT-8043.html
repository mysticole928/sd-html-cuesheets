Alabama Jubilee / Oh! Johnny
Collins & Harlan; Skillet Lickers; Red Foley; Chet Atkins; many others 1915
   (Old Timer 8043 side A )   
FIGURE (four times through)

Four ladies promenade the inside of the ring
Back to your partner and you give him a swing
Dosado that corner girl about one time around
Bow right down to your old man
Everybody swing and everybody dance
Four gents promenade the inside of the ring
Back to your partner and dosado all
Now give that corner girl a swing
Promenade her around the ring
To the Alabama Jubilee, heh, heh
To that Alabama Jubilee

BREAK

Turn your left hand lady with a left hand round
Right to your partner go all the way around
Turn the right hand lady with a left hand round
Now swing your partner til her fee leave the ground
Now the four gents promenade the inside the hall
Back to your partner and dosado all
Swing your partner around and around
Promenade her around the town
To that Alabama Jubilee
