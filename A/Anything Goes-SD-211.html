Anything Goes
Tony Bennett; Ella Fitzgerald; many others 1934
   (Shakedown 211)    Burt (Red) Cullop
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
In olden days a glimpse of stocking
Was looked on as something shocking
Now, Heaven knows, anything goes
4 boys promenade go once inside
You swing your own, promenade
Now Heaven knows, anything goes

FIGURE (Twice for heads, twice for sides)    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) square thru and count 'em 4 hands and go
Around the corner do a dosado
You swing thru and let the boys run right
You tag the line , cloverleaf girls lead
Ladies square thru 3 hands around you go
You swing your corner and promenade
Now Heaven knows, anything goes

MIDDLE BREAK    B - SSD Week 11

4 ladies promenade go once inside
Get back and box the gnat and everybody swing
Join your hands circle left
Do an allemande left your corner, do a dosado and then
You left allemande and promenade
Now Heaven knows, anything goes

CLOSER    B - SSD Week 2

4 ladies chain 3/4 around that ring
You roll away , circle left
'cause Heaven knows
Allemande left and weave
You wind it in and out around you go and then
Swing your own and promenade
Now, Heaven knows, anything goes
