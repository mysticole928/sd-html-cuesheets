Adios   (Blue Star 2134 )   Johnnie Wykoff

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain across that ring
You chain the ladies right back home again
Join hands, circle left 1/2 way round
Left allemande the corner, weave around and sing
Goodnight, goodnight, until we meet again
Swing with your own and promenade
Here's a wish and a prayer that every dream comes true
Adios, au revoir, auf wiedersen

FIGURE



Well, one and three (2 & 4) square thru 4 hands you go
Make a right hand star and you make it go
Those heads star left one time around
To the same two, get a right & left thru, and now you rollaway
Join both hands, single circle 1/2 and swing
Left allemande, then promenade the ring
Here's a wish and a prayer that every dream comes true
Adios, au revoir, auf wiedersen
