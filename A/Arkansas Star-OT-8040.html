Arkansas Star / Sioux City Sioux
   (Old Timer 8040 side A )    Cal Golden
 
FIGURE

Head couples center and back to the ring
Side couples center with a right hand star
Go all the way around
Back with a left and you're goin' to town
Pick up your corner as you come down
With a right foot up and your left foot down
Turn the outside in and the inside out
Turn that arkie star about
Turn the inside out and the outside in and
Turn that arkie star again
Break in the center and everybody swing
Allemande left just once and promenade the girl you've swung

(Repeat dance three more times until you get original partners back.)
