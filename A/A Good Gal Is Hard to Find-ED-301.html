FIGURE    Mainstream - SSD Week 12, corner progression

Heads square thru 4 hands you go
Around the corner lady do a dosado , make a wave
Recycle then, sweep 1/4 more
Right & left thru , turn the girl, then square thru
3/4 round, your corner swing
Swing that lady round then promenade again
A good gal now-a-days is hard to find

MIDDLE BREAK    Basic - SSD Week 11

Join hands and circle left , go movin' round you go
Left allemande , come back and weave the ring you go
When you find a good one you better treat her right
You're gonna swing your lady, promenade tonight
A good gal now-a-days is hard to find
