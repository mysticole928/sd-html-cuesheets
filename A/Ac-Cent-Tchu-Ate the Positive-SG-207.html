Ac-Cent-Tchu-Ate The Positive    (SG 207)   Tom Manning

OPENER



Circle left
You've got to
Ac-cent-tchu-ate the positive
E-lim-enate the negative
Left allemande, dosado your own
The men star left inside you roam
Turn thru, left allemande
You swing your lady round, you promenade the land
And latch on to the affirmative
Don't mess with Mr. In-Between

FIGURE



Heads (Sides) square thru four hands around you go
You meet your corner dosado go one time you know
Swing thru, boys run right again
Bend the line a right & left thru
You flutter wheel, slide thru,
Swing your corner, you promenade her too
And latch on to the affirmative
Don't mess with Mr. In-Between

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain
Your turn & chain her right back home again
Join all your hands you circle left go round
Left allemande, weave the ring
Spread joy up to the maximum
You swing your lady round, you promenade the town
And latch on to the affirmative
Don't mess with Mr. In-Between
