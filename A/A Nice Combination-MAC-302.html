A Nice Combination
   (MacGregor 302)    Don Ward
FIGURE

1-8 One you face (corner) dosado
9-16 Same one swing (End the swing facing down the set in a line of four)
17-24 Promenade down (4), wheel as couple (4) face back up
25-32 Promenade up (4), cast off (4) face across
33-40 With couple across, circle left 3/4 (to man's side of the set)
41-48 Partner swing (man's partner is on his left in the circle, face across)
49-56 Ladies chain across
57-64 (same 4) Left hand star, once around (progress to next in line)

Repeat the dance with the next person, up or down the set
