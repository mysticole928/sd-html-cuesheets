Come on now allemande your corner, dosado your own
Join up hands circle , make a ring
Circle halfway around the land and then
Go allemande left and weave around that ring
Hey now you meet your lady, do a dosado and then
Allemande left and come back one and promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
In the days of Old Lang Syne

And then side couples square thru full around
Find the corner lady swing the girl and then
Allemande left corner, promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
In the days of Auld Lang Syne
