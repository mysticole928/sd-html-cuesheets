(Roofers 116) Gerald McWhirter
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 little ladies hit the road , go walkin', talkin' round
Come on home and swing , swing your man and then
Join up hands and circle to the left go movin' round
Do a left allemande , gonna weave around and sing
Oh, you weave it in and out, until you meet your doll
Swing your darlin' there, promenade awhile, singin'
Hail, hail, the gang's all here
At the Alabama Jubilee

FIGURE

Heads square thru now 4 hands around you do
Make a right-hand star with the outside two and turn it, go, go, go
Heads star by the left, once around you go
Same old 2 pass thru , u-turn back and curlique
Boys run right and go left allemande
Come back one and swing and promenade the land, singin'
Hail, hail, the gang's all here
At the Alabama Jubilee

FIGURE (ALTERNATE) (Mainstream)

Heads square thru now 4 hands around you go
Make a right-hand star with the outside two and turn it, go, go, go
Heads star by the left, once around you go
Same old 2 right & left thru and then you roll away
Swing thru and then, ladies gonna trade
Boys run to the right and then you promenade , singin'
Hail, hail, the gang's all here
At the Alabama Jubilee

FIGURE (ALTERNATE) (Plus)

Heads promenade go halfway around
Sides go right & left thru and turn that girl around
Square thru in the middle 4 hands around you go
Right & left thru , turn the girl and then slide thru
Load the boat , sink or float, take your time and go
Swing your corner round, promenade you know, singin'
Hail, hail, the gang's all here
At the Alabama Jubilee
