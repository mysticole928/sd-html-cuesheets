Gents just wait 'til she gets back and give her a swing

FIGURE



All join hands and circle left, make a great big ring
Turn to the corner lady and you give that girl a swing
Put her on your right, circle left, then another corner swing
Put her on your right, away we go, circle left and then you know
Dopaso as you come down and back to your own with a left around
Go...all around..that left hand lady
See-saw...around your taw

ALTERNATE OPENER - CLOSER

4 little ladies hit the road go walkin' talkin' round
Come on home and swing, swing your man and then..
Join up hands and circle to the left go movin' round
Do a left allemande, gonna weave around and sing
Oh, you weave it in and out, until you meet your doll
Swing your darlin' there, promenade awhile, singin'...
At the Alabama Jubilee

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Heads (sides) square thru now, 4 hands around you go
Make a right hand star with the outside 2 and turn it, go, go, go
Heads star by the left, once around you go
Same old two, pass thru, u turn back, and star thru, girls do a u turn back
Boys run right and go left allemande
Come back one and swing and promenade the land, singin'...
At the Alabama Jubilee

ALTERNATE PLUS FIGURE
