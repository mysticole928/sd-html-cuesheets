Ain't She Sweet   (TNT 151 )   Al Brundage

BREAK



Four ladies promenade - see 'em walking down the street
Box the gnat, when you get there, four men promenade
Well, ain't they nice, you turn thru and then
Left allemande and come on home and dosado
Go full around, men star left now
Till you meet your maid, give her a swing and promenade
Ain't she sweet, see her walking down the street
Now, I ask you very confidentially, ain't she sweet?

FIGURE



Heads you promenade, halfway with your maid
Lead to the right, veer to the left, you bend the line
Go forward up and back, right & left thru my friend
Pass thru and tag the line, all face in
Box the gnat, right & left thru now
Slide thru, pass thru, corner swing, promenade

TAG
