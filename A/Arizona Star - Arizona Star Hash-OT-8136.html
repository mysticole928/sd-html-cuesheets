Arizona Star / Arizona Star Hash
   (Old Timer 8136 side A )    Johnny Schultz
OPENER

Walk all around your left hand lady
See saw your pretty little taw
Now face your corner
Allemande left with your left hand
Your partner by the right, a right & left grand
Hand over hand and around you go
Now meet your partner and promenade
Let's promenade go round the ring
Now get back home and give her a swing
Swing your lady go round and around
And when you get through, just settle down

FIGURE (1, 2, and 3)

Send the ladies to the center, and back to the bar
The gents to the center with a right hand star
With a right hand cross and around you go
Now back with a left and don't be slow
You meet your partner and pass her by
With a right arm around and don't be shy
Now the gents back out and the ladies walk in
It's a turn and a half and you're gone again
And there you got that star again
Now break right there and everybody swing your girl
Go round and around with the pretty little girl
Now allemande left just one, then promenade that gal you swung
Let's promenade right down the line
And get along home, you're doing fine
Get back home and swing your baby go round and around
Any old way but upside down

MIDDLE BREAK

You back right out and form a ring
Circle to the left like everything
Circle to the left and around you go
Now break it all up like a dopaso
Your partner by the left and your corner by the right
Your partner by the left now hang on tight
Roll promenade, don't take all night
Get back home and swing your girl
Go round and around with the pretty little girl
And when you get through just settle down

CLOSER

Now allemande left with your left hand
Your partner by the right, a right & left grand
Hand over hand go around the ring, meet your partner and give her a swing
Give her a swing, then give her a twirl
Then promenade you go round the world
Promenade with your leaping Lena, she's the girl from Pasadena
You get back home and what do you do
You bow to your partner, well ain't she sweet
Let's take off your shoes and cool your feet
