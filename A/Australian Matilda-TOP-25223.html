Australian Matilda    (TOP 25223)   Wally Cook

BREAK



Bow to your partner, allemande left your corner girl
Grand old right & left 'neath the blue gum trees
When you meet your pretty maid, promenade 'em in the shade
Heads wheel around, do a right & left thru
Cross trail back, allemande your corner
Dosado your own, then you promenade
And you can sing as you watch
And wait till the billy boils
Who'll come a waltzing Matilda with me

FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands round the ring you go
When you meet the sides spin chain thru
Work your way across the ring, and as you go across you sing
All 8 circulate then turn thru
Allemande your corner, walk by your partner
Swing with the next and then promenade
And you can sing as you watch and wait till the billy boils
Who'll come a waltzing Matilda with me
