Amarillo
Tony Christie 1971

OPENER    Basic - SSD Week 1

4 ladies promenade , go 'round
Back home and swing the man, 'round and 'round
Join hands , circle left 'round the ring
Allemande left with the corner, weave the ring , well...
Show me the way to Amarillo
Swing the girl, promenade 'round the ring go
Is this the way to Amarillo
And sweet Marie who waits for me

FIGURE    Basic - SSD Week 7, corner progression

Heads square thru , it's 4 hands 'round
All the way and then, circle to a line
Break out and make a line and then go right & left thru
Turn the girl and pass thru , wheel & deal
The centers square thru count me 3 now
Swing corner, promenade 'round the ring now
Is this the way to Amarillo
And sweet Marie who waits for me

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)    Mainstream - SSD Week 12, corner progression

Sides square thru it's 4 hands 'round
All the way and then, right & left thru
You veer to the left and then ferris wheel
You pass thru , touch 1/4
Scoot back , boy; scoot back , girl
Swing your corner and promenade 'round the world
Is this the way to Amarillo
And sweet Marie who waits for me
