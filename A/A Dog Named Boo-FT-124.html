A Dog Named Boo    (FT 124)   Rod Shuping

OPENER



Sides face grand square
I remember to this day
That bright red Georgia clay
And how it stuck to the tires
After the summer rain
Allemande left, weave the ring
Me and you and a dog named Boo
Traveling and livin' off the land
Promenade
Me and you and a dog named Boo
How I loved being a free man

FIGURE #1



Heads square thru you know
Meet your corner dosado
Swing thru, girls fold
Peel the top and a right & left thru
You pass thru and then chase right
Your corner swing, promenade
Another tank of gas and
Back on the road again

FIGURE #2



Heads promenade halfway
You lead right, veer left, crossfire
Column circulate, boys run
Single circle, rear back and a right & left grand
Old MacDonald he made us work
But then he paid us for what it was worth
Promenade
Another tank of gas and
Back on the road again

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four girls promenade
Get back and swing your man
Join hands, circle go left around the ring
Allemande left, weave the ring
Me and you and a dog named Boo
Traveling and livin' off the land
Promenade
Me and you and a dog named Boo
How I loved being a free man
