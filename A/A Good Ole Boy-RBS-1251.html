A Good Ole Boy
   (Red Boot Star 1251)    Elmer Sheffield, Jr.
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
I've been kicked out of might' near every bar in town
I've been locked up for driving a hundred and twenty through town
Join hands , circle left
I've been shot at and cut with a knife
Left allemande and promenade all right
But other than that, I ain't nothing but a good ole boy

FIGURE    Plus, corner progression

The heads square thru while the sides roll away
Dosado while you're that way
Swing thru and then those centers you run
You do a ferris wheel , spread and make your line
Then star thru and square thru go 3
You trade by , swing the corner, promenade for me
Well a good ole boy that's all I'll ever be

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
I threw my boss out the window and got fired from my last job
Hot wired a city truck and turned it over in the mayor's yard
Join hands , circle left
I beat my brother-in-law half to death
Left allemande and promenade all right
But other than that, I ain't nothing but a good ole boy

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
I've got an alimony payment that's 6 weeks overdue
I got caught with a trunk load of bootleg out-of-state booze
Join hands , circle left
I hocked my wife's diamond ring last June
Left allemande and promenade all right
But other than that, I ain't nothing but a good ole boy
