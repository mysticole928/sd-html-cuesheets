Auld Lang Syne    (GR 12059)   Earl Johnston

INTRO



Allemande left your corner, dosado at home
Join your hands, circle left and make a ring
Go halfway round the land, do a left allemande
Then weave around the ring
Meet your lady, dosado one time around
Left allemande, come home and promenade
Should old acquaintance be forgot
In the days of Auld Lang Syne

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right, circle round the track
Make a line, go forward up and back
Do a right and left thru, turn them around and then
Star thru, pass thru, u-turn back and box the gnat
Pull them by, left allemande, dosado round your own
Your corner promenade and sing
Should old acquaintance be forgot
In the days of Auld Lang Syne

SEQUENCE (Intro, figure twice, intro, figure twice, intro)
