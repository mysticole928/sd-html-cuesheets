OPENER    B - SSD Week 6

1 & 3 you promenade halfway with your maid
Sides square thru 4 hands around and then
Go right & left thru that outside two, turn a girl and dive thru
Pass thru , left allemande and weave the ring
Sweet magnolia fragrance is all around your maid
Dosado and then you promenade
Well the honeysuckle vine keep a clingin' all the time
As you dance with your Alabama maid
