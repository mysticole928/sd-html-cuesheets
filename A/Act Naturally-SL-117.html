Act Naturally    (SL 117)   Gaylon Shull

FIGURE



Head two couples lead to the right and circle
Break and make a line, dance up & back
Star thru and dosado this lady
Make an ocean wave and balance up & back
Half square thru and cast off 3/4
Square thru 3/4 round and don't be slow
California whirl, dance forward up & back now
Go right & left thru and turn this girl you know, cross trail
Allemande left your corner, docey partner
Swing the corner girl and promenade you see
I'm the biggest fool to ever hit the big time
And all I gotta do is act naturally

MIDDLE BREAK



Join your hands and then you start a circle
Circle on your heel and on your toe
Allemande left and docey round your partner
Gents star left it's once around you go
With an arm around star promenade you lady
Girls backtrack go twice around I sing
Second time you meet, box the gnat, pull by
Allemande left and weave around the ring
Weave in and out until you meet your lady
Dosado and promenade you see
I'm the biggest fool to ever hit the big time
And all I gotta do is act naturally
