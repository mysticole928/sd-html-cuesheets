Four ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
You roll away and circle to the left
Walk around your corner lady, see saw your own
Left allemande, weave the ring
America, America
Do a dosado and promenade

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands around you go
Around the corner lady do a dosado
Swing thru 2 by 2, boys run right
Bend the line and do a right & left thru
Flutter wheel across the ring, then slide thru
Swing the corner lady, promenade
And crowned thy good with brotherhood
From sea to shining sea
