Good Old Country Song    (4B 6026)   Bill Volner

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade the ring
get home and you swing
join hands circle to the left around you go
left allemande and weave the ring
come on and sing me a good old country song
dosado and then you promenade
I don't dig rock n'roll, rhythm and blues, I don't love soul
(come on and) sing me a good old country song

FIGURE



head/side two couples promenade 1/2 down the middle
with a right and left thru I say
flutter wheel in the middle of the land, sweep 1/4 more
pass thru, touch 1/4
girls run, touch 1/4
boys run to the right and swing
left allemande and promenade and go round the ring
I don't dig rock n'roll, rhythm and blues, I don't love soul
(come on and) sing me a good old country song
