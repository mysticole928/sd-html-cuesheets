Allemande left your corner, right hand round your own
Gents star left you travel, don't you ladies roam
Right hand turn your partner , allemande your corners all
Weave the ring go in and out you travel round the hall
When you meet you dosado her, right shoulders round you know
Bow to her and weave the ring , gonna weave around once more
Oh, an apple for the teacher , now swing 'em two by two
Once or twice and settle down, that seems the thing to do

FIGURE

Do-ci round your corner, bow down to your pet
Head two couples right & left thru , turn 'em cross the set
Cross trail back, go round two , hook on and make a line
Go forward eight and back so soon, then you shoot the moon*
Left allemande your corner, and do-ci partners all
Go back and swing that corner girl, promenade go round the hall
Oh, an apple for the teacher, that seems the thing to do
Take her home and balance out, she loves to swing with you

( *Shoot The Moon was originated by Del Coolman of Flint, MI and his explanation is as follows: From two lines of facing fours with two men on the left end of each line, the two in the middle of each line do a cross trail, those on the ends of the line star thru with the one facing them, you will end up facing your corner, ready for a left allemande.)

Shoot The Moon can be replaced with a Star Thru + Centers Pass Thru.
