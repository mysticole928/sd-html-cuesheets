Are You From Dixie
The Blue Sky Boys; many others 1915
   (Benz 1214)    Ben Baldwin, Jr.
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Head couple star thru , you right & left thru
Turn the girls, pass thru , swing this lady facing you
All circle left now, go round the ring now
Left allemande , walk by one , with the next you box the gnat
*The four little ladies promenade , go once around the ring
Box the gnat , promenade , take her home and sing
Are you from Dixie, I said from Dixie
Well I'm from Dixie, too

FIGURE   

Heads right & left thru , same ladies chain to the right
Two ladies chain , turn the girls, hold 'em tight
Sides right & left thru , four ladies chain thru**
Promenade this girl and don't you slow down
* Four little ladies whirl-away , star right go round the ring
Turn 'em left, roll promenade , take her home and sing
Are you from Dixie, I said from Dixie
Well I'm from Dixie, too

ALTERNATE LYRICS

*Those Southern Belles just whirl, etc.

*Those Memphis Maids just whirl, etc.

*Those Dixie Darlings whirl-a-way, etc.

(**Note: Chain Thru. Ladies chain across, using standard chain; Men then star thru, not a courtesy turn, to end in Promenade Position.)

SEQUENCE

Opener, Figure twice for heads, Middle Break, Figure twice for sides, Closer.
