Another Chance   (Petticoat Patter 104 )   Toots Richardson
INTRO

4 ladies promenade the inside of the ring
Why, git back home and give your guy a swing
Come on you join hands and circle to the left go walking round
You allemande left your corner, weave the ring
Oh, when you left, you said you were doing me a favor
Do a little dosado and promenade
Now it's been 2 short weeks and you're wanting to come back
But I think there's something that you ought to know

FIGURE



And now the head (side) two couples promenade 1/2 way round
Come down the middle, square thru, go 4
4 hands around and then, swing thru the outside two
The boys trade, boys run, bend the line and then
You slide thru and pass thru, trade by and swing
Oh, swing that corner girl and promenade
I've rearranged the living room to suit me
And I gave your favorite chair to charity

BREAK



4 ladies promenade the inside of the ring
Why, git back home and give your guy a swing
Come on you join hands and circle to the left go walking round
You allemande left your corner, weave the ring
Oh, when you left, you said you were doing me a favor
Do a little dosado and promenade
That closet you insisted on is mine now
Don't bring your hangup back to me

CLOSER



4 ladies promenade the inside of the ring
Why, git back home and give your guy a swing
Come on you join hands and circle to the left go walking round
You allemande left your corner, weave the ring
Oh, when you left, you said you were doing me a favor
Do a little dosado and promenade
I'm having so much fun down at the square dance hall
I've made friends and I'm learning how to dance
