Adieu    (BO 1225)   Glenn Zeno

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Come on and join hands, circle to the left, walking round the ring you go
Left allemande that corner, come on back do a dosado
The men star left it's once around and then
Turn your partner by the right, left allemande again
Well now you weave that ole ring, in and out around you go
When you meet your ladies, do a dosado and promeno
As you go around and tell her adieu
It's been so nice to dance with you

FIGURE



Now the head 2 ladies chain, turn that girl and then
It's 1 & 3 star thru, pass thru do a dosado
Go once around and then, swing thru my friend
Make a wave and balance, turn thru and then
Allemande left that corner, come on back do a dosado
That corner lady swing, promenade go round the ring
As you go around tell her adieu
It's been so nice to dance with you
