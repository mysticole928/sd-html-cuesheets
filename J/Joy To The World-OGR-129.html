Joy To The World
Trinity Choir; many others 1719
   (Omar 129)    Noriko Shinogaya , Max Ringe
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Joy to the world, the Lord is come
Let earth receive her King
Let every heart prepare Him room
And heaven and nature sing, and heaven and nature sing
Four boys star by the right , the nature sing
Pass your partner , allemande left , come back and promenade
And heaven and nature sing, and heaven and nature sing
And heaven, and heaven and nature sing

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Heads (sides) promenade and go halfway
Come down the middle pass the ocean then, extend
Hinge , walk & dodge , partner trade
* Reverse the flutter wheel , half sashay
* Pass the ocean and right & left grand
While fields and floods, rocks, hills and plains
Dosado and promenade
Repeat the sounding joy
Repeat, repeat the sounding joy

ALTERNATE CHOREO

* Reverse the flutter wheel , slide thru
* Box the gnat and right & left grand

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
Joy to the world, the Savior reigns
Let men their songs employ
While fields and floods, rocks, hills and plains
Repeat the sounding joy, repeat the sounding joy
Four boys promenade , the sounding joy
Get back home and dosado with your partner and promenade
Repeat the sounding joy, repeat the sounding joy
Repeat, repeat the sounding joy

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
He rules the world with truth and grace
And makes the nations prove
And glories of His righteousness, and wonders of His love
And wonders of His love, and wonders of His love
Four boys star by the right , inside the ring
Pass your partner , allemande left , come back and promenade
And wonders of His love, and wonders of His love
And wonders of His love
