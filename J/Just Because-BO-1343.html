Just Because    (BO 1343)   Tommy White, David Davis

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Well 4 little ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Join up hands and circle left go round
Those ladies roll away then you circle left I say
Allemande left that corner, weave the ring
Weave in and out around and when you meet that maid
You dosado that girl and promenade
Promenade around that ring, throw your head right back and sing
Because, just because

FIGURE



Now the head two couples square thru go 4 hands
With the corner lady do a dosado
Do a curlique and then, split circulate my friend
Boys run to the right, a right & left thru
Pass the ocean there, recycle round and swing
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Promenade around that ring, throw your head right back and sing
Because, just because
