OPENER



Sides face, grand square
South of the border, free from law and order
Danced with a pretty senorita
The wine and music flowed, until the rooster crowed
At a place called Juanita's Cantina (allemande and weave)
I'd robbed a Texas train, escaped across the Texas plains
I left all I knew in El Paso
Then right about sundown I hit this Mexican town
