John's Patter    (K 1263)   John Saunders

Pass thru position.

Linear Cycle: From ocean wave - everybody single hinge, outfacers fold behind



Ping Pong Circulate: From 1/4 tag setup - everybody extend 2 positions then outfacers trade.



Dixie Derby: From facing couples - ladies lead dixie style to an ocean wave, then
girls fold while boys trade - boys slide apart and girls step up between the boys.
Ends in a 2-faced line with girls in the center & boys on the ends.
