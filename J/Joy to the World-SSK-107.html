Joy To The World   (Silver Streak 107 )   Milt Floyd

OPENER



Heads ladies centers teacup chain
Jeremiah was a bull frog
Was a good friend of mine
I never understood a single word he said
But, I helped him drink his wine
Allemande and weave
Joy to the world, all the boys and girls
Joy to the fishes in the deep blue sea
Joy to you and me

FIGURE



Heads promenade go halfway around
You pass the ocean then all the boys extend
Everybody recycle round do a double pass thru
Track two gonna move it and go
Recycle round, touch 1/4 and then
Follow your neighbor half spread
Swing the corner promenade
Joy to the fishes in the deep blue sea
Joy to you and me

BREAK



Head ladies center teacup chain
If I was the king of the world
I'll tell you what I'd do
I'd throw away the cars and the bars and the wars
And make sweet love to you
Allemande, weave
Joy to the world, all the boys and girls
Joy to the fishes in the deep blue sea
Joy to you and me

CLOSER



Head ladies center teacup chain
Joy to the world, all the boys and girls
Joy to the fishes in the deep blue sea
Joy to you and me
Four ladies promenade inside the ring
Get back home and swing and promenade
Joy to the fishes in the deep blue sea
Joy to you and me

TAG



Four ladies chain across and back
Joy to the fishes in the deep blue sea
Joy to you and me

SEQUENCE (Opener, Figure heads twice, Break, Figure sides twice, Closer, Tag)
