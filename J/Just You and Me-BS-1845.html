Just You And Me    (BS 1845)   Al Brownlee

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands make that ring, circle round the land
Circle left with a ring-a-ding-ding till you're home again
Do an allemande left with the corner, dosado your own
Gents star left now around you roam
Turn a right hand round your partner and go left allemande
Dosado around your own, promenade the land
I love to stroll with you over hills and by the sea
Love was meant for just you and me

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain to the right, turn that lady fair
1 & 3 promenade halfway round the square
Star thru then pass thru, slide thru and then
Swing thru, turn thru, left allemande
Do a dosado around your own, new corner girl you swing
Swing that lady round and then you promenade that ring
I love to stroll with you over hills and by the sea
Love was meant for just you and me
