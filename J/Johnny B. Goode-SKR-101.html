Johnny B. Goode    (SKR 101)   O'Neal Collier

OPENER



Circle left
Down in Louisiana, close to New Orleans
Way back in the woods among the evergreens
Allemande left that corner, dosado and then
The men star left and roll it round the ring
You turn your partner by the right, your corner allemande
Swing your girl around and promenade I sing
Go Johnny go, go, go
Johnny B. Goode

FIGURE



Heads you promenade and travel halfway around
You walk to the middle and square thru 4 hands around
Go all the way and then, a right & left thru
Veer left, ferris wheel, in the middle you do
Square thru and go 3 hand around
Swing the girl around and promenade that town
Go Johnny go, go, go
Johnny B. Goode

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Sides face, grand square, go
(32 beat interlude)
Four ladies promenade go walking round the ring
Come back, swing your man and promenade I sing
Go Johnny go, go, go
Johnny B. Goode
