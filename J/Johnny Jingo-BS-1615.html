Johnny Jingo    (BS 1615)   Marshall Flippo

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Now the 4 little ladies chain across, you turn 'em with a left around
Roll away then circle 8, you go walking round that town
Partner left do a dopaso, corner by the right & don't be slow
Partner left like an allemande thar, gents back up, right hand star
Slip the clutch, left allemande, pass 1 maid
Swing the next little girl and promenade
Promenade the feeling's bingo...jingo
She'll never swing anyone like Johnny Jingo

FIRST FIGURE



Heads go forward up and star thru, pass thru
Right & left thru with the outside 2, you'll turn 2, dive thru
Right & left thru in the usual way, roll away with a 1/2 sashay
U-turn back, do an eight chain thru, all the way over and back with you
Ooh what you do, what you do, what you do when you hold me
Ooh what you do, what you do, when you box the gnat
Promenade this little dear, whisper these words in her ear
Ooh what you do, what you do when you look like that

SECOND FIGURE



Heads go forward up and star thru, pass thru
Right & left thru with the outside 2, you'll turn thru, star thru
Pass thru and bend the line, up to the middle & back in time
Star thru across from you, swing awhile a brand new Sue
Then allemande left that corner, do a grand old right & left
Ooh what you do, what you do, when you promenade
Promenade thie little dear, whisper these words in her ear
Ooh what you do, what you do when you swing and sway

TAG



She won't swing anyone like Johnny Jingo

(Note: Either figure can be used exclusively, in which case there will be 4 partner changes. If first figure is used twice, then second figure twice, you will dance with partner, corner, opposite, during first figure, and then second figure twice, you will dance with opposite, corner, and back to original partner.)
