Jingle Bells   (Mac Gregor 2416 )   Brian Hotchkies

INTRO - BREAK - CLOSER



(Pre-cue) Sides face grand square
Christmas eve is here, and we go off to bed
as we climb the stairs and nod our sleepy heads
Take our stockings off, hang them in a row
Then jump quickly into bed Four ladies chain you know
Chain the ladies over, you turn and chain them back
Now turn the girl with the rosy cheeks and promenade the track
Jingle bells, Jingle bells, Jingle all the way
Hurrah for dear old Santa Claus, Hurrah for Christmas Day

FIGURE



Heads right and left thru, you turn your little girl
And then half square thru, now touch a quarter friend
Split circulate and now scoot back, put the ladies in
Do a walk and dodge and then, do a partner trade my friend
Square thru three hands and the corner lady swing
Swing Merry Christmas (Rudolph, Carol, Santa Claus)
Now promenade the ring
Oh what fund it is to ride in a one horse sleigh tonight
