Jellybean Stompers Ball
Original Dixieland Jass Band; Six Brown Brothers; Ella Fitzgerald; many others 1915
(Bogan 1167) Bob Radford
FIGURE

Head 2 couples lead to the right , circle 4 and make a line
Forward 8 and back you reel, pass thru , wheel & deal
Center 2 star thru , square thru 4 hands you do
Split the outside pair , round one make a line right there
Go forward 8 and come on back with you, box the gnat
Right & left thru you turn them too, star thru in front of you
Dosado , make an ocean wave , rock it up and back with you
Pull her by that corner swing , promenade go round the ring
Gonna dance off both those shoes, when they play those jelly roll blues
Tomorrow night at the jelly bean stompers ball

BREAK

Heads face, grand square
(16 beats)
Reverse
(16 beats)
Left allemande that corner girl, dosado your own little pearl
Gents star left on a heel and toe, once around the ring you go
Promenade your lady fair, take a walk go round the square
Gonna dance off both those shoes, when they play those jelly roll blues
Tomorrow night at the jelly bean stompers ball
