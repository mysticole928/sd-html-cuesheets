Hall's Hash / Johnson's Jumble
   (Windsor 4408 side B )    Bob Hall , Bruce Johnson
OPENER

Now bow to your partners and your corners all, now circle to the left, go round the hall
And you circle to the left and around you go
Allemande left and what'll it be, go right & left and you turn back three
It's right & left to a right hand swing, now the ladies star in the middle of the ring
The ladies star 3/4 round, turn 'em by the right as they come down
It's once and a half and you don't let go, take the next with a left like "alamo"
Gonna balance out and balance in, dopaso and gone again
Partners left and corners round
Partners left to a roll promenade and you walk back home with your own little maid
Right back home with the pretty little thing, when you get there you stop and swing
Everybody gonna swing and whirl, it's round and round with your pretty little girl

FIGURE

First couple stand back-to-back, boompsa daisy, go round that track
It's all around the ring you go, while the birdies sing and the roosters crow
Right on by your own, you see, to the corner couple and circle three
When you get near home stand three in line, go forward and back, you're doin' fine
Couple number three you swing with pride, now zig-zag through the three on the sides
Go out and in and out once more, now sashay down the middle of the floor
And you back right up to a line of four
Go forward eight and back with you, center four do a right & left thru
Now the four men left with a half sashay, forward eight and back that way
Now pass right thru and turn to the right, go single file around that site
With a right hand in you form a star, and walk right around but not too far
Now the gents reach under with your left, and pull her on thru to an allemande thar
And you back 'em up, boys, in a right hand star, walk along backwards not too far
Eight roll away with a half sashay, back 'em, boys, in the same old way
Now throw out the clutch and put 'er in low, it's twice around that ring you go
Now turn the next with a right hand swing, it's twice around with the dear little thing
And the gents star left 3/4 round, to a right & left grand as you come down
Go hand over hand 'til you meet that maid, take 'er by the hand and promenade
Promenade, go round the ring, and when you get home let's stop and swing

FIGURE

Now the second and fourth you swing some more
Now go to the right and circle four, you're doin' fine: open up and form a line
Go forward eight and back with you, forward again and pass right thru
Join hands again, the ends turn in
Circle four in the middle of the floor, go one time around with the pretty little thing
Now pass right thru and split that ring, and around just one
Do an allemande left with the corners all, and a right & left grand, go round the hall
Shuffle along 'til you meet that maid, take her by the hand and promenade
Promenade and don't slow down, now two and four pivot around
Do a right & left thru with the couple you've found
And the ladies chain like you always do, then forward up and pass right thru
And turn to the right go single file, with the gent in the lead, Indian style
The gent's in front of his partner
Now the gents step out, go the other way, meet your partner with a left all around
And on to the right and promenade, and don't slow down
Now one and three pivot around, do a right & left thru with the couple you've found
Now down the line you chain that gal, and promenade the old corral
Promenade, all the way around, with the right foot up and the left foot down
When you get home swing around and around, it's any old way but upside down

CLOSER

Walk all around your left hand lady, see saw your pretty little taw
Give a left to the corner like allemande thar, back 'em up, boys, in a right hand star
Now shoot that star with a once and a half, and the ladies star with a pretty little laugh
To the opposite man for an allemande thar, girls back up but not too far
And you back up, girls, in a pretty little star
Shoot that star to your corner, pal, with a right go full around that gal
It's all the way round to a left allemande, partners by the right and a right & left grand
It's hand over hand around that ring
And when you meet your lady fair, promenade around that square
I've got a girl and she's a fright, she likes to square dance every night
When we get home she takes three swings, it makes me do the darnedest things!
Now bow to your partners, corners all; and keeno, boys, that's it...that's all!
