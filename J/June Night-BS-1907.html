June Night    (BS 1907)   Dave Taylor

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and circle left now, go dancing round the ring
Left allemande and weave the ring
Weave in and out until you meet, now box the gnat
And all 4 ladies promenade go one time
When you're home swing, swing that girl
Now promenade
Just give me a June night, some moonlight, and you

FIGURE



Heads you square thru 4 hands around
To the outside 2 do the right & left thru
Now dive thru, pass thru, star thru and then
You do the right & left thru and turn that girl
Square thru count 4 hands around, walk by your own
Swing the next girl, promenade
Just give me a June night, some moonlight, and you
