FIGURE

Heads will promenade halfway round the ring
Walk into the middle and square thru , four hands round I sing
Go all the way around, a right & left thru and turn your girl
Pass thru , trade by , touch 1/4 then
Well, scoot back and then, scoot back again
Swing with the corner girl, promenade the ring
Oh, jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
