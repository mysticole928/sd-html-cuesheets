Bow down to your partner to the Jessie Polka Square

Jessie Polka step: footwork same for each person. Left heel forward, back to place. Right toe back, touch right toe to left heel, (keep body weight on left) right heel forward and back to place, (change body weight to right foot) left heel forward, cross left foot over right and then do 4 forward two steps.
