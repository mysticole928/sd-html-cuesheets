Just A Stomp At Twilight    (GR 12070)   Johnny Davis

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 little ladies chain across, turn that girl and then
Roll away, circle to the left go walking round the land
Then allemande left and allemande thar, go right & left
Make an allemande thar, men back up go walking round the set
Slip the clutch, left allemande, grand ol' right & left
Meet your own and promenade the set
We'll have a stomp at twilight when the lights are low

FIGURE



Head 2 couples square thru 4 hands round you go
Face the sides and do a little dosado
Swing thru go two by two, then you spin the top
Join hands when you get through, it's up and back you rock
Cross trail thru, left allemande, come back one and swing
Swing that girl and promenade the ring
We'll have a stomp at twilight when the lights are low
