Jolly Is The Miller
   (Old Timer 8089)    Bill Castner
 
FORMATION

Partner both hands joined with partner and facing forward counter-clockwise
in a large circle. Three or four extra men or more in the center of the circle
without partners ready to enter the circle on second verse.

CALL

All sing and walk around the ring
Jolly is the miller who lives on the hill
His mill turns around of its own free will
A hand in the hopper and the other in the sack
The girls keep going but the boys turn back
The boys turn back all around that ring
Walk right past each sweet young thing
Keep on going and you'll sure be glad
When I tell you fellows to "grab, boys, grab!"
Two step all around the ring
Take your little lady and we'll all promenade
