Janie    (LO 1157)   Johnny Creel

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and make a ring, circle to the left around I sing
Allemande left, come back, do a dosado
Let the men star right in the middle of the town
Turn that star go once around, left allemande now weave that ring
You weave it in and out around until you meet that maid
Dosado and then take her by the hand and promenade
Promenade go two by two, take that lady go home with you
Oh, normally my Janie loves me

FIGURE



Head two you square thru 4 hands around you do
Get to the corner, do a dosado
Do a curlique, walk & dodge, do a partner trade
Right & left thru, turn that blue-eyed babe
Now flutter wheel go full around, slide thru and swing
Swing that corner girl, promenade go round the world
Promenade go two by two, take that lady go home you do
Oh, normally my Janie loves me
