Charming Little Widow / Jelly Bean
Pete Wendling 1920
   (MacGregor 7535 side B )   
FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

You docey round your corner , see saw your partner
The head two couples center, box the gnat
Pass thru , separate , go around just one
Star by the left out in the middle, once around that ring you run
Pick up your corner, take her along
The inside out, the outside in
The inside ladies roll away a half sashay
All 4 ladies backtrack , go round the other way
Turn 'em with a left around, you'll promenade the town
Who'd you get gals?
Why old Jellybean!
So swing old Jellybean

CLOSER   

All join hands and circle left , you circle the ring
Swing on the corner , swing and you swing
Allemande left your corner, allemande right your own
Go back and swing that corner lady just as though she were your own
Join your hands and circle , you circle the ring
Reverse back , go single file
Gents reach back with the old right hand , you box the gnat
Star by the right an eight hand star , then right after that
The gals reach back for a left hand swing , all promenade that ring
Who'd you get gals?
Why old Jellybean!
So swing old Jellybean
