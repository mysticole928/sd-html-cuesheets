(SEQUENCE: Figure, figure, middle break, figure, figure)

Heads forward, sides to the right

(Note: From head position, couples do 2 polka steps forward to center of square
From side position, couples always go directly to their right
Couples remain together through entire sequence
All couples move simultaneously from every position.)

(For teaching figure: From head position, couples do two polka steps forward to center of square; in four walking steps they will turn and back out to side position on right; from side position, couples do two polka steps directly to their right; in four walking steps they will walk to nearest head position to face center.)
