Johnny Oh Polka Square    (TOP 25214)   Dick Leger

FIGURE



Heads forward, sides to the right
Polka, polka, walk, 2, 3, 4, new heads forward
Polka, polka, walk, 2, 3, 4, new heads forward
Polka, polka, walk, 2, 3, 4, new heads forward
Polka, polka, walk, 2, 3, 4, ladies chain
Oh Johnny, oh Johnny you turn and chain them home
Oh Johnny, oh Johnny your corner promenade
You're not handsome, it's true, but when I look at you
I just oh Johnny, oh Johnny, oh

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face, grand square
Oh Johnny, oh Johnny, how you can love
Oh Johnny, oh Johnny, heavens above
Reverse
You make my sad heart jump with joy
And when you're near the ladies have to chain across the ring
Oh Johnny, oh Johnny please chain them back
What makes me love you so?
Promenade
You're not handsome, it's true, but when I look at you
I just oh Johnny, oh Johnny, oh

(Note: From head position, couples do 2 polka steps forward to center of square



Couples remain together through entire sequence
All couples move simultaneously from every position.)
