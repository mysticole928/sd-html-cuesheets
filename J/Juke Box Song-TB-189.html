Juke Box Song    (TB 189)   Bob Bennett

Four ladies chain now, 3/4 round you know



Gents center, girls sashay, circle left again
Allemande left your corner, then weave round the ring
*Put a quarter in the jukebox and play me that ole song
Dosado and then, go strolling right along
That jukebox is playing that ole familiar song
Like a fool I can't forget it, I gotta sing along

FIGURE



Head (side) couples square thru four hands round I say
Dosado your corner, then stop to an ocean wave
Girls trade, girls run, tag the line, face right
Now tag the line once again, face right my friend
Wheel & deal, turn thru, and go left allemande
Then weave by two and promenade the next around the land
That jukebox is playing that ole familiar song
Like a fool I can't forget it, I gotta sing along

FIGURE

Swing thru go 2 by 2, boys run right

Wheel and deal, and turn thru and go left allemande
