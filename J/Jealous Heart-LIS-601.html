Jealous Heart    (LIS 601)   Tod Williams

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Left allemande and promenade your partner
Promenade and don't you dare slow down
Heads wheel around and do a right & left thru
Turn 'em around and do a cross trail thru
Left allemande and dosado your partner
Gents star left, go once around the town
Star promenade and take her home and swing her
Swing her 'til she has no jealous heart

FIGURE



Heads pass thru, then separate, go round one
Then cross trail thru and get on back to home
Now swing your own and face the middle and square thru
Go all the way around and swing your corner girl
Left allemande and weave the ring now
When you meet you dosado this maid
Now promenade and take her home and swing her
Swing her 'til she has no jealous heart

ALTERNATE CLOSER



2 & 4 go right & left thru, turn 'em
Heads go right and circle to a line
Forward eight and back and then you pass thru
The dixie twirl and do a cross trail thru
Left allemande and dosado your partner
Gents star left, go once around the town
Star promenade and take her home and swing her
Swing her 'til she has no jealous heart
