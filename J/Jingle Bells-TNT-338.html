Jingle Bells
Edison Male Quartet (1898) 1857
(TNT 338) Don Coy
OPENER

Circle left
Dashing through the snow in a one horse open sleigh
O'er the fields we go laughing all the way
Men star by the right one time around
Allemande left the corner and weave around the ring
Singing jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Swing the ladies around and promenade I say
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride in a one horse open sleigh

FIGURE

Heads promenade halfway around the ring
Sides a right & left thru turn the girl and then
Sides square thru , 4 hands around the floor
Dosado the corner and do an eight chain 4 and sing
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Swing the corner lady and promenade I say
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride in a one horse open sleigh

MIDDLE BREAK

Circle left
A day or two ago I thought I'd take a ride
And soon Miss Fanny Brice was seated by my side
Men star by the right one time around
Allemande left the corner and weave around the ring
Singing jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Swing the girls around and promenade I say
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride in a one horse open sleigh

CLOSER

Circle left
Take the girls tonight, take them while you're young
Take them for a ride and sing a sleighing song
Men star by the right one time around
Allemande left and weave around the ring
Sing, jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Swing the girls around and promenade I say
Sing jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride
