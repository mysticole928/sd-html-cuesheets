Jingle Bells
Edison Male Quartet (1898) 1857
(Red Boot 1) Don Williamson
OPENER - CLOSER

All 4 ladies chain go straight across that ring
Roll away with a half sashay and circle left and then
All 4 ladies roll away and circle left again
Allemande left the corner and you weave the ring and sing
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Swing your partner once around and promenade and sing
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride in a one horse open sleigh

FIGURE

Head two couples promenade go halfway round you go
Walk right in and square thru 4 hands around you know
Go all the way round, dosado the outside two
Go once around and swing thru and then the boys you run
Couples circulate and then chain down the line
Keep that girl and promenade around the ring so fine
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride in a one horse open sleigh

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face, grand square
Dashing through the snow in a one horse open sleigh
O'er the fields we go, laughing all the way
Bells on bob tails ring, making spirits bright
What fun it is to ride and sing a sleighing song tonight
Four boys promenade go once around and then
Swing your partner round and round and promenade and sing
Jingle bells, jingle bells, jingle all the way
Oh what fun it is to ride in a one horse open sleigh
