Jealous Heart
(Top 25236) Harry Tucciarone, Jr.
BREAK

4 little ladies chain , go straight across the hall
Join your hands , circle left around you go
All 8 to the middle do the rainbow stroll
Go walking in...come out
8 to the middle again...and come back out
Allemande left your corner, grand right & left around
From this girl you'll never want to part
Promenade and you tell her of no other
Cause you know she has a jealous heart

FIGURE

Head 2 couples promenade about 3/4 round
While the sides square thru 3/4 too
Join your hands, circle left, side gents break and make a line
Wiggle up to the middle and do a right & left thru , turn your girl
Now star thru , then pass thru , and swing the corner girl
Promenade her all the way around
You promenade and you whisper oh so softly
Cause you know she has a jealous heart

(Rainbow stroll: while in a circle, all 8 go into the center,
do a u-turn back, come out with a new girl on your right.
Do a California twirl, then repeat once more.)
