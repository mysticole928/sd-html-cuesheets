OPENER

Circle left
Last night I lay a sleeping, there came a dream so fair
I stood in old Jerusalem, beside the temple there
Walk around your corner , see saw your own you fly
Left allemande your corner, and weave with hands up high
Jerusalem, Jerusalem!
Swing you girl, promenade
Hosanna in the highest! Hosanna to your king

FIGURE

Heads you promenade go half around that ring
Sides , go right & left thru and hear the children sing
Sides square thru around the ring count about 4
Dosado your corner, and do an eight chain 4
Jerusalem, Jerusalem!
Swing you girl, promenade
Hosanna in the highest! Hosanna to your king
