Johnny B Goode    (RBS 1340)   Kevin Lowe

OPENER - CLOSER



Sides face, grand square
Down in Louisiana next to New Orleans
Back up in the woods among the evergreens
Lives a little guitar picker
His name is Johnny B. Goode
Circle left
Go Johnny go, go Johnny go
Left allemande, come back & promenade her and go
Go, go, go Johnny B. Goode

FIGURE



Heads/sides promenade and get about 1/2 way round you go
Walk in and square thru it's 4 hands you know
Come on it's 4 hands and then do the right & left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel one time you do
You square thru and go 3/4 around
Swing and whirl the corner, promenade the town
Go, go, go Johnny B. Goode

MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies promenade to the guitar sound
Get on home & swing that man around and round
Well, join hands, circle to the left go round
Left allemande the corner, weave around the town
He carries his guitar in a gunny sack
Swing the lady round and promenade her on back
Go, go, go Johnny B. Goode

TAG



Swing her with enthusiasm boys
Go, go, go Johnny B. Goode
