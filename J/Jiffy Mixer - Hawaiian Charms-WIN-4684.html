Jiffy Mixer / Hawaiian Charms
(Windsor 4684)
FORMATION:

Double circle, partners facing, man's back to COH, in butterfly position
FOOTWORK: Opposite throughout dance, steps descibed are for the man.
INTRO: Wait 8 counts

1 - 8
HEEL AND TOE, HEEL AND TOE, SIDE, CLOSE, SIDE, TOUCH
In butterfly position (partner facing, both hands joined, out to sides, shoulder high),
touch left heel out to left side then left toe beside right foot.
Repeat.
Move in LOD by stepping to left on left foot, close right to left,
Step left to side again and touch right beside left, keeping weight on left foot.

9 - 16
HEEL AND TOE, HEEL AND TOE, SIDE, CLOSE, SIDE, TOUCH
Repeat all of counts 1 - 8 with opposite footwork moving in opposite direction. (RLOD)

17 - 24
CHUG BACK, CLAP, CHUG BACK, CLAP, CHUG, CLAP, CHUG, CLAP
Drop hands, partners "chug" away from each other with weight on both feet (4 times),
Man backing in towards COH, lady backing away from COH.
Both "chug" on the down-beat and clap hands on the up-beat of the music.

25 - 32
GO RIGHT, -,2-,3-,4,-,- -
Start on left foot and progress, diagonally right to a new partner with 4 slow,
swaggering steps, ending in butterfly position to begin dance again
