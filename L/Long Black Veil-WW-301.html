Long Black Veil   (Wagon Wheel 301 )   Beryl Main
INTRO

Sometimes at night, when the cold winds moan
In a long black veil, she cries o'er his bones

FIGURE



Repeat 4 times

Head (side) ladies chain, go across that ring
Join hands, circle left, go walking round the ring
Allemande left, come back and dosado
Promenade, keep walking, round the ring you go
The heads wheel around, do a right & left thru
Make a right hand star, one time around will do
Heads star left in the middle & turn it one time around
To that same old two, dosado and then you swing
You swing this girl, and then left allemande
Come back and dosado, then promenade the land
She walks these hills, in a long black veil
Where the night winds moan and wail

ALTERNATE CLOSER

Head (side) ladies chain, go across that ring
Join hands, circle left, go walking round the ring
Allemande left, come back and dosado
Promenade, keep walking, round the ring you go
The heads wheel around, do a right & left thru
Make a right hand star, one time around will do
Heads star left in the middle & turn it one time around
To that same old two, dosado and then you swing
You swing this girl, and then left allemande
Come back and allemande left again
Promenade this girl, in a long black veil
Where the night winds moan and wail

ALTERNATE VERSE

Nobody knows, nobody sees, nobody knows, but me
