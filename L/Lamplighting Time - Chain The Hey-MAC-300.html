Lamplighting Time / Chain The Hey
   (MacGregor 300)   
INTRO: Men face left, ladies face right
1-8 Corner dosado (The one you are facing.)
8-16 Same one swing (End swing facing across the set with this lady on man's right.)
16-24 Men center star by left 1 1/2 around
25-32 Partner swing (Swing your original partner on her side of set. End your swing with her on your right facing across. Men are on the opposite side of set.)
32-40 Half promenade with your partner
41-48 Ladies lead half a hey (Ladies will end facing each other from the opposite side of the set. Their partner is behind them.)
56-64 Ladies chain across (Courtesy turn and everyone is on the side they started on and have progressed one place.)
Repeat the dance (At the top of the set box the gnat with your partner every 2nd time through the dance and whenever two ladies are together at the foot of set.)
