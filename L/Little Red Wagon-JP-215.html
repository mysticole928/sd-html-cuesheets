Won't you ride in my little red wagon

FIGURE

1 & 3 (2 & 4) promenade you go halfway
Come down the middle and do the right & left thru
Flutter wheel full around, sweep 1/4 more
Pass thru and do a right & left thru
Square thru 3/4 round and you go left allemande
Come back swing that girl and promenade
Won't you ride in my little red wagon
For you are my sweetheart to be
