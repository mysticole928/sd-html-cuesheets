Loose Talk
(Chaparral 719) Marshall Flippo
OPENER

Circle left
When I go out walking there's lots of loose talking
They say we're unhappy and we'll break apart
Do an allemande left that corner turn the partner by the right
Left allemande and weave the ring
Oh we may have to leave here to find peace of mind dear
Swing the lady round, promenade the lady home
I know you love me and happy we could be
If some folks would leave us alone

FIGURE

Heads promenade halfway round that ring you travel
Down the middle pass the ocean , extend
Swing thru and now boys run to the right
Bend the line and do the right and left thru
Lady lead do a dixie style to a wave
Boys cast right 3/4 , girls face in
Boys extend , swing and promenade along
I know you love me and happy we could be
If some folks would leave us alone

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Four ladies chain 3/4 round that ring
Join your hands circle to the left around you go
You roll away circle to the left that way
Left allemande and weave the ring
Oh we may have to leave here to find peace of mind dear
Someplace where we can live a life of our own
I know you love me and happy we could be
If some folks would leave us alone

TAG

If loose talk would leave us alone

SEQUENCE

(Opener, Figure heads twice, Middle Break, Figure sides twice, Ending, Tag)
