Longfellow Serenade    (RYL 1616)  Hunter Keller

OPENER



Grand Square
Longfellow serenade, such were the plans I'd made
For she was a lady, And I was a dreamer, with only words to trade,
Allemande and Weave
I was born for a night like this, warmed by a stolen kiss
For I was lonely, and she was lonely

FIGURE



Heads (Sides) Square Thru, 4 hand round you do
Make a Right Hand Star, Once I Say
The Heads (Sides) Star Left, A Full Turn Round
A Right and Left Thru
Do a Half Sashay
Swing that corner, Promenade
Let me make it warm for you

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Grand Square
I'll weave this web of rhyme Upon the summer night
We'll leave this worldly time on his winged flight
Allemande and Weave
Then come and as we lay Beside this sleepy glade
There I will sing to you My, Longfellow serenade
