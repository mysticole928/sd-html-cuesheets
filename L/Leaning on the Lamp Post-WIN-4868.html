Rollaway and a grand old right and left
Hand in hand around you go
Meet your girl and dosado

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across & couples 1 & 3 (2 & 4)
Promenade outside 1/2 round the ring
Right & left thru, rollaway, star thru
Do a dosado, it's once around and then
