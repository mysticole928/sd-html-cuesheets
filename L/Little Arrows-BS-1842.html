Little Arrows    (BS 1842)   Al Brundage

INTRO



Four ladies promenade, go once around the square
Star thru and promenade, go two by two from there
1 & 3 wheel around, right & left thru
Cross trail thru, left allemande, weave the ring
Falling in love again, falling in love again
Dosado around your honey, left allemande will do
Promenade and watch arrows come pouring from the blue

FIGURE



Heads promenade 3/4 round you go
Sides do a right & left thru and turn the girl you know
Pass thru, and do a dosado and then
Swing thru, boys trade, swing thru
Slide thru, pass thru, go left allemande
Skip one girl, swing the next, she's the one so fair
Promenade and watch the arrows come flying through the air
