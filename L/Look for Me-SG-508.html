Look For Me    (SG 508)   Bob Asp

OPENER



Circle left
Through this world of pain
I have traveled night and day
Left allemande your corner
Turn your partner by the right
Left allemande, weave the ring
Oh! look for me, I'll be there
Swing your lady around and promenade
When you reach the gates of heaven
Look for me, I'll be there

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru and you get four hands and go
Around that corner do a dosado
Swing thru and then boy run to the right
Bend the line and do a right & left thru
Flutterwheel across and when you're done slide thru
You swing on the corner and promenade her home
When you reach the gates of heaven
Look for me, I'll be there

CLOSER



Four boys promenade get a walkin' round that ring I sing
Get on back and swing the girl you know
Join up hands circle left get a walkin' round the ring
Allemande left with the corner and you weave that ring
Oh look for me, for I'll be there
You swing and whirl the pretty little girl and you all promenade
When you reach the gates of heaven
Look for me, I'll be there

TAG



Swing her once
Look for me, I'll be there
