Let It Be Me    (SR 501)   Richard DePelsmeaker

OPENER



Sides face grand square
God bless the day I found you
I want to stay around you
Now and forever
Let it be me
Circle left
If you must cling to someone
Left allemande and then you promenade her
And so I beg you, let it be me

FIGURE



Heads promenade half way and go
You touch 1/4, walk & dodge you know
Swing thru, the boys run right
Bend the line, go right & left thru
Slide thru, square thru 3 hands
Swing that girls and then you promenade her
Now and forever, let it be me

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain the ring go
Roll away and then you circle left go
Four ladies roll away, circle and then
Left allemande and you weave
Don't take this heaven from one
Swing that girl and then you promenade her
And so I beg you, let it be me
