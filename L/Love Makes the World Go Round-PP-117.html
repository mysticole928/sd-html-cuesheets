Love Makes The World Go Round    (PP 117)   Toots Richardson

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain 3/4 around
Join hands and circle left you go
4 ladies roll away and circle left and then
Allemande left and weave the ring
High in the starry sky
Dosado and promenade
Making the earth turn softly
Love makes the world go round

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round and then
Walk in and square thru 4
4 hands and then, swing thru the outside 2
Boys run right and ferris wheel you go
Centers square thru 3/4 you know
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Making the earth turn softly
Love makes the world go round
