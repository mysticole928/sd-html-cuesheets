Lonesome Me    (TOP 25268)   Ben Baldwin, Jr.

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



Left allemande the corner, go right & left I say
Turn back one and box the gnat, dosado that way
4 ladies promenade inside, once around and then
Turn partner by the right, all the way to the corner, friend
Left allemande the corner and you weave the ring you know
Weave in and out, when you meet do a little dosado
Back-to-back and then left allemande that corner she
Come back and promenade, oh lonesome me

FIGURE



When you get home the heads swing, sides go right & left thru
The heads promenade about halfway
1 & 3 lead to the right and circle to a line
That line of 4 forward up and back, bend the line
Go right & left thru, turn on back and do a little dixie chain
Ladies turn left and walk, gents turn right
Allemande left that gal
Pass your own, promenade the next you see
Oh lonesome me

CLOSER



Swing your partner round and round, men make a left hand star
Go once around til you meet that lady, turn her by the right where you are
Left allemande the corner, turn partner right you see
Girls move in to a left hand star, once around for me
When you meet that man, dosado, go all the way and then
Left allemande and you come back one, promenade my friend
You've got your own, but me, I'm fancy free
Oh lonesome me
