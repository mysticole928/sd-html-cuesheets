Listen To The Music    (RIV 229)   Jerry Jestin

OPENER



Four ladies promenade one time
Come back and swing with your man
Join hands, circle left
Left allemande and weave the ring
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
Oh-Oh-Oh, swing the girl and promenade
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
All the time

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru four,
Right & left thru, veer left
Boys circulate, girls trade
Bend the line, right & left thru,
Square thru four
Swing the corner, promenade
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
All the time

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face grand square
If I'm feeling good to you and you're feelin' good to me
There ain't nothing we can do or say
Feeling good, feeling fine
Oh baby let the music play
Circle left
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
Oh-Oh-Oh, left allemande and promenade
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
All the time

CLOSER



Circle left
Like a lazy flowing river surrounding castles in the air
Men star right one time,
Left allemande and weave the ring
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
Oh-Oh-Oh, swing the girl and promenade
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music all the time

TAG



Sides face grand square
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music
Oh-Oh-Oh listen to the music all the time
