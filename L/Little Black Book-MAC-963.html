Little Black Book    (MAC 963)   Don Stewart

OPENER



Dosado round your corner girl, get back home and swing
Swing your lady round and round, then promenade the ring
Head 2 couples wheel around and star thru and then
Square thru 3/4, your corner allemande
Come back one, go right & left grand, walking hand in hand
Keep on going until you meet your girl and promenade the land
Promenade right back home and swing that lady round
With a little bit of luck and a little black book
I'll swing another when the sun goes down

FIGURE



Join your hands and make a ring, circle to the left and then
Ladies roll a 1/2 sashay, circle to the left again
Head 2 gents and a brand new girl dance up to the middle and back
Square thru now 4 hands around, 4 hands in the middle of the track
Star thru, go right & left thru, turn the girl my friend
Cross trail and swing the corner round and round and then
Promenade with the pretty little girl, you walk that lady round
With a little bit of luck and a little black book
I'll swing another when the sun goes down
