Lida Rose    (SIO 141)   Lee Helsel

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round
All four ladies chain
Sides promenade halfway round
Four ladies chain again
Join hands and circle
Go walkin' round the square
Reverse Single file
Girls backtrack from there, twice around now
When you meet turn your partner by the right
Gents star left one time
Same girl dosado, corner star thru
Circle left in time
Allemande left, weave the ring
Promenade 'em so fine
Lida Rose won't you be mine
