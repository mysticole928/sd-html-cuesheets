Light the Legend    (CHIC 1063)  Justin Russell

OPENER



Circle Left
Candles flashing in the night, tapers gleaming diamond bright,
Golden arabesque of light, Chanukah,
Four boys Star by the right one time around the ring,
Allemande Left your corner, Weave the Ring
Shining beacons pierce the dark, beaming joy in every spark
Bright reflections on the ark, Chanukah,
Shadows dancing on the wall, waxen sentries ten feet tall,
Light the legend for us all, Chanukah.

FIGURE



Head couples (side) Promenade halfway around the ring,
Down the middle and Square Thru four hands around now.
Go all the way and then Swing Thru the outside two
Boy run around the girl, Ferris Wheel
Centers Square Thru, three quarter round the ring
Swing your corner, Promenade her home
Shadows dancing on the wall, waxen sentries ten feet tall,
Light the legend for us all, Chanukah.

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Grand Square
Light the candles, say the blessing
Tell the age-old story
Maccabeans went to battle
Now we praise their ALLEMANDE Left & WEAVE
Shining beacons pierce the dark, beaming joy in every spark
Bright reflections on the ark, Chanukah,
Shadows dancing on the wall, waxen sentries ten feet tall,
Light the legend for us all, Chanukah.
