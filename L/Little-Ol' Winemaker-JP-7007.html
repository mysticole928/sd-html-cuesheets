Little-Ol' Winemaker

OPENER    B - SSD Week 1

Circle left
We're praying for rain in California
So the grapes can grow and they can make more wine
Left allemande , dosado
Left allemande and weave
Who's that laughing in the corner
Swing that girl and promenade
Who's that dancing in the corner
That's little ol' wine maker me

FIGURE    A1, corner progression

Heads touch 1/4 , walk & dodge
Touch 1/4 , split circulate once and a half
Girls swing thru , girls cast off 3/4
Boys reach in and courtesy turn her
Slide thru , square thru on the third hand
Swing and promenade
Who's that laughing in the corner
That's little ol' wine maker me

Shoot the star, forward two make another thar
Boys wheel in, back down the line
Shoot that star , a right & left grand
Swing a little girl and promenade
Who's that laughing in the corner
That's little ol' wine maker me
