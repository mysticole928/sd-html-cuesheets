Look On The Bright Side
(Windsor 4904) Bob Van Antwerp
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 ladies chain right on over
Join hands , circle left I sing
Allemande left , go forward 2 , a right & left and then
Turn back, your corner turn thru
Go left and right and turn back , your corner allemande
Come back and then you promenade
Promenade the ring, get on home and sing
Look on the bright side of life

FIGURE

1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around
Get to the corner, do a dosado ( step to a wave )
Everybody circulate and then star thru
Square thru 4 hands around
Give a right to your partner, pull by and go left allemande
Pass by the next , you promenade that land
Promenade the ring, and everybody sing
Look on the bright side of life

Contains a "blended" movement: Circulate followed by Star Thru.
