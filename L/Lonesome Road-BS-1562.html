Lonesome Road    (BS 1562)   Andy Andrus

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Dosado round that corner girl, come back home and swing and whirl
Those gents star left on down the road
Turn a right hand round your taw, allemande your corners all
Come back one, promenade around that hall
1 & 3 wheel around, pass thru and face your own
Square thru 3/4, 3/4 round you roam
Allemande left that corner maid, come back one and promenade
Way down that old lonesome road

FIGURE



Head 2 couples square thru 4 hands around you do
You find your corner there and swing
Face that 2 go right & left thru, turn your girl and pass thru
Promenade to the left 2 by 2
Back right out, join hands, circle left around that ring
Allemande left your corner, come back one and you swing
Promenade around that town, get back home and settle down
Way down that old lonesome road
