Let Me Call You Sweetheart    (SC 564)   Dick Waibel

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain, turn your girl and then
Join hands circle left and go
Allemande the corner, go forward 3 in time
Third girl turn thru and go left allemande
Come on back a dosado and to your corner go
Allemande the corner, promenade
Let me call you sweetheart
I'm in love with you

FIGURE



1 & 3 flutter wheel turn it around and then
Sweep 1/4, pass thru, swing the corner girl
Join hands, circle left move it round in time
Corner girl left allemande, weave the ring
In and out around until you meet your girl
Dosado, promenade
Let me call you sweetheart
I'm in love with you
