Bow to your partner , ladies chain across the ring
Join hands and circle to the left and once again
4 ladies grand chain , just to get across the set

And while the bells keep ringing allemande the corner s all
Grand ol' right & left as you go walkin' round the hall

Star by the right and turn it once around the ring
Turn partners by the left , go to the corner, box the gnat
Gents star left and swing the girl across the track
And let the bells ring out, while you swing and dance with joy
Allemande left the corner, promenade each girl and boy
Go walkin' round the ring, then swing 'neath stars above
While the bells ring out with happy love
