Light The Candels    (RIV 268)  Ken Bower/Gary Shoemake

OPENER



Grand Square
Light the candle, around the world
Create a light, we’ll never see
Till the nations of the world
Shake each others’ hands in peace
Four Ladies Promenade one time
Swing your girl around and Promenade
And make the night as bright as day
And light the candles around the world

FIGURE



Heads Square Thru count 'em 4 hands round
You make a Right Hand Star
The Heads Star Left, roll it one time and then
Same pair and do a Dosado
Swing Thru and then the Boys Trade
Swing the corner lady round and Promenade
Make the night as bright as day
And light the candles around the world

MIDDLE BREAK



Grand Square
Light the candles around the world
Pray the light will never see
Till the nations of the world
Take each others’ hands in peace
Four Ladies Promenade one time
Swing your girl around and Promenade
And make the night as bright as day
And light the candles around the world

CLOSER



Grand Square
Take each others’ hands in peace
For every boy and every girl
And make the night as bright as day
And light the candles around the world.
Four Ladies Promenade one time
Swing your girl around and Promenade
And make the night as bright as day
And light the candles around the world
