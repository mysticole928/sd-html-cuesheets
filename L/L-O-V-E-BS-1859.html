L-O-V-E    (BS 1859)   Bill Saunders

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Circle left
L means circle left, travel 'round the town
O means only that you keep on movin' round
V means very soon you'll go left allemande
E is even more you'll bow, and weave the ring around the floor
Love is all that I can give to you
Dosado and promenade her, too
Two in love can make it, take my heart and please don't break it
Love was meant for me and you

FIGURE



Heads you promenade halfway around you do
Sides go right & left thru and turn, then square thru
Oh, it's very, very extraordinary
Eight chain 4 across the floor, find the corner you adore
Swing with number 5, left allemande I say
Come back one and promenade 'em all they way
Two in love can make it, take my heart and please don't break it
Love was meant for me and you
