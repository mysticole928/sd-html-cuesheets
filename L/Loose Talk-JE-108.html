Loose Talk    (J 108)   Jewel O'Brien

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



You bow, swing your partner, allemande your corner
Come on back, promenade go walking around the ring
Those head two wheel around, go right & left thru the 2 you found
Pass thru and then you wheel across
Star thru, then pass thru, allemande left you do
Grand ole right & left, around the ring you go
You can promenade her then you can swing her
Then some folks will leave you alone

FIGURE



Head two ladies chain across, turn the girls and don't get lost
Lead to the right, circle 4 make the line for me
Pass thru, cast off 3/4 around, don't get lost
Pass thru, then you wheel & deal
A double pass thru you do, face your partner, star thru
Center 4 square thru 3/4 around and then
Swing the corner lady round, promenade go around the town
Then some folks will leave you alone
