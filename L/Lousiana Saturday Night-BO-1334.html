Louisiana Saturday Night    (BO 1334)   Hubert Kerr

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



4 ladies chain straight across you go
Roll away and then circle left you know
4 ladies roll away and circle left I sing
Left allemande that corner, weave the ring
Get down the fiddle and get down the bow
Dosado and then you promenade the floor
Dance in the kitchen till the morning light
Louisiana Saturday night

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round you go
Down the middle now then square thru you know
4 hands and then do the right & left thru
Veer left and ferris wheel you do
Centers square thru 3/4 round I sing
Swing that corner lady, then promenade the ring
Dance in the kitchen till the morning light
Louisiana Saturday night
