Little Bit of Soul   ESP 229   Tim Marriner

OPENER/MIDDLE
Sides Face Grand Square

Now when you’re feeling low and you’re broke in two,

You need a little bit of soul to see you through.

Four Ladies Promenade inside

Come back Swing your Partner once around I say

Allemande Left come back and Promenade

And then a little bit of soul will come your way.


*Now when you’re feelin’ blue and you’re money’s tight,

*You need a little bit of soul to put you right.


FIGURES
Four Couples Promenade Half Way round I sing

Heads (Sides) Flutter Wheel in the middle of the ring,

Sweep ¼ more, and Pass Thru, then Dosado,

Do an 8 Chain 4

And when you mix in rock and blues of today,

Swing your corner all Promenade,

And then a little bit of soul will come your way.

CLOSER

Sides Face Grand Square

**Now when you’re in a mess and you feel like cryin’,

**Just remember this little song of mine.
Allemande Left and Weave

And as you go through life tryin’ to reach your goal,

Just remember what I said about a little bit of soul.
