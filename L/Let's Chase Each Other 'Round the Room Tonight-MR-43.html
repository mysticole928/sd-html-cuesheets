Let's Chase Each Other 'Round The Room Tonight    (MR 43)   Kim Hohnholt

OPENER



sides face grand square
let's chase each other around the room tonite
and play the kind of games we played on our wedding night
circle left
to lock & bolt the door is only right
swing and promenade
let's chase each other around the room tonite

FIGURE



heads square thru 4 hands around
dosado and make a wave
spin chain thru, girls circulate 2 times, turn thru
left allemande, walk by one
swing the next girl around, promenade
and let's chase each other around the room tonite

MIDDLE BREAK



sides face grand square
seems like lately people like to play with fire
and the other games they play are just as bad
circle left
I'd rather stay at home & feel your burning lips
allemande left & promenade
and play the kind of games that make me glad

CLOSER



sides face grand square
let's don't chase around & make each other weary
let's keep all our love at home & out of sight
circle left
let's leave everything like jealousy behind us
allemande left and promenade
and let's chase each other around the room tonite
