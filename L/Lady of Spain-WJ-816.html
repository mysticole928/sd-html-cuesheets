Lady Of Spain    (WJ 816)   Ed Gilmore

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands and circle around the rancho
Dopaso, partner left and corner right you know
Partner left and swing your corner, swing her high and low
Now put her on the right and form a ring, circle left you go
Allemande your corner and you pass the one you swung
Swing the next lady, say she's your only one
A left hand round your corner, come back one and promenade
Promenade your lady of Spain

FIGURE



Head two couples swing while the side two couples arch
Heads go right, the sides go left, dip and dive you march
Now you're high and now you're low, right back home you go
Head ladies chain to the right and turn 'em, don't be slow
All 4 ladies chain across the rancho
Turn and chain 'em back to poor old Pancho
Now promenade a brand new senorita
Promenade that lady of Spain
