Long Long Ago
   (Swinging Square 2311)    Les DeWitt
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER   

Swing as you did in the long, long ago
Girls make a ring now, and circle left you know
Dosado your partner, men star right around
Once around the land, corners left allemande
Grand right and left as you did long ago
Meet your little lady dosado then promenade
Tell her the tales that delighted her so
Long, long ago, long ago

FIGURE   

Dosado the corner and see saw your taw
Men star by the left 3/4 round the hall
*Wrap that corner up and do that old *wrap around
Men make a left hand star , and go walking round the town
Roll your lady out then roll your lady in
She'll cuddle up to you and you turn that star again
Roll that lady out and promenade her right on home
As you did long ago, long ago

(* The wrap around is done with the men taking the corners left hand in their right, and walking around the lady while she stands still.)
