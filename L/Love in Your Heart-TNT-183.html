Love In Your Heart    (TNT 183)   Jack O'Leary

OPENER - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade one time around I say
Get home and swing with your man
Join hands, circle left go walkin' round the set
Put a little love in your heart
Men star by the right one time around
Allemande left the corner, come back one and promenade
Back home with me, everybody
Put a little love in your heart

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru and go 4 hands around you know
Dosado that corner girl
Swing thru two by two, boys run right you do
Bend the line and do your right & left thru
Flutter wheel (ladies go), then slide thru
Swing the corner waitin' there and promenade her too
Back home with me
Put a little love in your heart

MIDDLE BREAK



Sides face grand square
Think of your fellow man, lend him a helping hand
Put a little love in your heart
Hear what your children say, help them to find the way
Put a little love in your heart (Everybody)
One time around, girls get home and swing
Swing your man and promenade
Go walkin' round the ring back home with me (everybody)
Put a little love in your heart
