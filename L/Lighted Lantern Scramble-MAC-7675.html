OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK

(any good break)

FIGURE

1 & 3 swing and whirl
Round and around with the pretty little girl
Now promenade inside the ring
Once around, go wing to wing
Out to the right with a right & left thru
Turn your girl as you always do
A right & left back on the same ole track
Now the two ladies chain but don't chain back
2 & 4 forward up and back to the ring
Forward again and swap and swing
Round and around with the pretty little thing
Now face the heads and split those two
Around just one as you always do
Go forward 8 and back to the land
The girls step across to a left allemande
