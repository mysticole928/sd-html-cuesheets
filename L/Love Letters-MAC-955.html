Love Letters    (MAC 955)   Fenton Jones

BREAK



The head 2 couples right & left thru, turn those girls around
Cross trail thru, hey, you travel 'round just 1
In the center, box the gnat, then do a little dosado
Pass thru, swing and whirl, it's lots of fun
Allemande left, new corner, pass right by your own
Take the next girl, promenade
A-walkin', talkin' as you roam with your honey lamb
You're walking hand in hand
Writing love letters in the sand

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right, you circle to a line
Head gents break, you go forward up and back
Pass thru, wheel & deal, double pass thru and then
Frontier whirl, heads star right around that track
When you meet your corner, swing and you whirl
Give 'er a twirl, promenade
Go walkin', talkin' round this world with this honey lamb
You're walking hand in hand
Writing love letters in the sand

SEQUENCE: (break, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides)
