Lucky, Lucky, Lucky Me
Milton Berle and Buddy Arnold; Annette Funicello; Evelyn Knight; more 1950
   (Niagara 503)    Don Duffin
OPENER    Basic

Walk around the corner girl, see saw round your pet
The men move in, star by the right , go once around the set
Partner left, a dopaso , a full turn 'round and then
Your corner right, your partner left, a full turn again
Four little ladies chain across and when you turn this maid
You chain 'em right on back again, then roll promenade
Lucky, lucky, lucky me... I'm a lucky son-of-a-gun
I work 8 hours, sleep 8 hours and have 8 hours of fun

FIGURE    B - SSD Week 6, corner progression

Head ladies chain to the right , turn this pretty girl
Head two couples star thru , then California twirl
Circle four , go half around, then 1/4 more
Drop hands, pass thru , then bend the line of four
Star thru and when you do, go right & left thru and then
Your corner swing , go ring-a-ding-ding, then promenade again
Lucky, lucky, lucky me, though I haven't got a dime
I dance and play in a carefree way and I have a heck of a time
