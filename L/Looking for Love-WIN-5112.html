Looking For Love    (WIN 5112)   Larry Jack

OPENER



Four ladies promenade once around the ring
Box the gnat at home, then you swing
Join hands circle left you know
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring and go
Looking for love in all the wrong places
Dosado, promenade you'll sing
Searching her eyes, looking for traces
Looking for love

FIGURE



Heads (sides) square thru four hands you'll go
Around the corner girl you'll dosado
Relay the deucey
Just hoping to win
Telling those sweet lies, and losing again
Swing thru then boys trade, you turn thru
Allemande new corner, then promenade home
Searching her eyes, looking for traces
Looking for love
