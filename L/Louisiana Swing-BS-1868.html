Louisiana Swing    (BS 1868)   Marshall Flippo

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Hey Joe, swing your own, round and round you're gonna go
Allemande left, a grand ole right & left
Meet your lady mighty sweet, you all turn back to the bayou beat
Grand right 8 the other way back home
Now meet that lady down the line, box the gnat you're going fine
Swing that lady, go round and round
Promenade go 2 by 2, promenade to the Big Mamou
That's where they do the Louisiana Swing

FIGURE



1 & 3 up and back, cross trail across that track
Go around just 2, 4 in line you stand
Forward 8 and 8 fall back, box the gnat across the track
All join hands and circle round the land
Allemande left the corner girl, do a dosado walk around your own
Swing the corner round and round
Promenade go 2 by 2, promenade to the Big Mamou
That's where they do the Louisiana Swing
