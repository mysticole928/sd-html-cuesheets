Love Me Honey Do    (RBS 1315)  Bob Barnes

OPENER



Four Ladies Chain go straight across the ring
You Rollaway, Circle Left go round I sing
Four Ladies roll away, Circle Left and go
Left Allemande and Weave The Ring
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey do
Swing your girl around and Promenade
Well Love Me Honey Treat Me Right, Walk along with me tonight
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey Do

FIGURE



Heads Promenade half way round you go
Down the middle with a Right and Left Thru
Flutterwheel Across then Sweep a Quarter More
Pass Thru and Do-Sa-Do, one time around
Swing Thru and then the Boys Trade
Swing the corner lady round and Promenade
Well Love Me Honey Treat Me Right, Walk along with me tonight
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey Do

MIDDLE BREAK



All 8 to the Middle and 8 Fall Back -- 4 Ladies (Men) Promenade once around inside
Get back and swing the lady, everybody swing
Join hands and Circle left around you go
Left Allemande and Weave the Ring
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey do
Do-Sa-Do and the Promenade
Love Me Honey and Treat Me Right, Squeeze My Little Hand Tonight
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey Do

CLOSER



4 Ladies (Men) Promenade once around inside
Get back and swing the lady, everybody swing
Join hands and Circle left around you go
Left Allemande and Weave the Ring
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey do
Do-Sa-Do and the Promenade
Honey and Treat Me Right, Squeeze My Little Hand Tonight
Love Me, Love Me, Love Me Honey Do
