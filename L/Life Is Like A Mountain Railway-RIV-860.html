Life is like a mountain railway
With an engineer that's brave
Left allemande and do a dosado
Allemande left and weave the ring
Watch the curves and heed the tunnels

As you roll across the trestle

You behold the Union Depot
Into which your train will glide
Allemande and weave
There you'll meet the superintendent, God the father, God the son
With a hearty join us plaudit, weary pilgrim, welcome home
