Dancing In Victoria   (Top 25270 )   Wally Cook

OPENER



Sides face, grand slide
Australia is called the land way down under
But it's far from that, yes by thunder
So, head that way some day and you will get
A wonderful happy time that you won't forget

Left allemande your corner girl and weave round the ring
In and out you go, until you meet your own
And promenade your honey home you go
To the land of the kangaroo

FIGURE



Heads up and back, now square thru
Four hands around, meet the outside two
Do sa do go full around, then swing thru
Boys run to the right, couples circulate
Wheel and deal, inside arch, then dive thru
Pass thru you know, swing your corner go
Swing your corner promenade 'em home
To the land of the kangaroo

MIDDLE BREAK



Allemande your corner gonna swing with your own
Promenade your lady, head round the ring you roam
Girls keep going, boys turn in, star by the right
Twice around, meet your own, do paso tonight
Turn your partner left, your corner right, come back home
Turn your partner left, roll promenade 'em home
Promenade your lady home you go
To the land of the kangaroo

ENDING

Sides face, grand slide
Where the surf rolls in with all of its might
Far, far away from the bright city's lights
Where the blue gum trees reach up to the sky
That's where the kookaburras laugh and fly

Left allemande your corner girl and weave round the ring
In and out you go until you meet your own
And promenade your honey home you go
To the land of the kangaroo

SEQUENCE

Opener, figure twice for heads, middle break,
figure twice for sides, ending
