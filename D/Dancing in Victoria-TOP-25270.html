OPENER

Sides face, grand slide
Australia is called the land way down under
But it's far from that, yes by thunder
So, head that way some day and you will get
A wonderful happy time that you won't forget
Left allemande your corner girl and weave round the ring
In and out you go, until you meet your own
And promenade your honey home you go

Heads up and back , now square thru

Turn your partner left , roll promenade 'em home
Promenade your lady home you go
To the land of the kangaroo
