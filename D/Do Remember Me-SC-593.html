Do Remember Me    (SC 593)   Wes Wessinger

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Walk around corner, turn partner left hand swing
All four ladies chain, straight across that ring
You turn an' chain them back, turn your own and then
Join hands, circle left around and then
Allemande corner, then weave around that ring
Now meet your own with a dosado then promenade and sing
Do da, do da, do remember me, on our special day

FIGURE



Now head two ladies chain, an' couples 1 & 3
Curlique, then walk & dodge for me
Then single circle to a wave, now swing thru
You recycle, face those two
Hey, pass thru, trade by, corner swing
Left allemande new corner, promenade and sing
Do da, do da, do remember me, on this special day

ALTERNATE MIDDLE BREAK



Walk around corner, turn partner left hand swing
Head ladies center, teacup chain (side ladies right)
Heads center turn and 1/4, sides go right
Heads center turn and 1/4, side go right
Heads center turn 3/4, sides go right an' then
Roll promenade, go walkin' around and sing
Singing, do da, do da, do remember me, on our special day
