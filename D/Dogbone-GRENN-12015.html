Dogbone
(Grenn 12015 side B ) Ron Schneider
INTRO

All 4 couples up to th emiddle and back that way
1st & 3rd a half sashay, box the gnat across the way
Crosstrail back and face the sides, star by the right go once around
Exactly once, girls turn back, left allemande

DOG'S BONE

#1 couple swing a little more, lead to the right and circle up four
Head gent greak and line up four
Forward 4 and back in time, forward again and bend the line
Those who can 1/2 square thru, those who can right & left thru
Those who can 1/2 square thru, those who can right & left thru
Those who can pass thru, California twirl all 8 of you
Inside arch, dive thru, inside arch, dive thru, stop
Inside arch, dive thru, inside arch, dive thru
Two ladies chain, turn 'em twice with all your might
Circle up eight don't take all night
Four ladies star across the land, corners all left allemande

WILD CASTING

Side two couples 1/2 square thru, criss-cross thru the outside two
Around one, line up four, forward four and back again
Pass thru, cast off 3/4 around, inside 2 right & left thru
Same 2 pass thru, criss-cross thru the outside two
Around one, line up 4, forward and back
Pass thru, cast off 3/4 around, inside 2 right & left thru
Same 2 pass thru, criss-cross thru the outside two
Around one, line up 4, forward and back
Same 2 pass thru to a left allemande

REPEAT DOG'S BONE WITH #2 COUPLE

CLOSER

Head 2 ladies chain to the right, new head ladies chain 3/4 around
Side gents turn 'em and rollaway a 1/2 sashay
Forward 6 and back with you, same six pass on thru
Turn right single file around that man
Girls turn back, left allemande
