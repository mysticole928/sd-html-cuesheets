Do What You Do Do Well    (BSR 107)   Dave Taylor

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Now the 4 little ladies promenade go once around that ring
And when you're home you swing your man, everybody swing
Join all your hands circle left around that land
Left allemande, weave the ring
Why don't you do what you do, do well, boys
Dosado and promenade
Well now, you promenade that ring, everybody let out a yell
You didn't do too well

FIGURE



Hey now those heads square thru and count about 4 hands round you go
To the outside 2 and then you do a little dosado
Swing thru and then spin the top
And when you're through lock it*, and a right & left thru
You dive thru and star thru and now cross trail
Swing the corner lady round and round and promenade
You promenade that ring, everybody let out a yell
Oh, you did real well

*Lock it: from ocean waves, centers turn 1/4, while ends move up 1/4.
