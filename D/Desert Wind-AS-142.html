Desert Wind
(A & S 142) Barry Wonson
OPENER

4 ladies promenade inside the ring
Swing your man around
Join up hands and circle
Allemande left and weave the ring
Feel the cold wind blowing (upon your gentle skin)
Swing your girl and promenade
See the bonefish clutchin'
As the tide is rushin' in

FIGURE

Heads touch 1/4 , walk & dodge
Swing thru , spin chain thru for me
Boys circulate one place
Swing thru , boys trade
Turn thru , take your corner by the left
Promenade for me
See the bonefish clutchin'
As the tide is rushin' in

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face grand square
Ride the desert wind
Ride him up on high
Above the fluffy white
In the witchtale's eye
4 ladies promenade inside
Swing your guy and promenade
See the bonefish clutchin'
As the tide is rushin' in

CLOSER

Circle left
Ride the desert wind
Ride him up on high
Men star right, one time around I cry
Allemande left your corner
Swing and promenade
See the bonefish clutchin
*As the tide is rushin' in

ALTERNATE LYRICS

* As the gather by the creek
