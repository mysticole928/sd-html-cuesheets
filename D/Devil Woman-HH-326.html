Devil Woman    (HH 326)   Bill Green

FIGURE (4 times)



4 ladies chain, go 3/4
Join hands and all circle left
Left allemande, dosado your partner
Go full around, the men star left
Star promenade with your darlin'
Men roll out and take the next
Chain 'em across the square, turn a new lady fair
Allemande left, weave the ring, oh
Devil Woman ... Devil Woman you dosado
Promenade, go all the way 'round
Don't leave me alone, I want to go home
