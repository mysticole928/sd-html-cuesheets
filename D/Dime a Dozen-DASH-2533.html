Dime A Dozen
   (Dash 2533)    Gaylord Tannat
OPENER - CLOSER    Basic

Join up hands , make a ring, Circle left 1/2 way round now
Reverse back single file around the land
Girls backtrack around the ring. Go once when you meet her
Turn partner by the right and corner left
Dosado with the girl you meet. Swing this girl, she's mighty sweet
Swing and promenade that ring
Promenade with this lady. You swing there in the hall
Oh, a dime a dozen. I'm still in love with you

FIGURE    B - SSD Week 6, corner progression

Head (side) ladies chain across the ring. Turn the girl
Head two couples circle four 1/2 way round and 1/4 more
Centers do a u-turn back and sashay . Right and left thru
Dive thru and then you circle 4
Go once around . Pass thru . Swing the girl that's facing you
Swing the corner girl and promenade
Promenade with this lady. Go walking round the ring
Oh, a dime a dozen. I'm still in love with you

MIDDLE BREAK   

4 ladies chain across the ring and you turn with this lady
4 little ladies chain 3/4 round
Head (side) two couples star thru , then a right & left thru
Turn the girl. Pass thru . Star thru
Allemande with the corner girl. Grand right , go round the world
Hand over hand round that big old ring
Promenade with this lady. Go walking round the square
Oh, a dime a dozen. I'm still in love with you
