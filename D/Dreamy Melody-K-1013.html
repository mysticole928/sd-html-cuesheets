Dreamy Melody    (K 1013)   Billy Lewis

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande left your corner, and you dosado your own
Gents star right and turn the opposite by the left you know
Gents star back 3/4 and to the corner go
Allemande left and weave around that ring
Dosado your honey, promenade that street
Promenade go dancing, see-see, see-see
Swing her to this dreamy melody

FIGURE



Heads (sides) to the right and circle four hands round
Break to a line, go forward up and back
Then you pass thru and bend the line, go forward up and back
Square thru four hands, four hands around you go
Swing the corner lady, and then you promeno
Promenade go dancing, see-see, see-see
Swing her to this dreamy melody
