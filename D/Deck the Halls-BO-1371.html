Deck The Halls    (BO 1371)   John Aden, Daymond Mayhall

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Circle left
Deck the halls with boughs of holly
Fa-la-la-la-la, la-la-la-la
Men star right one time around now
Allemande left that corner, and weave that ring
Weave it in and out around until you meet your maid
Swing that girl around and promenade
'Tis the season to be jolly
Fa-la-la-la-la, la-la-la-la

FIGURE



Heads square thru 4 hands go round now
Round that corner lady, dosado one time
Touch 1/4 then, do a walk & dodge
Do a partner trade and then, do a right & left thru
Flutter wheel go full around, and then slide thru
Swing your corner girl, and then you'll all promenade
'Tis the season to be jolly
Fa-la-la-la-la, la-la-la-la
