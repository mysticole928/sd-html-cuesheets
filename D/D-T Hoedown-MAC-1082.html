D-T Hoedown
   (MacGregor 1082)    Tommy Stoye
 
FIGURE

Bow to your partner and to your corner
Join hands and circle left around
Now roll away 1/2 sashay
Circle left around you go
Left allemande , let's do an allemande thar
Go right & left and the men back in
Shoot that star , go forward two more, you make another star
Men back in and then, shoot that star and box the gnat
Pull her by , left allemande
Here we go with a right & left grand
Meet your own and promenade

Promenade and don't slow down
1 & 3 wheel around
Do a right & left thru and a full turn around
Walk on to the next , star thru , with a right & left thru
Now roll away 1/2 sashay , and dosado to an ocean wave
Right there do a right & left grand
Meet your own, promenade , run back home and swing

Heads go forward and back , star thru
Do a right & left thru
Now pass thru , dosado to an ocean wave , swing thru double
That's 4 turns, balance , right & left thru , dive thru
Substitute and square thru 3/4
Left allemande and weave the ring
In and out until you meet
Promenade back home and swing

Sides square thru count 4 hands
Dosado the outside two, make an ocean wave , balance
Swing thru , go right & left, now the men trade
To a box the gnat , right & left thru
Dive thru , dosado to an ocean wave
Square thru 3/4 , left allemande
Grand right & left , meet your own and promenade
Right back home and swing

Now 4 little ladies grand chain , turn 'em left and chain 'em back
Walk all around the left hand lady , see saw your taw
Do an allemande left and a right & left grand
Meet your own and promenade
Promenade 8 it's home you go
Now bow to your partner , that's all

November 1965.
