(Notes on Figure:
Allemande left corners, take right hand swing with partners, ladies promenade
CCW full around inside of square back to partner. Partners dosado and all promenade single file CCW around set, ladies in front of their gent, then gents make 1/2 right face turn in reverse direction, moving CW on outside of set while ladies continue to travel CCW in single file. At the first call of "Detour" all reverse directions, gents moving CCW and ladies CW. At second call of "Detour" all reverse directions again, gents moving CW and ladies CCW. On meeting second time partners swing briefly, then all swing corners to acquire a new partner. Do not promenade to home position but repeat figure from present position.)
