Deed I Do
Ella Fitzgerald 1926
   (Scope 641)    Mac McCullar
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Mainstream

1 & 3 promenade halfway around with your maid
Come into the middle , do a little dosado
Slide thru , pass thru , square thru 4 hands you do
Boys turn right, go left allemande
Do a grand old right & left until you meet her, promenade
There's no one any sweeter
Take a little walk, go around the ring
Do we really like to swing honey?
Deed we do

FIGURE    A1, corner progression

1 & 3 lead right in time, circle four , make a line
Dance to the middle , then you back away
Star thru , slide thru , cross trail thru to the corner do
Allemande left , grand old right & left
Now take each hand until you meet your baby
Dosado then swing the corner lady
Promenade go around the ring
Do we really like to swing?
Honey, deed we do

Choreography for the opener | middle break | closer is odd. It has everybody using the left hand twice in a row.
