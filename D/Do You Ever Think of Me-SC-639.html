Do You Ever Think Of Me    (SC 639)   Wes Wessinger

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



All four ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Join hands, circle left around the square
Four ladies roll away, circle left around again
Left allemande, then weave around the ring
And when your eyes disguise the same old lovin' lies
Dosado your girl and promenade
When you have another's arm around you
Do you ever think of me?

FIGURE



Heads square thru go full around the square
Meet your corner girl, then curlique
Scoot back and then, boys run right
A right & left thru and turn your lady round
Slide thru and swing your corner lady there
Left allemande, then you promenade
When you whisper "I can't live without you"
Do you ever think of me?
