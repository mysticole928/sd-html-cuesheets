Don't Worry, Be Happy   (Aussie Tempos 1028)   Jason Dean

OPENER - MIDDLE



Sides face grand square
Here's a little song I wrote
You might want to sing it note for note
But don't worry. Be happy
Left allemande and weave the ring
Every life we have some trouble
When you worry you make it double (Swing, promenade)
But don't worry. Be happy
(Give me your number, I'll call you, I make you happy)

FIGURE



Heads promenade go halfway in time
Lead right, circle four and then you make your line
(Don't worry)
Right and left thru and turn your girl
Square thru and count me four
Four hands around and then, Trade by, Left allemande
Come back and swing and promenade that land
But don't worry. Be happy

CLOSER



Sides face grand square
Ain't got no place to lay your head
Somebody come and took your bed
But don't worry. Be happy
Left allemande and weave the ring
Your landlord say your rent is late
He may have to litigate (Swing, promenade)
But don't worry. Be happy
