INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande left that corner girl, go forward 2 I sing
Right, left, turn back 1, with the partner swing, circle left
I never get lonesome livin' on the river
Allemande left and weave around that ring
Dixie Lily, chuggin' like a grand old lady
Dosado and promenade for me
Plowin' through the water, with her whistles blowin'
Down to Louisiana on the Dixieland run

FIGURE

Side 2 ladies flutter wheel you go
Square thru 4 hands around that ring now
Right & left thru and turn the girl you know
Pass thru, trade by, swing thru and then
Turn thru, swing that corner there, promenade
Plowin' through the water, with her whistles blowin'
Down to Louisiana on the Dixieland run
