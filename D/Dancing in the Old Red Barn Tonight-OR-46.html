Dancing In The Old Red Barn Tonight
Redd Harper; Texas Jim Lewis 1948
(Ocean 46) Dennis Levitt
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Four girls you promenade go walking round the ring
Gonna get back home and swing , everybody swing
Join up all your hands , you circle to the left and go
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring
Weave it in and out til you meet your lady fair
Swing your girl around and promenade from there
We're dancing in the old red barn tonight

FIGURE

Head two couples promenade, go halfway round the ring
Come down the middle and square thru 4 hands around I sing
Four hands and then, swing thru my friend
Boys run to the right , a wheel & deal
Square thru 3/4 , trade by and swing
Swing the corner lady there and promenade the ring
Promenade your lady go walking two by two
She's dancing in the old red barn with you

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)

Head two couples square thru 4 hands around you go
Get around the corner lady and do a little dosado
Star thru and pass thru , now chase to the right
Boys run around this girl, star thru and then
Veer to the left , and ferris wheel , pass thru and swing
Swing that corner lady and promenade the ring
Promenade your lady go walking two by two
She's dancing in the old red barn with you
