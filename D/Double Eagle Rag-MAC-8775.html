Double Eagle Rag
   (MacGregor 8775)    Fenton Jones
FIGURE    C1, no progression

Now the head ladies chain
Sides will promenade 3/4 round this big old ring
Cut through the heads , square thru 3 hands round you do
Hey, swing that lady, swing
Join hands , circle left round the town
Swing your corner lady around and around
4 ladies star left around you go
Now with your partner lady, you'll do a dosado
Allemande left your corner, allemande right your own
Box the flea with the corner lady, promenade her home
Promenade that ring, hey, take her home and swing her
To the double eagle rag
