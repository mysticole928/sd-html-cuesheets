Dream A Little Dream Of Me   (Hi Hat 5215 )   Kip Garvey

OPENER



Circle left
Stars shining bright above you
Night breezes seem to whisper I love you
Left Allemande and dosado your own
Left Allemande and weave the ring
Say nighty night and kiss me
You swing and Promenade, Say you'll miss me
And in your dreams whatever they be
Dream a little dream of me

FIGURE (twice for heads, twice for sides)



Four ladies chain three quarters across the ring now
You turn her and then, Heads you star thru
A double pass thru, Leaders trade, Dosado once again
You touch a quarter, centres trade, centres rund
Three quarter tag, Swing and promenadeson
And in your dreams whatever they be
Dream a little dream of me

MIDDLE AND CLOSER



Four ladies promenade a walking inside
Come back and swing your man, Swing him out wide
Join all your hands, circle left everyone
Left Allemande, weave that ring
Say nighty night and kiss me
You swing and Promenade, Say you'll miss me
And in your dreams whatever they be
Dream a little dream of me
