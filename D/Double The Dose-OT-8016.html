First couple bow and now you swing

Swing on your corners and make them laugh
Back to your own with a once and a half
Next two corners down the line
Back to your own, you're doin' fine

Side couples do a right & left thru

Back to your own and go like thunder
Next two corners down the line
Back to your own, you're doin' fine

Left to your opposite and hang on close
Back to the center and double the dose
