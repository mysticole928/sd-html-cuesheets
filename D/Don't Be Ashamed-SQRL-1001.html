The Square L/Blue Star story This piece of music was originally recorded in the early sixties using musicians from Texas lead by the famous Johnny Gimble. It was released on Square L Records special red vinyl by producer, Melton Luttrell. In 2019, the original studio tapes were digitally re-mastered by Buddy Weaver who also re-mixed and added percussion for a brand-new sound. This is a collaborative product of Square L and Blue Star.
