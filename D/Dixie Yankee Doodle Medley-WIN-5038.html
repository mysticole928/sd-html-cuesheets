Dixie Yankee Doodle Medley
   (Windsor 5038)    Al Stevens
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    A2

Hey, 4 ladies chain go across the ring (with enthusiasm)
4 ladies flutter wheel go moving around and then
All 4 couples sweep 1/4 , circle left
Left allemande , weave the ring
Oh, I wish I were in Dixie
Dosado , promenade her home
In Dixie land I'll take my stand
To live and die in Dixie

FIGURE    Mainstream, corner progression

Head (side) two couples promenade 1/2 round the ring
Down the middle and pass thru ,
Do a partner trade and 1/4 more to face her,
Turn thru and then
Dosado , make an ocean wave , my friend
Swing thru , the boys trade
Boys run , do a partner trade , promenade
Promenade that lady home and
Swing with Yankee Doodle
