Down Yonder    (WIN 4884)   Bob Van Antwerp

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across and turn the girls and then
Head 2 square thru 4 hands around my friend
With the sides a dosado once around will do
And ocean wave rock up and back, a spin chain thru
Turn a right, a left 3/4, turn a right again
Left 3/4, rock it, box the gnat pull 'em through
Allemande the corner, come back a dosado
Same girl, look her in the eye, pass by and promeno
They're raisin' a fuss and a hullabaloo
Awaiting down yonder for you

MIDDLE BREAK



Allemande left your corner, come home a dosado
Boys star left in the middle, it's once around you go
Meet your partner, dosado once around and then
With your corner star thru, circle left again
All the ladies star by the right, go once around tonight
Same man a left hand round, corner with a right hand round
Partner left an allemande thar, the men back up and star
Shoot the star and find your maid and then you promenade
Your sister and your brother, your daddy and your mother
Are waiting down yonder for you
