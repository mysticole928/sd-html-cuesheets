Deed I Do
Ella Fitzgerald 1926
(Blue Star 2365) Chris Vear
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

Walk all around the corner girl, seesaw around your own
Men star right hand, one time around you go
Pick up that lady now, star promenade a go round
Girls roll back , allemande left , weave that ring
Wind in and out around you travel
Swing a little lady round, and promenade the ring
Well, do I want you, ho my do I
Honey, deed I do

FIGURE

Heads (sides) you square thru , 4 hands in the middle and go
When you get there, swing thru the outside two
The boys run , ladies trade , couples circulate
Bend the line , get a right & left thru and go
You square thru now, 3/4 round and then
Swing a little corner girl, promenade that ring
Do I need you, oh my do I
Honey deed I do
