Dear Hearts & Gentle People   (TNT 204 )   Gordon Fineout
Four ladies chain, you turn the girl
Roll away and circle left
Four ladies roll away, circle left and then
Left allemande and weave the ring
And I'd be happy if when you meet
You'd swing this gal and promenade
I love those dear hearts and gentle people
Who square dance in my home town

FIGURE



Heads promenade half around the ring now
2 & 4 do a right & left thru
Same 4 square thru four hands you go
Dosado and do an eight chain four
I'd be happy if when you meet
Swing number five and promenade
I love those dear hearts and gentle people
Who square dance in my home town

ALTERNATE FIGURE

1 & 3 pass the ocean
Ping pong circulate
Extend, swing thru
Boys run, half tag, trade & roll
Pass thru, left allemande and weave the ring
I'd be happy if when you meet this gal
Swing her once or twice and promenade
I love those dear hearts and gentle people
Who square dance in my home town
