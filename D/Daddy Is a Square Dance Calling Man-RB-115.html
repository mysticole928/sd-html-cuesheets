Daddy Is A Square Dance Calling Man   (Red Boot 115 )   Bob Vinyard

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join hands and circle round the track
Left allemande, come back and box the gnat
4 ladies promenade around the land
You box the gnat, then do the right & left grand
Daddy is a square dance calling man
Dosado, allemande left and promenade you know
He calls so plain a child could understand
Yes, daddy is a square dance calling man

FIGURE



4 little ladies chain across the town
The heads promeande go half around
Lead to the right and circle around the track
You break to a line, move 8 to the middle & back
Star thru, with an 8 chain 3, count 3 hands and then
Corner swing, swing the girl and promenade the ring
He calls near and far across the land
Yes, daddy is a square dance calling man

SEQUENCE (opener, figure twice heads, middle break, figure twice sides, closer)
