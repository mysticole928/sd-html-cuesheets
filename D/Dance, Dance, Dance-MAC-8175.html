Tin Lizzy Quadrille / Dance, Dance, Dance
   (MacGregor 8175 side B )   
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

All around your corner girl, see saw your taw
All join hands , circle left the hall
Ladies roll away , circle left round the ring
Swing your left hand girl , give her a swing
Allemande left your corner and dosado your own
Promenade your partners home
Promenade go home , swing your maid
And dance, dance, dance

FIGURE

1st and 3rd go to the right , circle 1/2 around
Dive thru , star right one time round
Left hand round your corner , heads dosado
Sides star right once around you go
Allemande left your corner, pass by your own
Right hand swing the right hand girl and promenade her home
Promenade , you have a new maid
So dance, dance, dance
