Daisy A Day    (CK 125)   Doug Davis

OPENER



All four ladies promenade the inside
Get back home and swing there with your man
Join hands and circle to the left around I sing
Left allemande and weave the ring
I'll give you a daisy a day dear
Swing your own and promenade
I'll love you until the rivers run still
And the four winds we know blow away

FIGURE



Heads promenade, you go halfway
Down the middle and pass the ocean there, extend
Swing thru tonight, the boys run right
Bend the line and do the right and left thru
Pass the ocean and then you recycle
Swing the corner girl and promenade
I'll love you until the rivers run still
And the four winds we know blow away
