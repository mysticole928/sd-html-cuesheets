Dum Dum    (LH 1005)   Walt McNeel

Why don't you allemande left, you do a curlique



You circle left and hear me sing
Why don't you allemande left and then you weave that ring
A-singin' dum, dum, diddle-de-dum
You dosado and promenade
A-singin' dum, dum, diddle-de-dum

FIGURE



4 ladies chain and when you get there
You promenade halfway 'round the square
Heads square thru go walkin' 'round the floor
You dosado and do an eight chain 4
A-singin' dum, dum, diddle-de-dum
Swing that lady and promenade
A-singin' dum, dum, diddle-de-dum

SEQUENCE: (opener, figure twice heads, break, figure twice sides, ending)
