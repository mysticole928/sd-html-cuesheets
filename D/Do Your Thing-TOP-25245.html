Do Your Thing    (TOP 25245)   Ed Fraidenburg

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade go once inside the ring
Get home and give your man a swing
Join hands, circle to the left around and then
Left allemande and weave around the ring
Weave in and out around the ring until you meet your maid
Dosado this girl and promenade
Do your thing if you can bear the weight
Go your way if you can pay the freight

FIGURE



4 ladies chain across, turn the girl and then
Heads square thru in the middle and go
4 hands around the ring you do
Swing thru the outside two
Boys run right and then you bend the line, up and back
Flutter wheel, and move it straight across the ring you go
Left allemande come back and promenade
Do your thing if you can bear the weight
Go your way if you can pay the freight
