Dust Off That Old Pianna    (MAC 1050)   Julius King

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Allemande left your corner and walk on by Suzanna
Swing the right hand lady round and round
Circle to the left now halfway around the set now
4 ladies chain across the town
You roll a 1/2 sashay, grand right & left I say
Meet Suzanna over there and promenade go round the square
Sing, oh Suzanna, get out your old bandanna
Dust off that old pianna tonight

FIGURE



Heads go up and back, 1/2 square thru and then
With the outside 2 a right & left thru you know
Dive thru, star thru, and dosado go round
An ocean wave you rock it to and fro
Square thru about 4 hands, go all the way there, man
Swing the corner once around and promenade go round the town
Sing, oh Suzanna, we'll let you sing sopranna
Dust off that old pianna tonight
