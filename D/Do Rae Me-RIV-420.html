Do Rae MeRIV-420
Jack O'Leary

OPENER/BREAK/CLOSER

Circle Left
Do—a deer, a female deer
Ra—a drop of golden sun
Mi—a name I call myself
Fa—a long, long way to run
4 Ladies Promenade inside the ring
Do Sa Do your partner, Promenade the ring
Ti—a drink with jam and bread
That will bring us back to Do

FIGURE

4 Ladies chain; turn 'em around;
Chain the ladies home again
All join hands and circle to the Left go 'round
Allemande Left the corner, Weave the Ring
So—a needle pulling thread
La—a note to follow So
Ti—meet your Partner, Promenade
That will bring us back to Do
