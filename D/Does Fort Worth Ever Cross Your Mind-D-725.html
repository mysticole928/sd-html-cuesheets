Does Fort Worth Ever Cross Your Mind    (D 725)   Johnnie Wykoff

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies chain 3/4 round the ring
Join your hands and circle left
Four ladies roll away, circle left to Dallas
Left allemande, weave the ring
You left me here to be with him in Dallas
Swing and whirl promenade
Tell me dear, does it really make a difference?
Does Fort Worth ever cross your mind?

FIGURE



Head (side) couples promenade and go halfway round
Walk in and square thru count to four
Go to the outside, do the right & left thru
Veer to the left, couples circulate
Half tag the line, scoot back and the boys fold
Girls turn thru, star thru, promenade
Now tell me dear, does it really make a difference
Does Fort Worth ever cross your mind?
