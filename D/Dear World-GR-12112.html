Dear World    (GR 12112)   Earl Johnston

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande that corner, dosado your own
Join your hands, circle left around you roam
Left allemande and then you weave the ring
Weave in and out around until you meet agagin
You turn her by the right, girls promenade inside
One time around you come on home, swing and then
You promenade her go walkin' round the room
And be a dear world and get well soon

FIGURE



The head 2 couples square thru 4 hands around you go
Now with the corner dosado around you know
Swing thru, boys trade and then
Box the gnat, change hands, left allemande
Dosado your honey, that corner lady swing
Swing that lady, promenade the ring
*We'll bring you roses and get you back in tune
So be a dear world and get well soon

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*We want you dancing tomorrow afternoon
So be a dear world and get well soon
