Deed I Do    (RA 1347)   J.P. Jett

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande the corner one, turn thru with the partner son
Men star left and turn it round that land
Star promenade with the one you know, take a little walk around you go
Ladies backtrack once and then, partner right
Allemande that corner and a grand old right & left
Hand over hand around you go, meet a lady promeno
Do I love you, oh my, do I, honey, 'deed I do

FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain across, turn the girl don't get lost
Side two ladies chain across that ring
Forward up and back with you, star thru, pass thru
Dosado and make that ocean wave, balance
Swing thru across the line and box the gnat for me
Pull by and swing the corner, promenade and sing
Do I love you, oh my, do I, honey, 'deed I do
