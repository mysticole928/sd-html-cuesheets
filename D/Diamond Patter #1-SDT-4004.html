Diamond Patter #1
   (Square Dancetime 4004)    Al Stevens
FIGURE

Bow to partner, corner too
Heads to the middle and back right out
Dosado go once around (stay in the middle, heads)
Make a right hand star, star by the right
Back out at home
Now the sides walk to the middle and back on out
Go forward again, do a dosado
Stay in the middle, make a right hand star
Star by the right
Back out at home

The head two pair move to the middle and back right out
Go forward again, dosado go once around
Star by the right, go one full turn
This time with the corner left allemande
With the corner maid
Home you go with a dosado

Sides get ready
Walk into the middle and back right out
Dosado, go once around
Stay in the middle make a right hand star
Star by the right, find the corner man
Left allemande, come on back in a right & left grand
Grand right & left around, meet a girl and promenade
Gonna promenade that lady, go home

Heads to the middle and back right out
Now pass thru, take your partner and
Promenade left and go halfway
Go back to home
Side couples pass thru, take your partner
Promenade left just go halfway
It's back to home

Head couples forward, up to the middle and come right back
Pass thru, take partner, promenade left full turn
Side couples do a dosado, stay in the middle
Star by the right, make a right hand star, sides back out at home

Side couples forward and back, now pass thru
Take partner, promenade left a full turn
Heads dosado, now star right, a right hand star
Heads to the corner, left allemande
Left allemande, right & left grand, hand over hand around
Promenade to home

Head couples lead right, split two go between 'em
And separate, round one, down the middle, pass thru
Courtesy turn old you know who
Those ladies chain across
Side couples lead right, split two, round one
Down the middle, pass thru, courtesy turn ol' Dolly Parton
Those ladies chain

Let's have four ladies chain
Chain a grand chain four
Chain them back in the same ol' track
Make your feet go wickedy wack
Join hands, make a little ring
Circle to the left I sing
Do an allemande left with your left hand and
A grand right and left
When you meet your partner swing and whirl
When you get there stay right there
Now sides face, grand square, walk 2, 3, turn

All the heads walk to the middle and come right back
Lead to the right and again split two, round one
Down the middle, pass thru, courtesy turn
Turn the ladies, same ladies chain across

Sides lead right again split two, round one
Down the middle, pass thru, courtesy turn
Those ladies chain, then bow to your partner, that's all!
