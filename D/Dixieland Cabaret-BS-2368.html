Dixieland Cabaret   (Blue Star 2368 )   Johnnie Wykoff

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain..3/4 around
Join hands and circle to the left
4 ladies rollaway, circle left that way
Left allemande..weave the ring
No need to set alone in your room
Swing that gal and promenade
Life is a cabaret old chum
Come to the cabaret

FIGURE



Head (side) couples promenade go 1/2 way around
Side couples pass thru, around one, make a line
Go forward 8 to the middle and back
Star thru, all double pass thru, & the leaders partner trade
Touch 1/4, scoot back and then
Turn by the right, hold on and promenade
Life is a cabaret old chum
Come to the cabaret
