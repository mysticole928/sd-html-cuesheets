Zippity Do Dah   (Chaparral 414 )   Beryl Main

OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies promenade go once around the ring
Come on back, swing your girl, and everybody swing
Join hands, circle, go walking down the line
Left allemande the corner, weave on down the line
Wind in and out around and when you meet your girl
Dosado, promenade go round the world
Zippity do dah zippity de ya
What a wonderful feeling and a wonderful day

FIGURE (1st heads and sides)



Heads (sides) you promenade, take her 1/2 way round that ring
Well come on in square thru be sure to count to 4
Go all the way around and then a right and left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel, you walk right down the line
You square thru and go 3/4 round the ring
Swing that corner girl, promenade go round the ring
Zippity do dah zippity de ya
What a wonderful feeling and a wonderful day

FIGURE (2nd heads and sides)



Heads (sides) you promenade, take her 1/2 way round that ring
Well come on in square thru be sure to count to 4
Go all the way around and then a right and left thru
Veer to the left and ferris wheel, you walk right down the line
Mr. Bluebird on my shoulder
Swing that girl around, promenade and you get a little bolder
Zippity do dah zippity de ya
What a wonderful feeling and a wonderful day
