Mexican Love Song    (CRC 126)  Steve Jacques

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four ladies promenade that ring go
Gonna get back home and swing there with your man
You join hands circulate to the left go around the ring
Allemande the corner and you weave the ringo
And they play all those mexican love songs
Swing and you promenade
And the moment my heart heard the music
I know that the night
Was made for love

FIGURE



Those heads square thru and get me four
And with the corner there go right and left thru
You swing thru two by two and then the boys run right
You do a half tag and scoot back to night
Boys run right around the girl and square thru go tree
Swing a little corner girl and you promenade
And the moment my heart heard the music
I know that the night
Was made for love
