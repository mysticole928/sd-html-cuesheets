My Wagon    (MAC 953)   Don Stewart

OPENER



Allemande left and an allemande thar
Go right & left and form that star
Back 'em up boys, yes, but not too far
Shoot that star with a full turn around
Give a right to your corner, and pull her by
Left to the next, allemande that, walk right into a right hand star
Now shoot that star, go right & left grand around that ring you go
Hand over hand till you meet your maid, promenade, go round the ring
Promenade go 2 by 2, tet on home and swing you do
Get aboard my wagon if you wanta have some fun

FIGURE



Head 2 ladies chain to the right
Turn the girls and hug 'em up tight
New side ladies chain across that ring, turn the girl
1 & 3 square thru 3/4 in the middle you do
Separate around one, it's 4 in a line, rock forward and back
Center two go right & left thru, same two a 1/2 sashay
Swing corner lady, promenade go round the ring that way
Promenade, go two by two, get on home and swing you do
Get aboard my wagon if you wanta have some fun
