My Melody Of Love    (TB 183)   Bud Whitten

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Walk around the corner girl, see saw your own
Join hands circle to the left
Allemande left in alamo style, balance there awhile
Swing thru and move it down the line, balance
Swing thru again, turn thru my friend
Left allemande and promenade
Come back to me and always be my melody of love

FIGURE



Heads (sides) dosado then face that corner girl
Right & left thru, turn that girl you do, veer left
Ferris wheel and now centers pass thru
Dosado the outside two, swing thru, boys trade
And then you turn thru, left allemande and promenade
Come back to me and always be my melody of love
