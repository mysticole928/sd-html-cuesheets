Join your hands and circle to the mission
Allemande left, come back and dosado

Turn thru, left allemande
Weave around the ring, in and out you go
When you meet your girl you dosado
Promenade your lady to the mission

Wheel & deal then swing thru

Promenade your lady to the mission
And the mission bells will ring
