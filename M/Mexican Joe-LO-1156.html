OPENER



(Circle left)
South of the border, say I know a lad
He's got more fun than anybody has
Allemande in the alamo style, balance out and in
Swing thru, two by two, balance once again
Swing thru, two by two, boys run right
Girls run left, left allemande and promenade tonight
Dancing, romancing, always on the go
Sun shining down on old Mexican Joe

FIGURE



Head couples promenade, go 3/4 round
Two and four do the right and left thru and turn the girl around
Pass thru, do a right & left thru, turn the girl once more
You flutterwheel and move it across the floor
Square thru 3/4 round the corner lady swing
Dancing, romancing, always on the go
Sun shining down on old Mexican Joe

On heads and sides 2nd time figure substitute last line above for:
Sun's shining down so circle, circle left around you go
