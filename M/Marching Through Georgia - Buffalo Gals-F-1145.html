Marching Through Georgia / Buffalo Gals
Jay Ungar; others 1865
   (Folkraft 1145)   
 
FIGURE (Visiting couple style; four times through)

The first lady march around the inside of the ring
You step right up to your partner, give him a good, big swing
Everybody march around, you march around and sing
While we go marching through Georgia
Hooray, hooray, go back the other way
Hooray, hooray, you're going the wrong way (reverse)
When you reach your places all, everybody swing
While we go marching through Georgia

(repeat the entire figure calling in turn:
Second lady, third lady, last lady, first
gentleman, second gentleman, third gentleman,
last gentleman. Then do CLOSER.)

CLOSER

Forward all and stomp your feet 1, 2, 3, 4
Forward again and everybody shout
Dosado on your corner
Now dosado your own
Allemande left on the corner with the
Grand right and left around
