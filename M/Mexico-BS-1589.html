Mexico    (BS 1589)   Andy Andrus

FIGURE



Allemande left there, swing senorita
4 ladies chain across that ring, there's no one sweeter
1 & 3 square thru 4 hands around there
Face those sides and do a star twirl thru
Bend the line, forward 8 and back you go
Right & left thru and turn 'em twice you know
And then you cross trail, turn partners left around
Swing that right hand girl, and promenade Chiquita
2 by 2 across the Rio Grande
Swing with your girl in Mexico

BREAK



Dosado your corner girl, and then the one you know
Join hands and circle left down in old Mexico
Get back home and swing with senorita
Gents star right and turn it once around
Pass your own and corners all left allemande
Grand old right & left around the land
Your partner dosado, it's once around you go
Allemande left, come back 1 swing senorita
Promenade right back home to your rancho
Swing with your girl in Mexico
