Mexican Joe
Jim Reeves with The Circle O Ranch Boys 1953
(Tarheel 130) Buck Hastedt
OPENER

Circle left
South of the border, I know a lad
He's got more fun, than anybody has
Left allemande your corner, come back a dosado
Men star by the left and turn it round you go
Turn your partner by the right , the corner allemande
Swing that lady round and round and promenade the land
Ain't got no worry's, Ain't got no dough
Everybody's wonderin' 'bout Mexican Joe

FIGURE

Heads (sides) promenade halfway round you go
Down the middle and square thru and get me 4 you know
Four hands around now, swing thru and then
Boys run to the right, couples circulate and then
Ladies trade , bend the line , square thru 3 hands
Swing the corner lady round and promenade the land
Dancin', romancin', always on the go
Sun shinin' down on Mexican Joe

Swing that lady round and round and promenade that land
Dancin', romancin', always on the go
