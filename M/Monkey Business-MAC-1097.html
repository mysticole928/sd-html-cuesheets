Monkey Business    (MAC 1097)   Fenton Jones

INTRO



4 little ladies promenade go a-walking round the ring
Get back home, swing your baby, everybody swing
Men star left one time around, pick up your baby with an arm around
Back right out, circle left, we'll make a great, big ring
Allemande your corner, come back to promenade
He'll be true to monkey doodle doo
Way down in Jungle Town

FIGURE



1 & 3 lead to the right, you'll circle 1/2 the town
Dive thru, right & left thru, let's turn those girls around
Pass thru, circle to the left one full turn you go
One full turn, 2 ladies chain, turn the girl you know
Dive thru, square thru 3/4 round you go
Allemande left, come back one to promenade with me
Promenade on down with little Betsy Brown
Way down in Jungle Town
