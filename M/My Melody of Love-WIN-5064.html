OPENER - BREAK - CLOSER

Do an allemande left your corner, dosado at home
Four men star by the left, one time around you roam

FIGURE

Up to the middle and back, touch a quarter in time

Star thru, dive thru, square thru three quarters round you go
Weave that ring, go in and out around that ring you go
Swing that corner lady, promenade home
Return to me and always be
My melody of love
