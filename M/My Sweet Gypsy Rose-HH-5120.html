My Sweet Gypsy Rose    (HH 5120)   Tom Perry

Well the four little ladies promenade go once inside the ring
Get home and swing the man, now everybody swing
Join hands and circle to the left around you go
Allemande left with the corner girl and weave
Weave and wind and you meet that lady there
Swing that pretty little girl, promenade her go around the square
Rings on her fingers and bells on her toes
Hey! has anybody seen my sweet Gypsy Rose?

Star thru and pass thru and do a dosado
Make an ocean wave and then you swing thru you know
Hey, boys run to the right and couples circulate
Half tag the line, swing the corner, promenade go round I say
Rings on her fingers and bells on her toes
