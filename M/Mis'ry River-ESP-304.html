Mis'ry River   (ESP 304 )   Paul Marcum

OPENER



Circle to the left
Oh, Misery River, won't you take me along
Left allemande, dosado
Men star left around you go
Turn the partner right, left allemande
Swing your own and promenade
Oh, mis'ry river
Carry me back home where I belong

California twirl, and swing thru & then
Boys run and do a ferris wheel
Center two, go right & left thru & then veer left
Veer right, swing & promenade
Let me wash my wounds in your water
Take me back to the land where I was born
