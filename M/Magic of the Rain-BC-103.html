Magic Of The Rain    (BC 103)   Bob Augustin

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk all around the corner doll, see saw your taw
Join hands and circle to the left you know
Do an allemande left with the corner girl, come back and dosado
Left allemande and weave around the ring
*In the early morning magic of the rain
Dosado then promenade that ring
I found happiness I can't explain
In the early morning magic of the rain

FIGURE



4 little ladies chain the ring, turn the girl and then
Join hands and circle to the left you know about halfway
Head 2 pass thru, around just 1 you go
Make a line, go up to the middle and back
Pass thru, wheel & deal, centers turn thru and then
Star thru and promenade along
**I found happiness I can't explain
In the early morning magic of the rain

Heads promenade 3/4 round the ring, sides do a right & left thru you go
