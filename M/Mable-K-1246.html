Mable
   (Kalox 1246)    Harper Smith
 
OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic - SSD Week 2

The four ladies chain 3/4 go round
Join hands and circle left you move it round the town
Ladies roll away and circle left and then
Left allemande , weave around the ring
My pretty little girl's name is Mable
Dosado and promenade the ring
She doesn't care for mink or sable
Lovers Lane is good enough for Mable

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Heads promenade halfway round
2 & 4 (1 & 3) right & left thru and turn 'em around
Sides curlique and then the boys run right
Right & left thru and turn the girl and then star thru
Load the boat everybody load it right now
Swing your corner and promenade somehow
She doesn't care for mink or sable
Lovers Lane is good enough for Mable

FIGURE (ALTERNATE)    Plus, corner progression

Heads promenade halfway round
Come down the middle and square thru full around
Go 4 hands and then swing thru the outside two
Boys run right, half tag , trade, roll
Pass thru , trade by , swing that corner round
Left allemande and promenade the town
She don't care for mink or sable
Lovers Lane is good enough for Mable

FIGURE (PLUS 1 & 2)    C4, corner progression

Heads promenade halfway round
2 & 4 (1 & 3) right & left thru and turn 'em around
Sides curlique and the boys run right
Spin chain the gears (or relay the deucey ), don't you take all night
Move it right along until you get through
Swing the corner lady and promenade you do
She don't care for mink or sable
Lovers Lane is good enough for Mable
