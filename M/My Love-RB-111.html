SEQUENCE PLEASE NOTE:

Opener, heads , sides , middle break, heads , sides , closer, tag.
If you use the customary heads twice and sides twice, the dance will work well
but you will miss one lady. If you follow the suggested sequence you will dance with each lady as usual.
