Memory-Go-Round   (Square Tunes 206 )   Jim Lee

OPENER - BREAK



4 ladies chain
3/4 around, turn her I say
Rollaway, circle to the left, go around the ring
Well, memory go round
You got me spinning like a circus clown
Swing that girl, promenade, go walking around
Get off the memory go round

FIGURE



Head (side) 2, you square thru, going to get 4 hands you go
Make an ocean wave, girls trade
Swing thru, boys run, tag the line
Face to the right, now boys cross run
Ferris wheel, center 2 pass thru
Swing that corner and promenade
I got to get my feet on solid ground
Get off the memory go round

CLOSER



4 ladies promenade
Go once around & now you swing your man
Join hands, circle to the left, go around the ring
Corner allemande left, come back & now you weave that ring
Well, memory go round
You got me spinning like a circus clown
Swing that girl, promenade, go walking around
Get off the memory go round

TAG



Walk around that corner & bow to the partner, corners, too
