Bad Situation    (WW 313)   Beryl Main

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Walk around your corner, see saw your taw
Join hands, circle round the ring
The men star right, turn it one time around there
Left allemande the corner girl and weave the ring
Well sometimes living the life I lead can cause some aggravation
Do a dosado and promenade
I guess I'm making the best of a bad situation
Reckon you'd do the same if you were me

FIGURE



Heads square thru and count 4 hands you go
Dosado the corner waiting there
Curlique and then cast off 3/4 round
Girls trade and do a right & left thru
Flutter wheel and then swing the corner girl
Left allemande, come back and promenade
I guess I'm making the best of a bad situation
Reckon you'd do the same if you were me
