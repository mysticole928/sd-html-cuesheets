Mexican Joe    (MAC 1035)   Vera Baerg

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Join your hands and circle round that big old ring
Get on home, remember, boys you don't have time to swing
1 & 3 lead to the right, circle round that track
Head gents break to make a line, go walkin' up and back
Pass thru, fold the ends, you star thru and then
Bend the line and cross trail, your corners allemande
Come back 1 and promenade and don't you dare be slow
Get on home and swing with your Mexican Joe

FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain across, turn the girls and then
Same 4 ladies chain right back you turn 'em left again
1 & 3 lead to the right, circle 1/2 the town
Dive thru, swing thru go one time around
Box the gnat, and square thru 3 hands round you go
Swing that corner lady and then you promeno
Dancing, romancing, always on the go
Get on home and swing with your Mexican Joe
