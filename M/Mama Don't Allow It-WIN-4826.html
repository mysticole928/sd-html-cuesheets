Mama Don't Allow It
Papa Charlie Jackson; Riley Puckett; Washboard Sam; many others 1925

FIGURE AND BREAK

Allemande left your corner, grand ol' right & left you hear
Mama don't 'low no banjo playin' in here
Turn back 3 when you meet your girl, go left, right, a left hand whirl
A left hand full around then face your own

Box the gnat and dosado , then to your corners go
Swing that corner lady high and low
Gents star left and don't be late, same ol' gal a catch all eight
Right hand 1/2, a left hand full around

4 little ladies grand chain , chain across that ring
Turn and chain 'em right back home again
Ladies center, back to back, men go round the outside track
Mama don't 'low that banjo man in here

Dopaso , with the partner left and the corners right hand round
Partner left, then you promenade on down
We don't care what mama don't 'low, swing that purty gal any ol' how
Mama don't 'low that banjo man in here
