Mighty Mississippi    (GR 12073)   Earl Johnston

INTRO



Allemande left that corner, gonna run along home and swing
Join hands and circle to the left go walking round that ring
Halfway around, you go left allemande
Now weave the ring, weave in and out until you meet again
When you meet that girl you dosado, it's one time around
Allemande left that corner, run home and promenade
You promenade along, you take her right on home
That mighty Mississippi's rolling on

FIGURE



4 ladies chain 3/4, go round inside the ring
4 ladies chain straight across, you turn that girl and then
Dosado that corner, run home and see saw
Men star by the right, go once around that hall
This partner left a dopaso, and the corner by the right hand round
This partner by the left, and promenade along
You promenade around, you take her right on home
That mighty Mississippi's rollin' on
