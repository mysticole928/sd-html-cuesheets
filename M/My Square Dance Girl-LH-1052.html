My Square Dance Girl    (LH 1052)   Wayne West

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



Four little ladies promenade, go once around inside that ring
Get back home and swing there with your man
You join hands circle left go walk'n round that set
Allemande left then weave around that ring
Go in and out, around you go until you meet your own
Swing your partner once around then promenade back home
She can dance, she can sing, and she really likes to swing
My square dance girl sure has country style

FIGURE



Head two couples square thru count four hands around you do
When you meet the outside two step to a wave and the ladies trade
Boys trade and swing thru, boys run right and then
The ladies trade, then do a wheel and deal
Right & left thru the same ole two, veer to the left and then
Partner trade and promenade around the ring and sing
She can dance, she can sing, and she really likes to swing
My square dance girl sure has country style
