Mission Bells    (LH 141)   Red Warrick

FIGURE (four times)



Head two couples promenade go halfway
A right & left thru across and turn your Sue
Square thru go full around your corner bound
A right & left thru and turn your girl, star thru
Pass thru to the next, go right & left thru
Cross trail thru, the corner swing
Dosado new corner, come back and promenade
And the mission bells will ring
Keep promenading
Men roll back and promenade a new girl
Heads wheel around, go right & left thru I sing
Pass thru, star thru, and square thru
3/4 round and there you swing
Join hands and circle to the mission
Roll away and swing somebody new
Promenade on home, and she'll be wishin'
For the mission bells and you
