Mexican Polka    (TA 501)   Jack Barbour

Join hands and circle left, let's do a little Polka step



Partners left the men star right, go walking round that town
Turn your partner by the left, go all the way around
Corners right, a wrong way thar, back up boys, not too far
Back up boys and don't be slow, throw in the clutch and round you go
Same lady dosado and then you promenade
Don't stop, don't slow down, keep walking with this maid
1 & 3 you wheel around, star thru and then
Square thru 3/4, your corners allemande
*Come back one and dosado and give that gal a swing
Swing this gal and back right out and make a great big ring

(Last time through:)



Promenade, it's home you go and give this gal a swing
