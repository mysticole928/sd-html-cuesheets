FIGURE

1 & 3 half sashay , then lead to the right
You circle to the left , then it's back to the right
(Circle about 1/4 turn to the left, then circle right until the two ladies in each circle have their backs to the center)
All 4 couples half sashay
(Gents will roll your own partner across in front of you (nose to nose), which will put the ladies on the outside of an allemande thar, the gents form an arch with their right hands)
The ladies duck right under
(The ladies will duck under the arch that is formed by the men and go into a left hand star in the center, and go once around and the gents will drop their arch and face the center)
To a left hand star , go all the way around
Now turn your partner
(With a right forearm all the way around and face your corner)
