Moffitt Oklahoma   (Kalox 1104 )   Billy Lewis

OPENER



Circle to the left
I rolled into Moffitt Oklahoma
Down by that Arkansas state line
Allemande left the corner, turn a right hand round your own
4 ladies promenade inside the ring
Home you go, do a dosado, allemande left the corner Joe
Come back and promenade her home
They play poker, bridge or gin
Spit in the ocean for the men
But they don't play any kukamonga style

FIGURE



All 4 ladies chain across, you turn 'em
1 & 3 (2 & 4) do the right and left thru
Now square thru 4 hands round and count 'em
All the way round and do a little dosado
Square the barge 3/4 round
Ends trade, centers pass thru
Swing the corner girl and promenade
They play poker, bridge or gin
Spit in the ocean for the men
But they don't play any kukamonga style

BREAK - CLOSER



Circle to the left
I sat myself down at a black jack table
Allemande left, the men star right two times around
I turned over 18 and said hit me
He did and that's the last thing I recall
Allemande left the corners all, dosado around your taw
Left allemande and promenade
They play poker, bridge or gin
Spit in the ocean for the men
But they don't play any kukamonga style

TAG



If I ever see old Moffitt Oklahoma again
It'll be west of that California line
They'll have to move it, west of that California line
