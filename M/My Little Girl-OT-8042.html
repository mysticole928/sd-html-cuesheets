First couple promenade the outside
Around the outside of the ring
Those ladies chain right down the center
And they chain them back again
Those ladies chain the right hand couples
And they chain them back again
Those ladies chain the left hand couples

All around your corner lady
See saw your pretty little taw
Allemande left your left hand lady
And you grand right & left right along

And you swing her round and round

Promenade her round the town
