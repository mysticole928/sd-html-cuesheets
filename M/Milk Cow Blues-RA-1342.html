Milk Cow Blues    (RA 1342)   DeWayne Bridges

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Join hands circle, circle you know
Allemande left the corner, come on home dosado
Men star right, turn it go, man go
Then allemande left the corner, weave the ring you know
Go walkin' and a talkin', when you meet your maid
Dosado your honey and you promenade
You promenade your honey around that old ring
Get 'em home, get 'em happy, give 'em a swing

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway round
Side ladies chain straight across that old town
Sides star thru, pass thru and circle 4
Sides break and make a line, go up and back once more
Star thru and pass thru, corner swing
Swing the corner lady, go left allemande
You promenade your honey around that old ring
Get 'em home, get 'em happy, give 'em a swing
