Many A Long And Lonesome Hiway   (Hi Hat 5130 )   Bronc Wise

OPENER - BREAK



4 ladies chain the ring, you turn the girl around & then
You rollaway and circle left
4 ladies rollaway, you circle left around today
Allemande on the corner and you weave the ring
Oh! Many a long and lonesome hiway
Swing your girl around and promenade you know
And in the end, I did it my way
Look for me where the 4 winds blow

FIGURE



Head (side) couples promenade go 1/2 way around that ring
Walk in and square thru and go
4 hands around and then, you swing thru that outside two
Spin the top, boys move up & then go right & left thru
Rollaway and then you star thru
Trade by, swing your corner, promenade
And in the end, I did it my way
Look for me where the 4 winds blow

CLOSER



4 ladies chain the ring, you turn the girl around & then
You rollaway and circle left
4 ladies rollaway, you circle left around today
Allemande on the corner and you weave the ring
Oh! Many a long and lonesome hiway
Oh! Lies before us as we go
And in the end, I did it my way
Look for me where the 4 winds blow
