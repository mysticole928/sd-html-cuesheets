More Than A Name On A Wall
   (EGO 115)    Paul Cote
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
I saw her from a distance as she walked up to the wall
In her hand she held some flowers, as her tears began to fall
Allemande left and weave the ring
She took out pen and paper, as to trace a memory
Swing and promenade
She looked up to heaven, and the words she said were these

FIGURE    B - SSD Week 10, corner progression

The heads (sides) you'll promenade now, halfway round the ring you fly
Walk in and square thru go four
Go all the way and then, do the right & left thru and turn the girl
Veer to the left and ferris wheel
Hey centers square thru 3 hands, swing the corner, promenade
She looked up to heaven, and the words she said were these

MIDDLE BREAK    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
She said Lord my boy was special, and he meant so much to me
And Lord I'd love to see him, just one more time you see
Do an allemande left and weave
All I have are the memories, and the moments to recall
Swing and promenade
So Lord, would you tell him he's more than a name on a wall

CLOSER    Mainstream

Sides face grand square
She said Lord my boy was special, and he meant so much to me
And Lord I'd love to see him, but I know that just can't be
Do an allemande left and weave
So I thank You for the memories and the moments to recall
Swing and promenade
And Lord, will you tell him he's more than a name on a wall
