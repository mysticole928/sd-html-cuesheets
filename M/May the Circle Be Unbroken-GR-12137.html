May The Circle Be Unbroken    (GR 12137)   Skip Smith, Dick Leger

BREAK



Allemande left with an allemande thar, go right & left now
The boys back in, you've got an allemande thar
Shoot that star, full turn, your corner box the gnat
Dosado her then weave the ring like that
May the circle be unbroken
Dosado and promenade
May the circle be unbroken
In the sky, Lord, in the sky

FIGURE



Side ladies chain, sides square thru 5 hands
Heads promenade 3/4 round the ring
3/4 round now, swing the corner
Yes, you swing, then circle round the ring
May the circle be unbroken
Allemande left, grand right & left you fly
When you meet her, promenade her
To her new home in the sky
