Music Box Dancer   (TNT 211 )   Dan Faria

BREAK



4 ladies chain across the ring
Roll away half sashay, circle left I sing
4 ladies roll away, circle left that way
Left allemande the corner, weave the ring I say
In and out around and when you meet your girl
Swing her once around, promenade the world
Go walking the ring...she's twirling for me
My little music box dancer no one can see

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway you go
Down the middle, right & left thru and turn them you know
Square thru 4 hands my friend
Dosado the corner lady one time and then
Swing thru 2 by 2, boys trade and swing
Swing the lady around, promenade the ring
Go walking around, she's twirling for me
My little music box dancer no one can see

ALTERNATE FIGURE

Head two couples touch 1/4, boys run
Pass the ocean, we will have some fun
Do a grand swing thru, go moving down the line
Do a grand swing thru, you're doing so fine
Right & left thru, turn your girl, slide thru and swing
Swing the corner lady, promenade the ring
Go walking around, she's twirling for me
My little music box dancer no one can see
