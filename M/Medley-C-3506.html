OPENER (Five Foot Two)
Four little ladies promenade go walking 'round the ring he said
Get back home and swing now with your own there
Well join hands make that ring, circle around I sing
Allemande your corner, weave the ring
Well if you run into a five foot two, covered with pearls
Dosado and then promenade your girl
Could she love, could she woo, could she, could she, could she coo
Has anybody seen my gal?

FIGURE (Every Street's A Boulevard)

Head couples promenade go halfway round the floor
Come down the middle and do a right & left thru
Square thru and count 'em four hands around the ring
Come on and do a dosado one time around
Swing thru and then boys trade , turn thru
Left allemande , come back and promenade her home
The Eastside, the Westside, the uptown and down
Oh how I like to be in New York town

FIGURE (Hazel Eyes)

Head two couples promenade halfway around
Square thru oh Hazel eyes
Get all four and then do right & left thru again
Veer left and couples circulate
Well do a half tag , trade and roll
Pull by and then, allemande left that corner lady, promenade again
Well are they blue? No! Green? No!
Sort of in between, wow! Hazel eyes

FIGURE (Heart Of My Heart)

Side two couples promenade go halfway around and go
Come down the middle and do a right & left thru
Square thru and count 'em four hands around the ring
Round that corner do a dosado one time around
Swing thru and then boys trade , turn thru and then
Left allemande , come back and promenade her home
Now I know a tear would glisten, if once more I could listen
To the gang that sang heart of my heart

FIGURE (Best Things In Life Are Free)

Side couples promenade go halfway round the ring
Walk into the middle and square thru go 4
Go all the way around and then go right & left thru
Veer left and ferris wheel once more
Walk in and square thru 3/4 round the ring
Swing that corner girl, promenade go around the ring
Love belongs to everyone
The best things in life are free
