FIGURE



Heads (sides) pass thru do a partner trade and then
Reverse the flutter full around
Sweep 1/4, then pass thru again
Do the right & left thru and turn that girl
Slide thru, square thru four hands around trade by
Swing that corner girl and promenade
It's a sight to behold
For the young and the old
We're dancing the Christmas polka

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*Square Dancer's Polka

TAG



Bow to the partner, and the corner too, wave at the girl
across from you, very good dancing guys, you're through.

GRAND SASHAY



Allemande Left Grand Sashay
Do Si Do the first girl, give a Right hand Pull By
Seesaw the next girl, Left hand Pull By
Do Si Do the next girl, Right hand Pull By
Seesaw the next girl, Left hand Pull By
Promenade the next
(In order to time the dance out we used a Do Si Do at the end and then Promenade)
