Mister Piano Man    (MAC 1061)   Tommy Stoye

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK



4 ladies chain across from you
You chain 'em back home, turn 'em too
Now 1 & 3 promenade halfway around there
Go right & left thru, take 'em home
Now, square thru 4 hands around
You swing that corner around, left allemande the corner
Walk by your partner, swing the next, and then promenade
Mister piano man, please, play on those ivory keys

FIGURE



Heads lead right and circle 4
You make a line, go up and back that way
Pass thru, wheel & deal, double pass thru
First go left, next go left, all promenade
Those heads wheel around, right & left thru
Cross trail thru and corner swing
Swing with that lady, do the old left allemande
Dosado and then promenade the land
Singing Mr. Piano Man, please, play on those ivory keys
