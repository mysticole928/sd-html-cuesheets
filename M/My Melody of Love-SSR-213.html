My Melody Of Love   (Silver Sounds 213 )   Bruce Williamson

OPENER



Four ladies promenade the inside
Back home and swing with your man
Join hands a circle left around the ring
Left allemande and weave the ring, singing
La, la, la, la, la, la, la, la
Swing your girl and promenade
Return to me and always be
My melody of love

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway around
Walk in, pass the ocean there, extend
Swing thru, spin that old top
Boys move up and do the right & left thru
Square thru 3 hands around the ring
Swing that corner and promenade
Return to me and always be
My melody of love
