Meet Me Under The Mistletoe   (Circle D 246 )   Dean Crowell, Andy Petrere

OPENER



Circle left
Meet me under the mistletoe, midnight, Christmas Eve
Men star right around the ring, allemande left and weave
*There's frost on all the windows, swing and promenade
Meet me under the mistletoe, midnight, Christmas Eve

FIGURE



Heads promenade halfway, walk in square thru 4
Right & left thru, veer left, couples circulate
Ladies trade, bend the line, boys scoot back
Swing and promenade
Meet me under the mistletoe, midnight, Christmas Eve

CLOSER



Boys promenade, swing your partner
Join hands, circle to the left, allemande left and weave
The presents that we bought will have to wait beneath the tree
Meet me under the mistletoe, midnight, Christmas Eve

MIDDLE BREAK



There's stockings on the mantle, swing and promenade
