Morning Glory Do
   (Blue Star 2220)    Nate Bliss
 
OPENER    C4

Sides face , grand spin
The morning sun falls softly all around her
My pretty morning glory stretches out to flower
She opens up her arms and whispers, "Honey I love you"
Nothing clings to me like my Morning Glory do

FIGURE    C4, corner progression

Heads square thru take 'em 4 hands around
To the corner touch 1/4 and then scoot back
Scoot back again you do and then curlique
Boys run and wheel & deal
Swing thru hand in hand and then a right & left grand
Meet her again, swing the lady, promenade
Nothing clings to me like my Morning Glory do

MIDDLE BREAK    C4

Sides face , grand spin
I wonder Lord just what she sees in me
A simple man who offers only love eternally
But if she left me, Lord I don't know what on earth I'd do
Nothing clings to me like my Morning Glory do

CLOSER    C4

Sides face , grand spin
Nothing clings to me like my Morning Glory do
She opens up and paints my world a happy shade of blue
And comes the night, she closes her soft arms around this fool
Nothing clings to me like my Morning Glory do

TAG

Grand Spin: side position face partner, do 8 steps of grand square, dosado, star thru. Head position pass the ocean, spin the top and star thru, California twirl. Dancers do both parts twice.
