FIGURE



Heads promeande, go halfway around
Down the middle with a right & left thru
Turn the girls around, square thru go 4 hands you know
Then a right & left thru with the outside two
And then all swing thru, men trade, men run right
All tag the line, face left, promenade
It's a very pleasant dance and we thank you Master Jack

ALTERNATE FIGURE



Head couples square thru 4 hands
You go around the corner you dosado
Swing thru, men run and all ferris wheel
Centers pass thru, curlique
Follow your neighbor and 1/2 spread
Swing the corner and promenade
It's a very pleasant dance and we thank you Master Jack
