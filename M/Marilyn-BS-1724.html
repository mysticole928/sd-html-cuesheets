OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Basic

Walk all around your corner , see saw round your taw
Go back to the corner, star thru , circle to the left you all
Circle to the left and around you go, partner left, dopaso
Corner lady by the right, partner left like an allemande thar
Wheel in you got that star, walk right along you do
Shoot it , take the next and promenade
Promenade , go round the ring, take your lady on home and swing
With Marilyn, blue-eyed Marilyn

FIGURE    Basic, corner progression

Head 2 couples promenade, go halfway round the ring
Down the middle do a right & left thru , and turn that girl and then
Star thru , pass thru , right & left thru and then
Dive thru , pass thru , your corner lady swing
Allemande left your corner, weave that old ring
Go in and out, around the ring you go
Promenade , go round the ring, take your lady on home and sing
To Marilyn, dear little Marilyn
