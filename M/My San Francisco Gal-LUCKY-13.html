My San Franscisco Gal   (Lucky 013 )   Bob Van Antwerp
INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER

4 ladies promenade inside of the land
Box the gnat and do a little dosado
Join hands and circle to the left now
Left allemande and weave the ring
My gal, she came from San Francisco
Dosado and then you promenade
She danced the highways and the byways
She's my gal from San Francisco Bay

FIGURE



The heads square thru 4 hands you go
Your corner do a little dosado
Swing thru and then, the boys trade again
Box the gnat and do the right & left thru
Dive thru, square thru 3/4
Swing the corner and promenade
She danced along the by-ways
She's my gal from San Francisco Bay

SEQUENCE (intro, figure twice for heads, break, figure twice for sides, closer)
