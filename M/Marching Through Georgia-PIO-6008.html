Marching Through Georgia    (PIO 6008)   Brian Hotchkies

OPENER - MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER



4 ladies chain right across the ring
Men turn her with a 1/2 sashay
Girls chain him across
Girls turn him with a 1/2 sashay and everybody
Join hands circle to the left and you move it right along
Allemande left with the corner, come back and promenade
Promenade that lady, go walking round the hall
That's the way we dance down in Georgia

FIGURE



Head couples reverse promenade just 1/4
Sides go right & left thru and turn 'em, too
Pass thru and now swing thru the outside pair
Boys run right, you bend the line from there
Star thru and now square thru and get to 3
Corner swing and you promenade for me
Promenade that lady, go walking round the hall
That's the way we dance down in Georgia

ALTERNATE FIGURE 4



Head couples reverse flutterwheel you know
Same four sweep 1/4 and go
Pass thru and now swing thru the outside two
Boys trade, boys run and bend the line
Square thru 3 hands and the corner lady swing
Swing the corner girl and then you promenade the ring
Promenade that lady, go walking round the hall
That's the way we dance down in Georgia
