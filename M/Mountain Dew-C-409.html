Mountain Dew   (Chaparral 409 )   Beryl Main

OPENER



four little ladies promenade that ring
Get on back and swing now with your Jane
Join hands, circle, go walking 'round the ring
Left allemande and weave the ring
Oh they call it that good ole Mountain Dew
Dosado that girl and promenade her too
Well I'll hush up my mug if you'll fill up my jug
With that good ole Mountain Dew

FIGURE



Heads you promenade go halfway
Walk into the middle and curlique I say
Boys run, right & left thru, turn the girl and then
Veer to the left and ferris wheel and when you're done
Square thru go 3/4 round the ring
Swing that corner girl and promenade her home
Well I'll hush up my mug if you'll fill up my jug
With that good ole Mountain Dew

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Sides face, grand square
*A preacher came by with a tear in his eye
Saying my wife came down with the flu
I think I ought to take me a shot of that
Good old Mountain Dew
Four ladies promenade go round the ring
Get back home swing and promenade
I'll hush up my mug if you'll fill up my jug
With that good ole Mountain Dew

ALTERNATE LYRICS



*My uncle Mort was sawed off and short
He measured about four foot two
Oh, you'd think he was a giant when making 'bout a pint
Of that good old Mountain Dew
