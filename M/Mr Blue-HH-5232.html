OPENER



Circle left
I'm Mister Blue. When you say you love me
Left allemande and then you
Dosado your own
The men star left here
Turn thru and then
Left allemande you will all promenade
Then you run around headed for the lights of town

FIGURE



The Heads promenade halfway 'round
You pass the ocean and then extend
Swing thru and now, You all spin the top
The man move up, go right and left thru, (and turn the girl)
Now square thru count to three
Swing and promenade me
Then you run around headed for the lights of town
Call me Mister Blue

MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Four ladies chain across you do
Half sashay and circle
The lady roll away, Circle left around today
Allemande left, Weave the ring now
I'm Mister Blue Swing and promenade me
Then you prove it by going out on the fly
Call me Mister Blue
