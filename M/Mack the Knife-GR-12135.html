Mack The Knife    (GR 12135)   Earl Johnston

INTRO - MIDDLE BREAK - ENDING



Allemande that corner, come home and dosado
Join hands and circle left you know
You circle round the land, then go left allemande
Grand old right & left, touchin' every little hand
Meet that lady with a dosado once around your maid
Allemande that corner, come back and promenade
Oh the line forms on the right, girls
Old Mack is back in town

FIGURE



The head two couples do a right & left thru, then you rollaway
Star thru and dosado the outside two I say
Curlique, scoot back
Boys run around a girl, do a right & left thru
Turn a girl around, slide thru and the corner swing
Swing that corner lady and promenade the ring
Oh the line forms on the right, girls
Old Mack is back in town
