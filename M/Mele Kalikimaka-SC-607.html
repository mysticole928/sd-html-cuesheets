Mele Kalikimaka
Bing Crosby; others 1949
   (Scope 607)    Don Pfister
 
OPENER    Mainstream

Sides face, grand square
Mele Kalikimaka is the thing to say
On a bright Hawaiian Christmas day
It's the island greeting that I send to you
From the land where palm trees sway
Allemande left the corner lady
Do a little dosado
Allemande the corner again, come back and promeno
Mele Kalikimaka is Hawaiian's way to say
"Merry Christmas to you"

FIGURE    Mainstream, R-H lady progression

1 & 3 you promenade 3/4 around
2 & 4 you do the right & left thru
Do a double pass thru and a cloverleaf my friend
Do a dosado in the middle, full around, swing thru
Turn thru when you meet the partner, swing the corner there
Allemande left new corner, promenade around the square
Mele Kalikimaka is Hawaiian's way to say
"Merry Christmas to you"

MIDDLE BREAK - CLOSER    Mainstream

Allemande left the corner and you do a little dosado
Men star by the left and ladies sway
Turn the partner by the right and the corners allemande
Come back, swing the partner, sides face, grand square
Here we know that Christmas will be green and bright
The sun will shine by day and all the stars at night
Mele Kalikimaka is Hawaiian's way to say
"Merry Christmas to you"
