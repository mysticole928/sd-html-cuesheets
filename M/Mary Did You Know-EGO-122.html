Mary Did You Know?
Michael English; Pentatonix 2014
(EGO 122) Paul Cote
OPENER

Sides face grand square
Mary, did you know...that your Baby Boy
Would one day walk on water?
Mary, did you know...that your Baby Boy
Would save our sons and daughters?
Allemande and weave
Did you know that your Baby Boy...(has come to make you new?) (Has come to make you new?)
(Or Swing and promenade )
This Child that you delivered...will soon deliver you

FIGURE

Heads (Sides) you square thru 4 , four hands around
Then dosado that corner, swing thru and then
You spin your top , walk up and do a right & left thru now
Square thru 3 , three quarters round and then
You swing that corner, promenade
When you kiss your little Baby
You know you kissed the face of God?

MIDDLE BREAK

Sides face grand square
The blind will see, the deaf will hear
The dead will live again!
The lame will leap, the dumb will speak
The praises of The Lamb!
Four ladies promenade once inside the ring
Get back and then you swing and promenade
The lame will leap, the dumb will speak
The praises of The Lamb!

CLOSER

Sides face grand square
The blind will see, the deaf will hear
The dead will live again!
The lame will leap, the dumb will speak
The praises of The Lamb!
Allemande and weave
Did you know...that your Baby Boy...would calm the storm with his hand
The sleeping Child you're holding... (Stretch out the last line)
Is the Great, the Great "I Am"
